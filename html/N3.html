Numbers Chapter 3

1 and~THESE (וְאֵלֶּה / wê'ey'leh) BIRTHING~s (תּוֹלְדֹת / tol'dot) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) and~Mosheh (וּמֹשֶׁה / u'mo'sheh) in~DAY (בְּיוֹם / bê'yom) he~did~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דִּבֶּר / di'ber) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT (אֶת / et) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) in~HILL (בְּהַר / bê'har) Sinai (סִינָי / si'nai)

and these are the birthings of Aharon and Mosheh in the day YHWH spoke with Mosheh on the hill of Sinai,

2 and~THESE (וְאֵלֶּה / wê'ey'leh) TITLE~s (שְׁמוֹת / shê'mot) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) the~FIRSTBORN (הַבְּכוֹר / ha'be'khor) Nadav (נָדָב / na'dav) and~Aviyhu (וַאֲבִיהוּא / wa'a'vi'hu) Elazar (אֶלְעָזָר / el'a'zar) and~Iytamar (וְאִיתָמָר / wê'i'ta'mar)

and these are the titles of the sons of Aharon, the firstborn is Nadav, and Aviyhu, Elazar and Iytamar.

3 THESE (אֵלֶּה / ey'leh) TITLE~s (שְׁמוֹת / shê'mot) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) the~ADMINISTRATOR~s (הַכֹּהֲנִים / ha'ko'ha'nim) the~SMEAR(Verb)~ed(mp) (הַמְּשֻׁחִים / ham'shu'hhim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~much~FILL(Verb) (מִלֵּא / mi'ley) [769] HAND~them(m) (יָדָם / ya'dam) to~>~much~ADORN(Verb) (לְכַהֵן / lê'kha'heyn)

These are the titles of the sons of Aharon, the smeared administrators, who filled his hand to be adorned,

4 and~he~will~DIE(Verb) (וַיָּמָת / wai'ya'mat) Nadav (נָדָב / na'dav) and~Aviyhu (וַאֲבִיהוּא / wa'a'vi'hu) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) in~>~make~COME.NEAR(Verb)~them(m) (בְּהַקְרִבָם / bê'haq'ri'vam) FIRE (אֵשׁ / eysh) BE.STRANGE(Verb)~ing(fs) (זָרָה / za'rah) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) in~WILDERNESS (בְּמִדְבַּר / bê'mid'bar) Sinai (סִינַי / si'nai) and~SON~s (וּבָנִים / u'va'nim) NOT (לֹא / lo) they~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיוּ / hai'u) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) and~he~will~much~ADORN(Verb) (וַיְכַהֵן / wa'ye'kha'heyn) Elazar (אֶלְעָזָר / el'a'zar) and~Iytamar (וְאִיתָמָר / wê'i'ta'mar) UPON (עַל / al) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) FATHER~them(m) (אֲבִיהֶם / a'vi'hem)

and Nadav died, and Aviyhu, to the face of YHWH in their bringing near strange fire to the face of YHWH in the wilderness of Sinai, and sons did not exist for them, so Elazar was adorned, and Iytamar, in place of Aharon their father,

5 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

6 !(ms)~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (הַקְרֵב / haq'reyv) AT (אֶת / et) BRANCH (מַטֵּה / ma'teyh) Lewi (לֵוִי / ley'wi) and~you(ms)~did~make~STAND(Verb) (וְהַעֲמַדְתָּ / wê'ha'a'mad'ta) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~they~did~much~MINISTER(Verb) (וְשֵׁרְתוּ / wê'sheyr'tu) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to)

Bring near the branch of Lewi, and you will make him stand to the face of Aharon the administrator, and they will minister with him,

7 and~they~did~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (וְשָׁמְרוּ / wê'sham'ru) AT (אֶת / et) CHARGE~him (מִשְׁמַרְתּוֹ / mish'mar'to) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) CHARGE (מִשְׁמֶרֶת / mish'me'ret) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~COMPANY (הָעֵדָה / ha'ey'dah) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) to~>~SERVE(Verb) (לַעֲבֹד / la'a'vod) AT (אֶת / et) SERVICE (עֲבֹדַת / a'vo'dat) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan)

and they will safeguard his charge and the charge of all the company to the face of the appointed tent, to serve the service of the dwelling,

8 and~they~did~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (וְשָׁמְרוּ / wê'sham'ru) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) UTENSIL~s (כְּלֵי / kê'ley) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) CHARGE (מִשְׁמֶרֶת / mish'me'ret) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) to~>~SERVE(Verb) (לַעֲבֹד / la'a'vod) AT (אֶת / et) SERVICE (עֲבֹדַת / a'vo'dat) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan)

and they will safeguard all the utensils of the appointed tent, and the charge of the sons of Yisra'eyl, to serve the service of the dwelling,

9 and~you(ms)~did~GIVE(Verb)~& (וְנָתַתָּה / wê'na'ta'tah) AT (אֶת / et) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim) to~Aharon (לְאַהֲרֹן / lê'a'ha'ron) and~to~SON~s~him (וּלְבָנָיו / ul'va'naw) GIVE(Verb)~ed(mp) (נְתוּנִם / nê'tu'nim) GIVE(Verb)~ed(mp) (נְתוּנִם / nê'tu'nim) [770] THEY(m) (הֵמָּה / hey'mah) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) from~AT (מֵאֵת / mey'eyt) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl)

and you will give the ones of Lewi to Aharon and to his sons, each of them are given to him from the sons of Yisra'eyl,

10 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) SON~s~him (בָּנָיו / ba'naw) you(ms)~will~REGISTER(Verb) (תִּפְקֹד / tiph'qod) and~they~did~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (וְשָׁמְרוּ / wê'sham'ru) AT (אֶת / et) ADMINISTRATION~them(m) (כְּהֻנָּתָם / kê'hu'na'tam) and~the~BE.STRANGE(Verb)~ing(ms) (וְהַזָּר / wê'ha'zar) the~INSIDE (הַקָּרֵב / ha'qa'reyv) he~will~be~make~DIE(Verb) (יוּמָת / yu'mat)

and Aharon and his sons you will register, and they will safeguard their administration, and the stranger inside will be killed,

11 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

12 and~I (וַאֲנִי / wa'ani) LOOK (הִנֵּה / hin'neyh) I~did~TAKE(Verb) (לָקַחְתִּי / la'qahh'ti) AT (אֶת / et) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim) from~MIDST (מִתּוֹךְ / mi'tokh) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) UNDER (תַּחַת / ta'hhat) ALL (כָּל / kol) FIRSTBORN (בְּכוֹר / bê'khor) BURSTING (פֶּטֶר / pe'ter) BOWELS (רֶחֶם / re'hhem) from~SON~s (מִבְּנֵי / mi'be'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~they~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיוּ / wê'hai'u) to~~me (לִי / li) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim)

and I, look, I took the ones of Lewi from the midst of the sons of Yisra'eyl in place of all the firstborn bursting the bowels from the sons of Yisra'eyl, and the ones of Lewi exist for me.

13 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) to~~me (לִי / li) ALL (כָּל / kol) FIRSTBORN (בְּכוֹר / bê'khor) in~DAY (בְּיוֹם / bê'yom) >~make~HIT(Verb)~me (הַכֹּתִי / ha'ko'ti) ALL (כָל / khol) FIRSTBORN (בְּכוֹר / bê'khor) in~LAND (בְּאֶרֶץ / bê'e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) I~did~make~SET.APART(Verb) (הִקְדַּשְׁתִּי / hiq'dash'ti) to~~me (לִי / li) ALL (כָל / khol) FIRSTBORN (בְּכוֹר / bê'khor) in~Yisra'eyl (בְּיִשְׂרָאֵל / bê'yis'ra'eyl) from~HUMAN (מֵאָדָם / mey'a'dam) UNTIL (עַד / ad) BEAST (בְּהֵמָה / bê'hey'mah) to~~me (לִי / li) they(m)~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיוּ / yih'yu) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

Given that belonging to me are all the firstborn on the day I hit all the firstborn in the land of Mits'rayim, and I will set apart for me all the firstborn in Yisra'eyl, from the human until the beast, for me they will exist, I am YHWH,

14 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) in~WILDERNESS (בְּמִדְבַּר / bê'mid'bar) Sinai (סִינַי / si'nai) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh in the wilderness of Sinai saying,

15 !(ms)~REGISTER(Verb) (פְּקֹד / pê'qod) AT (אֶת / et) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Lewi (לֵוִי / ley'wi) to~HOUSE (לְבֵית / lê'veyt) FATHER~s~them(m) (אֲבֹתָם / a'vo'tam) to~CLAN~s~them(m) (לְמִשְׁפְּחֹתָם / lê'mish'pe'hho'tam) ALL (כָּל / kol) MALE (זָכָר / za'khar) from~SON (מִבֶּן / mi'ben) NEW.MOON (חֹדֶשׁ / hho'desh) and~UPWARD~unto (וָמַעְלָה / wa'ma'lah) you(ms)~will~REGISTER(Verb)~them(m) (תִּפְקְדֵם / tiph'qe'deym)

Register the sons of Lewi according to their house, according to their clans, every male from the son of a new moon and upward you will register them,

16 and~he~will~REGISTER(Verb) (וַיִּפְקֹד / wai'yiph'qod) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) UPON (עַל / al) MOUTH (פִּי / pi) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~be~much~DIRECT(Verb) (צֻוָּה / tsu'wah)

and Mosheh registered them by the mouth of YHWH just as he directed,

17 and~they(m)~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיִּהְיוּ / wai'yih'yu) THESE (אֵלֶּה / ey'leh) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Lewi (לֵוִי / ley'wi) in~TITLE~s~them(m) (בִּשְׁמֹתָם / bish'mo'tam) Gershon (גֵּרְשׁוֹן / geyr'shon) and~Qehat (וּקְהָת / uq'hat) and~Merari (וּמְרָרִי / um'ra'ri)

and these are the sons of Lewi by their titles, Gershon and Qehat and Merari,

18 and~THESE (וְאֵלֶּה / wê'ey'leh) TITLE~s (שְׁמוֹת / shê'mot) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Gershon (גֵרְשׁוֹן / ger'shon) to~CLAN~s~them(m) (לְמִשְׁפְּחֹתָם / lê'mish'pe'hho'tam) Liyvniy (לִבְנִי / liv'ni) and~Shiymiy (וְשִׁמְעִי / wê'shim'i)

and these are the titles of the sons of Gershon according to their clan, Liyvniy and Shiymiy,

19 and~SON~s (וּבְנֵי / uv'ney) Qehat (קְהָת / qê'hat) to~CLAN~s~them(m) (לְמִשְׁפְּחֹתָם / lê'mish'pe'hho'tam) Amram (עַמְרָם / am'ram) and~Yits'har (וְיִצְהָר / wê'yits'har) Hhevron (חֶבְרוֹן / hhev'ron) and~Uziy'eyl (וְעֻזִּיאֵל / wê'u'zi'eyl)

and the sons of Qehat according to their clans, Amram and Yits'har, Hhevron and Uziy'eyl,

20 and~SON~s (וּבְנֵי / uv'ney) Merari (מְרָרִי / mê'ra'ri) to~CLAN~s~them(m) (לְמִשְׁפְּחֹתָם / lê'mish'pe'hho'tam) Mahh'liy (מַחְלִי / mahh'li) and~Mushiy (וּמוּשִׁי / u'mush'i) THESE (אֵלֶּה / ey'leh) THEY(m) (הֵם / heym) CLAN~s (מִשְׁפְּחֹת / mish'pe'hhot) the~Lewi (הַלֵּוִי / ha'ley'wi) to~HOUSE (לְבֵית / lê'veyt) FATHER~s~them(m) (אֲבֹתָם / a'vo'tam)

and the sons of Merari according to their clans, Mahh'liy and Mushiy, these are they, the clans of Lewi according to the house of their fathers.

21 to~Gershon (לְגֵרְשׁוֹן / lê'ger'shon) CLAN (מִשְׁפַּחַת / mish'pa'hhat) the~Liyvniy (הַלִּבְנִי / ha'liv'ni) and~CLAN (וּמִשְׁפַּחַת / u'mish'pa'hhat) the~Shiymiy (הַשִּׁמְעִי / ha'shim'i) THESE (אֵלֶּה / ey'leh) THEY(m) (הֵם / heym) CLAN~s (מִשְׁפְּחֹת / mish'pe'hhot) the~Gershon~of (הַגֵּרְשֻׁנִּי / ha'geyr'shu'ni)

To Gershon, the clan of Liyvniy and the clan of Shiymiy, these are they, the clans of Gershon.

22 REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp)~them(m) (פְּקֻדֵיהֶם / pê'qu'dey'hem) in~NUMBER (בְּמִסְפַּר / bê'mis'par) ALL (כָּל / kol) MALE (זָכָר / za'khar) from~SON (מִבֶּן / mi'ben) NEW.MOON (חֹדֶשׁ / hho'desh) and~UPWARD~unto (וָמָעְלָה / wa'ma'lah) REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp)~them(m) (פְּקֻדֵיהֶם / pê'qu'dey'hem) SEVEN (שִׁבְעַת / shiv'at) THOUSAND~s (אֲלָפִים / a'la'phim) and~FIVE (וַחֲמֵשׁ / wa'hha'meysh) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot)

Their numbered ones, in number, every male from a son of a new moon and upward, their registered ones are seven thousand and five hundred.

23 CLAN~s (מִשְׁפְּחֹת / mish'pe'hhot) the~Gershon~of (הַגֵּרְשֻׁנִּי / ha'geyr'shu'ni) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan) they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (יַחֲנוּ / ya'hha'nu) SEA~unto (יָמָּה / ya'mah)

The clans of Gershon, behind the dwelling they will camp, unto the west,

24 and~CAPTAIN (וּנְשִׂיא / un'si) HOUSE (בֵית / veyt) FATHER (אָב / av) to~the~Gershon~of (לַגֵּרְשֻׁנִּי / la'gar'shu'ni) Elyasaph (אֶלְיָסָף / el'ya'saph) SON (בֶּן / ben) La'eyl (לָאֵל / la'eyl)

and the captain of the house of the father belonging to Gershon, Elyasaph the son of La'eyl,

25 and~CHARGE (וּמִשְׁמֶרֶת / u'mish'me'ret) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Gershon (גֵרְשׁוֹן / ger'shon) in~TENT (בְּאֹהֶל / bê'o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan) and~the~TENT (וְהָאֹהֶל / wê'ha'o'hel) ROOF.COVERING~him (מִכְסֵהוּ / mikh'sey'hu) and~CANOPY (וּמָסַךְ / u'ma'sakh) OPENING (פֶּתַח / pe'tahh) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd)

and the charge of the sons of Gershon in the appointed tent, the dwelling and the tent, his roof covering and the opening of the appointed tent,
