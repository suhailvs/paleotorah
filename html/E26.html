Exodus Chapter 26

1 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) TEN (עֶשֶׂר / e'ser) TENT.WALL~s (יְרִיעֹת / yê'ri'ot) LINEN (שֵׁשׁ / sheysh) be~make~TWIST.TOGETHER(Verb)~ing(ms) (מָשְׁזָר / mash'zar) and~BLUE (וּתְכֵלֶת / ut'khey'let) and~PURPLE (וְאַרְגָּמָן / wê'ar'ga'man) and~KERMES (וְתֹלַעַת / wê'to'la'at) SCARLET (שָׁנִי / sha'ni) KERUV~s (כְּרֻבִים / kê'ru'vim) WORK (מַעֲשֵׂה / ma'a'seyh) THINK(Verb)~ing(ms) (חֹשֵׁב / hho'sheyv) [455] you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam)

and you will make the dwelling, ten tent walls of twisted linen and blue and purple and kermes of scarlet, you will make them with keruvs of a work of thinking.

2 LENGTH (אֹרֶךְ / o'rekh) the~TENT.WALL (הַיְרִיעָה / hai'ri'ah) the~UNIT (הָאַחַת / ha'a'hhat) EIGHT (שְׁמֹנֶה / shê'mo'neh) and~TEN~s (וְעֶשְׂרִים / wê'es'rim) in~the~FOREARM (בָּאַמָּה / ba'a'mah) and~WIDTH (וְרֹחַב / wê'ro'hhav) FOUR (אַרְבַּע / ar'ba) in~the~FOREARM (בָּאַמָּה / ba'a'mah) the~TENT.WALL (הַיְרִיעָה / hai'ri'ah) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָת / ha'e'hhat) MEASUREMENT (מִדָּה / mi'dah) UNIT (אַחַת / a'hhat) to~ALL (לְכָל / lê'khol) the~TENT.WALL~s (הַיְרִיעֹת / hai'ri'ot)

The length of the one tent wall is eight and twenty by the forearm, and the width is four by the forearm, the one tent wall measurement is one for all the tent walls.

3 FIVE (חֲמֵשׁ / hha'meysh) the~TENT.WALL~s (הַיְרִיעֹת / hai'ri'ot) they(f)~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִּהְיֶיןָ / tih'yey'na) COUPLE(Verb)~ing(fp) (חֹבְרֹת / hhov'rot) WOMAN (אִשָּׁה / i'shah) TO (אֶל / el) SISTER~her (אֲחֹתָהּ / a'hho'tah) and~FIVE (וְחָמֵשׁ / wê'hha'meysh) TENT.WALL~s (יְרִיעֹת / yê'ri'ot) COUPLE(Verb)~ing(fp) (חֹבְרֹת / hhov'rot) WOMAN (אִשָּׁה / i'shah) TO (אֶל / el) SISTER~her (אֲחֹתָהּ / a'hho'tah)

Five of the tent walls will exist, coupling each to her sister, and five tent walls coupling each to her sister,

4 and~you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשִׂיתָ / wê'a'si'ta) LOOP~s (לֻלְאֹת / lul'ot) BLUE (תְּכֵלֶת / tê'khey'let) UPON (עַל / al) LIP (שְׂפַת / sê'phat) the~TENT.WALL (הַיְרִיעָה / hai'ri'ah) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָת / ha'e'hhat) from~EXTREMITY (מִקָּצָה / mi'qa'tsah) in~the~COUPLING (בַּחֹבָרֶת / ba'hho'va'ret) and~SO (וְכֵן / wê'kheyn) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) in~LIP (בִּשְׂפַת / bis'phat) the~TENT.WALL (הַיְרִיעָה / hai'ri'ah) the~OUTER (הַקִּיצוֹנָה / ha'qi'tso'nah) in~the~JOINT (בַּמַּחְבֶּרֶת / ba'mahh'be'ret) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִית / ha'shey'nit)

and you will make loops of blue upon the lip of the one tent wall from the extremity in the coupling, and so you will make in the lip of the outer tent wall in the joint of the second.

5 FIVE~s (חֲמִשִּׁים / hha'mi'shim) LOOP~s (לֻלָאֹת / lu'la'ot) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) in~the~TENT.WALL (בַּיְרִיעָה / bai'ri'ah) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָת / ha'e'hhat) and~FIVE~s (וַחֲמִשִּׁים / wa'hha'mi'shim) LOOP~s (לֻלָאֹת / lu'la'ot) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) in~EXTREMITY (בִּקְצֵה / biq'tseyh) the~TENT.WALL (הַיְרִיעָה / hai'ri'ah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~the~JOINT (בַּמַּחְבֶּרֶת / ba'mahh'be'ret) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִית / ha'shey'nit) make~RECEIVE(Verb)~ing(fp) (מַקְבִּילֹת / maq'bi'lot) the~LOOP~s (הַלֻּלָאֹת / ha'lu'la'ot) WOMAN (אִשָּׁה / i'shah) TO (אֶל / el) SISTER~her (אֲחֹתָהּ / a'hho'tah)

You will make fifty loops in the one tent wall, and you will make fifty loops in the extremity of the tent wall which is in the joint of the second receiving the loops of each to her sister,

6 and~you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשִׂיתָ / wê'a'si'ta) FIVE~s (חֲמִשִּׁים / hha'mi'shim) HOOK~s (קַרְסֵי / qar'sey) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) and~you(ms)~did~much~COUPLE(Verb) (וְחִבַּרְתָּ / wê'hhi'bar'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~TENT.WALL~s (הַיְרִיעֹת / hai'ri'ot) WOMAN (אִשָּׁה / i'shah) TO (אֶל / el) SISTER~her (אֲחֹתָהּ / a'hho'tah) in~the~HOOK~s (בַּקְּרָסִים / baq'ra'sim) and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad)

and you will make fifty hooks of gold, and you will couple the tent walls each to her sister in the hooks, and the dwelling will exist as a unit,

7 and~you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשִׂיתָ / wê'a'si'ta) TENT.WALL~s (יְרִיעֹת / yê'ri'ot) GOAT~s (עִזִּים / i'zim) [456] to~TENT (לְאֹהֶל / lê'o'hel) UPON (עַל / al) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan) ONE (עַשְׁתֵּי / ash'tey) TEN (עֶשְׂרֵה / es'reyh) TENT.WALL~s (יְרִיעֹת / yê'ri'ot) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam)

and you will make the tent walls of goats for the tent upon the dwelling, eleven tent walls you will make.

8 LENGTH (אֹרֶךְ / o'rekh) the~TENT.WALL (הַיְרִיעָה / hai'ri'ah) the~UNIT (הָאַחַת / ha'a'hhat) THREE~s (שְׁלֹשִׁים / shê'lo'shim) in~the~FOREARM (בָּאַמָּה / ba'a'mah) and~WIDTH (וְרֹחַב / wê'ro'hhav) FOUR (אַרְבַּע / ar'ba) in~the~FOREARM (בָּאַמָּה / ba'a'mah) the~TENT.WALL (הַיְרִיעָה / hai'ri'ah) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָת / ha'e'hhat) MEASUREMENT (מִדָּה / mi'dah) UNIT (אַחַת / a'hhat) to~ONE (לְעַשְׁתֵּי / lê'ash'tey) TEN (עֶשְׂרֵה / es'reyh) TENT.WALL~s (יְרִיעֹת / yê'ri'ot)

The length of the one tent wall is thirty by the forearm, and the width is four by the forearm, the one tent wall measurement is one for the eleven tent walls,

9 and~you(ms)~did~much~COUPLE(Verb) (וְחִבַּרְתָּ / wê'hhi'bar'ta) AT (אֶת / et) FIVE (חֲמֵשׁ / hha'meysh) the~TENT.WALL~s (הַיְרִיעֹת / hai'ri'ot) to~STRAND (לְבָד / lê'vad) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) SIX (שֵׁשׁ / sheysh) the~TENT.WALL~s (הַיְרִיעֹת / hai'ri'ot) to~STRAND (לְבָד / lê'vad) and~you(ms)~did~DOUBLE.OVER(Verb) (וְכָפַלְתָּ / wê'kha'phal'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~TENT.WALL (הַיְרִיעָה / hai'ri'ah) the~SIXTH (הַשִּׁשִּׁית / ha'shi'shit) TO (אֶל / el) FOREFRONT (מוּל / mul) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) the~TENT (הָאֹהֶל / ha'o'hel)

and you will couple five of the tent walls alone and six of the tent walls alone, and you will double over the sixth tent wall to the forefront face of the tent,

10 and~you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשִׂיתָ / wê'a'si'ta) FIVE~s (חֲמִשִּׁים / hha'mi'shim) LOOP~s (לֻלָאֹת / lu'la'ot) UPON (עַל / al) LIP (שְׂפַת / sê'phat) the~TENT.WALL (הַיְרִיעָה / hai'ri'ah) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָת / ha'e'hhat) the~OUTER (הַקִּיצֹנָה / ha'qi'tso'nah) in~the~COUPLING (בַּחֹבָרֶת / ba'hho'va'ret) and~FIVE~s (וַחֲמִשִּׁים / wa'hha'mi'shim) LOOP~s (לֻלָאֹת / lu'la'ot) UPON (עַל / al) LIP (שְׂפַת / sê'phat) the~TENT.WALL (הַיְרִיעָה / hai'ri'ah) the~COUPLING (הַחֹבֶרֶת / ha'hho've'ret) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִית / ha'shey'nit)

and you will make fifty loops upon the lip of the one outer tent wall with the coupling, and fifty loops upon the lip of the tent wall of the second coupling,

11 and~you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשִׂיתָ / wê'a'si'ta) HOOK~s (קַרְסֵי / qar'sey) COPPER (נְחֹשֶׁת / nê'hho'shet) FIVE~s (חֲמִשִּׁים / hha'mi'shim) and~you(ms)~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבֵאתָ / wê'hey'vey'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~HOOK~s (הַקְּרָסִים / haq'ra'sim) in~the~LOOP~s (בַּלֻּלָאֹת / ba'lu'la'ot) and~you(ms)~did~much~COUPLE(Verb) (וְחִבַּרְתָּ / wê'hhi'bar'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~TENT (הָאֹהֶל / ha'o'hel) and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad)

and you will make fifty copper hooks, and you will bring the hooks in the loops, and you will couple the tent, and he will exist as a unit,

12 and~OVERHANG (וְסֶרַח / wê'se'rahh) the~EXCEED(Verb)~ing(ms) (הָעֹדֵף / ha'o'deyph) in~TENT.WALL~s (בִּירִיעֹת / bi'ri'ot) the~TENT (הָאֹהֶל / ha'o'hel) HALF (חֲצִי / hha'tsi) the~TENT.WALL (הַיְרִיעָה / hai'ri'ah) the~EXCEED(Verb)~ing(fs) (הָעֹדֶפֶת / ha'o'de'phet) you(ms)~will~OVERHANG(Verb) (תִּסְרַח / tis'rahh) UPON (עַל / al) BACK~s (אֲחֹרֵי / a'hho'rey) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan)

and the overhang of the exceeding part in the tent walls of the tent, half of the tent wall, the exceeding part, you will overhang upon the backs of the dwelling,

13 and~the~FOREARM (וְהָאַמָּה / wê'ha'a'mah) from~THIS (מִזֶּה / mi'zeh) and~the~FOREARM (וְהָאַמָּה / wê'ha'a'mah) from~THIS (מִזֶּה / mi'zeh) in~the~EXCEED(Verb)~ing(ms) (בָּעֹדֵף / ba'o'deyph) in~LENGTH (בְּאֹרֶךְ / bê'o'rekh) TENT.WALL~s (יְרִיעֹת / yê'ri'ot) the~TENT (הָאֹהֶל / ha'o'hel) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) OVERHANG(Verb)~ed(ms) (סָרוּחַ / sa'ru'ahh) UPON (עַל / al) SIDE~s (צִדֵּי / tsi'dey) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan) from~THIS (מִזֶּה / mi'zeh) and~from~THIS (וּמִזֶּה / u'mi'zeh) to~>~much~COVER.OVER(Verb)~him (לְכַסֹּתוֹ / lê'kha'so'to)

and the forearm from this side, and the forearm from that side, in the exceeding part in the length of the tent walls of the tent, will be overhung upon the sides of the dwelling, from this side and from that side to cover him over,

14 and~you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשִׂיתָ / wê'a'si'ta) ROOF.COVERING (מִכְסֶה / mikh'seh) to~the~TENT (לָאֹהֶל / la'o'hel) SKIN~s (עֹרֹת / o'rot) BUCK~s (אֵילִם / ey'lim) be~much~BE.RED(Verb)~ing(mp) (מְאָדָּמִים / mê'a'da'mim) and~ROOF.COVERING (וּמִכְסֵה / u'mikh'seyh) SKIN~s (עֹרֹת / o'rot) BADGER~s (תְּחָשִׁים / tê'hha'shim) from~to~UPWARD~unto (מִלְמָעְלָה / mil'ma'lah)

and you will make a roof covering for the tent, skins of bucks being red, and a roof covering of skins of badgers on top,

15 and~you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשִׂיתָ / wê'a'si'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~BOARD~s (הַקְּרָשִׁים / haq'ra'shim) to~the~DWELLING (לַמִּשְׁכָּן / la'mish'kan) TREE~s (עֲצֵי / a'tsey) ACACIA~s (שִׁטִּים / shi'tim) STAND(Verb)~ing(mp) (עֹמְדִים / om'dim)

and you will make the boards for the dwelling of wood of acacia standing up.

16 TEN (עֶשֶׂר / e'ser) FOREARM~s (אַמּוֹת / a'mot) LENGTH (אֹרֶךְ / o'rekh) the~BOARD (הַקָּרֶשׁ / ha'qa'resh) and~FOREARM (וְאַמָּה / wê'a'mah) and~HALF (וַחֲצִי / wa'hha'tsi) the~FOREARM (הָאַמָּה / ha'a'mah) WIDTH (רֹחַב / ro'hhav) the~BOARD (הַקֶּרֶשׁ / ha'qe'resh) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad)

Ten forearms is the length of the board, and a forearm and a half a forearm is the width of the one board.

17 TWO (שְׁתֵּי / shê'tey) HAND~s (יָדוֹת / ya'dot) [457] to~the~BOARD (לַקֶּרֶשׁ / la'qe'resh) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad) be~much~JOINED.TOGETHER(Verb)~ing(fp) (מְשֻׁלָּבֹת / mê'shu'la'vot) WOMAN (אִשָּׁה / i'shah) TO (אֶל / el) SISTER~her (אֲחֹתָהּ / a'hho'tah) SO (כֵּן / keyn) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) to~ALL (לְכֹל / lê'khol) BOARD~s (קַרְשֵׁי / qar'shey) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan)

Two hands for the one board for being joined together, each to her sister, so you will make for all the boards of the dwelling,

18 and~you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשִׂיתָ / wê'a'si'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~BOARD~s (הַקְּרָשִׁים / haq'ra'shim) to~the~DWELLING (לַמִּשְׁכָּן / la'mish'kan) TEN~s (עֶשְׂרִים / es'rim) BOARD (קֶרֶשׁ / qe'resh) to~EDGE (לִפְאַת / liph'at) SOUTH~unto (נֶגְבָּה / neg'bah) SOUTHWARD~unto (תֵימָנָה / tey'ma'nah)

and you will make the boards for the dwelling, twenty boards for the edge unto the south, unto the southward,

19 and~FOUR~s (וְאַרְבָּעִים / wê'ar'ba'im) FOOTING~s (אַדְנֵי / ad'ney) SILVER (כֶסֶף / khe'seph) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) UNDER (תַּחַת / ta'hhat) TEN~s (עֶשְׂרִים / es'rim) the~BOARD (הַקָּרֶשׁ / ha'qa'resh) TWO (שְׁנֵי / shê'ney) FOOTING~s (אֲדָנִים / a'da'nim) UNDER (תַּחַת / ta'hhat) the~BOARD (הַקֶּרֶשׁ / ha'qe'resh) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad) to~TWO (לִשְׁתֵּי / lish'tey) HAND~s~him (יְדֹתָיו / yê'do'taw) [458] and~TWO (וּשְׁנֵי / ush'ney) FOOTING~s (אֲדָנִים / a'da'nim) UNDER (תַּחַת / ta'hhat) the~BOARD (הַקֶּרֶשׁ / ha'qe'resh) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad) to~TWO (לִשְׁתֵּי / lish'tey) HAND~s~him (יְדֹתָיו / yê'do'taw)

and you will make forty footings of silver under twenty of the boards, two footings under the one board for his two hands, and two footings under the other board for his two hands,

20 and~to~RIB (וּלְצֶלַע / ul'tse'la) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִית / ha'shey'nit) to~EDGE (לִפְאַת / liph'at) NORTH (צָפוֹן / tsa'phon) TEN~s (עֶשְׂרִים / es'rim) BOARD (קָרֶשׁ / qa'resh)

and for the second rib of the dwelling, to the north edge, is twenty boards,
