Leviticus Chapter 20

1 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

2 and~TO (וְאֶל / wê'el) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) you(ms)~will~SAY(Verb) (תֹּאמַר / to'mar) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) from~SON~s (מִבְּנֵי / mi'be'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~FROM (וּמִן / u'min) the~IMMIGRANT (הַגֵּר / ha'geyr) the~IMMIGRATE(Verb)~ing(ms) (הַגָּר / ha'gar) in~Yisra'eyl (בְּיִשְׂרָאֵל / bê'yis'ra'eyl) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~GIVE(Verb) (יִתֵּן / yi'teyn) from~SEED~him (מִזַּרְעוֹ / mi'zar'o) to~the~Molekh (לַמֹּלֶךְ / la'mo'lekh) >~DIE(Verb) (מוֹת / mot) he~will~be~make~DIE(Verb) (יוּמָת / yu'mat) PEOPLE (עַם / am) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) they(m)~will~KILL.BY.STONING(Verb)~him (יִרְגְּמֻהוּ / yir'ge'mu'hu) in~the~STONE (בָאָבֶן / va'a'ven)

and to the sons of Yisra'eyl you will say, each man from the sons of Yisra'eyl and from the immigrant immigrating in Yisra'eyl, who give from his seed to Molekh, he will certainly be killed, the people of the land will kill him by stoning with the stone,

3 and~I (וַאֲנִי / wa'ani) I~will~GIVE(Verb) (אֶתֵּן / e'teyn) AT (אֶת / et) FACE~s~me (פָּנַי / pa'nai) in~the~MAN (בָּאִישׁ / ba'ish) the~HE (הַהוּא / ha'hu) and~I~did~make~CUT(Verb) (וְהִכְרַתִּי / wê'hikh'ra'ti) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) from~INSIDE (מִקֶּרֶב / mi'qe'rev) PEOPLE~him (עַמּוֹ / a'mo) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) from~SEED~him (מִזַּרְעוֹ / mi'zar'o) he~did~GIVE(Verb) (נָתַן / na'tan) to~the~Molekh (לַמֹּלֶךְ / la'mo'lekh) to~THAT (לְמַעַן / lê'ma'an) he~did~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (טַמֵּא / ta'mey) AT (אֶת / et) SANCTUARY~me (מִקְדָּשִׁי / miq'da'shi) and~to~>~much~DRILL(Verb) (וּלְחַלֵּל / ul'hha'leyl) AT (אֶת / et) TITLE (שֵׁם / sheym) SPECIAL~me (קָדְשִׁי / qad'shi)

and I, I will give my face in that man, and I will cause him to be cut from inside his people, given that from his seed he gave to Molekh, because of that he dirtied my sanctuary, and defiled my special title,

4 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) >~make~BE.OUT.OF.SIGHT(Verb) (הַעְלֵם / ha'leym) they(m)~will~make~BE.OUT.OF.SIGHT(Verb) (יַעְלִימוּ / ya'li'mu) PEOPLE (עַם / am) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) AT (אֶת / et) EYE~s2~them(m) (עֵינֵיהֶם / ay'ney'hem) FROM (מִן / min) the~MAN (הָאִישׁ / ha'ish) the~HE (הַהוּא / ha'hu) in~>~GIVE(Verb)~him (בְּתִתּוֹ / bê'ti'to) from~SEED~him (מִזַּרְעוֹ / mi'zar'o) to~the~Molekh (לַמֹּלֶךְ / la'mo'lekh) to~EXCEPT (לְבִלְתִּי / lê'vil'ti) >~make~DIE(Verb) (הָמִית / ha'mit) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to)

and if the people of the land will surely cause their eyes to be out of sight from that man, in his giving from his seed to Molekh, to not kill him,

5 and~I~did~PLACE(Verb) (וְשַׂמְתִּי / wê'sam'ti) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) AT (אֶת / et) FACE~s~me (פָּנַי / pa'nai) in~the~MAN (בָּאִישׁ / ba'ish) the~HE (הַהוּא / ha'hu) and~in~CLAN~him (וּבְמִשְׁפַּחְתּוֹ / uv'mish'pahh'to) and~I~did~make~CUT(Verb) (וְהִכְרַתִּי / wê'hikh'ra'ti) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~BE.A.HARLOT(Verb)~ing(mp) (הַזֹּנִים / ha'zo'nim) AFTER~him (אַחֲרָיו / a'hha'raw) to~>~BE.A.HARLOT(Verb) (לִזְנוֹת / liz'not) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) the~Molekh (הַמֹּלֶךְ / ha'mo'lekh) from~INSIDE (מִקֶּרֶב / mi'qe'rev) PEOPLE~them(m) (עַמָּם / a'mam)

then I will place my face in that man and in his clan, and I will cause him and all the ones being a harlot after him, being a harlot after Molekh, to be cut from inside their people,

6 and~the~SOUL (וְהַנֶּפֶשׁ / wê'ha'ne'phesh) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) she~will~TURN(Verb) (תִּפְנֶה / tiph'neh) TO (אֶל / el) the~NECROMANCER~s (הָאֹבֹת / ha'o'vot) and~TO (וְאֶל / wê'el) the~KNOWER~s (הַיִּדְּעֹנִים / hai'yid'o'nim) to~>~BE.A.HARLOT(Verb) (לִזְנוֹת / liz'not) AFTER~them(m) (אַחֲרֵיהֶם / a'hha'rey'hem) and~I~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַתִּי / wê'na'ta'ti) AT (אֶת / et) FACE~s~me (פָּנַי / pa'nai) in~the~SOUL (בַּנֶּפֶשׁ / ba'ne'phesh) the~SHE (הַהִוא / ha'hi) and~I~did~make~CUT(Verb) (וְהִכְרַתִּי / wê'hikh'ra'ti) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) from~INSIDE (מִקֶּרֶב / mi'qe'rev) PEOPLE~him (עַמּוֹ / a'mo)

and the soul that will turn to the necromancers and to the knowers, to be a harlot after them, then I will give my face in that soul, and I will cause him to be cut from inside his people,

7 and~you(mp)~did~self~SET.APART(Verb) (וְהִתְקַדִּשְׁתֶּם / wê'hit'qa'dish'tem) and~you(mp)~did~EXIST(Verb) (וִהְיִיתֶם / wih'yi'tem) UNIQUE~s (קְדֹשִׁים / qê'do'shim) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem)

and you will set yourself apart, and you will exist as unique ones, given that I am YHWH your Elohiym,

8 and~you(mp)~did~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (וּשְׁמַרְתֶּם / ush'mar'tem) AT (אֶת / et) CUSTOM~s~me (חֻקֹּתַי / hhu'qo'tai) and~you(mp)~did~DO(Verb) (וַעֲשִׂיתֶם / wa'a'si'tem) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) much~SET.APART(Verb)~ing(ms)~you(ms) (מְקַדִּשְׁכֶם / mê'qa'dish'khem)

and you will safeguard my customs, and you will do them, I am YHWH setting you apart.

9 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~much~BELITTLE(Verb) (יְקַלֵּל / yê'qa'leyl) AT (אֶת / et) FATHER~him (אָבִיו / a'viw) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) MOTHER~him (אִמּוֹ / i'mo) >~DIE(Verb) (מוֹת / mot) he~will~be~make~DIE(Verb) (יוּמָת / yu'mat) FATHER~him (אָבִיו / a'viw) and~MOTHER~him (וְאִמּוֹ / wê'i'mo) he~did~much~BELITTLE(Verb) (קִלֵּל / qi'leyl) BLOOD~s~him (דָּמָיו / da'maw) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo)

Given that each man which will belittle his father and his mother, he will certainly be killed, he belittled his father and his mother, his blood is on him,

10 and~MAN (וְאִישׁ / wê'ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~COMMIT.ADULTERY(Verb) (יִנְאַף / yin'aph) AT (אֶת / et) WOMAN (אֵשֶׁת / ey'shet) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~COMMIT.ADULTERY(Verb) (יִנְאַף / yin'aph) AT (אֶת / et) WOMAN (אֵשֶׁת / ey'shet) [735] COMPANION~him (רֵעֵהוּ / rey'ey'hu) >~DIE(Verb) (מוֹת / mot) he~will~be~make~DIE(Verb) (יוּמַת / yu'mat) the~COMMIT.ADULTERY(Verb)~ing(ms) (הַנֹּאֵף / ha'no'eyph) and~the~COMMIT.ADULTERY(Verb)~ing(fs) (וְהַנֹּאָפֶת / wê'ha'no'a'phet) [736]

and a man that will commit adultery with the woman of his companion, will certainly be killed, the one committing adultery and the one committing adultery,

11 and~MAN (וְאִישׁ / wê'ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~LIE.DOWN(Verb) (יִשְׁכַּב / yish'kav) AT (אֶת / et) WOMAN (אֵשֶׁת / ey'shet) FATHER~him (אָבִיו / a'viw) NAKEDNESS (עֶרְוַת / er'wat) FATHER~him (אָבִיו / a'viw) he~did~much~REMOVE.THE.COVER(Verb) (גִּלָּה / gi'lah) >~DIE(Verb) (מוֹת / mot) they~will~be~make~DIE(Verb) (יוּמְתוּ / yu'me'tu) TWO~them(m) (שְׁנֵיהֶם / shê'ney'hem) BLOOD~s~them(m) (דְּמֵיהֶם / dê'mey'hem) in~~them(m) (בָּם / bam)

and the man that will lie down with the woman of his father, he removed the cover of the nakedness of his father, the two of them will certainly be killed, their blood is on them,

12 and~MAN (וְאִישׁ / wê'ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~LIE.DOWN(Verb) (יִשְׁכַּב / yish'kav) AT (אֶת / et) DAUGHTER-IN-LAW~him (כַּלָּתוֹ / ka'la'to) >~DIE(Verb) (מוֹת / mot) they~will~be~make~DIE(Verb) (יוּמְתוּ / yu'me'tu) TWO~them(m) (שְׁנֵיהֶם / shê'ney'hem) UNNATURAL.MIX (תֶּבֶל / te'vel) they~did~DO(Verb) (עָשׂוּ / a'su) BLOOD~s~them(m) (דְּמֵיהֶם / dê'mey'hem) in~~them(m) (בָּם / bam)

and a man who will lie down with his daughter-in-law, the two of them will certainly be killed, they did an unnatural mix, their blood is on them,

13 and~MAN (וְאִישׁ / wê'ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~LIE.DOWN(Verb) (יִשְׁכַּב / yish'kav) AT (אֶת / et) MALE (זָכָר / za'khar) LYING.PLACE~s (מִשְׁכְּבֵי / mish'ke'vey) WOMAN (אִשָּׁה / i'shah) DISGUSTING (תּוֹעֵבָה / to'ey'vah) they~did~DO(Verb) (עָשׂוּ / a'su) TWO~them(m) (שְׁנֵיהֶם / shê'ney'hem) >~DIE(Verb) (מוֹת / mot) they~will~be~make~DIE(Verb) (יוּמָתוּ / yu'ma'tu) BLOOD~s~them(m) (דְּמֵיהֶם / dê'mey'hem) in~~them(m) (בָּם / bam)

and a man who will lie down with a male, lying places of a woman, the two of them did a disgusting thing, they will certainly be killed, their blood is on them,

14 and~MAN (וְאִישׁ / wê'ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~TAKE(Verb) (יִקַּח / yi'qahh) AT (אֶת / et) WOMAN (אִשָּׁה / i'shah) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) MOTHER~her (אִמָּהּ / i'mah) MISCHIEF (זִמָּה / zi'mah) SHE (הִוא / hi) in~the~FIRE (בָּאֵשׁ / ba'eysh) they(m)~will~CREMATE(Verb) (יִשְׂרְפוּ / yis're'phu) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) and~AT~them(f) (וְאֶתְהֶן / wê'et'hen) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) she~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִהְיֶה / tih'yeh) MISCHIEF (זִמָּה / zi'mah) in~MIDST~you(mp) (בְּתוֹכְכֶם / bê'tokh'khem)

and a man who will take a woman and her mother, this is mischief, they will cremate him and them in the fire, and mischief will not exist in your midst,

15 and~MAN (וְאִישׁ / wê'ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~GIVE(Verb) (יִתֵּן / yi'teyn) COPULATION~him (שְׁכָבְתּוֹ / shê'khav'to) in~BEAST (בִּבְהֵמָה / biv'hey'mah) >~DIE(Verb) (מוֹת / mot) he~will~be~make~DIE(Verb) (יוּמָת / yu'mat) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~BEAST (הַבְּהֵמָה / ha'be'hey'mah) they(m)~will~KILL(Verb) (תַּהֲרֹגוּ / ta'ha'ro'gu)

and a man who will give his copulation in a beast, he will certainly be killed, and they will kill the beast,

16 and~WOMAN (וְאִשָּׁה / wê'i'shah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~will~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תִּקְרַב / tiq'rav) TO (אֶל / el) ALL (כָּל / kol) BEAST (בְּהֵמָה / bê'hey'mah) to~>~BE.SQUARE(Verb)~her (לְרִבְעָה / lê'riv'ah) [737] AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) and~you(ms)~did~KILL(Verb) (וְהָרַגְתָּ / wê'ha'rag'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~WOMAN (הָאִשָּׁה / ha'i'shah) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~BEAST (הַבְּהֵמָה / ha'be'hey'mah) >~DIE(Verb) (מוֹת / mot) they~will~be~make~DIE(Verb) (יוּמָתוּ / yu'ma'tu) BLOOD~s~them(m) (דְּמֵיהֶם / dê'mey'hem) in~~them(m) (בָּם / bam)

and a woman who will come near to any beast to be squared with her, then you will kill the woman and the beast, they will certainly be killed, their blood is on them,

17 and~MAN (וְאִישׁ / wê'ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~TAKE(Verb) (יִקַּח / yi'qahh) AT (אֶת / et) SISTER~him (אֲחֹתוֹ / a'hho'to) DAUGHTER (בַּת / bat) FATHER~him (אָבִיו / a'viw) OR (אוֹ / o) DAUGHTER (בַת / vat) MOTHER~him (אִמּוֹ / i'mo) and~he~did~SEE(Verb) (וְרָאָה / wê'ra'ah) AT (אֶת / et) NAKEDNESS~her (עֶרְוָתָהּ / er'wa'tah) and~SHE (וְהִיא / wê'hi) you(ms)~will~SEE(Verb) (תִרְאֶה / tir'eh) AT (אֶת / et) NAKEDNESS~him (עֶרְוָתוֹ / er'wa'to) KINDNESS (חֶסֶד / hhe'sed) [738] HE (הוּא / hu) and~they~did~be~CUT(Verb) (וְנִכְרְתוּ / wê'nikh're'tu) to~EYE~s2 (לְעֵינֵי / lê'ey'ney) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) PEOPLE~them(m) (עַמָּם / a'mam) NAKEDNESS (עֶרְוַת / er'wat) SISTER~him (אֲחֹתוֹ / a'hho'to) he~did~much~REMOVE.THE.COVER(Verb) (גִּלָּה / gi'lah) TWISTEDNESS~him (עֲוֹנוֹ / a'o'no) he~will~LIFT.UP(Verb) (יִשָּׂא / yi'sa)

and a man who will take his sister, the daughter of his father or the daughter of his mother, and he will see her nakedness, and she will see his nakedness, this is kindness, and they will be cut to the eyes of the sons of their people, he removed the cover of the nakedness of his sister, he will lift up his twistedness,

18 and~MAN (וְאִישׁ / wê'ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~LIE.DOWN(Verb) (יִשְׁכַּב / yish'kav) AT (אֶת / et) WOMAN (אִשָּׁה / i'shah) ILLNESS (דָּוָה / da'wah) and~he~did~much~REMOVE.THE.COVER(Verb) (וְגִלָּה / wê'gi'lah) AT (אֶת / et) NAKEDNESS~her (עֶרְוָתָהּ / er'wa'tah) AT (אֶת / et) FOUNTAIN~her (מְקֹרָהּ / mê'qo'rah) he~did~make~UNCOVER(Verb) (הֶעֱרָה / he'e'rah) and~SHE (וְהִיא / wê'hi) she~did~much~REMOVE.THE.COVER(Verb) (גִּלְּתָה / gil'tah) AT (אֶת / et) FOUNTAIN (מְקוֹר / mê'qor) BLOOD~s~her (דָּמֶיהָ / da'mey'ah) and~they~did~be~CUT(Verb) (וְנִכְרְתוּ / wê'nikh're'tu) TWO~them(m) (שְׁנֵיהֶם / shê'ney'hem) from~INSIDE (מִקֶּרֶב / mi'qe'rev) PEOPLE~them(m) (עַמָּם / a'mam)

and a man who will lay down with a woman of illness, and he will remove the cover of her nakedness, he caused the uncovering of her fountain, and she, she removed the cover of the fountain of her bloodshed, and the two of them will be cut from inside their people,
