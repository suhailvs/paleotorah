Leviticus Chapter 13

1 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) and~TO (וְאֶל / wê'el) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh and to Aharon, saying,

2 HUMAN (אָדָם / a'dam) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) in~SKIN (בְעוֹר / vê'or) FLESH~him (בְּשָׂרוֹ / bê'sar'o) >~LIFT.UP(Verb) (שְׂאֵת / sê'eyt) OR (אוֹ / o) SCAB (סַפַּחַת / sa'pa'hhat) OR (אוֹ / o) BRIGHT.SPOT (בַהֶרֶת / va'he'ret) and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) in~SKIN (בְעוֹר / vê'or) FLESH~him (בְּשָׂרוֹ / bê'sar'o) to~TOUCH (לְנֶגַע / lê'ne'ga) INFECTION (צָרָעַת / tsa'ra'at) and~he~did~be~make~COME(Verb) (וְהוּבָא / wê'hu'va) TO (אֶל / el) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) OR (אוֹ / o) TO (אֶל / el) UNIT (אַחַד / a'hhad) from~SON~s~him (מִבָּנָיו / mi'ba'naw) the~ADMINISTRATOR~s (הַכֹּהֲנִים / ha'ko'ha'nim)

A human that has in the skin of his flesh a lifting up or a scab or a bright spot, and has in the skin of his flesh a plague of infection, then he will be brought to Aharon the administrator or to one of his sons the administrators,

3 and~he~did~SEE(Verb) (וְרָאָה / wê'ra'ah) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) AT (אֶת / et) the~TOUCH (הַנֶּגַע / ha'ne'ga) in~SKIN (בְּעוֹר / bê'or) the~FLESH (הַבָּשָׂר / ha'ba'sar) and~HAIR (וְשֵׂעָר / wê'sey'ar) in~the~TOUCH (בַּנֶּגַע / ba'ne'ga) he~did~OVERTURN(Verb) (הָפַךְ / ha'phakh) WHITE (לָבָן / la'van) and~APPEARANCE (וּמַרְאֵה / u'mar'eyh) the~TOUCH (הַנֶּגַע / ha'ne'ga) SUNKEN (עָמֹק / a'moq) from~SKIN (מֵעוֹר / mey'or) FLESH~him (בְּשָׂרוֹ / bê'sar'o) TOUCH (נֶגַע / ne'ga) INFECTION (צָרַעַת / tsa'ra'at) HE (הוּא / hu) and~he~did~SEE(Verb)~him (וְרָאָהוּ / wê'ra'a'hu) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~he~did~much~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (וְטִמֵּא / wê'ti'mey) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to)

and the administrator will see the plague in the skin of the flesh, and a hair in the plague turned white, and the appearance of the plague is sunken from the skin of his flesh, he is the plague of infection, and the administrator will see him and he will declare him dirty,

4 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) BRIGHT.SPOT (בַּהֶרֶת / ba'he'ret) WHITE (לְבָנָה / lê'va'nah) SHE (הִוא / hi) in~SKIN (בְּעוֹר / bê'or) FLESH~him (בְּשָׂרוֹ / bê'sar'o) and~SUNKEN (וְעָמֹק / wê'a'moq) WITHOUT (אֵין / eyn) APPEARANCE~her (מַרְאֶהָ / mar'e'ah) FROM (מִן / min) the~SKIN (הָעוֹר / ha'or) and~HAIR (וּשְׂעָרָה / us'a'rah) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~did~OVERTURN(Verb) (הָפַךְ / ha'phakh) WHITE (לָבָן / la'van) and~he~did~make~SHUT(Verb) (וְהִסְגִּיר / wê'his'gir) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) AT (אֶת / et) the~TOUCH (הַנֶּגַע / ha'ne'ga) SEVEN (שִׁבְעַת / shiv'at) DAY~s (יָמִים / ya'mim)

and if the bright spot is white, she is in the skin of his flesh and her appearance is not sunken from the skin and a hair is not turned white, then the administrator will shut the plague seven days,

5 and~he~did~SEE(Verb)~him (וְרָאָהוּ / wê'ra'a'hu) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) in~the~DAY (בַּיּוֹם / ba'yom) the~SEVENTH (הַשְּׁבִיעִי / hash'vi'i) and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) the~TOUCH (הַנֶּגַע / ha'ne'ga) he~did~STAND(Verb) (עָמַד / a'mad) in~EYE~s2~him (בְּעֵינָיו / bê'ey'naw) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~did~SPREAD.ACROSS(Verb) (פָשָׂה / pha'sah) the~TOUCH (הַנֶּגַע / ha'ne'ga) in~the~SKIN (בָּעוֹר / ba'or) and~he~did~make~SHUT(Verb)~him (וְהִסְגִּירוֹ / wê'his'gi'ro) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) SEVEN (שִׁבְעַת / shiv'at) DAY~s (יָמִים / ya'mim) SECOND (שֵׁנִית / shey'nit)

and the administrator will see him in the seventh day and look, the plague stood in his eyes, the plague did not spread across the skin, then the administrator will cause him to be shut a second seven days,

6 and~he~did~SEE(Verb) (וְרָאָה / wê'ra'ah) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) in~the~DAY (בַּיּוֹם / ba'yom) the~SEVENTH (הַשְּׁבִיעִי / hash'vi'i) SECOND (שֵׁנִית / shey'nit) and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) DIMNESS (כֵּהָה / key'hah) the~TOUCH (הַנֶּגַע / ha'ne'ga) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~did~SPREAD.ACROSS(Verb) (פָשָׂה / pha'sah) the~TOUCH (הַנֶּגַע / ha'ne'ga) in~the~SKIN (בָּעוֹר / ba'or) and~he~did~much~BE.CLEAN(Verb)~him (וְטִהֲרוֹ / wê'ti'ha'ro) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) SCAB (מִסְפַּחַת / mis'pa'hhat) SHE (הִיא / hi) and~he~did~much~WASH(Verb) (וְכִבֶּס / wê'khi'bes) GARMENT~s~him (בְּגָדָיו / bê'ga'daw) and~he~did~BE.CLEAN(Verb) (וְטָהֵר / wê'ta'har)

and the administrator will see him in the second seventh day, and look, dimness of the plague and the plague did not spread across the skin, then the administrator will declare him clean, she is a scab, and he will wash his garments and he will be clean,

7 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) >~SPREAD.ACROSS(Verb) (פָּשֹׂה / pa'soh) she~will~SEIZE.HOLD(Verb) (תִפְשֶׂה / tiph'seh) the~SCAB (הַמִּסְפַּחַת / ha'mis'pa'hhat) in~the~SKIN (בָּעוֹר / ba'or) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) >~be~SEE(Verb)~him (הֵרָאֹתוֹ / hey'ra'o'to) TO (אֶל / el) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) to~CLEAN~him (לְטָהֳרָתוֹ / lê'ta'ha'ra'to) and~he~did~be~SEE(Verb) (וְנִרְאָה / wê'nir'ah) SECOND (שֵׁנִית / shey'nit) TO (אֶל / el) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn)

but if spreading across, the scab of the skin will seize hold, after he appears to the administrator for his cleanliness, he will appear a second time to the administrator,

8 and~he~did~SEE(Verb) (וְרָאָה / wê'ra'ah) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) she~did~SPREAD.ACROSS(Verb) (פָּשְׂתָה / pas'tah) the~SCAB (הַמִּסְפַּחַת / ha'mis'pa'hhat) in~the~SKIN (בָּעוֹר / ba'or) and~he~did~much~BE.DIRTY(Verb)~him (וְטִמְּאוֹ / wê'tim'o) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) INFECTION (צָרַעַת / tsa'ra'at) SHE (הִוא / hi)

and the administrator will see, and look, the scab spread across the skin, and the administrator will declare him dirty, she is an infection.

9 TOUCH (נֶגַע / ne'ga) INFECTION (צָרַעַת / tsa'ra'at) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) she~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִהְיֶה / tih'yeh) in~HUMAN (בְּאָדָם / bê'a'dam) and~he~did~be~make~COME(Verb) (וְהוּבָא / wê'hu'va) TO (אֶל / el) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn)

A plague of infection that exists in the human will be brought to the administrator,

10 and~he~did~SEE(Verb) (וְרָאָה / wê'ra'ah) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) >~LIFT.UP(Verb) (שְׂאֵת / sê'eyt) WHITE (לְבָנָה / lê'va'nah) in~the~SKIN (בָּעוֹר / ba'or) and~SHE (וְהִיא / wê'hi) she~did~OVERTURN(Verb) (הָפְכָה / haph'khah) HAIR (שֵׂעָר / sey'ar) WHITE (לָבָן / la'van) and~REVIVING (וּמִחְיַת / u'mihh'yat) [693] FLESH (בָּשָׂר / ba'sar) LIVING (חַי / hhai) in~the~ELEVATION (בַּשְׂאֵת / bas'eyt) [694]

and the administrator will see, and look, a lifting up of white on the skin, and the hair turned white, and a reviving of the living flesh in the elevation.

11 INFECTION (צָרַעַת / tsa'ra'at) be~SLEEP(Verb)~ing(fs) (נוֹשֶׁנֶת / no'she'net) SHE (הִוא / hi) in~SKIN (בְּעוֹר / bê'or) FLESH~him (בְּשָׂרוֹ / bê'sar'o) and~he~did~much~BE.DIRTY(Verb)~him (וְטִמְּאוֹ / wê'tim'o) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~make~SHUT(Verb)~him (יַסְגִּרֶנּוּ / yas'gi're'nu) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) DIRTY (טָמֵא / ta'mey) HE (הוּא / hu)

It is an infection, she is sleeping in the skin of his flesh, and the administrator will declare him dirty, he will not cause him shut given that he is dirty,

12 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) >~BURST.OUT(Verb) (פָּרוֹחַ / pa'ro'ahh) she~will~BURST.OUT(Verb) (תִּפְרַח / tiph'rahh) the~INFECTION (הַצָּרַעַת / ha'tsa'ra'at) in~the~SKIN (בָּעוֹר / ba'or) and~she~did~much~COVER.OVER(Verb) (וְכִסְּתָה / wê'khis'tah) the~INFECTION (הַצָּרַעַת / ha'tsa'ra'at) AT (אֵת / eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) SKIN (עוֹר / or) the~TOUCH (הַנֶּגַע / ha'ne'ga) from~HEAD~him (מֵרֹאשׁוֹ / mey'ro'sho) and~UNTIL (וְעַד / wê'ad) FOOT~s2~him (רַגְלָיו / rag'law) to~ALL (לְכָל / lê'khol) APPEARANCE (מַרְאֵה / mar'eyh) EYE~s2 (עֵינֵי / ey'ney) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) [695]

and if the infection in the skin will completely burst out, and the infection will cover over all the skin of the plague, from his head to his feet to all the appearance of the eyes of the administrator,

13 and~he~did~SEE(Verb) (וְרָאָה / wê'ra'ah) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) she~did~much~COVER.OVER(Verb) (כִסְּתָה / khis'tah) the~INFECTION (הַצָּרַעַת / ha'tsa'ra'at) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) FLESH~him (בְּשָׂרוֹ / bê'sar'o) and~he~did~much~BE.CLEAN(Verb) (וְטִהַר / wê'ti'har) AT (אֶת / et) the~TOUCH (הַנָּגַע / ha'na'ga) ALL~him (כֻּלּוֹ / ku'lo) he~did~OVERTURN(Verb) (הָפַךְ / ha'phakh) WHITE (לָבָן / la'van) CLEAN (טָהוֹר / ta'hor) HE (הוּא / hu)

and the administrator will see, and look, the infection covered over all his flesh, then the plague of all of him will be declared clean, he turned white, he is clean,

14 and~in~DAY (וּבְיוֹם / uv'yom) >~be~SEE(Verb) (הֵרָאוֹת / hey'ra'ot) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo) FLESH (בָּשָׂר / ba'sar) LIVING (חַי / hhai) [696] he~will~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (יִטְמָא / yit'ma)

and in the day living flesh appears in him, he will be dirty,

15 and~he~did~SEE(Verb) (וְרָאָה / wê'ra'ah) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) AT (אֶת / et) the~FLESH (הַבָּשָׂר / ha'ba'sar) the~LIVING (הַחַי / ha'hhai) and~he~did~much~BE.DIRTY(Verb)~him (וְטִמְּאוֹ / wê'tim'o) the~FLESH (הַבָּשָׂר / ha'ba'sar) the~LIVING (הַחַי / ha'hhai) DIRTY (טָמֵא / ta'mey) HE (הוּא / hu) INFECTION (צָרַעַת / tsa'ra'at) HE (הוּא / hu)

and the administrator will see the living flesh, and he will declare him dirty, the living flesh is dirty, he is an infection.

16 OR (אוֹ / o) GIVEN.THAT (כִי / khi) he~will~TURN.BACK(Verb) (יָשׁוּב / ya'shuv) the~FLESH (הַבָּשָׂר / ha'ba'sar) the~LIVING (הַחַי / ha'hhai) and~he~did~be~OVERTURN(Verb) (וְנֶהְפַּךְ / wê'neh'pakh) to~Lavan (לְלָבָן / lê'la'van) and~he~did~COME(Verb) (וּבָא / u'va) TO (אֶל / el) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn)

Or given that the living flesh will turn back, and he was turned to white, and he will come to the administrator,

17 and~he~did~SEE(Verb)~him (וְרָאָהוּ / wê'ra'a'hu) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) he~did~be~OVERTURN(Verb) (נֶהְפַּךְ / neh'pakh) the~TOUCH (הַנֶּגַע / ha'ne'ga) to~WHITE (לְלָבָן / lê'la'van) and~he~did~much~BE.CLEAN(Verb) (וְטִהַר / wê'ti'har) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) AT (אֶת / et) the~TOUCH (הַנֶּגַע / ha'ne'ga) CLEAN (טָהוֹר / ta'hor) HE (הוּא / hu)

and the administrator will see him, and look, the plague was turned white, and the administrator will declare the plague clean, he is clean,

18 and~FLESH (וּבָשָׂר / u'va'sar) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) in~~him (בוֹ / vo) in~SKIN~him (בְעֹרוֹ / vê'o'ro) BOILS (שְׁחִין / shê'hhin) and~he~did~be~HEAL(Verb) (וְנִרְפָּא / wê'nir'pa)

and the flesh that exists in him, in his skin are boils, and he will be healed,

19 and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) in~AREA (בִּמְקוֹם / bim'qom) the~BOILS (הַשְּׁחִין / hash'hhin) >~LIFT.UP(Verb) (שְׂאֵת / sê'eyt) WHITE (לְבָנָה / lê'va'nah) OR (אוֹ / o) BRIGHT.SPOT (בַהֶרֶת / va'he'ret) WHITE (לְבָנָה / lê'va'nah) REDDISH (אֲדַמְדָּמֶת / a'dam'da'met) and~he~did~be~SEE(Verb) (וְנִרְאָה / wê'nir'ah) TO (אֶל / el) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn)

and in the place of the boils exists a lifting up of white, or a white reddish bright spot, then he will appear to the administrator,

20 and~he~did~SEE(Verb) (וְרָאָה / wê'ra'ah) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) APPEARANCE~her (מַרְאֶהָ / mar'e'ah) LOW (שָׁפָל / sha'phal) FROM (מִן / min) the~SKIN (הָעוֹר / ha'or) and~HAIR~her (וּשְׂעָרָהּ / us'a'rah) he~did~OVERTURN(Verb) (הָפַךְ / ha'phakh) WHITE (לָבָן / la'van) and~he~did~much~BE.DIRTY(Verb)~him (וְטִמְּאוֹ / wê'tim'o) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) TOUCH (נֶגַע / ne'ga) INFECTION (צָרַעַת / tsa'ra'at) SHE (הִוא / hi) in~the~BOILS (בַּשְּׁחִין / bash'hhin) she~will~BURST.OUT(Verb) (פָּרָחָה / pa'ra'hhah)

and the administrator will see, and look, her appearance is low from the skin, and her hair turned white, then the administrator will declare him dirty, she is a plague of infection, she will burst out in the boils,

21 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) he~will~SEE(Verb)~her (יִרְאֶנָּה / yir'e'nah) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) WITHOUT (אֵין / eyn) in~~her (בָּהּ / bah) HAIR (שֵׂעָר / sey'ar) WHITE (לָבָן / la'van) and~LOW (וּשְׁפָלָה / ush'pha'lah) WITHOUT~her (אֵינֶנָּה / ey'ne'nah) FROM (מִן / min) the~SKIN (הָעוֹר / ha'or) and~SHE (וְהִיא / wê'hi) DIMNESS (כֵהָה / khey'hah) and~he~did~make~SHUT(Verb)~him (וְהִסְגִּירוֹ / wê'his'gi'ro) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) SEVEN (שִׁבְעַת / shiv'at) DAY~s (יָמִים / ya'mim)
