Numbers Chapter 22

1 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~DESERT~s (בְּעַרְבוֹת / bê'ar'vot) Mo'av (מוֹאָב / mo'av) from~OTHER.SIDE (מֵעֵבֶר / mey'ey'ver) to~Yarden (לְיַרְדֵּן / lê'yar'deyn) Ye'rey'hho (יְרֵחוֹ / yê'rey'hho)

and the sons of Yisra'eyl journeyed, and they camped in the deserts of Mo'av, from the other side to the Yarden of Ye'rey'hho,

2 and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) Balaq (בָּלָק / ba'laq) SON (בֶּן / ben) Tsipor (צִפּוֹר / tsi'por) AT (אֵת / eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) to~the~Emor~of (לָאֱמֹרִי / la'e'mo'ri)

and Balaq the son of Tsipor saw all which Yisra'eyl did to the Emor,

3 and~he~will~IMMIGRATE(Verb) (וַיָּגָר / wai'ya'gar) Mo'av (מוֹאָב / mo'av) from~FACE~s (מִפְּנֵי / mip'ney) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) MANY (מְאֹד / mê'od) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) ABUNDANT (רַב / rav) HE (הוּא / hu) and~he~will~LOATHE(Verb) (וַיָּקָץ / wai'ya'qats) Mo'av (מוֹאָב / mo'av) from~FACE~s (מִפְּנֵי / mip'ney) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl)

and Mo'av immigrated from the face of the many people, given that he was abundant, and Mo'av was loathed from the face of the sons of Yisra'eyl,

4 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Mo'av (מוֹאָב / mo'av) TO (אֶל / el) BEARD~s (זִקְנֵי / ziq'ney) Mid'yan (מִדְיָן / mid'yan) NOW (עַתָּה / a'tah) they(m)~will~much~LICK(Verb) (יְלַחֲכוּ / yê'la'hha'khu) the~ASSEMBLY (הַקָּהָל / ha'qa'hal) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) ALL.AROUND~s~us (סְבִיבֹתֵינוּ / sê'vi'vo'tey'nu) like~>~LICK(Verb) (כִּלְחֹךְ / kil'hhokh) the~OX (הַשּׁוֹר / ha'shor) AT (אֵת / eyt) GREEN (יֶרֶק / ye'req) the~FIELD (הַשָּׂדֶה / ha'sa'deh) and~Balaq (וּבָלָק / u'va'laq) SON (בֶּן / ben) Tsipor (צִפּוֹר / tsi'por) KING (מֶלֶךְ / me'lekh) to~Mo'av (לְמוֹאָב / lê'mo'av) in~the~APPOINTED.TIME (בָּעֵת / ba'eyt) the~SHE (הַהִוא / ha'hi)

and Mo'av said to the bearded ones of Mid'yan, now the assembly will lick all around us, like the licking of the ox at the green field, and Balaq the son of Tsipor was king to Mo'av in that appointed time,

5 and~he~will~SEND(Verb) (וַיִּשְׁלַח / wai'yish'lahh) MESSENGER~s (מַלְאָכִים / mal'a'khim) TO (אֶל / el) Bilam (בִּלְעָם / bil'am) SON (בֶּן / ben) Be'or (בְּעוֹר / bê'or) Petor~unto (פְּתוֹרָה / pê'to'rah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) UPON (עַל / al) the~RIVER (הַנָּהָר / ha'na'har) LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) PEOPLE~him (עַמּוֹ / a'mo) to~>~CALL.OUT(Verb) (לִקְרֹא / liq'ro) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) LOOK (הִנֵּה / hin'neyh) PEOPLE (עַם / am) he~did~GO.OUT(Verb) (יָצָא / ya'tsa) from~Mits'rayim (מִמִּצְרַיִם / mi'mits'ra'yim) LOOK (הִנֵּה / hin'neyh) he~did~much~COVER.OVER(Verb) (כִסָּה / khi'sah) AT (אֶת / et) EYE (עֵין / eyn) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) and~HE (וְהוּא / wê'hu) SETTLE(Verb)~ing(ms) (יֹשֵׁב / yo'sheyv) from~FOREFRONT~me (מִמֻּלִי / mi'mu'li)

and he sent messengers to Bilam the son of Be'or, unto Petor, which is upon the river of the land of the sons of his people, to call out to him saying, look, a people went out from Mits'rayim, look, he covered over the eye of that land, settling in front of me,

6 and~NOW (וְעַתָּה / wê'a'tah) !(ms)~WALK(Verb)~& (לְכָה / lê'khah) PLEASE (נָּא / na) !(ms)~SPIT.UPON(Verb)~& (אָרָה / a'rah) to~~me (לִּי / li) AT (אֶת / et) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) NUMEROUS (עָצוּם / a'tsum) HE (הוּא / hu) FROM~me (מִמֶּנִּי / mi'me'ni) POSSIBLY (אוּלַי / u'lai) I~will~BE.ABLE(Verb) (אוּכַל / u'khal) we~will~make~HIT(Verb) (נַכֶּה / na'keh) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo) and~I~will~much~CAST.OUT(Verb)~him (וַאֲגָרְשֶׁנּוּ / wa'a'gar'she'nu) FROM (מִן / min) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I~did~KNOW(Verb) (יָדַעְתִּי / ya'da'ti) AT (אֵת / eyt) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~will~much~KNEEL(Verb) (תְּבָרֵךְ / tê'va'reykh) be~much~KNEEL(Verb)~ing(ms) (מְבֹרָךְ / mê'vo'rakh) and~WHICH (וַאֲשֶׁר / wa'a'sher) you(ms)~will~SPIT.UPON(Verb) (תָּאֹר / ta'or) he~will~be~make~SPIT.UPON(Verb) (יוּאָר / yu'ar)

and now, please walk, spit upon for me this people, given that he is more numerous than me, possibly I will be able, we will attack him, and I will cast him out from the land, given that I know what you will exalt will be exalted, and what you spit upon will be spit upon,

7 and~they(m)~will~WALK(Verb) (וַיֵּלְכוּ / wai'yeyl'khu) BEARD~s (זִקְנֵי / ziq'ney) Mo'av (מוֹאָב / mo'av) and~BEARD~s (וְזִקְנֵי / wê'ziq'ney) Mid'yan (מִדְיָן / mid'yan) and~DIVINATION~s (וּקְסָמִים / uq'sa'mim) in~HAND~them(m) (בְּיָדָם / bê'ya'dam) and~they(m)~will~COME(Verb) (וַיָּבֹאוּ / wai'ya'vo'u) TO (אֶל / el) Bilam (בִּלְעָם / bil'am) and~they(m)~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבְּרוּ / wa'ye'da'be'ru) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) WORD~s (דִּבְרֵי / div'rey) Balaq (בָלָק / va'laq)

and the bearded ones of Mo'av and the bearded ones of Mid'yan walked, and divinations were in their hand, and they came to Bilam, and they spoke to him the words of Balaq,

8 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵיהֶם / a'ley'hem) !(mp)~STAY.THE.NIGHT(Verb) (לִינוּ / li'nu) HERE (פֹה / phoh) the~NIGHT (הַלַּיְלָה / ha'lai'lah) and~I~did~make~TURN.BACK(Verb) (וַהֲשִׁבֹתִי / wa'ha'shi'vo'ti) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem) WORD (דָּבָר / da'var) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (יְדַבֵּר / yê'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO~me (אֵלָי / ey'lai) and~they(m)~will~SETTLE(Verb) (וַיֵּשְׁבוּ / wai'yeysh'vu) NOBLE~s (שָׂרֵי / sa'rey) Mo'av (מוֹאָב / mo'av) WITH (עִם / im) Bilam (בִּלְעָם / bil'am)

and he said to them, stay here the night, and I will bring you back word, just as YHWH spoke to me, and the nobles of Mo'av settled with Bilam,

9 and~he~will~COME(Verb) (וַיָּבֹא / wai'ya'vo) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) TO (אֶל / el) Bilam (בִּלְעָם / bil'am) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) WHO (מִי / mi) the~MAN~s (הָאֲנָשִׁים / ha'a'na'shim) the~THESE (הָאֵלֶּה / ha'ey'leh) WITH~you(fs) (עִמָּךְ / i'makh)

and Elohiym came to Bilam, and he said, who are these men with you,

10 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Bilam (בִּלְעָם / bil'am) TO (אֶל / el) the~Elohiym (הָאֱלֹהִים / ha'e'lo'him) Balaq (בָּלָק / ba'laq) SON (בֶּן / ben) Tsipor (צִפֹּר / tsi'por) KING (מֶלֶךְ / me'lekh) Mo'av (מוֹאָב / mo'av) he~did~SEND(Verb) (שָׁלַח / sha'lahh) TO~me (אֵלָי / ey'lai) [865]

and Bilam said to the Elohiym, Balaq the son of Tsipor, king of Mo'av, he sent to me saying,

11 LOOK (הִנֵּה / hin'neyh) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) the~GO.OUT(Verb)~ing(ms) (הַיֹּצֵא / hai'yo'tsey) from~Mits'rayim (מִמִּצְרַיִם / mi'mits'ra'yim) and~he~will~much~COVER.OVER(Verb) (וַיְכַס / wai'khas) AT (אֶת / et) EYE (עֵין / eyn) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) NOW (עַתָּה / a'tah) !(ms)~WALK(Verb)~& (לְכָה / lê'khah) !(ms)~HOLLOW.OUT(Verb)~& (קָבָה / qa'vah) to~~me (לִּי / li) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) POSSIBLY (אוּלַי / u'lai) I~will~BE.ABLE(Verb) (אוּכַל / u'khal) to~>~be~FIGHT(Verb) (לְהִלָּחֶם / lê'hi'la'hhem) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo) and~I~did~much~CAST.OUT(Verb)~him (וְגֵרַשְׁתִּיו / wê'gey'rash'tiw)

Look, the people are going out from Mits'rayim, and he covered over the eye of that land, now walk, hollow him out for me, possibly I will be able to fight him, and I will cast him out,

12 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) TO (אֶל / el) Bilam (בִּלְעָם / bil'am) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~WALK(Verb) (תֵלֵךְ / tey'leykh) WITH~them(m) (עִמָּהֶם / i'ma'hem) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~SPIT.UPON(Verb) (תָאֹר / ta'or) AT (אֶת / et) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) KNEEL(Verb)~ed(ms) (בָרוּךְ / va'rukh) HE (הוּא / hu)

and Elohiym said to Bilam, you will not walk with them, you will not spit upon the people, given that he is exalted,

13 and~he~will~RISE(Verb) (וַיָּקָם / wai'ya'qam) Bilam (בִּלְעָם / bil'am) in~the~MORNING (בַּבֹּקֶר / ba'bo'qer) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) TO (אֶל / el) NOBLE~s (שָׂרֵי / sa'rey) Balaq (בָלָק / va'laq) !(mp)~WALK(Verb) (לְכוּ / lê'khu) TO (אֶל / el) LAND~you(mp) (אַרְצְכֶם / ar'tse'khem) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~much~REFUSE(Verb) (מֵאֵן / mey'eyn) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) to~>~GIVE(Verb)~me (לְתִתִּי / lê'ti'ti) to~>~WALK(Verb) (לַהֲלֹךְ / la'ha'lokh) WITH~you(mp) (עִמָּכֶם / i'ma'khem)

and Bilam rose in the morning, and he said to the nobles of Balaq, walk to your land, given that YHWH refused to give me to walk with you,

14 and~they(m)~will~RISE(Verb) (וַיָּקוּמוּ / wai'ya'qu'mu) NOBLE~s (שָׂרֵי / sa'rey) Mo'av (מוֹאָב / mo'av) and~they(m)~will~COME(Verb) (וַיָּבֹאוּ / wai'ya'vo'u) TO (אֶל / el) Balaq (בָּלָק / ba'laq) and~they(m)~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמְרוּ / wai'yom'ru) he~did~much~REFUSE(Verb) (מֵאֵן / mey'eyn) Bilam (בִּלְעָם / bil'am) >~WALK(Verb) (הֲלֹךְ / ha'lokh) WITH~us (עִמָּנוּ / i'ma'nu)

and the nobles of Mo'av rose, and they came to Balaq, and they said, Bilam refused to walk with us,

15 and~he~will~make~ADD(Verb) (וַיֹּסֶף / wai'yo'seph) YET.AGAIN (עוֹד / od) [866] Balaq (בָּלָק / ba'laq) >~SEND(Verb) (שְׁלֹחַ / shê'lo'ahh) NOBLE~s (שָׂרִים / sa'rim) ABUNDANT~s (רַבִּים / ra'bim) and~be~BE.HEAVY(Verb)~ing(mp) (וְנִכְבָּדִים / wê'nikh'ba'dim) [867] from~THESE (מֵאֵלֶּה / mey'ey'leh)

and again Balaq sent nobles, abundant ones and being heavier than these,

16 and~they(m)~will~COME(Verb) (וַיָּבֹאוּ / wai'ya'vo'u) TO (אֶל / el) Bilam (בִּלְעָם / bil'am) and~they(m)~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמְרוּ / wai'yom'ru) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) IN.THIS.WAY (כֹּה / koh) he~did~SAY(Verb) (אָמַר / a'mar) Balaq (בָּלָק / ba'laq) SON (בֶּן / ben) Tsipor (צִפּוֹר / tsi'por) DO.NOT (אַל / al) PLEASE (נָא / na) you(ms)~will~be~WITHHOLD(Verb) (תִמָּנַע / ti'ma'na) from~>~WALK(Verb) (מֵהֲלֹךְ / mey'ha'lokh) TO~me (אֵלָי / ey'lai)

and they came to Bilam, and they said to him, in this way Balaq the son of Tsipor said, please, you will not be withheld from walking to me.

17 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) >~much~BE.HEAVY(Verb) (כַבֵּד / kha'beyd) I~will~much~BE.HEAVY(Verb)~you(ms) (אֲכַבֶּדְךָ / a'kha'bed'kha) MANY (מְאֹד / mê'od) and~ALL (וְכֹל / wê'khol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~will~SAY(Verb) (תֹּאמַר / to'mar) TO~me (אֵלַי / ey'lai) I~will~DO(Verb) (אֶעֱשֶׂה / e'e'seh) and~!(ms)~WALK(Verb)~& (וּלְכָה / ul'khah) PLEASE (נָּא / na) !(ms)~HOLLOW.OUT(Verb)~& (קָבָה / qa'vah) to~~me (לִּי / li) AT (אֵת / eyt) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh)

Given that I will certainly honor you greatly, and everything that you will say to me, I will do, and please walk, hollow out for me this people,

18 and~he~will~ANSWER(Verb) (וַיַּעַן / wai'ya'an) Bilam (בִּלְעָם / bil'am) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) TO (אֶל / el) SERVANT~s (עַבְדֵי / av'dey) Balaq (בָלָק / va'laq) IF (אִם / im) he~will~GIVE(Verb) (יִתֶּן / yi'ten) to~~me (לִי / li) Balaq (בָלָק / va'laq) FILLING (מְלֹא / mê'lo) HOUSE~him (בֵיתוֹ / vey'to) SILVER (כֶּסֶף / ke'seph) and~GOLD (וְזָהָב / wê'za'hav) NOT (לֹא / lo) I~will~BE.ABLE(Verb) (אוּכַל / u'khal) to~the~>~CROSS.OVER(Verb) (לַעֲבֹר / la'a'vor) AT (אֶת / et) MOUTH (פִּי / pi) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~me (אֱלֹהָי / e'lo'hai) to~>~DO(Verb) (לַעֲשׂוֹת / la'a'sot) SMALL (קְטַנָּה / qê'ta'nah) OR (אוֹ / o) GREAT (גְדוֹלָה / gê'do'lah)

and Bilam answered, and he said to the servants of Balaq, if Balaq will give to me the fillings of his house, silver and gold, I will not be able to cross over the mouth of YHWH my Elohiym to do small or great,
