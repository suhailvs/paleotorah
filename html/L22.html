Leviticus Chapter 22

1 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

2 !(ms)~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דַּבֵּר / da'beyr) TO (אֶל / el) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) and~TO (וְאֶל / wê'el) SON~s~him (בָּנָיו / ba'naw) and~they(m)~will~be~DEDICATE(Verb) (וְיִנָּזְרוּ / wê'yi'naz'ru) from~SPECIAL~s (מִקָּדְשֵׁי / mi'qad'shey) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) they(m)~will~much~DRILL(Verb) (יְחַלְּלוּ / yê'hhal'lu) AT (אֶת / et) TITLE (שֵׁם / sheym) SPECIAL~me (קָדְשִׁי / qad'shi) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) THEY(m) (הֵם / heym) make~SET.APART(Verb)~ing(mp) (מַקְדִּשִׁים / maq'di'shim) to~~me (לִי / li) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

Speak to Aharon and to his sons, and they will be dedicated from the special things of the sons of Yisra'eyl, and they will not defile my special title, which they are setting apart for me, I am YHWH.

3 !(ms)~SAY(Verb) (אֱמֹר / e'mor) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) to~GENERATION~s~you(mp) (לְדֹרֹתֵיכֶם / lê'do'ro'tey'khem) ALL (כָּל / kol) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~COME.NEAR(Verb) (יִקְרַב / yiq'rav) from~ALL (מִכָּל / mi'kol) SEED~you(mp) (זַרְעֲכֶם / zar'a'khem) TO (אֶל / el) the~SPECIAL~s (הַקֳּדָשִׁים / ha'qa'da'shim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they(m)~will~make~SET.APART(Verb) (יַקְדִּישׁוּ / yaq'di'shu) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) and~DIRTY~him (וְטֻמְאָתוֹ / wê'tum'a'to) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) and~she~did~be~CUT(Verb) (וְנִכְרְתָה / wê'nikh're'tah) the~SOUL (הַנֶּפֶשׁ / ha'ne'phesh) the~SHE (הַהִוא / ha'hi) from~to~FACE~s~me (מִלְּפָנַי / mi'le'pha'nai) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

Say to them, to your generations, every man from all your seed that will come near to the special things, which the sons of Yisra'eyl will set apart for YHWH, and his dirtiness is upon him, and that soul will be cut from before my face, I am YHWH.

4 MAN (אִישׁ / ish) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) from~SEED (מִזֶּרַע / mi'ze'ra) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) and~HE (וְהוּא / wê'hu) INFECT(Verb)~ed(ms) (צָרוּעַ / tsa'ru'a) OR (אוֹ / o) ISSUE(Verb)~ing(ms) (זָב / zav) in~the~SPECIAL~s (בַּקֳּדָשִׁים / ba'qa'da'shim) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~EAT(Verb) (יֹאכַל / yo'khal) UNTIL (עַד / ad) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~BE.CLEAN(Verb) (יִטְהָר / yit'har) and~the~TOUCH(Verb)~ing(ms) (וְהַנֹּגֵעַ / wê'ha'no'gey'a) in~ALL (בְּכָל / bê'khol) DIRTY (טְמֵא / tê'mey) SOUL (נֶפֶשׁ / ne'phesh) OR (אוֹ / o) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) she~will~GO.OUT(Verb) (תֵּצֵא / tey'tsey) FROM~him (מִמֶּנּוּ / mi'me'nu) LYING.DOWN (שִׁכְבַת / shikh'vat) SEED (זָרַע / za'ra)

Each man from the seed of Aharon and is infected or issuing, he will not eat with the special things, until he is clean, and anyone touching a dirty soul or a man that had the lying down of seed from him go out.

5 OR (אוֹ / o) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~TOUCH(Verb) (יִגַּע / yiga) in~ALL (בְּכָל / bê'khol) SWARMER (שֶׁרֶץ / she'rets) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (יִטְמָא / yit'ma) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) OR (אוֹ / o) in~HUMAN (בְאָדָם / vê'a'dam) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (יִטְמָא / yit'ma) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) to~ALL (לְכֹל / lê'khol) DIRTY~him (טֻמְאָתוֹ / tu'm'a'to)

Or a man that touched any swarmer that is dirty to him, or with a human that is dirty to him to all his dirtiness.

6 SOUL (נֶפֶשׁ / ne'phesh) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) she~will~TOUCH(Verb) (תִּגַּע / ti'ga) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo) and~she~did~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (וְטָמְאָה / wê'tam'ah) UNTIL (עַד / ad) the~EVENING (הָעָרֶב / ha'a'rev) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~will~EAT(Verb) (יֹאכַל / yo'khal) FROM (מִן / min) the~SPECIAL~s (הַקֳּדָשִׁים / ha'qa'da'shim) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) IF (אִם / im) he~did~BATHE(Verb) (רָחַץ / ra'hhats) FLESH~him (בְּשָׂרוֹ / bê'sar'o) in~the~WATER~s2 (בַּמָּיִם / ba'ma'yim)

A soul that touches in him, then she will be dirty until the evening, and he will not eat from the special things, unless he bathed his flesh in the waters,

7 and~he~did~COME(Verb) (וּבָא / u'va) the~SUN (הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ / ha'she'mesh) and~he~did~BE.CLEAN(Verb) (וְטָהֵר / wê'ta'har) and~AFTER (וְאַחַר / wê'a'hhar) he~will~EAT(Verb) (יֹאכַל / yo'khal) FROM (מִן / min) the~SPECIAL~s (הַקֳּדָשִׁים / ha'qa'da'shim) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) BREAD~him (לַחְמוֹ / lahh'mo) HE (הוּא / hu)

and the sun came, and he will be clean, and afterward he will eat from the special things, given that he is his bread.

8 CARCASS (נְבֵלָה / nê'vey'lah) and~TORN (וּטְרֵפָה / ut'rey'phah) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~EAT(Verb) (יֹאכַל / yo'khal) to~DIRTY~her (לְטָמְאָה / lê'tam'ah) in~~her (בָהּ / vah) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

A carcass or a torn one he will not eat, for her dirtiness is in her, I am YHWH,

9 and~they~did~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (וְשָׁמְרוּ / wê'sham'ru) AT (אֶת / et) CHARGE~me (מִשְׁמַרְתִּי / mish'ma're'ti) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) they(m)~will~LIFT.UP(Verb) (יִשְׂאוּ / yis'u) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) FAILURE (חֵטְא / hheyt) and~they~did~DIE(Verb) (וּמֵתוּ / u'mey'tu) in~~him (בוֹ / vo) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~much~DRILL(Verb)~her (יְחַלְּלֻהוּ / yê'hhal'lu'hu) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) from~>~much~SET.APART(Verb)~them(m) (מְקַדְּשָׁם / mê'qad'sham)

and they will safeguard my charge and they will not lift up upon him failure, and they will die in him if he defiles her, I am YHWH setting them apart,

10 and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) BE.STRANGE(Verb)~ing(ms) (זָר / zar) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~EAT(Verb) (יֹאכַל / yo'khal) SPECIAL (קֹדֶשׁ / qo'desh) SETTLER (תּוֹשַׁב / to'shav) ADMINISTRATOR (כֹּהֵן / ko'heyn) and~HIRELING (וְשָׂכִיר / wê'sa'khir) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~EAT(Verb) (יֹאכַל / yo'khal) SPECIAL (קֹדֶשׁ / qo'desh)

and anyone being a stranger will not eat the special thing, a settler of the administrator or a hireling will not eat the special thing,

11 and~ADMINISTRATOR (וְכֹהֵן / wê'kho'heyn) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~PURCHASE(Verb) (יִקְנֶה / yiq'neh) SOUL (נֶפֶשׁ / ne'phesh) MATERIAL (קִנְיַן / qin'yan) SILVER~him (כַּסְפּוֹ / kas'po) HE (הוּא / hu) he~will~EAT(Verb) (יֹאכַל / yo'khal) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo) and~BORN (וִילִיד / wi'lid) HOUSE~him (בֵּיתוֹ / bey'to) THEY(m) (הֵם / heym) they(m)~will~EAT(Verb) (יֹאכְלוּ / yokh'lu) in~BREAD~him (בְלַחְמוֹ / vê'lahh'mo)

and the administrator that will purchase a soul, he is the material of his purchase, he will eat with him, and the ones born of his house, they will eat his bread,

12 and~DAUGHTER (וּבַת / u'vat) ADMINISTRATOR (כֹּהֵן / ko'heyn) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) she~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִהְיֶה / tih'yeh) to~MAN (לְאִישׁ / lê'ish) BE.STRANGE(Verb)~ing(ms) (זָר / zar) SHE (הִוא / hi) in~OFFERING (בִּתְרוּמַת / bit'ru'mat) the~SPECIAL~s (הַקֳּדָשִׁים / ha'qa'da'shim) NOT (לֹא / lo) she~will~EAT(Verb) (תֹאכֵל / to'kheyl)

and the daughter of the administrator that will exist to a man being a stranger, she will not eat the special offering,

13 and~DAUGHTER (וּבַת / u'vat) ADMINISTRATOR (כֹּהֵן / ko'heyn) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) she~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִהְיֶה / tih'yeh) WIDOW (אַלְמָנָה / al'ma'nah) and~CAST.OUT(Verb)~ed(fs) (וּגְרוּשָׁה / ug'ru'shah) and~SEED (וְזֶרַע / wê'ze'ra) WITHOUT (אֵין / eyn) to~~her (לָהּ / lah) and~she~did~TURN.BACK(Verb) (וְשָׁבָה / wê'sha'vah) TO (אֶל / el) HOUSE (בֵּית / beyt) FATHER~her (אָבִיהָ / a'vi'ah) like~YOUNG.AGE~s~her (כִּנְעוּרֶיהָ / kin'ur'yah) from~BREAD (מִלֶּחֶם / mi'le'hhem) FATHER~her (אָבִיהָ / a'vi'ah) she~will~EAT(Verb) (תֹּאכֵל / to'kheyl) and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) BE.STRANGE(Verb)~ing(ms) (זָר / zar) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~EAT(Verb) (יֹאכַל / yo'khal) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo)

and the daughter of the administrator that will exist as a widow or is casted out or is without seed, and she is returned to the house of her father like in her young age, from the bread of her father she will eat, and anyone being a stranger will not eat with him,

14 and~MAN (וְאִישׁ / wê'ish) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~EAT(Verb) (יֹאכַל / yo'khal) SPECIAL (קֹדֶשׁ / qo'desh) in~ERROR (בִּשְׁגָגָה / bish'ga'gah) and~he~will~ADD(Verb) (וְיָסַף / wê'ya'saph) FIVE~him (חֲמִשִׁיתוֹ / hha'mi'shi'to) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) and~he~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַן / wê'na'tan) to~the~ADMINISTRATOR (לַכֹּהֵן / la'ko'heyn) AT (אֶת / et) the~SPECIAL (הַקֹּדֶשׁ / ha'qo'desh)

and a man that will eat the special thing in error, then he will add his fifth part upon him and he will give to the administrator with the special thing,

15 and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) they(m)~will~much~DRILL(Verb) (יְחַלְּלוּ / yê'hhal'lu) AT (אֶת / et) SPECIAL~s (קָדְשֵׁי / qad'shey) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) AT (אֵת / eyt) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they(m)~will~make~RAISE.UP(Verb) (יָרִימוּ / ya'ri'mu) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

and the sons of Yisra'eyl will not defile the special things that they raise up to YHWH,

16 and~they~did~make~LIFT.UP(Verb) (וְהִשִּׂיאוּ / wê'hi'si'u) AT~them(m) (אוֹתָם / o'tam) TWISTEDNESS (עֲו‍ֹן / a'won) GUILT (אַשְׁמָה / ash'mah) in~>~EAT(Verb)~them(m) (בְּאָכְלָם / bê'akh'lam) AT (אֶת / et) SPECIAL~s~them(m) (קָדְשֵׁיהֶם / qad'shey'hem) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) from~>~much~SET.APART(Verb)~them(m) (מְקַדְּשָׁם / mê'qad'sham)

and they will lift them up, the twistedness of guilt, in their eating their special things, given that I am YHWH setting them apart,

17 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

18 !(ms)~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דַּבֵּר / da'beyr) TO (אֶל / el) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) and~TO (וְאֶל / wê'el) SON~s~him (בָּנָיו / ba'naw) and~TO (וְאֶל / wê'el) ALL (כָּל / kol) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~you(ms)~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמַרְתָּ / wê'a'mar'ta) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) from~HOUSE (מִבֵּית / mi'beyt) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~FROM (וּמִן / u'min) the~IMMIGRANT (הַגֵּר / ha'geyr) in~Yisra'eyl (בְּיִשְׂרָאֵל / bê'yis'ra'eyl) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (יַקְרִיב / yaq'riv) DONATION~him (קָרְבָּנוֹ / qar'ba'no) to~ALL (לְכָל / lê'khol) VOW~s~them(m) (נִדְרֵיהֶם / nid'rey'hem) and~to~ALL (וּלְכָל / ul'khol) FREEWILL.OFFERING~them(m) (נִדְבוֹתָם / nid'vo'tam) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they(m)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (יַקְרִיבוּ / yaq'ri'vu) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) to~ASCENSION.OFFERING (לְעֹלָה / lê'lah)

Speak to Aharon and to his sons, and to the sons of Yisra'eyl, and you will say to them, each man from the house of Yisra'eyl and from the immigrant in Yisra'eyl, which will bring near his donation for all their vows and for all their freewill offerings, which they bring near to YHWH for a ascension offering.

19 to~SELF-WILL~you(mp) (לִרְצֹנְכֶם / lir'tson'khem) WHOLE (תָּמִים / ta'mim) MALE (זָכָר / za'khar) in~the~CATTLE (בַּבָּקָר / ba'ba'qar) in~the~SHEEP~s (בַּכְּשָׂבִים / bak'sa'vim) and~in~the~GOAT~s (וּבָעִזִּים / u'va'i'zim)

For yourself, a whole male of the cattle, of the sheep and of the goats.

20 ALL (כֹּל / kol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo) BLEMISH (מוּם / mum) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תַקְרִיבוּ / taq'riv'u) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) NOT (לֹא / lo) to~SELF-WILL (לְרָצוֹן / lê'ra'tson) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem)

All that have in him a blemish you will not bring near, given that he will not exist for you for yourself,

21 and~MAN (וְאִישׁ / wê'ish) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (יַקְרִיב / yaq'riv) SACRIFICE (זֶבַח / ze'vahh) OFFERING.OF.RESTITUTION~s (שְׁלָמִים / shê'la'mim) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) to~>~much~PERFORM(Verb) (לְפַלֵּא / lê'pha'ley) VOW (נֶדֶר / ne'der) OR (אוֹ / o) to~FREEWILL.OFFERING (לִנְדָבָה / lin'da'vah) in~the~CATTLE (בַּבָּקָר / ba'ba'qar) OR (אוֹ / o) in~the~FLOCKS (בַצֹּאן / va'tson) WHOLE (תָּמִים / ta'mim) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) to~SELF-WILL (לְרָצוֹן / lê'ra'tson) ALL (כָּל / kol) BLEMISH (מוּם / mum) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo)

and a man that will bring near a sacrifice of offerings of restitution to YHWH, to perform a vow or for a freewill offering in the cattle or in the flocks, he will exist whole to be accepted, not any blemish will exist in him.

22 BLINDNESS (עַוֶּרֶת / a'we'ret) OR (אוֹ / o) CRACK(Verb)~ed(ms) (שָׁבוּר / sha'vur) OR (אוֹ / o) CUT.SHARPLY(Verb)~ed(ms) (חָרוּץ / hha'ruts) OR (אוֹ / o) ULCER (יַבֶּלֶת / ya'be'let) OR (אוֹ / o) IRRITATION (גָרָב / ga'rav) OR (אוֹ / o) SKIN.SORE (יַלֶּפֶת / ya'le'phet) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תַקְרִיבוּ / taq'riv'u) THESE (אֵלֶּה / ey'leh) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) and~FIRE.OFFERING (וְאִשֶּׁה / wê'i'sheh) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~GIVE(Verb) (תִתְּנוּ / tit'nu) from~~them(m) (מֵהֶם / mey'hem) UPON (עַל / al) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

Blindness or cracked or cut sharply or an ulcer or an irritation or a skin sore, you will not bring these near to YHWH, and a fire offering you will not give from them upon the altar to YHWH,
