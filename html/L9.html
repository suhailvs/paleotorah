Leviticus Chapter 9

1 and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) in~the~DAY (בַּיּוֹם / ba'yom) the~EIGHTH (הַשְּׁמִינִי / hash'mini) he~did~CALL.OUT(Verb) (קָרָא / qa'ra) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~Aharon (לְאַהֲרֹן / lê'a'ha'ron) and~to~SON~s~him (וּלְבָנָיו / ul'va'naw) and~to~BEARD~s (וּלְזִקְנֵי / ul'ziq'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl)

and it came to pass in the eighth day, Mosheh called out to Aharon and to his sons and to the bearded ones of Yisra'eyl,

2 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) TO (אֶל / el) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) !(ms)~TAKE(Verb) (קַח / qahh) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) BULLOCK (עֵגֶל / ey'gel) SON (בֶּן / ben) CATTLE (בָּקָר / ba'qar) to~FAILURE (לְחַטָּאת / lê'hha'tat) and~BUCK (וְאַיִל / wê'a'yil) to~ASCENSION.OFFERING (לְעֹלָה / lê'lah) WHOLE~s (תְּמִימִם / tê'mi'mim) and~!(ms)~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וְהַקְרֵב / wê'haq'reyv) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and he said to Aharon, take for you a bullock, a son of the cattle, for the failure, and a buck for the ascension offering, whole ones, and bring near to the face of YHWH,

3 and~TO (וְאֶל / wê'el) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) you(ms)~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (תְּדַבֵּר / tê'da'beyr) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) !(mp)~TAKE(Verb) (קְחוּ / qê'hhu) HAIRY.GOAT (שְׂעִיר / sê'ir) GOAT~s (עִזִּים / i'zim) to~FAILURE (לְחַטָּאת / lê'hha'tat) and~BULLOCK (וְעֵגֶל / wê'ey'gel) and~SHEEP (וָכֶבֶשׂ / wa'khe'ves) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) [680] WHOLE~s (תְּמִימִם / tê'mi'mim) to~ASCENSION.OFFERING (לְעֹלָה / lê'lah)

and to the sons of Yisra'eyl you will speak saying, take a hairy goat of the goats for the failure, and a bullock and a sheep, sons of a year, whole ones, for the ascension offering,

4 and~OX (וְשׁוֹר / wê'shor) and~BUCK (וָאַיִל / wa'a'yil) to~OFFERING.OF.RESTITUTION~s (לִשְׁלָמִים / lish'la'mim) to~>~SACRIFICE(Verb) (לִזְבֹּחַ / liz'bo'ahh) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~DEPOSIT (וּמִנְחָה / u'min'hhah) MIX(Verb)~ed(fs) (בְּלוּלָה / bê'lu'lah) in~the~OIL (בַשָּׁמֶן / va'sha'men) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) he~did~be~SEE(Verb) (נִרְאָה / nir'ah) TO~you(mp) (אֲלֵיכֶם / a'ley'khem)

and an ox and a buck for the offering of restitution, for a sacrifice to the face of YHWH, and a deposit mixed in the oil, given that today YHWH appeared to you,

5 and~they(m)~will~TAKE(Verb) (וַיִּקְחוּ / wai'yiq'hhu) AT (אֵת / eyt) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~much~DIRECT(Verb) (צִוָּה / tsi'wah) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) TO (אֶל / el) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) and~they(m)~will~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וַיִּקְרְבוּ / wai'yiq're'vu) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~COMPANY (הָעֵדָה / ha'ey'dah) and~they(m)~will~STAND(Verb) (וַיַּעַמְדוּ / wai'ya'am'du) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and they took what Mosheh directed to the face of the appointed tent, and all the company came near, and they stood to the face of YHWH,

6 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) THIS (זֶה / zeh) the~WORD (הַדָּבָר / ha'da'var) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~much~DIRECT(Verb) (צִוָּה / tsi'wah) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) you(mp)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשׂוּ / ta'a'su) and~he~will~be~SEE(Verb) (וְיֵרָא / wê'yey'ra) TO~you(mp) (אֲלֵיכֶם / a'ley'khem) ARMAMENT (כְּבוֹד / kê'vod) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and Mosheh said, this is the word which YHWH directed you to do, and the armament of YHWH appeared to you,

7 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) TO (אֶל / el) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) !(ms)~COME.NEAR(Verb) (קְרַב / qê'rav) TO (אֶל / el) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) and~!(ms)~DO(Verb) (וַעֲשֵׂה / wa'a'seyh) AT (אֶת / et) FAILURE~you(ms) (חַטָּאתְךָ / hha'ta'te'kha) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) ASCENSION.OFFERING~you(ms) (עֹלָתֶךָ / o'la'te'kha) and~!(ms)~much~COVER(Verb) (וְכַפֵּר / wê'kha'peyr) in~UNTIL~you(ms) (בַּעַדְךָ / ba'ad'kha) and~in~UNTIL (וּבְעַד / uv'ad) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) and~!(ms)~DO(Verb) (וַעֲשֵׂה / wa'a'seyh) AT (אֶת / et) DONATION (קָרְבַּן / qar'ban) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) and~!(ms)~much~COVER(Verb) (וְכַפֵּר / wê'kha'peyr) in~UNTIL~them(m) (בַּעֲדָם / ba'a'dam) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~much~DIRECT(Verb) (צִוָּה / tsi'wah) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and Mosheh said to Aharon, come near to the altar and do your failure and your ascension offering and make a covering on behalf of yourself and on behalf of the people, and do the donation of the people, and make a covering on their behalf, just as YHWH directed,

8 and~he~will~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וַיִּקְרַב / wai'yiq'rav) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) TO (אֶל / el) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) and~he~will~SLAY(Verb) (וַיִּשְׁחַט / wai'yish'hhat) AT (אֶת / et) BULLOCK (עֵגֶל / ey'gel) the~FAILURE (הַחַטָּאת / ha'hha'tat) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) to~~him (לוֹ / lo)

and Aharon came near to the altar, and he slew the bullock of the failure, which belonged to him,

9 and~they(m)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וַיַּקְרִבוּ / wai'yaq'ri'vu) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) AT (אֶת / et) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) and~he~will~DIP(Verb) (וַיִּטְבֹּל / wai'yit'bol) FINGER~him (אֶצְבָּעוֹ / ets'ba'o) in~the~BLOOD (בַּדָּם / ba'dam) and~he~will~GIVE(Verb) (וַיִּתֵּן / wai'yi'teyn) UPON (עַל / al) HORN~s (קַרְנוֹת / qar'not) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam) he~did~POUR.DOWN(Verb) (יָצַק / ya'tsaq) TO (אֶל / el) BOTTOM.BASE (יְסוֹד / yê'sod) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh)

and the sons of Aharon brought near the blood to him, and he dipped his finger in the blood, and he placed it upon the horns of the altar, and he poured down the blood to the bottom base of the altar,

10 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~FAT (הַחֵלֶב / ha'hhey'lev) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~KIDNEY~s (הַכְּלָיֹת / hak'la'yot) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~LOBE (הַיֹּתֶרֶת / hai'yo'te'ret) FROM (מִן / min) the~HEAVY (הַכָּבֵד / ha'ka'veyd) FROM (מִן / min) the~FAILURE (הַחַטָּאת / ha'hha'tat) he~did~make~BURN.INCENSE(Verb) (הִקְטִיר / hiq'tir) the~ALTAR~unto (הַמִּזְבֵּחָה / ha'miz'bey'hhah) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~much~DIRECT(Verb) (צִוָּה / tsi'wah) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT (אֶת / et) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh)

and the fat and the kidneys and the heavy lobe from the failure, he burned as incense upon the altar, just as YHWH directed Mosheh,

11 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~FLESH (הַבָּשָׂר / ha'ba'sar) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~SKIN (הָעוֹר / ha'or) he~did~CREMATE(Verb) (שָׂרַף / sa'raph) in~the~FIRE (בָּאֵשׁ / ba'eysh) from~OUTSIDE (מִחוּץ / mi'hhuts) to~the~CAMP (לַמַּחֲנֶה / la'ma'hha'neh)

and the flesh and the skin he cremated in the fire outside the camp,

12 and~he~will~SLAY(Verb) (וַיִּשְׁחַט / wai'yish'hhat) AT (אֶת / et) the~ASCENSION.OFFERING (הָעֹלָה / ha'o'lah) and~they(m)~will~make~FIND(Verb) (וַיַּמְצִאוּ / wai'yam'tsi'u) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) AT (אֶת / et) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam) and~he~will~SPRINKLE(Verb)~him (וַיִּזְרְקֵהוּ / wai'yiz're'qey'hu) UPON (עַל / al) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) ALL.AROUND (סָבִיב / sa'viv)

and he slew the ascension offering, and the sons of Aharon revealed to him the blood, and he sprinkled him upon the altar all around,

13 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~ASCENSION.OFFERING (הָעֹלָה / ha'o'lah) they~did~make~FIND(Verb) (הִמְצִיאוּ / him'tsi'u) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) to~PIECE~s~her (לִנְתָחֶיהָ / lin'ta'hhey'ah) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~HEAD (הָרֹאשׁ / ha'rosh) and~he~will~make~BURN.INCENSE(Verb) (וַיַּקְטֵר / wai'yaq'teyr) UPON (עַל / al) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh)

and they revealed the ascension offering to him, to her pieces and the head, and he burned it as incense upon the altar,

14 and~he~will~BATHE(Verb) (וַיִּרְחַץ / wai'yir'hhats) AT (אֶת / et) the~INSIDE (הַקֶּרֶב / ha'qe'rev) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~LEG~s2 (הַכְּרָעָיִם / hak'ra'a'yim) and~he~will~make~BURN.INCENSE(Verb) (וַיַּקְטֵר / wai'yaq'teyr) UPON (עַל / al) the~ASCENSION.OFFERING (הָעֹלָה / ha'o'lah) the~ALTAR~unto (הַמִּזְבֵּחָה / ha'miz'bey'hhah)

and he bathed the insides and the legs, and he burned them as incense upon the ascension offering, unto the altar,

15 and~he~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וַיַּקְרֵב / wai'yaq'reyv) AT (אֵת / eyt) DONATION (קָרְבַּן / qar'ban) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) and~he~will~TAKE(Verb) (וַיִּקַּח / wai'yi'qahh) AT (אֶת / et) HAIRY.GOAT (שְׂעִיר / sê'ir) the~FAILURE (הַחַטָּאת / ha'hha'tat) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) to~the~PEOPLE (לָעָם / la'am) and~he~will~SLAY(Verb)~him (וַיִּשְׁחָטֵהוּ / wai'yish'hha'tey'hu) and~he~will~much~FAIL(Verb)~him (וַיְחַטְּאֵהוּ / wai'hhat'ey'hu) like~the~FIRST (כָּרִאשׁוֹן / ka'ri'shon)

and he brought near the donation of the people, and he took the hairy goat, the failure, which belonged to the people, and slew him, and he bore the blame with him like the first one,

16 and~he~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וַיַּקְרֵב / wai'yaq'reyv) AT (אֶת / et) the~ASCENSION.OFFERING (הָעֹלָה / ha'o'lah) and~he~will~DO(Verb)~her (וַיַּעֲשֶׂהָ / wai'ya'a'se'ah) like~the~DECISION (כַּמִּשְׁפָּט / ka'mish'pat)

and he brought near the ascension offering, and he did her according to the decision,

17 and~he~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וַיַּקְרֵב / wai'yaq'reyv) AT (אֶת / et) the~DEPOSIT (הַמִּנְחָה / ha'min'hhah) and~he~will~much~FILL(Verb) (וַיְמַלֵּא / wai'ma'ley) PALM~him (כַפּוֹ / kha'po) FROM~her (מִמֶּנָּה / mi'me'nah) and~he~will~make~BURN.INCENSE(Verb) (וַיַּקְטֵר / wai'yaq'teyr) UPON (עַל / al) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) from~to~STRAND (מִלְּבַד / mi'le'vad) [681] ASCENSION.OFFERING (עֹלַת / o'lat) the~MORNING (הַבֹּקֶר / ha'bo'qer)

and he brought near the deposit, and he filled his palm from her, and he burned it as incense upon the altar apart from the ascension offering of the morning,

18 and~he~will~SLAY(Verb) (וַיִּשְׁחַט / wai'yish'hhat) AT (אֶת / et) the~OX (הַשּׁוֹר / ha'shor) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~BUCK (הָאַיִל / ha'a'yil) SACRIFICE (זֶבַח / ze'vahh) the~OFFERING.OF.RESTITUTION~s (הַשְּׁלָמִים / ha'she'la'mim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) to~the~PEOPLE (לָעָם / la'am) and~they(m)~will~make~FIND(Verb) (וַיַּמְצִאוּ / wai'yam'tsi'u) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) AT (אֶת / et) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) and~he~will~SPRINKLE(Verb)~him (וַיִּזְרְקֵהוּ / wai'yiz're'qey'hu) UPON (עַל / al) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) ALL.AROUND (סָבִיב / sa'viv)

and he slew the ox and the buck of the sacrifice of the offerings of restitution, which belonged to the people, and the sons of Aharon revealed the blood to him, and he sprinkled him upon the altar, all around,

19 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~FAT~s (הַחֲלָבִים / ha'hha'la'vim) FROM (מִן / min) the~OX (הַשּׁוֹר / ha'shor) and~FROM (וּמִן / u'min) the~BUCK (הָאַיִל / ha'a'yil) the~RUMP (הָאַלְיָה / ha'al'yah) and~the~much~COVER.OVER(Verb)~ing(ms) (וְהַמְכַסֶּה / wê'ham'kha'seh) and~the~KIDNEY~s (וְהַכְּלָיֹת / wê'hak'la'yot) and~LOBE (וְיֹתֶרֶת / wê'yo'te'ret) the~HEAVY (הַכָּבֵד / ha'ka'veyd)

and the fats from the ox and from the buck the rump, and what is covering over the kidneys and the lobe of the heavy one,

20 and~they(m)~will~PLACE(Verb) (וַיָּשִׂימוּ / wai'ya'si'mu) AT (אֶת / et) the~FAT~s (הַחֲלָבִים / ha'hha'la'vim) UPON (עַל / al) the~CHEST (הֶחָזוֹת / he'hha'zot) and~he~will~make~BURN.INCENSE(Verb) (וַיַּקְטֵר / wai'yaq'teyr) the~FAT~s (הַחֲלָבִים / ha'hha'la'vim) the~ALTAR~unto (הַמִּזְבֵּחָה / ha'miz'bey'hhah)

and they will place the fats upon the chest, and he will burn the fats as incense unto the altar,

21 and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) the~CHEST (הֶחָזוֹת / he'hha'zot) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) THIGH (שׁוֹק / shoq) the~RIGHT.HAND (הַיָּמִין / hai'ya'min) he~did~make~WAVE(Verb) (הֵנִיף / hey'niph) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) WAVING (תְּנוּפָה / tê'nu'phah) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~much~DIRECT(Verb) (צִוָּה / tsi'wah) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh)

and the chest and the right thigh Aharon will wave a waving to the face of YHWH, just as Mosheh directed,

and Aharon lifted up his hand to the people, and he exalted them, and he went down from doing the failure and the ascension offering and the offerings of restitution,
