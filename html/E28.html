Exodus Chapter 28

1 and~YOU(MS) (וְאַתָּה / wê'a'tah) !(ms)~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (הַקְרֵב / haq'reyv) TO~you(ms) (אֵלֶיךָ / ey'ley'kha) AT (אֶת / et) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) BROTHER~you(ms) (אָחִיךָ / a'hhi'kha) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) SON~s~him (בָּנָיו / ba'naw) AT~him (אִתּוֹ / i'to) from~MIDST (מִתּוֹךְ / mi'tokh) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) to~>~much~ADORN(Verb)~him (לְכַהֲנוֹ / lê'kha'ha'no) to~~me (לִי / li) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) Nadav (נָדָב / na'dav) and~Aviyhu (וַאֲבִיהוּא / wa'a'vi'hu) Elazar (אֶלְעָזָר / el'a'zar) and~Iytamar (וְאִיתָמָר / wê'i'ta'mar) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron)

and you, bring near to you Aharon, your brother, and his sons with him, from the midst of the sons of Yisra'eyl, to adorn him for me, Nadav and Aviyhu, Elazar and Iytamar, the sons of Aharon,

2 and~you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשִׂיתָ / wê'a'si'ta) GARMENT~s (בִגְדֵי / vig'dey) SPECIAL (קֹדֶשׁ / qo'desh) to~Aharon (לְאַהֲרֹן / lê'a'ha'ron) BROTHER~you(ms) (אָחִיךָ / a'hhi'kha) to~ARMAMENT (לְכָבוֹד / lê'kha'vod) and~to~DECORATION (וּלְתִפְאָרֶת / ul'tiph'a'ret)

and you will make garments of specialness for Aharon, your brother, for armament and for decoration,

3 and~YOU(MS) (וְאַתָּה / wê'a'tah) you(ms)~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (תְּדַבֵּר / tê'da'beyr) TO (אֶל / el) ALL (כָּל / kol) SKILLED.ONE~s (חַכְמֵי / hhakh'mey) HEART (לֵב / leyv) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I~did~much~FILL(Verb)~him (מִלֵּאתִיו / mi'ley'tiw) WIND (רוּחַ / ru'ahh) [474] SKILL (חָכְמָה / hhakh'mah) and~they~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשׂוּ / wê'a'su) AT (אֶת / et) GARMENT~s (בִּגְדֵי / big'dey) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) to~>~much~SET.APART(Verb)~him (לְקַדְּשׁוֹ / lê'qad'sho) to~>~much~ADORN(Verb)~him (לְכַהֲנוֹ / lê'kha'ha'no) to~~me (לִי / li)

and you, you will speak to all the skilled ones of heart, whom I filled with the wind of skill, and they will make the garments of Aharon to set him apart, to adorn him for me,

4 and~THESE (וְאֵלֶּה / wê'ey'leh) the~GARMENT~s (הַבְּגָדִים / ha'be'ga'dim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they(m)~will~DO(Verb) (יַעֲשׂוּ / ya'a'su) BREASTPLATE (חֹשֶׁן / hho'shen) and~EPHOD (וְאֵפוֹד / wê'ey'phod) and~CLOAK (וּמְעִיל / um'il) and~TUNIC (וּכְתֹנֶת / ukh'to'net) WOVEN.MATERIAL (תַּשְׁבֵּץ / tash'beyts) TURBAN (מִצְנֶפֶת / mits'ne'phet) and~SASH (וְאַבְנֵט / wê'av'neyt) and~they~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשׂוּ / wê'a'su) GARMENT~s (בִגְדֵי / vig'dey) SPECIAL (קֹדֶשׁ / qo'desh) to~Aharon (לְאַהֲרֹן / lê'a'ha'ron) BROTHER~you(ms) (אָחִיךָ / a'hhi'kha) and~to~SON~s~him (וּלְבָנָיו / ul'va'naw) to~>~much~ADORN(Verb)~him (לְכַהֲנוֹ / lê'kha'ha'no) to~~me (לִי / li)

and these are the garments which they will make, breastplate and ephod and cloak and tunic of woven material, turban and sash, and they will make garments of specialness for Aharon, your brother, and for his sons, to adorn him for me,

5 and~THEY(m) (וְהֵם / wê'heym) they(m)~will~TAKE(Verb) (יִקְחוּ / yiq'hhu) AT (אֶת / et) the~GOLD (הַזָּהָב / ha'za'hav) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~BLUE (הַתְּכֵלֶת / hat'khey'let) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~PURPLE (הָאַרְגָּמָן / ha'ar'ga'man) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) KERMES (תּוֹלַעַת / to'la'at) the~SCARLET (הַשָּׁנִי / ha'sha'ni) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~LINEN (הַשֵּׁשׁ / ha'sheysh)

and they, they will take the gold and the blue and the purple and the kermes of the scarlet and the linen,

6 and~they~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשׂוּ / wê'a'su) AT (אֶת / et) the~EPHOD (הָאֵפֹד / ha'ey'phod) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) BLUE (תְּכֵלֶת / tê'khey'let) and~PURPLE (וְאַרְגָּמָן / wê'ar'ga'man) KERMES (תּוֹלַעַת / to'la'at) SCARLET (שָׁנִי / sha'ni) and~LINEN (וְשֵׁשׁ / wê'sheysh) be~make~TWIST.TOGETHER(Verb)~ing(ms) (מָשְׁזָר / mash'zar) WORK (מַעֲשֵׂה / ma'a'seyh) THINK(Verb)~ing(ms) (חֹשֵׁב / hho'sheyv) [475]

and they will make the ephod with gold, blue and purple, kermes of scarlet and twisted linen, a work of a thinking.

7 TWO (שְׁתֵּי / shê'tey) SHOULDER.PIECE~s (כְתֵפֹת / khê'tey'phot) COUPLE(Verb)~ing(fp) (חֹבְרֹת / hhov'rot) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) to~~him (לּוֹ / lo) TO (אֶל / el) TWO (שְׁנֵי / shê'ney) EXTREMITY~s~him (קְצוֹתָיו / qê'tso'taw) and~he~did~be~much~COUPLE(Verb) (וְחֻבָּר / wê'hhu'bar)

He will have two shoulder pieces coupled together at the two of his extremities, and he will be coupled,

8 and~DECORATIVE.BAND (וְחֵשֶׁב / wê'hhey'shev) EPHOD~him (אֲפֻדָּתוֹ / a'phu'da'to) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) like~WORK~him (כְּמַעֲשֵׂהוּ / kê'ma'a'sey'hu) FROM~him (מִמֶּנּוּ / mi'me'nu) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) BLUE (תְּכֵלֶת / tê'khey'let) and~PURPLE (וְאַרְגָּמָן / wê'ar'ga'man) and~KERMES (וְתוֹלַעַת / wê'to'la'at) SCARLET (שָׁנִי / sha'ni) and~LINEN (וְשֵׁשׁ / wê'sheysh) be~make~TWIST.TOGETHER(Verb)~ing(ms) (מָשְׁזָר / mash'zar)

and the decorative band of his ephod, which is upon him, he will exist just like his work, gold, blue and purple and kermes of scarlet and twisted linen,

9 and~you(ms)~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקַחְתָּ / wê'la'qahh'ta) AT (אֶת / et) TWO (שְׁתֵּי / shê'tey) STONE~s (אַבְנֵי / av'ney) ONYX (שֹׁהַם / sho'ham) and~you(ms)~did~much~OPEN(Verb) (וּפִתַּחְתָּ / u'phi'tahh'ta) UPON~them(m) (עֲלֵיהֶם / a'ley'hem) TITLE~s (שְׁמוֹת / shê'mot) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl)

and you will take the two stones of the onyx, and you will engrave upon them the titles of the sons of Yisra'eyl.

10 SIX (שִׁשָּׁה / shi'shah) from~TITLE~s~them(m) (מִשְּׁמֹתָם / mish'mo'tam) UPON (עַל / al) the~STONE (הָאֶבֶן / ha'e'ven) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָת / ha'e'hhat) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) TITLE~s (שְׁמוֹת / shê'mot) the~SIX (הַשִּׁשָּׁה / ha'shi'shah) the~be~LEAVE.BEHIND(Verb)~ing(mp) (הַנּוֹתָרִים / ha'no'ta'rim) [476] UPON (עַל / al) the~STONE (הָאֶבֶן / ha'e'ven) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִית / ha'shey'nit) like~BIRTHING~s~them(m) (כְּתוֹלְדֹתָם / kê'tol'do'tam) [477]

Six from their titles upon the one stone and the six titles being left behind upon the second stone, like their birthings.

11 WORK (מַעֲשֵׂה / ma'a'seyh) ENGRAVER (חָרַשׁ / hha'rash) STONE (אֶבֶן / e'ven) OPEN(Verb)~ed(mp) (פִּתּוּחֵי / pi'tu'hhey) SEAL (חֹתָם / hho'tam) you(ms)~will~much~OPEN(Verb) (תְּפַתַּח / tê'pha'tahh) AT (אֶת / et) TWO (שְׁתֵּי / shê'tey) the~STONE~s (הָאֲבָנִים / ha'a'va'nim) UPON (עַל / al) TITLE~s (שְׁמֹת / shê'mot) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) be~make~GO.AROUND(Verb)~ing(fp) (מֻסַבֹּת / mu'sa'bot) PLAIT~s (מִשְׁבְּצוֹת / mish'be'tsot) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam)

Like the work of a stone engraver, like the carvings of a seal, you will engrave the two stones according to the titles of the sons of Yisra'eyl, you will make them enclosed in plaits of gold,

12 and~you(ms)~did~PLACE(Verb) (וְשַׂמְתָּ / wê'sam'ta) AT (אֶת / et) TWO (שְׁתֵּי / shê'tey) the~STONE~s (הָאֲבָנִים / ha'a'va'nim) UPON (עַל / al) SHOULDER.PIECE~s (כִּתְפֹת / kit'phot) the~EPHOD (הָאֵפֹד / ha'ey'phod) STONE~s (אַבְנֵי / av'ney) REMEMBRANCE (זִכָּרֹן / zi'ka'ron) to~SON~s (לִבְנֵי / liv'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~he~did~LIFT.UP(Verb) (וְנָשָׂא / wê'na'sa) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) AT (אֶת / et) TITLE~s~them(m) (שְׁמוֹתָם / shê'mo'tam) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) UPON (עַל / al) TWO (שְׁתֵּי / shê'tey) SHOULDER.PIECE~s~him (כְתֵפָיו / khê'tey'phaw) to~REMEMBRANCE (לְזִכָּרֹן / lê'zi'ka'ron)

and you will place the two stones upon the shoulder piece of the ephod, to be stones of remembrance for the sons of Yisra'eyl, and Aharon will lift up their titles to the face of YHWH, upon his two shoulder pieces for a remembrance,

13 and~you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשִׂיתָ / wê'a'si'ta) PLAIT~s (מִשְׁבְּצֹת / mish'be'tsot) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav)

and you will make plaits of gold,

14 and~TWO (וּשְׁתֵּי / ush'tey) CHAIN~s (שַׁרְשְׁרֹת / shar'she'rot) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) CLEAN (טָהוֹר / ta'hor) BOUNDARY~s (מִגְבָּלֹת / mig'ba'lot) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) WORK (מַעֲשֵׂה / ma'a'seyh) THICK.WOVEN (עֲבֹת / a'vot) and~you(ms)~did~GIVE(Verb)~& (וְנָתַתָּה / wê'na'ta'tah) AT (אֶת / et) CHAIN~s (שַׁרְשְׁרֹת / shar'she'rot) the~THICK.WOVEN~s (הָעֲבֹתֹת / ha'a'vo'tot) UPON (עַל / al) the~PLAIT~s (הַמִּשְׁבְּצֹת / ha'mish'be'tsot)

and two chains of clean gold are at the boundaries, you will make them a work of thick woven things, and you will place the chains of thick woven things upon the plaits,

15 and~you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשִׂיתָ / wê'a'si'ta) BREASTPLATE (חֹשֶׁן / hho'shen) DECISION (מִשְׁפָּט / mish'pat) WORK (מַעֲשֵׂה / ma'a'seyh) THINK(Verb)~ing(ms) (חֹשֵׁב / hho'sheyv) [478] like~WORK (כְּמַעֲשֵׂה / kê'ma'a'seyh) EPHOD (אֵפֹד / ey'phod) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb)~him (תַּעֲשֶׂנּוּ / ta'a'se'nu) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) BLUE (תְּכֵלֶת / tê'khey'let) and~PURPLE (וְאַרְגָּמָן / wê'ar'ga'man) and~KERMES (וְתוֹלַעַת / wê'to'la'at) SCARLET (שָׁנִי / sha'ni) and~LINEN (וְשֵׁשׁ / wê'sheysh) be~make~TWIST.TOGETHER(Verb)~ing(ms) (מָשְׁזָר / mash'zar) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to)

and you will make a breastplate of decision, a work of thinking, like the work of the ephod you will make him, with gold, blue and purple and kermes of scarlet and twisted linen you will make him.

16 BE.SQUARE(Verb)~ed(ms) (רָבוּעַ / ra'vu'a) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) DOUBLE.OVER(Verb)~ed(ms) (כָּפוּל / ka'phul) FINGER.SPAN (זֶרֶת / ze'ret) LENGTH~him (אָרְכּוֹ / ar'ko) and~FINGER.SPAN (וְזֶרֶת / wê'ze'ret) WIDTH~him (רָחְבּוֹ / rahh'bo)

He will exist squared, doubled over, a finger span is his length, and a finger span is his width,

17 and~you(ms)~did~much~FILL(Verb) (וּמִלֵּאתָ / u'mi'ley'ta) in~~him (בוֹ / vo) SETTING (מִלֻּאַת / mi'lu'at) STONE (אֶבֶן / e'ven) FOUR (אַרְבָּעָה / ar'ba'ah) ROW~s (טוּרִים / tu'rim) STONE (אָבֶן / a'ven) ROW (טוּר / tur) CARNELIAN (אֹדֶם / o'dem) OLIVINE (פִּטְדָה / pit'dah) and~EMERALD (וּבָרֶקֶת / u'va're'qet) the~ROW (הַטּוּר / ha'tur) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad)

and you will set in him settings of stone, four rows of stone, a row of carnelian, olivine and emerald is the one row,

18 and~the~ROW (וְהַטּוּר / wê'ha'tur) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִי / ha'shey'ni) TURQUOISE (נֹפֶךְ / no'phekh) LAPIS.LAZULI (סַפִּיר / sa'pir) and~FLINT (וְיָהֲלֹם / wê'ya'ha'lom)

and the second row is turquoise, lapis-lazuli and flint,

19 and~the~ROW (וְהַטּוּר / wê'ha'tur) the~THIRD (הַשְּׁלִישִׁי / hash'li'shi) OPAL (לֶשֶׁם / le'shem) AGATE (שְׁבוֹ / shê'vo) and~AMETHYST (וְאַחְלָמָה / wê'ahh'la'mah)

and the third row is opal, agate and amethyst,

20 and~the~ROW (וְהַטּוּר / wê'ha'tur) the~FOURTH (הָרְבִיעִי / har'vi'i) TOPAZ (תַּרְשִׁישׁ / tar'shish) and~ONYX (וְשֹׁהַם / wê'sho'ham) and~JASPER (וְיָשְׁפֵה / wê'yash'pheyh) be~much~WEAVE(Verb)~ing(mp) (מְשֻׁבָּצִים / mê'shu'ba'tsim) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) they(m)~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיוּ / yih'yu) in~SETTING~s~them(m) (בְּמִלּוּאֹתָם / bê'mi'lu'o'tam)

and the fourth row is topaz and onyx and jasper, being woven with gold they will exist in their settings,

21 and~the~STONE~s (וְהָאֲבָנִים / wê'ha'a'va'nim) they(f)~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִּהְיֶיןָ / tih'yey'na) UPON (עַל / al) TITLE~s (שְׁמֹת / shê'mot) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) TWO (שְׁתֵּים / shê'teym) TEN (עֶשְׂרֵה / es'reyh) UPON (עַל / al) TITLE~s~them(m) (שְׁמֹתָם / shê'mo'tam) OPEN(Verb)~ed(mp) (פִּתּוּחֵי / pi'tu'hhey) SEAL (חוֹתָם / hho'tam) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) UPON (עַל / al) TITLE~him (שְׁמוֹ / shê'mo) they(f)~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִּהְיֶיןָ / tih'yey'na) to~TWO (לִשְׁנֵי / lish'ney) TEN (עָשָׂר / a'sar) STAFF (שָׁבֶט / sha'vet) [479]

and the stones will exist according to the titles of the sons of Yisra'eyl, twelve according to their titles, carvings of the seal of each according to his title, they will exist for the twelve staffs,

22 and~you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשִׂיתָ / wê'a'si'ta) UPON (עַל / al) the~BREASTPLATE (הַחֹשֶׁן / ha'hho'shen) CHAIN~s (שַׁרְשֹׁת / shar'shot) EDGING (גַּבְלֻת / gav'lut) WORK (מַעֲשֵׂה / ma'a'seyh) THICK.WOVEN (עֲבֹת / a'vot) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) CLEAN (טָהוֹר / ta'hor)

and you will make upon the breastplate chains, the edging is a work of a thick woven thing of clean gold,
