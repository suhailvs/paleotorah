Genesis Chapter 32

1 (55) and~he~will~make~DEPART.EARLY(Verb) (וַיַּשְׁכֵּם / wai'yash'keym) Lavan (לָבָן / la'van) in~the~MORNING (בַּבֹּקֶר / ba'bo'qer) and~he~will~much~KISS(Verb) (וַיְנַשֵּׁק / wa'ye'na'sheyq) to~SON~s~him (לְבָנָיו / lê'va'naw) and~to~DAUGHTER~s~him (וְלִבְנוֹתָיו / wê'liv'no'taw) and~he~will~much~KNEEL(Verb) (וַיְבָרֶךְ / wai'va'rekh) AT~them(m) (אֶתְהֶם / et'hem) and~he~will~WALK(Verb) (וַיֵּלֶךְ / wai'yey'lekh) and~he~will~TURN.BACK(Verb) (וַיָּשָׁב / wai'ya'shav) Lavan (לָבָן / la'van) to~AREA~him (לִמְקֹמוֹ / lim'qo'mo) [173]

and Lavan departed early in the morning and he kissed his sons and his daughters and he exalted them and he walked and Lavan turned to his place,

2 (1) and~Ya'aqov (וְיַעֲקֹב / wê'ya'a'qov) he~did~WALK(Verb) (הָלַךְ / ha'lakh) to~ROAD~him (לְדַרְכּוֹ / lê'dar'ko) and~they(m)~will~REACH(Verb) (וַיִּפְגְּעוּ / wai'yiph'ge'u) in~~him (בוֹ / vo) MESSENGER~s (מַלְאֲכֵי / mal'a'khey) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him)

and Ya'aqov had walked to his road and messengers of Elohiym reached him,

3 (2) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~SEE(Verb)~them(m) (רָאָם / ra'am) CAMP (מַחֲנֵה / ma'hha'neyh) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) THIS (זֶה / zeh) and~he~will~CALL.OUT(Verb) (וַיִּקְרָא / wai'yiq'ra) TITLE (שֵׁם / sheym) the~AREA (הַמָּקוֹם / ha'ma'qom) the~HE (הַהוּא / ha'hu) Mahhanayim (מַחֲנָיִם / ma'hha'na'yim)

and Ya'aqov said just as he saw them, this is the camp of Elohiym and he called out the title of that place Mahhanayim,

4 (3) and~he~will~SEND(Verb) (וַיִּשְׁלַח / wai'yish'lahh) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) MESSENGER~s (מַלְאָכִים / mal'a'khim) to~FACE~s~him (לְפָנָיו / lê'pha'naw) TO (אֶל / el) Esaw (עֵשָׂו / ey'saw) BROTHER~him (אָחִיו / a'hhiw) LAND~unto (אַרְצָה / ar'tsah) Se'iyr (שֵׂעִיר / sey'ir) FIELD (שְׂדֵה / sê'deyh) Edom (אֱדוֹם / e'dom)

and Ya'aqov sent messengers before him to Esaw his brother, unto the land of Se'iyr, the field of Edom,

5 (4) and~he~will~much~DIRECT(Verb) (וַיְצַו / wai'tsaw) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) IN.THIS.WAY (כֹּה / koh) you(ms)~will~SAY(Verb)~must (תֹאמְרוּן / tom'run) to~LORD~me (לַאדֹנִי / la'do'ni) to~Esaw (לְעֵשָׂו / lê'ey'saw) IN.THIS.WAY (כֹּה / koh) he~did~SAY(Verb) (אָמַר / a'mar) SERVANT~you(ms) (עַבְדְּךָ / av'de'kha) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) WITH (עִם / im) Lavan (לָבָן / la'van) I~did~IMMIGRATE(Verb) (גַּרְתִּי / gar'ti) and~I~will~DELAY(Verb) (וָאֵחַר / wa'ey'hhar) UNTIL (עַד / ad) NOW (עָתָּה / a'tah)

and he directed them saying, in this way you will say to my lord, to Esaw, in this way your servant Ya'aqov said, I had immigrated with Lavan and I delayed until now,

6 (5) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) to~~me (לִי / li) OX (שׁוֹר / shor) and~DONKEY (וַחֲמוֹר / wa'hha'mor) FLOCKS (צֹאן / tson) and~SERVANT (וְעֶבֶד / wê'e'ved) and~MAID (וְשִׁפְחָה / wê'shiph'hhah) and~I~will~SEND(Verb)~& (וָאֶשְׁלְחָה / wa'esh'le'hhah) to~>~make~BE.FACE.TO.FACE(Verb) (לְהַגִּיד / lê'ha'gid) to~LORD~me (לַאדֹנִי / la'do'ni) to~>~FIND(Verb) (לִמְצֹא / lim'tso) BEAUTY (חֵן / hheyn) in~EYE~s2~you(ms) (בְּעֵינֶיךָ / bê'ey'ney'kha)

and it came to pass, I have ox and donkey, flocks and servant and maid and I sent to tell to my lord to find beauty in your eyes,

7 (6) and~they(m)~will~TURN.BACK(Verb) (וַיָּשֻׁבוּ / wai'ya'shu'vu) the~MESSENGER~s (הַמַּלְאָכִים / ha'mal'a'khim) TO (אֶל / el) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) we~did~COME(Verb) (בָּאנוּ / ba'nu) TO (אֶל / el) BROTHER~you(ms) (אָחִיךָ / a'hhi'kha) TO (אֶל / el) Esaw (עֵשָׂו / ey'saw) and~ALSO (וְגַם / wê'gam) WALK(Verb)~ing(ms) (הֹלֵךְ / ho'leykh) to~>~MEET(Verb)~you(ms) (לִקְרָאתְךָ / liq'rat'kha) and~FOUR (וְאַרְבַּע / wê'ar'ba) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) WITH~him (עִמּוֹ / i'mo)

and the messengers turned back to Ya'aqov saying, we came to your brother, to Esaw and also walking to meet you and four hundred men with him,

8 (7) and~he~will~FEAR(Verb) (וַיִּירָא / wai'yi'ra) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) MANY (מְאֹד / mê'od) and~he~will~MOLD(Verb) (וַיֵּצֶר / wai'yey'tser) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) and~he~will~DIVIDE(Verb) (וַיַּחַץ / wai'ya'hhats) AT (אֶת / et) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) AT~him (אִתּוֹ / i'to) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~FLOCKS (הַצֹּאן / ha'tson) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~CATTLE (הַבָּקָר / ha'ba'qar) and~the~CAMEL~s (וְהַגְּמַלִּים / wê'ha'ge'ma'lim) to~TWO (לִשְׁנֵי / lish'ney) CAMP~s (מַחֲנוֹת / ma'hhanot)

and Ya'aqov greatly feared and he distressed for him and he divided the people which were with him and the flocks and the cattle and the camels to two camps,

9 (8) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) IF (אִם / im) he~will~COME(Verb) (יָבוֹא / ya'vo) Esaw (עֵשָׂו / ey'saw) TO (אֶל / el) the~CAMP (הַמַּחֲנֶה / ha'ma'hha'neh) the~UNIT (הָאַחַת / ha'a'hhat) and~he~did~make~HIT(Verb)~him (וְהִכָּהוּ / wê'hi'ka'u) and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) the~CAMP (הַמַּחֲנֶה / ha'ma'hha'neh) the~be~REMAIN(Verb)~ing(ms) (הַנִּשְׁאָר / ha'nish'ar) to~ESCAPED (לִפְלֵיטָה / liph'ley'tah)

and he said, if Esaw will come to the one camp and he will attack him, then the camp remaining will exist for an escape,

10 (9) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) Elohiym (אֱלֹהֵי / e'lo'hey) FATHER~me (אָבִי / a'vi) Avraham (אַבְרָהָם / av'ra'ham) and~Elohiym (וֵאלֹהֵי / wey'lo'hey) FATHER~me (אָבִי / a'vi) Yits'hhaq (יִצְחָק / yits'hhaq) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) the~SAY(Verb)~ing(ms) (הָאֹמֵר / ha'o'meyr) TO~me (אֵלַי / ey'lai) !(ms)~TURN.BACK(Verb) (שׁוּב / shuv) to~LAND~you(ms) (לְאַרְצְךָ / lê'ar'tse'kha) and~to~KINDRED~you(ms) (וּלְמוֹלַדְתְּךָ / ul'mo'lad'te'kha) and~I~will~make~DO.WELL(Verb)~& (וְאֵיטִיבָה / wê'ey'ti'vah) WITH~you(fs) (עִמָּךְ / i'makh)

and Ya'aqov said, the Elohiym of my father Avraham, the Elohiym of my father Yits'hhaq, YHWH is the one saying to me, turn back to your land and to your kindred and I will make it do well with you.

11 (10) I~did~BE.SMALL(Verb) (קָטֹנְתִּי / qa'ton'ti) from~ALL (מִכֹּל / mi'kol) the~KINDNESS~s (הַחֲסָדִים / ha'hha'sa'dim) and~from~ALL (וּמִכָּל / u'mi'kol) the~TRUTH (הָאֱמֶת / ha'e'met) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (עָשִׂיתָ / a'si'ta) AT (אֶת / et) SERVANT~you(ms) (עַבְדֶּךָ / av'de'kha) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) in~ROD~me (בְמַקְלִי / vê'maq'li) I~did~CROSS.OVER(Verb) (עָבַרְתִּי / a'var'ti) AT (אֶת / et) the~Yarden (הַיַּרְדֵּן / hai'yar'deyn) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh) and~NOW (וְעַתָּה / wê'a'tah) I~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיִיתִי / hai'yi'ti) to~TWO (לִשְׁנֵי / lish'ney) CAMP~s (מַחֲנוֹת / ma'hhanot)

I am small from all the kindnesses and from all the truth which you did to your servant given that with my rod I crossed over this Yarden and now I exist as two camps.

12 (11) !(ms)~make~DELIVER(Verb)~me (הַצִּילֵנִי / ha'tsi'ley'ni) PLEASE (נָא / na) from~HAND (מִיַּד / mi'yad) BROTHER~me (אָחִי / a'hhi) from~HAND (מִיַּד / mi'yad) Esaw (עֵשָׂו / ey'saw) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~FEAR(Verb) (יָרֵא / ya'rey) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) OTHERWISE (פֶּן / pen) he~will~COME(Verb) (יָבוֹא / ya'vo) and~he~did~make~HIT(Verb)~me (וְהִכַּנִי / wê'hi'ka'ni) MOTHER (אֵם / eym) UPON (עַל / al) SON~s (בָּנִים / ba'nim)

Please deliver me from the hand of my brother, from the hand of Esaw given that I am fearing him otherwise he will come and he will attack me, mother upon sons,

13 (12) and~YOU(MS) (וְאַתָּה / wê'a'tah) you(ms)~did~SAY(Verb) (אָמַרְתָּ / a'mar'ta) >~make~DO.WELL(Verb) (הֵיטֵב / hey'teyv) I~will~make~DO.WELL(Verb) (אֵיטִיב / ey'tiv) WITH~you(fs) (עִמָּךְ / i'makh) and~I~did~PLACE(Verb) (וְשַׂמְתִּי / wê'sam'ti) AT (אֶת / et) SEED~you(ms) (זַרְעֲךָ / zar'a'kha) like~SAND (כְּחוֹל / kê'hhol) the~SEA (הַיָּם / hai'yam) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~be~COUNT(Verb) (יִסָּפֵר / yi'sa'pheyr) from~ABUNDANCE (מֵרֹב / mey'rov)

and you said, I will surely make it do well with you and I set your seed in place like the sand of the sea which cannot be counted out of the abundance,

14 (13) and~he~will~STAY.THE.NIGHT(Verb) (וַיָּלֶן / wai'ya'len) THERE (שָׁם / sham) in~the~NIGHT (בַּלַּיְלָה / ba'lai'lah) the~HE (הַהוּא / ha'hu) and~he~will~TAKE(Verb) (וַיִּקַּח / wai'yi'qahh) FROM (מִן / min) the~COME(Verb)~ing(ms) (הַבָּא / ha'ba) in~HAND~him (בְיָדוֹ / vê'ya'do) DEPOSIT (מִנְחָה / min'hhah) to~Esaw (לְעֵשָׂו / lê'ey'saw) BROTHER~him (אָחִיו / a'hhiw)

and he stayed the night there in that night and he took from the one coming in his hand a donation for Esaw his brother.

15 (14) GOAT~s (עִזִּים / i'zim) HUNDRED~s2 (מָאתַיִם / ma'ta'yim) and~HE.GOAT~s (וּתְיָשִׁים / ut'ya'shim) TEN~s (עֶשְׂרִים / es'rim) EWE~s (רְחֵלִים / rê'hhey'lim) HUNDRED~s2 (מָאתַיִם / ma'ta'yim) and~BUCK~s (וְאֵילִים / wê'ey'lim) TEN~s (עֶשְׂרִים / es'rim)

Two hundred goats and twenty he-goats, two hundred ewes and twenty strong ones.

16 (15) CAMEL~s (גְּמַלִּים / gê'ma'lim) make~SUCKLE(Verb)~ing(fp) (מֵינִיקוֹת / mey'ni'qot) and~SON~s~them(m) (וּבְנֵיהֶם / uv'ney'hem) THREE~s (שְׁלֹשִׁים / shê'lo'shim) COW~s (פָּרוֹת / pa'rot) FOUR~s (אַרְבָּעִים / ar'ba'im) and~BULL~s (וּפָרִים / u'pha'rim) TEN (עֲשָׂרָה / a'sa'rah) SHE.DONKEY~s (אֲתֹנֹת / a'to'not) TEN~s (עֶשְׂרִים / es'rim) and~COLT~s (וַעְיָרִם / wa'ya'rim) TEN (עֲשָׂרָה / a'sa'rah)

Thirty nurse camels and their sons, forty cows and ten bulls, twenty she-donkeys, ten colts,

17 (16) and~he~will~GIVE(Verb) (וַיִּתֵּן / wai'yi'teyn) in~HAND (בְּיַד / bê'yad) SERVANT~s~him (עֲבָדָיו / a'va'daw) DROVE (עֵדֶר / ey'der) DROVE (עֵדֶר / ey'der) to~STRAND~him (לְבַדּוֹ / lê'va'do) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) TO (אֶל / el) SERVANT~s~him (עֲבָדָיו / a'va'daw) !(mp)~CROSS.OVER(Verb) (עִבְרוּ / iv'ru) to~FACE~s~me (לְפָנַי / lê'pha'nai) and~WIND (וְרֶוַח / wê're'wahh) you(mp)~will~PLACE(Verb) (תָּשִׂימוּ / ta'si'mu) BETWEEN (בֵּין / beyn) DROVE (עֵדֶר / ey'der) and~BETWEEN (וּבֵין / u'veyn) DROVE (עֵדֶר / ey'der)

and he gave a drove in the hand of his servants, a drove by himself and he said to the servants, cross over to my face and you will set a wind in place between a drove and a drove,

18 (17) and~he~will~much~DIRECT(Verb) (וַיְצַו / wai'tsaw) AT (אֶת / et) the~FIRST (הָרִאשׁוֹן / ha'ri'shon) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~ENCOUNTER(Verb)~you(ms) (יִפְגָשְׁךָ / yiph'gash'kha) Esaw (עֵשָׂו / ey'saw) BROTHER~me (אָחִי / a'hhi) and~he~did~INQUIRE(Verb)~you(ms) (וּשְׁאֵלְךָ / ush'eyl'kha) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) to~WHO (לְמִי / lê'mi) YOU(MS) (אַתָּה / a'tah) and~WHEREVER (וְאָנָה / wê'a'nah) you(ms)~will~WALK(Verb) (תֵלֵךְ / tey'leykh) and~to~WHO (וּלְמִי / ul'mi) THESE (אֵלֶּה / ey'leh) to~FACE~s~you(ms) (לְפָנֶיךָ / lê'pha'ney'kha)

and he directed to the first saying, given that Esaw my brother will encounter you and he will inquire of you saying, to who are you, and wherever are you walking, and to who are these to your face,

19 (18) and~you(ms)~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמַרְתָּ / wê'a'mar'ta) to~SERVANT~you(ms) (לְעַבְדְּךָ / lê'av'de'kha) to~Ya'aqov (לְיַעֲקֹב / lê'ya'a'qov) DEPOSIT (מִנְחָה / min'hhah) SHE (הִוא / hi) SEND(Verb)~ing(fs) (שְׁלוּחָה / shê'lu'hhah) to~LORD~me (לַאדֹנִי / la'do'ni) to~Esaw (לְעֵשָׂו / lê'ey'saw) and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) ALSO (גַם / gam) HE (הוּא / hu) AFTER~us (אַחֲרֵינוּ / a'hha'rey'nu)

and you will say, to your servant, to Ya'aqov, she is a donation being sent to my lord, to Esaw and look, he is also behind us,
