Numbers Chapter 10

1 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

2 !(ms)~DO(Verb) (עֲשֵׂה / a'seyh) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) TWO (שְׁתֵּי / shê'tey) STRAIGHT.TRUMPET~s (חֲצוֹצְרֹת / hha'tsots'rot) SILVER (כֶּסֶף / ke'seph) BEATEN.WORK (מִקְשָׁה / miq'shah) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) and~they~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיוּ / wê'hai'u) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) to~MEETING (לְמִקְרָא / lê'miq'ra) the~COMPANY (הָעֵדָה / ha'ey'dah) and~to~JOURNEY (וּלְמַסַּע / ul'ma'sa) AT (אֶת / et) the~CAMP~s (הַמַּחֲנוֹת / ha'ma'hha'not)

Make for you two silver straight trumpets, beaten work you will make them, and they will exist for you for the meeting of the company, and for the journeying with the camps,

3 and~they~did~THRUST(Verb) (וְתָקְעוּ / wê'taq'u) in~~them(f) (בָּהֵן / ba'heyn) and~they~did~be~APPOINT(Verb) (וְנוֹעֲדוּ / wê'no'a'du) TO~you(ms) (אֵלֶיךָ / ey'ley'kha) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~COMPANY (הָעֵדָה / ha'ey'dah) TO (אֶל / el) OPENING (פֶּתַח / pe'tahh) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd)

and they will blow in them, and all the company will meet with you at the opening of the appointed tent,

4 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) in~UNIT (בְּאַחַת / bê'a'ahhat) [796] they(m)~will~THRUST(Verb) (יִתְקָעוּ / yit'qa'u) and~they~did~be~APPOINT(Verb) (וְנוֹעֲדוּ / wê'no'a'du) TO~you(ms) (אֵלֶיךָ / ey'ley'kha) the~CAPTAIN~s (הַנְּשִׂיאִים / han'si'im) HEAD~s (רָאשֵׁי / ra'shey) THOUSAND~s (אַלְפֵי / al'phey) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl)

and if with one they will blow, and they will meet with you, the captains, the heads of thousands of Yisra'eyl,

5 and~you(mp)~did~THRUST(Verb) (וּתְקַעְתֶּם / ut'qa'tem) SHOUT (תְּרוּעָה / tê'ru'ah) and~they~did~JOURNEY(Verb) (וְנָסְעוּ / wê'nas'u) the~CAMP~s (הַמַּחֲנוֹת / ha'ma'hha'not) the~CAMP(Verb)~ing(mp) (הַחֹנִים / ha'hho'nim) EAST~unto (קֵדְמָה / qeyd'mah)

and you will blow an alarm, and the camps camping to the east will journey,

6 and~you(mp)~did~THRUST(Verb) (וּתְקַעְתֶּם / ut'qa'tem) SHOUT (תְּרוּעָה / tê'ru'ah) SECOND (שֵׁנִית / shey'nit) and~they~did~JOURNEY(Verb) (וְנָסְעוּ / wê'nas'u) the~CAMP~s (הַמַּחֲנוֹת / ha'ma'hha'not) the~CAMP(Verb)~ing(mp) (הַחֹנִים / ha'hho'nim) SOUTHWARD~unto (תֵּימָנָה / tey'ma'nah) SHOUT (תְּרוּעָה / tê'ru'ah) they(m)~will~THRUST(Verb) (יִתְקְעוּ / yit'qe'u) to~JOURNEY~s~them(m) (לְמַסְעֵיהֶם / lê'mas'ey'hem)

and you will blow a second shout, and the camps camping to the south will journey, an alarm they will blow for their journeys,

7 and~>~make~ASSEMBLE(Verb) (וּבְהַקְהִיל / uv'haq'hil) AT (אֶת / et) the~ASSEMBLY (הַקָּהָל / ha'qa'hal) you(mp)~will~THRUST(Verb) (תִּתְקְעוּ / tit'qe'u) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(mp)~will~make~SHOUT(Verb) (תָרִיעוּ / ta'ri'u)

and make the assembly assemble, you will blow and you will not make an alarm,

8 and~SON~s (וּבְנֵי / uv'ney) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) the~ADMINISTRATOR~s (הַכֹּהֲנִים / ha'ko'ha'nim) they(m)~will~THRUST(Verb) (יִתְקְעוּ / yit'qe'u) in~the~STRAIGHT.TRUMPET (בַּחֲצֹצְרוֹת / ba'hha'tsots'rot) and~they~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיוּ / wê'hai'u) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) to~CUSTOM (לְחֻקַּת / lê'hhu'qat) DISTANT (עוֹלָם / o'lam) to~GENERATION~s~you(mp) (לְדֹרֹתֵיכֶם / lê'do'ro'tey'khem)

and the sons of Aharon, the administrators, will blow in the straight trumpet, and they will exist for you to a distant custom to your generations,

9 and~GIVEN.THAT (וְכִי / wê'khi) you(mp)~will~COME(Verb) (תָבֹאוּ / ta'vo'u) BATTLE (מִלְחָמָה / mil'hha'mah) in~LAND~you(mp) (בְּאַרְצְכֶם / bê'ar'tse'khem) UPON (עַל / al) the~NARROW (הַצַּר / ha'tsar) the~PRESS.IN(Verb)~ing(ms) (הַצֹּרֵר / ha'tso'reyr) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem) and~you(mp)~did~make~SHOUT(Verb) (וַהֲרֵעֹתֶם / wa'ha'rey'o'tem) in~the~STRAIGHT.TRUMPET (בַּחֲצֹצְרוֹת / ba'hha'tsots'rot) and~you(mp)~did~be~REMEMBER(Verb) (וֲנִזְכַּרְתֶּם / wa'niz'kar'tem) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem) and~you(mp)~did~be~RESCUE(Verb) (וְנוֹשַׁעְתֶּם / wê'no'sha'tem) from~ATTACK(Verb)~ing(mp)~you(mp) (מֵאֹיְבֵיכֶם / mey'oy'vey'khem)

and given that you will come to battle in your land, against the enemy, the one pressing in on you, and you will make a shout with the straight trumpet, and you will be remembered to the face of YHWH your Elohiym, and you will be rescued from your attackers,

10 and~in~DAY (וּבְיוֹם / uv'yom) REJOICING~you(mp) (שִׂמְחַתְכֶם / sim'hhat'khem) and~in~APPOINTED~s~you(mp) (וּבְמוֹעֲדֵיכֶם / uv'mo'a'dey'khem) and~in~HEAD~s (וּבְרָאשֵׁי / uv'ra'shey) NEW.MOON~s~you(mp) (חָדְשֵׁיכֶם / hhad'shey'khem) and~you(mp)~did~THRUST(Verb) (וּתְקַעְתֶּם / ut'qa'tem) in~the~STRAIGHT.TRUMPET (בַּחֲצֹצְרֹת / ba'hha'tsots'rot) UPON (עַל / al) ASCENSION.OFFERING~s~you(mp) (עֹלֹתֵיכֶם / o'lo'tey'khem) and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) SACRIFICE~s (זִבְחֵי / zav'hhey) OFFERING.OF.RESTITUTION~s~you(mp) (שַׁלְמֵיכֶם / shal'mey'khem) and~they~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיוּ / wê'hai'u) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) to~REMEMBRANCE (לְזִכָּרוֹן / lê'zi'ka'ron) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem)

and in the day of your rejoicing, and in your appointed times, and in the heads of your new moons, then you will blow with the straight trumpet over your ascension offerings and over your sacrifices of offerings of restitution, and they will exist for you for a remembrance to the face of your Elohiym, I am YHWH your Elohiym,

11 and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) in~the~YEAR (בַּשָּׁנָה / ba'sha'nah) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִית / ha'shey'nit) in~the~NEW.MOON (בַּחֹדֶשׁ / ba'hho'desh) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִי / ha'shey'ni) in~TEN~s (בְּעֶשְׂרִים / bê'es'rim) in~the~NEW.MOON (בַּחֹדֶשׁ / ba'hho'desh) he~did~be~GO.UP(Verb) (נַעֲלָה / na'a'lah) the~CLOUD (הֶעָנָן / he'a'nan) from~UPON (מֵעַל / mey'al) DWELLING (מִשְׁכַּן / mish'kan) the~EVIDENCE (הָעֵדֻת / ha'ey'dut)

and it will come to pass in the second year, in the second new moon, the cloud went up from upon the dwelling of the evidence,

12 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) to~JOURNEY~s~them(m) (לְמַסְעֵיהֶם / lê'mas'ey'hem) from~WILDERNESS (מִמִּדְבַּר / mi'mid'bar) Sinai (סִינָי / si'nai) and~he~will~DWELL(Verb) (וַיִּשְׁכֹּן / wai'yish'kon) the~CLOUD (הֶעָנָן / he'a'nan) in~WILDERNESS (בְּמִדְבַּר / bê'mid'bar) Paran (פָּארָן / pa'ran)

and the sons of Yisra'eyl will journey in their journeys from the wilderness of Sinai, and the cloud dwelt in the wilderness of Paran,

13 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) in~the~FIRST (בָּרִאשֹׁנָה / ba'ri'sho'nah) UPON (עַל / al) MOUTH (פִּי / pi) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) in~HAND (בְּיַד / bê'yad) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh)

and they first journeyed according to the mouth of YHWH by the hand of Mosheh,

14 and~he~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסַּע / wai'yi'sa) BANNER (דֶּגֶל / de'gel) CAMP (מַחֲנֵה / ma'hha'neyh) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Yehudah (יְהוּדָה / yê'hu'dah) in~the~FIRST (בָּרִאשֹׁנָה / ba'ri'sho'nah) to~ARMY~s~them(m) (לְצִבְאֹתָם / lê'tsiv'o'tam) and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) ARMY~him (צְבָאוֹ / tsê'va'o) Nahhshon (נַחְשׁוֹן / nahh'shon) SON (בֶּן / ben) Amiynadav (עַמִּינָדָב / a'mi'na'dav)

and the banner of the camp of the sons of Yehudah journeyed first, by their armies, and over his army is Nahhshon the son of Amiynadav,

15 and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) ARMY (צְבָא / tsê'va) BRANCH (מַטֵּה / ma'teyh) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yis'sas'khar (יִשָׂשכָר / yi'sas'khar) Nataneyl (נְתַנְאֵל / nê'tan'eyl) SON (בֶּן / ben) Tso'ar (צוּעָר / tsu'ar)

and over the army of the branch of the sons of Yis'sas'khar in Nataneyl the son of Tso'ar,

16 and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) ARMY (צְבָא / tsê'va) BRANCH (מַטֵּה / ma'teyh) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Zevulun (זְבוּלֻן / zê'vu'lun) Eli'av (אֱלִיאָב / e'li'av) SON (בֶּן / ben) Hheylon (חֵלוֹן / hhey'lon)

and over the army of the branch of the sons of Zevulun is Eli'av the son of Hheylon,

17 and~he~did~be~make~GO.DOWN(Verb) (וְהוּרַד / wê'hu'rad) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan) and~they~did~JOURNEY(Verb) (וְנָסְעוּ / wê'nas'u) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Gershon (גֵרְשׁוֹן / ger'shon) and~SON~s (וּבְנֵי / uv'ney) Merari (מְרָרִי / mê'ra'ri) LIFT.UP(Verb)~ing(mp) (נֹשְׂאֵי / nos'ey) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan)

and the dwelling will be brought down, and the sons of Gershon will journey, and the sons of Merari will lift up the dwelling,

18 and~he~did~JOURNEY(Verb) (וְנָסַע / wê'na'sa) BANNER (דֶּגֶל / de'gel) CAMP (מַחֲנֵה / ma'hha'neyh) Re'uven (רְאוּבֵן / rê'u'veyn) to~ARMY~s~them(m) (לְצִבְאֹתָם / lê'tsiv'o'tam) and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) ARMY~him (צְבָאוֹ / tsê'va'o) Elitsur (אֱלִיצוּר / e'li'tsur) SON (בֶּן / ben) Shedeyur (שְׁדֵיאוּר / shê'dey'ur)

and the banner of the camp of Re'uven will journey according to their armies, and over his army is Elitsur the son of Shedeyur,

19 and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) ARMY (צְבָא / tsê'va) BRANCH (מַטֵּה / ma'teyh) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Shimon (שִׁמְעוֹן / shim'on) Shelumi'eyl (שְׁלֻמִיאֵל / shê'lu'mi'eyl) SON (בֶּן / ben) Tsurishaddai (צוּרִי-שַׁדָּי / tsu'ri)

and over the army of the branch of the sons of Shimon is Shelumi'eyl the son of Tsurishaddai,

20 and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) ARMY (צְבָא / tsê'va) BRANCH (מַטֵּה / ma'teyh) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Gad (גָד / gad) Elyasaph (אֶלְיָסָף / el'ya'saph) SON (בֶּן / ben) De'u'eyl (דְּעוּאֵל / dê'u'eyl)

and over the army of the branch of the sons of Gad is Elyasaph the son of De'u'eyl,

21 and~they~did~JOURNEY(Verb) (וְנָסְעוּ / wê'nas'u) the~Qehat~s (הַקְּהָתִים / haq'ha'tim) LIFT.UP(Verb)~ing(mp) (נֹשְׂאֵי / nos'ey) the~SANCTUARY (הַמִּקְדָּשׁ / ha'miq'dash) and~they~did~make~RISE(Verb) (וְהֵקִימוּ / wê'hey'qi'mu) AT (אֶת / et) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan) UNTIL (עַד / ad) >~COME(Verb)~them(m) (בֹּאָם / bo'am)

and the ones of Qehat will journey, lifting up the sanctuary, and they will make the dwelling rise until their coming,

22 and~he~did~JOURNEY(Verb) (וְנָסַע / wê'na'sa) BANNER (דֶּגֶל / de'gel) CAMP (מַחֲנֵה / ma'hha'neyh) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Ephrayim (אֶפְרַיִם / eph'ra'yim) to~ARMY~s~them(m) (לְצִבְאֹתָם / lê'tsiv'o'tam) and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) ARMY~him (צְבָאוֹ / tsê'va'o) Elishama (אֱלִישָׁמָע / e'li'sha'ma) SON (בֶּן / ben) Amihud (עַמִּיהוּד / a'mi'hud)

and the banner of the camp of Ephrayim will journey according to their armies, and over his army is Elishama the son of Amihud,

23 and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) ARMY (צְבָא / tsê'va) BRANCH (מַטֵּה / ma'teyh) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Menasheh (מְנַשֶּׁה / mê'na'sheh) Gamli'eyl (גַּמְלִיאֵל / gam'li'eyl) SON (בֶּן / ben) Pedatsur (פְּדָה־צוּר / pê'dah-tsur)

and over the army of the branch of the sons of Menasheh is Gamli'eyl the son of Pedatsur,

24 and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) ARMY (צְבָא / tsê'va) BRANCH (מַטֵּה / ma'teyh) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Binyamin (בִנְיָמִן / vin'ya'min) Avidan (אֲבִידָן / a'vi'dan) SON (בֶּן / ben) Gidoni (גִּדְעוֹנִי / gid'o'ni)

and over the army of the branch of the sons of Binyamin is Avidan the son of Gidoni,

25 and~he~did~JOURNEY(Verb) (וְנָסַע / wê'na'sa) BANNER (דֶּגֶל / de'gel) CAMP (מַחֲנֵה / ma'hha'neyh) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Dan (דָן / dan) much~GATHER(Verb)~ing(ms) (מְאַסֵּף / mê'a'seyph) [797] to~ALL (לְכָל / lê'khol) the~CAMP~s (הַמַּחֲנֹת / ha'ma'hha'not) to~ARMY~s~them(m) (לְצִבְאֹתָם / lê'tsiv'o'tam) and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) ARMY~him (צְבָאוֹ / tsê'va'o) Ahhi'ezer (אֲחִיעֶזֶר / a'hhi'e'zer) SON (בֶּן / ben) Amishaddai (עַמִּישַׁדָּי / a'mi'sha'dai)

and the banner of the camp of Dan will journey, gathering for all the camps, according to their armies, and over his army is Ahhi'ezer the son of Amishaddai,

26 and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) ARMY (צְבָא / tsê'va) BRANCH (מַטֵּה / ma'teyh) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Asher (אָשֵׁר / a'sheyr) Pagi'eyl (פַּגְעִיאֵל / pag'i'eyl) SON (בֶּן / ben) Akhran (עָכְרָן / akh'ran)

and over the army of the branch of the sons of Asher is Pagi'eyl the son of Akhran,

27 and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) ARMY (צְבָא / tsê'va) BRANCH (מַטֵּה / ma'teyh) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Naphtali (נַפְתָּלִי / naph'ta'li) Ahhira (אֲחִירַע / a'hhi'ra) SON (בֶּן / ben) Eynan (עֵינָן / ey'nan)

and over the army of the branch of the sons of Naphtali is Ahhira the son of Eynan.
