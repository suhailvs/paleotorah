Leviticus Chapter 26

1 NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~DO(Verb) (תַעֲשׂוּ / ta'a'su) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) WORTHLESS~s (אֱלִילִם / e'li'lim) and~SCULPTURE (וּפֶסֶל / u'phe'sel) and~MONUMENT (וּמַצֵּבָה / u'ma'tsey'vah) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~make~RISE(Verb) (תָקִימוּ / ta'qi'mu) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) and~STONE (וְאֶבֶן / wê'e'ven) IMAGERY (מַשְׂכִּית / mas'kit) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~GIVE(Verb) (תִתְּנוּ / tit'nu) in~LAND~you(mp) (בְּאַרְצְכֶם / bê'ar'tse'khem) to~>~self~BEND.DOWN(Verb) (לְהִשְׁתַּחֲוֹת / lê'hish'ta'hha'ot) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem)

You will not make for you worthless ones, and sculpture and monument you will not make rise for you, and stone of imagery you will not place in your land to bow yourself down upon her, given that I am YHWH your Elohiym.

2 AT (אֶת / et) CEASING~s~me (שַׁבְּתֹתַי / sha'be'to'tai) you(mp)~will~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (תִּשְׁמֹרוּ / tish'moru) and~SANCTUARY~me (וּמִקְדָּשִׁי / u'miq'da'shi) you(mp)~will~FEAR(Verb) (תִּירָאוּ / ti'ra'u) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

My ceasings you will safeguard, and my sanctuary you will fear, I am YHWH.

3 IF (אִם / im) in~CUSTOM~s~me (בְּחֻקֹּתַי / bê'hhu'qo'tai) you(mp)~will~WALK(Verb) (תֵּלֵכוּ / tey'ley'khu) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) DIRECTIVE~s~me (מִצְו‍ֹתַי / mits'o'tai) you(mp)~will~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (תִּשְׁמְרוּ / tish'me'ru) and~you(mp)~did~DO(Verb) (וַעֲשִׂיתֶם / wa'a'si'tem) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam)

If in my customs you will walk, and my directives you will safeguard, and you will do them,

4 and~I~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַתִּי / wê'na'ta'ti) RAIN.SHOWER~s~you(mp) (גִשְׁמֵיכֶם / gish'mey'khem) in~APPOINTED.TIME~them(m) (בְּעִתָּם / bê'i'tam) and~she~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתְנָה / wê'nat'nah) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) PRODUCT~her (יְבוּלָהּ / yê'vu'lah) and~TREE (וְעֵץ / wê'eyts) the~FIELD (הַשָּׂדֶה / ha'sa'deh) he~will~GIVE(Verb) (יִתֵּן / yi'teyn) PRODUCE~him (פִּרְיוֹ / pir'yo)

then I will give your rain showers in their appointed time, and the land will give her produce, and the tree of the field will give his produce,

5 and~he~did~make~OVERTAKE(Verb) (וְהִשִּׂיג / wê'hi'sig) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) THRESHING (דַּיִשׁ / da'yish) AT (אֶת / et) VINTAGE (בָּצִיר / ba'tsir) and~VINTAGE (וּבָצִיר / u'va'tsir) he~will~make~OVERTAKE(Verb) (יַשִּׂיג / ya'sig) AT (אֶת / et) SEED (זָרַע / za'ra) and~you(mp)~did~EAT(Verb) (וַאֲכַלְתֶּם / wa'a'khal'tem) BREAD~you(mp) (לַחְמְכֶם / lahh'me'khem) to~SATISFACTION (לָשֹׂבַע / la'so'va) and~you(mp)~did~SETTLE(Verb) (וִישַׁבְתֶּם / wi'shav'tem) to~the~SAFELY (לָבֶטַח / la've'tahh) in~LAND~you(mp) (בְּאַרְצְכֶם / bê'ar'tse'khem)

and the threshing will overtake the vintage for you, and the vintage will overtake the seed, and you will eat your bread to satisfaction, and you will settle safely in the land,

6 and~I~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַתִּי / wê'na'ta'ti) COMPLETENESS (שָׁלוֹם / sha'lom) in~the~LAND (בָּאָרֶץ / ba'a'rets) and~you(mp)~did~LIE.DOWN(Verb) (וּשְׁכַבְתֶּם / ush'khav'tem) and~WITHOUT (וְאֵין / wê'eyn) make~TREMBLE(Verb)~ing(ms) (מַחֲרִיד / ma'hha'rid) and~I~did~make~CEASE(Verb) (וְהִשְׁבַּתִּי / wê'hish'ba'ti) LIVING (חַיָּה / hhai'yah) [759] DYSFUNCTIONAL (רָעָה / ra'ah) FROM (מִן / min) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) and~SWORD (וְחֶרֶב / wê'hhe'rev) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~CROSS.OVER(Verb) (תַעֲבֹר / ta'a'vor) in~LAND~you(mp) (בְּאַרְצְכֶם / bê'ar'tse'khem)

and I will give completeness in the land, and you will lay down, and without trembling, and I will make the dysfunctional living ones cease from the land, and the sword will not cross over in your land,

7 and~you(mp)~did~PURSUE(Verb) (וּרְדַפְתֶּם / ur'daph'tem) AT (אֶת / et) ATTACK(Verb)~ing(mp)~you(mp) (אֹיְבֵיכֶם / oy'vey'khem) and~they~did~FALL(Verb) (וְנָפְלוּ / wê'naph'lu) to~FACE~s~you(mp) (לִפְנֵיכֶם / liph'ney'khem) to~SWORD (לֶחָרֶב / le'hha'rev)

and you will pursue your attackers, and they will fall to your faces by the sword,

8 and~they~did~PURSUE(Verb) (וְרָדְפוּ / wê'rad'phu) from~~you(mp) (מִכֶּם / mi'kem) FIVE (חֲמִשָּׁה / hha'mi'shah) HUNDRED (מֵאָה / mey'ah) and~HUNDRED (וּמֵאָה / u'mey'ah) from~~you(mp) (מִכֶּם / mi'kem) MYRIAD (רְבָבָה / rê'va'vah) they(m)~will~PURSUE(Verb) (יִרְדֹּפוּ / yir'do'phu) and~they~did~FALL(Verb) (וְנָפְלוּ / wê'naph'lu) ATTACK(Verb)~ing(mp)~you(mp) (אֹיְבֵיכֶם / oy'vey'khem) to~FACE~s~you(mp) (לִפְנֵיכֶם / liph'ney'khem) to~SWORD (לֶחָרֶב / le'hha'rev)

and five from you will pursue a hundred, and a hundred from you will pursue a myriad, and your attackers will fall to your faces by the sword,

9 and~I~did~TURN(Verb) (וּפָנִיתִי / u'pha'ni'ti) TO~you(mp) (אֲלֵיכֶם / a'ley'khem) and~I~did~make~REPRODUCE(Verb) (וְהִפְרֵיתִי / wê'hiph'rey'ti) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem) and~I~did~make~INCREASE(Verb) (וְהִרְבֵּיתִי / wê'hir'bey'ti) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem) and~I~did~make~RISE(Verb) (וַהֲקִימֹתִי / wa'ha'qi'mo'ti) AT (אֶת / et) COVENANT~me (בְּרִיתִי / bê'ri'ti) AT~you(mp) (אִתְּכֶם / it'khem)

and I will turn to you, and I will make you reproduce, and I will make you increase, and I will make my covenant rise with you,

10 and~you(mp)~did~EAT(Verb) (וַאֲכַלְתֶּם / wa'a'khal'tem) SLEEPING (יָשָׁן / ya'shan) be~SLEEP(Verb)~ing(ms) (נוֹשָׁן / no'shan) and~SLEEPING (וְיָשָׁן / wê'ya'shan) from~FACE~s (מִפְּנֵי / mip'ney) NEW (חָדָשׁ / hha'dash) you(mp)~will~make~GO.OUT(Verb) (תּוֹצִיאוּ / to'tsi'u)

and you will eat the stores that are being stored, and you will make the storage go out from the face of the new ones,

11 and~I~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַתִּי / wê'na'ta'ti) DWELLING~me (מִשְׁכָּנִי / mish'ka'ni) in~MIDST~you(mp) (בְּתוֹכְכֶם / bê'tokh'khem) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) she~will~CAST.AWAY(Verb) (תִגְעַל / tig'al) SOUL~me (נַפְשִׁי / naph'shi) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem)

and I will place my dwelling in your midst, and my soul will not cast you away,

12 and~I~did~self~WALK(Verb) (וְהִתְהַלַּכְתִּי / wê'hit'ha'lakh'ti) in~MIDST~you(mp) (בְּתוֹכְכֶם / bê'tokh'khem) and~I~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיִיתִי / wê'hai'yi'ti) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) to~Elohiym (לֵאלֹהִים / ley'lo'him) and~YOU(MP) (וְאַתֶּם / wê'a'tem) you(mp)~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִּהְיוּ / tih'yu) to~~me (לִי / li) to~PEOPLE (לְעָם / lê'am)

and I will walk myself in your midst, and I will exist for you for Elohiym, and you, you will exist for me for a people.

13 I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I~did~make~GO.OUT(Verb) (הוֹצֵאתִי / ho'tsey'ti) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem) from~LAND (מֵאֶרֶץ / mey'e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) from~>~EXIST(Verb) (מִהְיֹת / mih'yot) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) SERVANT~s (עֲבָדִים / a'va'dim) and~I~will~CRACK(Verb) (וָאֶשְׁבֹּר / wa'esh'bor) POLE~s (מֹטֹת / mo'tot) YOKE~you(mp) (עֻלְּכֶם / ul'khem) and~I~will~make~WALK(Verb) (וָאוֹלֵךְ / wa'o'leykh) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem) VERTICAL (קוֹמְמִיּוּת / qom'mi'yut)

I am YHWH your Elohiym who made you go out from the land of Mits'rayim, from existing for them as servants, and I will crack the poles of your yoke, and I will make you walk vertical,

14 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~HEAR(Verb) (תִשְׁמְעוּ / tish'me'u) to~~me (לִי / li) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(mp)~will~DO(Verb) (תַעֲשׂוּ / ta'a'su) AT (אֵת / eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~DIRECTIVE~s (הַמִּצְוֹת / ha'mits'ot) the~THESE (הָאֵלֶּה / ha'ey'leh)

but if you will not listen to me, and you will not do all these directives,

15 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) in~CUSTOM~s~me (בְּחֻקֹּתַי / bê'hhu'qo'tai) you(mp)~will~REJECT(Verb) (תִּמְאָסוּ / tim'a'su) and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) AT (אֶת / et) DECISION~s~me (מִשְׁפָּטַי / mish'pa'tai) she~will~CAST.AWAY(Verb) (תִּגְעַל / tig'al) SOUL~you(ms) (נַפְשְׁכֶם / naph'she'khem) to~EXCEPT (לְבִלְתִּי / lê'vil'ti) >~DO(Verb) (עֲשׂוֹת / a'sot) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) DIRECTIVE~s~me (מִצְו‍ֹתַי / mits'o'tai) to~>~make~BREAK(Verb)~you(mp) (לְהַפְרְכֶם / lê'haph're'khem) AT (אֶת / et) COVENANT~me (בְּרִיתִי / bê'ri'ti)

and if you will reject my customs, and if your soul will cast away my decisions, to not do all my directives to cause you to break my covenant.

16 MOREOVER (אַף / aph) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) I~will~DO(Verb) (אֶעֱשֶׂה / e'e'seh) THIS (זֹּאת / zot) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) and~I~did~make~REGISTER(Verb) (וְהִפְקַדְתִּי / wê'hiph'qad'ti) UPON~you(mp) (עֲלֵיכֶם / a'ley'khem) DISMAY (בֶּהָלָה / be'ha'lah) AT (אֶת / et) the~CONSUMPTION (הַשַּׁחֶפֶת / ha'sha'hhe'phet) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~FEVER (הַקַּדַּחַת / ha'qa'da'hhat) much~FINISH(Verb)~ing(fp) (מְכַלּוֹת / mê'kha'lot) EYE~s2 (עֵינַיִם / ey'na'yim) and~make~SORROW(Verb)~ing(fp) (וּמְדִיבֹת / um'di'vot) SOUL (נָפֶשׁ / na'phesh) and~you(mp)~did~SOW(Verb) (וּזְרַעְתֶּם / uz'ra'e'tem) to~the~EMPTY (לָרִיק / la'riq) SEED~you(mp) (זַרְעֲכֶם / zar'a'khem) and~they~did~EAT(Verb)~him (וַאֲכָלֻהוּ / wa'a'kha'lu'hu) ATTACK(Verb)~ing(mp)~you(mp) (אֹיְבֵיכֶם / oy'vey'khem)

Moreover, I will do this to you, and I will make register upon you dismay, the consumption and the fever, a finishing of the eyes, and making the soul sorrowful, and you will sow your seed to emptiness, and your attackers will eat him,

17 and~I~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַתִּי / wê'na'ta'ti) FACE~s~me (פָנַי / pha'nai) in~~you(mp) (בָּכֶם / ba'khem) and~you(mp)~did~be~SMITE(Verb) (וְנִגַּפְתֶּם / wê'ni'gaph'tem) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) ATTACK(Verb)~ing(mp)~you(mp) (אֹיְבֵיכֶם / oy'vey'khem) and~they~did~RULE(Verb) (וְרָדוּ / wê'ra'du) in~~you(mp) (בָכֶם / va'khem) HATE(Verb)~ing(mp)~you(mp) (שֹׂנְאֵיכֶם / son'ey'khem) and~you(mp)~did~FLEE(Verb) (וְנַסְתֶּם / wê'nas'tem) and~WITHOUT (וְאֵין / wê'eyn) PURSUE(Verb)~ing(ms) (רֹדֵף / ro'deyph) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem)

and I will place my face in you, and you will be smitten at the face of your attackers, and your haters will rule over you, and you will flee without anyone pursuing you,

18 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) UNTIL (עַד / ad) THESE (אֵלֶּה / ey'leh) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~HEAR(Verb) (תִשְׁמְעוּ / tish'me'u) to~~me (לִי / li) and~I~did~ADD(Verb) (וְיָסַפְתִּי / wê'ya'saph'ti) to~>~much~CORRECT(Verb)~her (לְיַסְּרָה / lê'yas'rah) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem) SEVEN (שֶׁבַע / she'va) UPON (עַל / al) FAILURE~s~you(mp) (חַטֹּאתֵיכֶם / hha'to'tey'khem)

and if unto these you will not listen to me, then I will add seven times upon your failures to correct you,

19 and~I~did~CRACK(Verb) (וְשָׁבַרְתִּי / wê'sha'var'ti) AT (אֶת / et) MAJESTY (גְּאוֹן / gê'on) BOLDNESS~you(mp) (עֻזְּכֶם / uz'khem) and~I~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַתִּי / wê'na'ta'ti) AT (אֶת / et) SKY~s2~you(mp) (שְׁמֵיכֶם / sh'mey'khem) like~IRON (כַּבַּרְזֶל / ka'bar'zel) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) LAND~you(mp) (אַרְצְכֶם / ar'tse'khem) like~the~BRASS (כַּנְּחֻשָׁה / kan'hhu'shah)

and I will crack the majesty of your boldness, and I will make your skies like iron and your land like brass,

20 and~he~did~BE.WHOLE(Verb) (וְתַם / wê'tam) to~the~EMPTY (לָרִיק / la'riq) STRENGTH~you(mp) (כֹּחֲכֶם / ko'hha'khem) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) she~will~GIVE(Verb) (תִתֵּן / ti'teyn) LAND~you(mp) (אַרְצְכֶם / ar'tse'khem) AT (אֶת / et) PRODUCT~her (יְבוּלָהּ / yê'vu'lah) and~TREE (וְעֵץ / wê'eyts) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~GIVE(Verb) (יִתֵּן / yi'teyn) PRODUCE~him (פִּרְיוֹ / pir'yo)

and your strength will be whole in emptiness, and your land will not give her produce, and the tree of the land will not give his produce,

21 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) you(mp)~will~WALK(Verb) (תֵּלְכוּ / teyl'khu) WITH~me (עִמִּי / i'mi) CONTRARY (קֶרִי / qe'ri) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(mp)~will~CONSENT(Verb) (תֹאבוּ / to'vu) to~>~HEAR(Verb) (לִשְׁמֹעַ / lish'mo'a) to~~me (לִי / li) and~I~did~ADD(Verb) (וְיָסַפְתִּי / wê'ya'saph'ti) UPON~you(mp) (עֲלֵיכֶם / a'ley'khem) HITTING (מַכָּה / ma'kah) SEVEN (שֶׁבַע / she'va) like~FAILURE~s~you(mp) (כְּחַטֹּאתֵיכֶם / kê'hha'to'tey'khem)

and if you will walk contrary with me, and you will not consent to listen to me, then I will add a hitting upon you seven times according to your failures,
