Deuteronomy Chapter 29

1 (2) and~he~will~CALL.OUT(Verb) (וַיִּקְרָא / wai'yiq'ra) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) TO (אֶל / el) ALL (כָּל / kol) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) YOU(MP) (אַתֶּם / a'tem) you(mp)~did~SEE(Verb) (רְאִיתֶם / rê'i'tem) AT (אֵת / eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) to~EYE~s2~you(mp) (לְעֵינֵיכֶם / lê'ey'ney'khem) in~LAND (בְּאֶרֶץ / bê'e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) to~Paroh (לְפַרְעֹה / lê'phar'oh) and~to~ALL (וּלְכָל / ul'khol) SERVANT~s~him (עֲבָדָיו / a'va'daw) and~to~ALL (וּלְכָל / ul'khol) LAND~him (אַרְצוֹ / ar'tso)

and Mosheh called out to all Yisra'eyl, and he said to them, you saw all which YHWH did to your eyes in the land of Mits'rayim to Paroh, and to all his servants, and to all his land.

2 (3) the~TRIAL~s (הַמַּסּוֹת / ha'ma'sot) the~GREAT~s (הַגְּדֹלֹת / ha'ge'do'lot) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they~did~SEE(Verb) (רָאוּ / ra'u) EYE~s2~you(ms) (עֵינֶיךָ / ey'ney'kha) the~SIGN~s (הָאֹתֹת / ha'ot'ot) and~the~WONDER~s (וְהַמֹּפְתִים / wê'ha'moph'tim) the~GREAT~s (הַגְּדֹלִים / ha'ge'do'lim) the~THEY(m) (הָהֵם / ha'heym)

The great trials which your eyes saw, the signs and those great wonders,

3 (4) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~did~GIVE(Verb) (נָתַן / na'tan) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) HEART (לֵב / leyv) to~>~KNOW(Verb) (לָדַעַת / la'da'at) and~EYE~s2 (וְעֵינַיִם / wê'ey'na'yim) to~>~SEE(Verb) (לִרְאוֹת / lir'ot) and~EAR~s (וְאָזְנַיִם / wê'az'na'yim) to~>~HEAR(Verb) (לִשְׁמֹעַ / lish'mo'a) UNTIL (עַד / ad) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh)

and YHWH did not give to you a heart to know, and eyes to see, and ears to hear, until this day,

4 (5) and~I~will~make~WALK(Verb) (וָאוֹלֵךְ / wa'o'leykh) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem) FOUR~s (אַרְבָּעִים / ar'ba'im) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) in~the~WILDERNESS (בַּמִּדְבָּר / ba'mid'bar) NOT (לֹא / lo) they~did~WEAR.OUT(Verb) (בָלוּ / va'lu) OUTER.GARMENT~s~you(mp) (שַׂלְמֹתֵיכֶם / sal'mo'tey'khem) from~UPON~you(mp) (מֵעֲלֵיכֶם / mey'a'ley'khem) and~SANDAL~s~you(ms) (וְנַעַלְךָ / wê'na'al'kha) NOT (לֹא / lo) she~did~WEAR.OUT(Verb) (בָלְתָה / val'tah) from~UPON (מֵעַל / mey'al) FOOT~you(ms) (רַגְלֶךָ / rag'le'kha)

and I made you walk forty years in the wilderness, your outer garments did not wear out, and your sandals did not wear out from upon your feet.

5 (6) BREAD (לֶחֶם / le'hhem) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~did~EAT(Verb) (אֲכַלְתֶּם / a'khal'tem) and~WINE (וְיַיִן / wê'ya'yin) and~LIQUOR (וְשֵׁכָר / wê'shey'khar) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~did~GULP(Verb) (שְׁתִיתֶם / shê'ti'tem) to~THAT (לְמַעַן / lê'ma'an) you(mp)~will~KNOW(Verb) (תֵּדְעוּ / teyd'u) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem)

Bread you did not eat, and wine and liquor you did not gulp, so that you will know that I am YHWH your Elohiym,

6 (7) and~you(mp)~will~COME(Verb) (וַתָּבֹאוּ / wa'ta'vo'u) TO (אֶל / el) the~AREA (הַמָּקוֹם / ha'ma'qom) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh) and~he~will~GO.OUT(Verb) (וַיֵּצֵא / wai'yey'tsey) Sihhon (סִיחֹן / si'hhon) KING (מֶלֶךְ / me'lekh) Hheshbon (חֶשְׁבּוֹן / hhesh'bon) and~Og (וְעוֹג / wê'og) KING (מֶלֶךְ / me'lekh) the~Bashan (הַבָּשָׁן / ha'ba'shan) to~>~MEET(Verb)~us (לִקְרָאתֵנוּ / liq'ra'tey'nu) to~the~BATTLE (לַמִּלְחָמָה / la'mil'hha'mah) and~we~will~make~HIT(Verb) (וַנַּכֵּם / wa'na'keym)

and you came to this area, and Sihhon the king of Hheshbon, and Og the king of the Bashan, met us for battle, and we attacked,

7 (8) and~we~will~TAKE(Verb) (וַנִּקַּח / wa'ni'qahh) AT (אֶת / et) LAND~them(m) (אַרְצָם / ar'tsam) and~we~will~GIVE(Verb) (וַנִּתְּנָהּ / wa'nit'nah) to~INHERITANCE (לְנַחֲלָה / lê'na'hha'lah) to~the~Re'uven~of (לָראוּבֵנִי / la'ru'vey'ni) and~to~the~Gad~of (וְלַגָּדִי / wê'la'ga'di) and~to~the~HALF (וְלַחֲצִי / wê'la'hha'tsi) STAFF (שֵׁבֶט / shey'vet) [1052] the~Menasheh~of (הַמְנַשִּׁי / ham'na'shi)

and we took their land, and gave it for an inheritance to the ones of Re'uven, and to the ones of Gad, and to the half staff of the ones of Menasheh,

8 (9) and~you(mp)~did~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (וּשְׁמַרְתֶּם / ush'mar'tem) AT (אֶת / et) WORD~s (דִּבְרֵי / div'rey) the~COVENANT (הַבְּרִית / ha'be'rit) the~THIS (הַזֹּאת / ha'zot) and~you(mp)~did~DO(Verb) (וַעֲשִׂיתֶם / wa'a'si'tem) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) to~THAT (לְמַעַן / lê'ma'an) you(mp)~will~make~CALCULATE(Verb) (תַּשְׂכִּילוּ / tas'ki'lu) AT (אֵת / eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb)~must (תַּעֲשׂוּן / ta'a'sun)

and you will safeguard the words of this covenant, and you will do them, so that you will calculate all which you must do.

9 (10) YOU(MP) (אַתֶּם / a'tem) be~STAND.UP(Verb)~ing(mp) (נִצָּבִים / ni'tsa'vim) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) ALL~you(mp) (כֻּלְּכֶם / kul'khem) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem) HEAD~s~you(mp) (רָאשֵׁיכֶם / ra'shey'khem) STAFF~s~you(mp) (שִׁבְטֵיכֶם / shiv'tey'khem) BEARD~s~you(mp) (זִקְנֵיכֶם / ziq'ney'khem) and~DOMINATE(Verb)~s~you(mp) (וְשֹׁטְרֵיכֶם / wê'shot'rey'khem) ALL (כֹּל / kol) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl)

You are standing up today, all of you, to the face of YHWH your Elohiym, your heads, your staffs, your bearded ones, and your dominators, all the men of Yisra'eyl.

10 (11) BABIES~you(mp) (טַפְּכֶם / tap'khem) WOMAN~s~you(mp) (נְשֵׁיכֶם / nê'shey'khem) and~IMMIGRANT~you(ms) (וְגֵרְךָ / wê'ger'kha) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~INSIDE (בְּקֶרֶב / bê'qe'rev) CAMP~s~you(ms) (מַחֲנֶיךָ / ma'hha'ney'kha) from~CARVE(Verb)~ing(ms) (מֵחֹטֵב / mey'hho'teyv) TREE~s~you(ms) (עֵצֶיךָ / ey'tsey'kha) UNTIL (עַד / ad) DRAW.WATER(Verb)~ing(ms) (שֹׁאֵב / sho'eyv) WATER~s2~you(ms) (מֵימֶיךָ / mey'mey'kha)

Your babies, your women and your immigrant which are in among your camp, from the carver of your trees, until the drawer of your waters.

11 (12) to~>~CROSS.OVER(Verb)~you(ms) (לְעָבְרְךָ / lê'av're'kha) in~COVENANT (בִּבְרִית / biv'rit) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) and~in~OATH~him (וּבְאָלָתוֹ / uv'a'la'to) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) CUT(Verb)~ing(ms) (כֹּרֵת / ko'reyt) WITH~you(ms) (עִמְּךָ / im'kha) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom)

For your crossing over in the covenant of YHWH your Elohiym, and with his oath, which YHWH your Elohiym is cutting with you today.

12 (13) to~THAT (לְמַעַן / lê'ma'an) >~make~RISE(Verb) (הָקִים / ha'qim) AT~you(ms) (אֹתְךָ / ot'kha) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) to~PEOPLE (לְעָם / lê'am) and~HE (וְהוּא / wê'hu) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) to~~you(ms) (לְּךָ / lê'kha) to~Elohiym (לֵאלֹהִים / ley'lo'him) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דִּבֶּר / di'ber) to~~you(fs) (לָךְ / lakh) and~like~WHICH (וְכַאֲשֶׁר / wê'kha'a'sher) he~did~be~SWEAR(Verb) (נִשְׁבַּע / nish'ba) to~FATHER~s~you(ms) (לַאֲבֹתֶיךָ / la'a'vo'tey'kha) to~Avraham (לְאַבְרָהָם / lê'av'ra'ham) to~Yits'hhaq (לְיִצְחָק / lê'yits'hhaq) and~to~Ya'aqov (וּלְיַעֲקֹב / ul'ya'a'qov)

So that you will rise today for him for a people, and he, he will exist for you for an Elohiym, just as he spoke to you, and just as he swore to your fathers, to Avraham, to Yits'hhaq, and to Ya'aqov,

13 (14) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) AT~you(mp) (אִתְּכֶם / it'khem) to~STRAND~you(mp) (לְבַדְּכֶם / lê'vad'khem) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) CUT(Verb)~ing(ms) (כֹּרֵת / ko'reyt) AT (אֶת / et) the~COVENANT (הַבְּרִית / ha'be'rit) the~THIS (הַזֹּאת / ha'zot) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~OATH (הָאָלָה / ha'a'lah) the~THIS (הַזֹּאת / ha'zot)

and not with you alone am I cutting this covenant and this oath,

14 (15) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) AT (אֶת / et) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) THERE.IS~him (יֶשְׁנוֹ / yesh'no) HERE (פֹּה / poh) WITH~us (עִמָּנוּ / i'ma'nu) STAND(Verb)~ing(ms) (עֹמֵד / o'meyd) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~us (אֱלֹהֵינוּ / e'lo'hey'nu) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) WITHOUT~him (אֵינֶנּוּ / ey'ne'nu) HERE (פֹּה / poh) WITH~us (עִמָּנוּ / i'ma'nu) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom)

but with who is here with us, standing today to the face of YHWH our Elohiym, and with who is not here with us today.

15 (16) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) YOU(MP) (אַתֶּם / a'tem) you(mp)~did~KNOW(Verb) (יְדַעְתֶּם / yê'da'tem) AT (אֵת / eyt) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) we~did~SETTLE(Verb) (יָשַׁבְנוּ / ya'shav'nu) in~LAND (בְּאֶרֶץ / bê'e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרָיִם / mits'ra'yim) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) >~CROSS.OVER(Verb)~us (עָבַרְנוּ / a'var'nu) in~INSIDE (בְּקֶרֶב / bê'qe'rev) the~NATION~s (הַגּוֹיִם / ha'go'yim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(mp)~did~CROSS.OVER(Verb) (עֲבַרְתֶּם / a'var'tem)

Given that you, you knew that we settled in the land of Mits'rayim, and how we crossed over in among the nations which you crossed over,

16 (17) and~you(mp)~will~SEE(Verb) (וַתִּרְאוּ / wa'tir'u) AT (אֶת / et) FILTHINESS~s~them(m) (שִׁקּוּצֵיהֶם / shi'qu'tsey'hem) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) IDOL~s~them(m) (גִּלֻּלֵיהֶם / gi'lu'ley'hem) TREE (עֵץ / eyts) and~STONE (וָאֶבֶן / wa'e'ven) SILVER (כֶּסֶף / ke'seph) and~GOLD (וְזָהָב / wê'za'hav) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) WITH~them(m) (עִמָּהֶם / i'ma'hem)

and you saw their filthiness, and their idols of tree and stone, silver and gold, which are with them.

17 (18) OTHERWISE (פֶּן / pen) THERE.IS (יֵשׁ / yesh) in~~you(mp) (בָּכֶם / ba'khem) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) OR (אוֹ / o) WOMAN (אִשָּׁה / i'shah) OR (אוֹ / o) CLAN (מִשְׁפָּחָה / mish'pa'hhah) OR (אוֹ / o) STAFF (שֵׁבֶט / shey'vet) [1053] WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) HEART~him (לְבָבוֹ / lê'va'vo) TURN(Verb)~ing(ms) (פֹנֶה / pho'neh) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) from~WITH (מֵעִם / mey'im) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~us (אֱלֹהֵינוּ / e'lo'hey'nu) to~>~WALK(Verb) (לָלֶכֶת / la'le'khet) to~>~SERVE(Verb) (לַעֲבֹד / la'a'vod) AT (אֶת / et) Elohiym (אֱלֹהֵי / e'lo'hey) the~NATION~s (הַגּוֹיִם / ha'go'yim) the~THEY(m) (הָהֵם / ha'heym) OTHERWISE (פֶּן / pen) THERE.IS (יֵשׁ / yesh) in~~you(mp) (בָּכֶם / ba'khem) ROOT (שֹׁרֶשׁ / sho'resh) REPRODUCE(Verb)~ing(ms) (פֹּרֶה / po'reh) VENOM (רֹאשׁ / rosh) and~HEMLOCK (וְלַעֲנָה / wê'la'a'nah)

Otherwise, there is in you a man, or woman, or clan, or staff, which is turning his heart today from YHWH our Elohiym, to walk, to serve the Elohiym of these nations, otherwise there is in you a root reproducing venom and hemlock,

18 (19) and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) in~>~HEAR(Verb)~him (בְּשָׁמְעוֹ / bê'sham'o) AT (אֶת / et) WORD~s (דִּבְרֵי / div'rey) the~OATH (הָאָלָה / ha'a'lah) the~THIS (הַזֹּאת / ha'zot) and~he~did~self~KNEEL(Verb) (וְהִתְבָּרֵךְ / wê'hit'ba'reykh) in~HEART~him (בִּלְבָבוֹ / bil'va'vo) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) COMPLETENESS (שָׁלוֹם / sha'lom) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) to~~me (לִּי / li) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) in~IMAGINATION (בִּשְׁרִרוּת / bish'ri'rut) HEART~me (לִבִּי / li'bi) I~will~WALK(Verb) (אֵלֵךְ / ey'leykh) to~THAT (לְמַעַן / lê'ma'an) >~CONSUME(Verb) (סְפוֹת / sê'phot) the~WATERED (הָרָוָה / ha'ra'wah) AT (אֶת / et) the~THIRST (הַצְּמֵאָה / hats'mey'ah)

and it will come to pass, in his hearing the words of this oath, and he respected himself in his heart saying, completeness will exist in me, given that in the imagination of my heart, I will walk, so that the watered will consume the thirst.
