Deuteronomy Chapter 28

1 and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) IF (אִם / im) >~HEAR(Verb) (שָׁמוֹעַ / sha'mo'a) you(ms)~will~HEAR(Verb) (תִּשְׁמַע / tish'ma) in~VOICE (בְּקוֹל / bê'qol) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) to~>~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (לִשְׁמֹר / lish'mor) to~>~DO(Verb) (לַעֲשׂוֹת / la'a'sot) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) DIRECTIVE~s~him (מִצְוֹתָיו / mits'o'taw) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) much~DIRECT(Verb)~ing(ms)~you(ms) (מְצַוְּךָ / mê'tsa'we'kha) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) and~he~did~GIVE(Verb)~you(ms) (וּנְתָנְךָ / un'tan'kha) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) UPPER (עֶלְיוֹן / el'yon) UPON (עַל / al) ALL (כָּל / kol) NATION~s (גּוֹיֵי / go'yey) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets)

and it will come to pass, if you will diligently hear the voice of YHWH your Elohiym, to safeguard to do all his directives, which I am directing you today, then YHWH your Elohiym will make you above all the nations of the land,

2 and~they~did~COME(Verb) (וּבָאוּ / u'va'u) UPON~you(ms) (עָלֶיךָ / a'ley'kha) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~PRESENT~s (הַבְּרָכוֹת / ha'be'ra'khot) the~THESE (הָאֵלֶּה / ha'ey'leh) and~they~did~make~OVERTAKE(Verb)~you(ms) (וְהִשִּׂיגֻךָ / wê'hi'si'gu'kha) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(ms)~will~HEAR(Verb) (תִשְׁמַע / tish'ma) in~VOICE (בְּקוֹל / bê'qol) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha)

and these presents will come upon you, given that you will hear the voice of YHWH your Elohiym.

3 KNEEL(Verb)~ed(ms) (בָּרוּךְ / ba'rukh) YOU(MS) (אַתָּה / a'tah) in~the~CITY (בָּעִיר / ba'ir) and~KNEEL(Verb)~ed(ms) (וּבָרוּךְ / u'va'rukh) YOU(MS) (אַתָּה / a'tah) in~the~FIELD (בַּשָּׂדֶה / ba'sa'deh)

Respected are you in the city and respected are you in the field.

4 KNEEL(Verb)~ed(ms) (בָּרוּךְ / ba'rukh) PRODUCE (פְּרִי / pê'ri) WOMB~you(ms) (בִטְנְךָ / vit'ne'kha) and~PRODUCE (וּפְרִי / uph'ri) GROUND~you(ms) (אַדְמָתְךָ / ad'mat'kha) and~PRODUCE (וּפְרִי / uph'ri) BEAST~you(ms) (בְהֶמְתֶּךָ / vê'hem'te'kha) BIRTH (שְׁגַר / shê'gar) BOVINE~s~you(ms) (אֲלָפֶיךָ / a'la'phey'kha) and~YOUNG.SHEEP~s (וְעַשְׁתְּרוֹת / wê'ash'te'rot) FLOCKS~you(ms) (צֹאנֶךָ / tso'ne'kha)

Respected are the produce of your womb, and the produce of your ground, and the produce of your beast, the birth of your bovine and the young sheep of your flocks.

5 KNEEL(Verb)~ed(ms) (בָּרוּךְ / ba'rukh) REED-BASKET~you(ms) (טַנְאֲךָ / tan'a'kha) and~KNEADING.BOWL~you(ms) (וּמִשְׁאַרְתֶּךָ / u'mish'ar'te'kha)

Respected are your reed basket and your kneading bowl.

6 KNEEL(Verb)~ed(ms) (בָּרוּךְ / ba'rukh) YOU(MS) (אַתָּה / a'tah) in~>~COME(Verb)~you(ms) (בְּבֹאֶךָ / bê'vo'e'kha) and~KNEEL(Verb)~ed(ms) (וּבָרוּךְ / u'va'rukh) YOU(MS) (אַתָּה / a'tah) in~>~GO.OUT(Verb)~you(ms) (בְּצֵאתֶךָ / bê'tsey'te'kha)

Respected are you in your coming, and respected are you in your going out.

7 he~will~GIVE(Verb) (יִתֵּן / yi'teyn) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT (אֶת / et) ATTACK(Verb)~ing(mp)~s~you(ms) (אֹיְבֶיךָ / oi'vey'kha) the~RISE(Verb)~ing(mp) (הַקָּמִים / ha'qa'mim) UPON~you(ms) (עָלֶיךָ / a'ley'kha) be~SMITE(Verb)~ing(mp) (נִגָּפִים / ni'ga'phim) to~FACE~s~you(ms) (לְפָנֶיךָ / lê'pha'ney'kha) in~ROAD (בְּדֶרֶךְ / bê'de'rekh) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) they(m)~will~GO.OUT(Verb) (יֵצְאוּ / yeyts'u) TO~you(ms) (אֵלֶיךָ / ey'ley'kha) and~in~SEVEN (וּבְשִׁבְעָה / uv'shiv'ah) ROAD~s (דְרָכִים / dê'ra'khim) they(m)~will~FLEE(Verb) (יָנוּסוּ / ya'nu'su) to~FACE~s~you(ms) (לְפָנֶיךָ / lê'pha'ney'kha)

YHWH will make your attackers, the ones rising upon you, be smitten to your face, in one road, they will go out to you on one road and they will flee your face on seven roads.

8 he~will~much~DIRECT(Verb) (יְצַו / yê'tsaw) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT~you(ms) (אִתְּךָ / it'kha) AT (אֶת / et) the~PRESENT (הַבְּרָכָה / ha'be'ra'kha) in~BARN~s~you(ms) (בַּאֲסָמֶיךָ / ba'a'sa'mey'kha) and~in~ALL (וּבְכֹל / uv'khol) SENDING (מִשְׁלַח / mi'she'lahh) HAND~you(ms) (יָדֶךָ / ya'de'kha) and~he~did~much~KNEEL(Verb)~you(ms) (וּבֵרַכְךָ / u'vey'rakh'kha) in~the~LAND (בָּאָרֶץ / ba'a'rets) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) GIVE(Verb)~ing(ms) (נֹתֵן / no'teyn) to~~you(fs) (לָךְ / lakh)

YHWH will direct with you the present in your barns and in all your hand sends, and he will respect you in the land, which YHWH your Elohiym is giving to you.

9 he~will~make~RISE(Verb)~you(ms) (יְקִימְךָ / yê'qim'kha) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) to~PEOPLE (לְעַם / lê'am) UNIQUE (קָדוֹשׁ / qa'dosh) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~be~SWEAR(Verb) (נִשְׁבַּע / nish'ba) to~~you(fs) (לָךְ / lakh) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(ms)~will~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (תִשְׁמֹר / tish'mor) AT (אֶת / et) DIRECTIVE~s (מִצְוֹת / mits'ot) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) and~you(ms)~did~WALK(Verb) (וְהָלַכְתָּ / wê'ha'lakh'ta) in~ROAD~s~him (בִּדְרָכָיו / bid'ra'khaw)

YHWH will make you rise for him for a unique people, just as he swore to you, given that you will safeguard the directives of YHWH your Elohiym, and you will walk in his roads,

10 and~they~did~SEE(Verb) (וְרָאוּ / wê'ra'u) ALL (כָּל / kol) PEOPLE~s (עַמֵּי / a'mey) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) TITLE (שֵׁם / sheym) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) he~did~be~MEET(Verb) (נִקְרָא / niq'ra) UPON~you(ms) (עָלֶיךָ / a'ley'kha) and~they~did~FEAR(Verb) (וְיָרְאוּ / wê'yar'u) FROM~you(ms) (מִמֶּךָּ / mi'me'ka)

and the people of the land will see that the title of YHWH will come to meet with you, and they will fear you,

11 and~he~did~make~LEAVE.BEHIND(Verb)~you(ms) (וְהוֹתִרְךָ / wê'ho'tir'kha) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) to~FUNCTIONAL (לְטוֹבָה / lê'to'vah) in~PRODUCE (בִּפְרִי / biph'ri) WOMB~you(ms) (בִטְנְךָ / vit'ne'kha) and~in~PRODUCE (וּבִפְרִי / u'viph'ri) BEAST~you(ms) (בְהַמְתְּךָ / vê'ham'te'kha) and~in~PRODUCE (וּבִפְרִי / u'viph'ri) GROUND~you(ms) (אַדְמָתֶךָ / ad'ma'te'kha) UPON (עַל / al) the~GROUND (הָאֲדָמָה / ha'a'da'mah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~be~SWEAR(Verb) (נִשְׁבַּע / nish'ba) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) to~FATHER~s~you(ms) (לַאֲבֹתֶיךָ / la'a'vo'tey'kha) to~>~GIVE(Verb) (לָתֶת / la'tet) to~~you(fs) (לָךְ / lakh)

and YHWH will leave behind you the functional produce of your womb, and the produce of your beast, and the produce of your ground, upon the ground which YHWH swore to your fathers to give to you.

YHWH will open for you his functional supply house, the skies will give the precipitation of your land in his appointed time, and to respect all the work of your hand, and you will make many nations join, but you will not join,

13 and~he~did~GIVE(Verb)~you(ms) (וּנְתָנְךָ / un'tan'kha) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) to~HEAD (לְרֹאשׁ / lê'rosh) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) to~TAIL (לְזָנָב / lê'za'nav) and~you(ms)~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיִיתָ / wê'ha'yi'ta) ONLY (רַק / raq) to~UPWARD~unto (לְמַעְלָה / lê'ma'lah) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִהְיֶה / tih'yeh) to~the~BENEATH (לְמָטָּה / lê'ma'tah) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(ms)~will~HEAR(Verb) (תִשְׁמַע / tish'ma) TO (אֶל / el) DIRECTIVE~s (מִצְוֹת / mits'ot) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) much~DIRECT(Verb)~ing(ms)~you(ms) (מְצַוְּךָ / mê'tsa'we'kha) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) to~>~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (לִשְׁמֹר / lish'mor) and~to~>~DO(Verb) (וְלַעֲשׂוֹת / wê'la'a'sot)

and YHWH will give to the head and not to the tail, and you will only exist on top, and you will not exist at the bottom, given that you heard the directives of YHWH your Elohiym, which I am directing you today to safeguard and to do,

14 and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~TURN.ASIDE(Verb) (תָסוּר / ta'sur) from~ALL (מִכָּל / mi'kol) the~WORD~s (הַדְּבָרִים / ha'de'va'rim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) much~DIRECT(Verb)~ing(ms) (מְצַוֶּה / mê'tsa'weh) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) RIGHT.HAND (יָמִין / ya'min) and~LEFT.HAND (וּשְׂמֹאול / us'mo'ul) to~>~WALK(Verb) (לָלֶכֶת / la'le'khet) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) OTHER~s (אֲחֵרִים / a'hhey'rim) to~>~SERVE(Verb)~them(m) (לְעָבְדָם / lê'av'dam)

and you will not turn aside from any of the words which I am directing you today, the right hand and the left hand, to walk after other Elohiym to serve them,

15 and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) IF (אִם / im) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~HEAR(Verb) (תִשְׁמַע / tish'ma) in~VOICE (בְּקוֹל / bê'qol) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) to~>~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (לִשְׁמֹר / lish'mor) to~>~DO(Verb) (לַעֲשׂוֹת / la'a'sot) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) DIRECTIVE~s~him (מִצְוֹתָיו / mits'o'taw) and~CUSTOM~s~him (וְחֻקֹּתָיו / wê'hhu'qo'taw) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) much~DIRECT(Verb)~ing(ms)~you(ms) (מְצַוְּךָ / mê'tsa'we'kha) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) and~they~did~COME(Verb) (וּבָאוּ / u'va'u) UPON~you(ms) (עָלֶיךָ / a'ley'kha) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~ANNOYANCE~s (הַקְּלָלוֹת / haq'la'lot) the~THESE (הָאֵלֶּה / ha'ey'leh) and~they~did~make~OVERTAKE(Verb)~you(ms) (וְהִשִּׂיגוּךָ / wê'hi'si'gu'kha)

and it will come to pass, if you will not hear the voice of YHWH your Elohiym, to safeguard to do all his directives and his customs, which I am directing you today, then all these annoyances will come upon you, and they will overtake you.
