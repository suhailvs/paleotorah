Deuteronomy Chapter 26

1 and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(ms)~will~COME(Verb) (תָבוֹא / ta'vo) TO (אֶל / el) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) GIVE(Verb)~ing(ms) (נֹתֵן / no'teyn) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) INHERITANCE (נַחֲלָה / na'hha'lah) and~you(ms)~did~POSSESS(Verb)~her (וִירִשְׁתָּהּ / wi'rish'tah) and~you(ms)~did~SETTLE(Verb) (וְיָשַׁבְתָּ / wê'ya'shav'ta) in~~her (בָּהּ / bah)

and it will come to pass, when you come to the land, which YHWH your Elohiym is giving to you as an inheritance, and you will possess her, and you will settle in her,

2 and~you(ms)~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקַחְתָּ / wê'la'qahh'ta) from~ORIGIN (מֵרֵאשִׁית / mey'rey'shit) ALL (כָּל / kol) PRODUCE (פְּרִי / pê'ri) the~GROUND (הָאֲדָמָה / ha'a'da'mah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~will~make~COME(Verb) (תָּבִיא / ta'vi) from~LAND~you(ms) (מֵאַרְצְךָ / mey'ar'tse'kha) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) GIVE(Verb)~ing(ms) (נֹתֵן / no'teyn) to~~you(fs) (לָךְ / lakh) and~you(ms)~did~PLACE(Verb) (וְשַׂמְתָּ / wê'sam'ta) in~the~REED-BASKET (בַטֶּנֶא / va'te'ne) and~you(ms)~did~WALK(Verb) (וְהָלַכְתָּ / wê'ha'lakh'ta) TO (אֶל / el) the~AREA (הַמָּקוֹם / ha'ma'qom) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~CHOOSE(Verb) (יִבְחַר / yiv'hhar) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) to~>~DWELL(Verb) (לְשַׁכֵּן / lê'sha'keyn) TITLE~him (שְׁמוֹ / shê'mo) THERE (שָׁם / sham)

and you will take from the origin of all the produce of the ground, which you will bring from your land, which YHWH your Elohiym is giving to you, and you will place in the reed basket, and you will walk to the area which YHWH your Elohiym will choose for his title to dwell there,

3 and~you(ms)~did~COME(Verb) (וּבָאתָ / u'va'ta) TO (אֶל / el) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) in~the~DAY~s (בַּיָּמִים / bai'ya'mim) the~THEY(m) (הָהֵם / ha'heym) and~you(ms)~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמַרְתָּ / wê'a'mar'ta) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) I~did~make~BE.FACE.TO.FACE(Verb) (הִגַּדְתִּי / hi'gad'ti) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I~did~COME(Verb) (בָאתִי / va'ti) TO (אֶל / el) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~be~SWEAR(Verb) (נִשְׁבַּע / nish'ba) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) to~FATHER~s~us (לַאֲבֹתֵינוּ / la'a'vo'tey'nu) to~>~GIVE(Verb) (לָתֶת / la'tet) to~~us (לָנוּ / la'nu)

and you will come to the administrator, which will exist in those days, and you will say to him, I will tell today to YHWH your Elohiym, given that I came to the land, which YHWH swore to our fathers to give to us,

4 and~he~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקַח / wê'la'qahh) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) the~REED-BASKET (הַטֶּנֶא / ha'te'neh) from~HAND~you(ms) (מִיָּדֶךָ / mi'ya'de'kha) and~he~did~make~REST(Verb)~him (וְהִנִּיחוֹ / wê'hi'ni'hho) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) ALTAR (מִזְבַּח / miz'bahh) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha)

and the administrator will take the reed basket from your hand, and he will make him rest to the face of the altar of YHWH your Elohiym,

5 and~you(ms)~did~ANSWER(Verb) (וְעָנִיתָ / wê'a'ni'ta) and~you(ms)~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמַרְתָּ / wê'a'mar'ta) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) Aram~of (אֲרַמִּי / a'ra'mi) PERISH(Verb)~ing(ms) (אֹבֵד / o'veyd) FATHER~me (אָבִי / a'vi) and~he~will~GO.DOWN(Verb) (וַיֵּרֶד / wai'yey'red) Mits'rayim~unto (מִצְרַיְמָה / mits'rai'mah) and~he~will~IMMIGRATE(Verb) (וַיָּגָר / wai'ya'gar) THERE (שָׁם / sham) in~MORTAL.MAN~s (בִּמְתֵי / bim'tey) SMALL.AMOUNT (מְעָט / mê'at) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) THERE (שָׁם / sham) to~NATION (לְגוֹי / lê'goy) GREAT (גָּדוֹל / ga'dol) NUMEROUS (עָצוּם / a'tsum) and~ABUNDANT (וָרָב / wa'rav)

and you will answer, and you will say to the face of YHWH your Elohiym, a perishing one of Aram is my father, and he will go down unto Mits'rayim, and he will immigrate there with a few mortal men, and he will exist there for a great numerous and abundant nation,

6 and~they(m)~will~make~BE.DYSFUNCTIONAL(Verb) (וַיָּרֵעוּ / wai'ya'rey'u) AT~us (אֹתָנוּ / o'ta'nu) the~Mits'rayim~s (הַמִּצְרִים / ha'mits'rim) and~they(m)~will~much~AFFLICT(Verb)~us (וַיְעַנּוּנוּ / wa'ye'a'nu'nu) and~they(m)~will~GIVE(Verb) (וַיִּתְּנוּ / wai'yit'nu) UPON~us (עָלֵינוּ / a'ley'nu) SERVICE (עֲבֹדָה / a'vo'dah) HARD (קָשָׁה / qa'shah)

and the ones of Mits'rayim will make us dysfunctional, and they will afflict us, and they will place upon us a hard service,

7 and~we~will~CRY.OUT(Verb) (וַנִּצְעַק / wa'nits'aq) TO (אֶל / el) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym (אֱלֹהֵי / e'lo'hey) FATHER~s~us (אֲבֹתֵינוּ / a'vo'tey'nu) and~he~will~HEAR(Verb) (וַיִּשְׁמַע / wai'yish'ma) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT (אֶת / et) VOICE~us (קֹלֵנוּ / qo'ley'nu) and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) AT (אֶת / et) AFFLICTION~us (עָנְיֵנוּ / an'yey'nu) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) LABOR~us (עֲמָלֵנוּ / a'ma'ley'nu) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) SQUEEZING~us (לַחֲצֵנוּ / la'hha'tsey'nu)

and we cried out to YHWH the Elohiym of our fathers, and YHWH heard our voice, and he saw our affliction and our labor and our squeezing,

8 and~he~will~make~GO.OUT(Verb)~us (וַיּוֹצִאֵנוּ / wai'yo'tsi'ey'nu) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) from~Mits'rayim (מִמִּצְרַיִם / mi'mits'ra'yim) in~HAND (בְּיָד / bê'yad) FORCEFUL (חֲזָקָה / hha'za'qah) and~in~ARM (וּבִזְרֹעַ / u'viz'ro'a) EXTEND(Verb)~ed(fs) (נְטוּיָה / nê'tu'yah) and~in~FEARING (וּבְמֹרָא / uv'mo'ra) GREAT (גָּדֹל / ga'dol) and~in~SIGN~s (וּבְאֹתוֹת / uv'o'tot) and~in~WONDER~s (וּבְמֹפְתִים / uv'moph'tim)

and YHWH brought us out from Mits'rayim with a forceful hand, and with an extended arm, and with a great fearing, and with signs, and with wonders,

9 and~he~will~make~COME(Verb)~us (וַיְבִאֵנוּ / wa'ye'vi'ey'nu) TO (אֶל / el) the~AREA (הַמָּקוֹם / ha'ma'qom) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh) and~he~will~GIVE(Verb) (וַיִּתֶּן / wai'yi'ten) to~~us (לָנוּ / la'nu) AT (אֶת / et) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) the~THIS (הַזֹּאת / ha'zot) LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) ISSUE(Verb)~ing(fs) (זָבַת / za'vat) FAT (חָלָב / hha'lav) and~HONEY (וּדְבָשׁ / ud'vash)

and he brought us to this area, and he gave to us this land issuing fat and honey,

10 and~NOW (וְעַתָּה / wê'a'tah) LOOK (הִנֵּה / hin'neyh) I~did~make~COME(Verb) (הֵבֵאתִי / hey'vey'ti) AT (אֶת / et) ORIGIN (רֵאשִׁית / rey'shit) PRODUCE (פְּרִי / pê'ri) the~GROUND (הָאֲדָמָה / ha'a'da'mah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~did~GIVE(Verb) (נָתַתָּה / na'ta'tah) to~~me (לִּי / li) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~you(ms)~did~make~REST(Verb)~him (וְהִנַּחְתּוֹ / wê'hi'nahh'to) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) and~you(ms)~did~self~BEND.DOWN(Verb) (וְהִשְׁתַּחֲוִיתָ / wê'hish'ta'hha'wi'ta) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha)

and now look, I brought the origin of the produce of the ground, which you YHWH gave to me, and you will make him rest to the face of YHWH your Elohiym, and you will bend yourself down to the face of YHWH your Elohiym,

11 and~you(ms)~did~REJOICE(Verb) (וְשָׂמַחְתָּ / wê'sa'mahh'ta) in~ALL (בְכָל / vê'khol) the~FUNCTIONAL (הַטּוֹב / ha'tov) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~GIVE(Verb) (נָתַן / na'tan) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) and~to~HOUSE~you(ms) (וּלְבֵיתֶךָ / ul'vey'te'kha) YOU(MS) (אַתָּה / a'tah) and~the~Lewi (וְהַלֵּוִי / wê'ha'ley'wi) and~the~IMMIGRANT (וְהַגֵּר / wê'ha'geyr) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~INSIDE~you(ms) (בְּקִרְבֶּךָ / bê'qir'be'kha)

and you will rejoice in all the functional things which YHWH your Elohiym gave to you, and to your house, you and the one of Lewi, and the immigrant which is in among you.

12 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(ms)~will~much~FINISH(Verb) (תְכַלֶּה / tê'kha'leh) to~>~make~GIVE.A.TENTH(Verb) (לַעְשֵׂר / la'seyr) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) TENTH.PART (מַעְשַׂר / ma'sar) PRODUCTION~you(ms) (תְּבוּאָתְךָ / tê'vu'at'kha) in~the~YEAR (בַּשָּׁנָה / ba'sha'nah) the~THIRD (הַשְּׁלִישִׁת / ha'she'li'shit) YEAR (שְׁנַת / shê'nat) the~TENTH.PART (הַמַּעֲשֵׂר / ha'ma'a'seyr) and~you(ms)~did~GIVE(Verb)~& (וְנָתַתָּה / wê'na'ta'tah) to~Lewi (לַלֵּוִי / la'ley'wi) to~the~IMMIGRANT (לַגֵּר / la'geyr) to~the~ORPHAN (לַיָּתוֹם / lai'ya'tom) and~to~the~WIDOW (וְלָאַלְמָנָה / wê'la'al'ma'nah) and~they~did~EAT(Verb) (וְאָכְלוּ / wê'akh'lu) in~GATE~s~you(ms) (בִשְׁעָרֶיךָ / vish'a'rey'kha) and~they~did~BE.SATISFIED(Verb) (וְשָׂבֵעוּ / wê'sa'vey'u)

When you will finish giving a tenth of all the tenth part of your production in the third year, the year of the tenth part, and you will give to Lewi, to the immigrant, to the orphan, and to the widow, and they will eat in your gates, and they will be satisfied,

13 and~you(ms)~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמַרְתָּ / wê'a'mar'ta) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) I~did~much~BURN(Verb) (בִּעַרְתִּי / bi'ar'ti) the~SPECIAL (הַקֹּדֶשׁ / ha'qo'desh) FROM (מִן / min) the~HOUSE (הַבַּיִת / ha'ba'yit) and~ALSO (וְגַם / wê'gam) I~did~GIVE(Verb)~him (נְתַתִּיו / nê'ta'tiw) to~Lewi (לַלֵּוִי / la'ley'wi) and~to~IMMIGRANT (וְלַגֵּר / wê'la'geyr) to~the~ORPHAN (לַיָּתוֹם / lai'ya'tom) and~to~the~WIDOW (וְלָאַלְמָנָה / wê'la'al'ma'nah) like~ALL (כְּכָל / kê'khol) DIRECTIVE~s~you(ms) (מִצְוָתְךָ / mits'wat'kha) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~did~much~DIRECT(Verb)~me (צִוִּיתָנִי / tsi'wi'ta'ni) NOT (לֹא / lo) I~did~CROSS.OVER(Verb) (עָבַרְתִּי / a'var'ti) from~DIRECTIVE~s~you(ms) (מִמִּצְוֹתֶיךָ / mi'mits'wo'tey'kha) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) I~did~FORGET(Verb) (שָׁכָחְתִּי / sha'khahh'ti)

and you will say to the face of YHWH your Elohiym, I burned the special thing from the house, and also I gave him to Lewi and to the immigrant, to the orphan and to the widow, like all your directives which you directed me, I did not cross over from your directives, and I did not forget.

14 NOT (לֹא / lo) I~did~EAT(Verb) (אָכַלְתִּי / a'khal'ti) in~BARRENNESS~me (בְאֹנִי / vê'o'ni) FROM~him (מִמֶּנּוּ / mi'me'nu) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) I~did~much~BURN(Verb) (בִעַרְתִּי / vi'ar'ti) FROM~him (מִמֶּנּוּ / mi'me'nu) in~DIRTY (בְּטָמֵא / bê'ta'mey) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) I~did~GIVE(Verb) (נָתַתִּי / na'ta'ti) FROM~him (מִמֶּנּוּ / mi'me'nu) to~DIE(Verb)~ing(ms) (לְמֵת / lê'meyt) I~did~HEAR(Verb) (שָׁמַעְתִּי / sha'ma'ti) in~VOICE (בְּקוֹל / bê'qol) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~me (אֱלֹהָי / e'lo'hai) I~did~DO(Verb) (עָשִׂיתִי / a'si'ti) like~ALL (כְּכֹל / kê'khol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~did~much~DIRECT(Verb)~me (צִוִּיתָנִי / tsi'wi'ta'ni)

I did not eat in my barrenness from him, and I did not burn from him in dirtiness, and I did not give from him to the dying, I heard the voice of YHWH my Elohiym, I did as all which you directed me.

15 !(ms)~make~LOOK.DOWN(Verb)~& (הַשְׁקִיפָה / hash'qi'phah) from~HABITATION (מִמְּעוֹן / mi'me'on) SPECIAL~you(ms) (קָדְשְׁךָ / qad'she'kha) FROM (מִן / min) the~SKY~s2 (הַשָּׁמַיִם / ha'sha'ma'yim) and~!(ms)~much~KNEEL(Verb) (וּבָרֵךְ / u'va'reykh) AT (אֶת / et) PEOPLE~you(ms) (עַמְּךָ / am'kha) AT (אֶת / et) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) the~GROUND (הָאֲדָמָה / ha'a'da'mah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~did~GIVE(Verb) (נָתַתָּה / na'ta'tah) to~~us (לָנוּ / la'nu) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) you(ms)~did~be~SWEAR(Verb) (נִשְׁבַּעְתָּ / nish'ba'ta) to~FATHER~s~us (לַאֲבֹתֵינוּ / la'a'vo'tey'nu) LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) ISSUE(Verb)~ing(fs) (זָבַת / za'vat) FAT (חָלָב / hha'lav) and~HONEY (וּדְבָשׁ / ud'vash)

Look down from your special habitation from the skies, and respect your people Yisra'eyl, and the ground which you gave to us, just as you swore to our fathers, a land issuing fat and honey.

16 the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) much~DIRECT(Verb)~ing(ms)~you(ms) (מְצַוְּךָ / mê'tsa'we'kha) to~>~DO(Verb) (לַעֲשׂוֹת / la'a'sot) AT (אֶת / et) the~CUSTOM~s (הַחֻקִּים / ha'hhu'qim) the~THESE (הָאֵלֶּה / ha'ey'leh) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~DECISION~s (הַמִּשְׁפָּטִים / ha'mish'pa'tim) and~you(ms)~did~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (וְשָׁמַרְתָּ / wê'sha'mar'ta) and~you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשִׂיתָ / wê'a'si'ta) AT~them(m) (אוֹתָם / o'tam) in~ALL (בְּכָל / bê'khol) HEART~you(ms) (לְבָבְךָ / lê'vav'kha) and~in~ALL (וּבְכָל / uv'khol) SOUL~you(ms) (נַפְשֶׁךָ / naph'she'kha)

This day YHWH your Elohiym is directing you to do these customs and decisions, and you will safeguard and you will do them with all your heart and with all your soul.
