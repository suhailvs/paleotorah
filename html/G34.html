Genesis Chapter 34

1 and~she~will~GO.OUT(Verb) (וַתֵּצֵא / wa'tey'tsey) Dinah (דִינָה / di'nah) DAUGHTER (בַּת / bat) Le'ah (לֵאָה / ley'ah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) she~did~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (יָלְדָה / yal'dah) to~Ya'aqov (לְיַעֲקֹב / lê'ya'a'qov) to~>~SEE(Verb) (לִרְאוֹת / lir'ot) in~DAUGHTER~s (בִּבְנוֹת / biv'not) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets)

and Dinah, the daughter of Le'ah which she brought forth to Ya'aqov, went out to see in the daughters of the land,

2 and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) Shekhem (שְׁכֶם / shê'khem) SON (בֶּן / ben) DONKEY (חֲמוֹר / hha'mor) the~Hhiw~of (הַחִוִּי / ha'hhi'wi) CAPTAIN (נְשִׂיא / nê'si) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) and~he~will~TAKE(Verb) (וַיִּקַּח / wai'yi'qahh) AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) and~he~will~LIE.DOWN(Verb) (וַיִּשְׁכַּב / wai'yish'kav) AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) and~he~will~much~AFFLICT(Verb)~her (וַיְעַנֶּהָ / wa'ye'a'neh)

and Shekhem, the son of Hhamor the one of Hhiw, the captain of the land, saw her and he took her and he laid her down and he afflicted her,

3 and~she~will~ADHERE(Verb) (וַתִּדְבַּק / wa'tid'baq) SOUL~him (נַפְשׁוֹ / naph'sho) in~Dinah (בְּדִינָה / bê'di'nah) DAUGHTER (בַּת / bat) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) and~he~will~LOVE(Verb) (וַיֶּאֱהַב / wai'e'e'hav) AT (אֶת / et) the~YOUNG.WOMAN (הַנַּעֲרָ / ha'na'a'ra) and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) UPON (עַל / al) HEART (לֵב / leyv) the~YOUNG.WOMAN (הַנַּעֲרָ / ha'na'a'ra)

and his soul adhered with Dinah, the daughter of Ya'aqov, and he loved the young woman and spoke upon the heart of the young woman,

4 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Shekhem (שְׁכֶם / shê'khem) TO (אֶל / el) DONKEY (חֲמוֹר / hha'mor) FATHER~him (אָבִיו / a'viw) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) !(ms)~TAKE(Verb) (קַח / qahh) to~~me (לִי / li) AT (אֶת / et) the~GIRL (הַיַּלְדָּה / hai'yal'dah) the~THIS (הַזֹּאת / ha'zot) to~WOMAN (לְאִשָּׁה / lê'i'shah)

and Shekhem said to Hhamor his father saying, take for me this girl for a woman,

5 and~Ya'aqov (וְיַעֲקֹב / wê'ya'a'qov) he~did~HEAR(Verb) (שָׁמַע / sha'ma) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~much~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (טִמֵּא / ti'mey) AT (אֶת / et) Dinah (דִּינָה / di'nah) DAUGHTER~him (בִתּוֹ / vi'to) and~SON~s~him (וּבָנָיו / u'va'naw) they~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיוּ / hai'u) AT (אֶת / et) LIVESTOCK~him (מִקְנֵהוּ / miq'ney'hu) in~the~FIELD (בַּשָּׂדֶה / ba'sa'deh) and~he~did~make~KEEP.SILENT(Verb) (וְהֶחֱרִשׁ / wê'he'hhe'rish) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) UNTIL (עַד / ad) >~COME(Verb)~them(m) (בֹּאָם / bo'am)

and Ya'aqov had heard that he made Dinah his daughter dirty, and his sons existed with his livestock in the field and Ya'aqov kept silent until they came,

6 and~he~will~GO.OUT(Verb) (וַיֵּצֵא / wai'yey'tsey) DONKEY (חֲמוֹר / hha'mor) FATHER~of (אֲבִי / a'vi) Shekhem (שְׁכֶם / shê'khem) TO (אֶל / el) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) to~>~much~SPEAK(Verb) (לְדַבֵּר / lê'da'beyr) AT~him (אִתּוֹ / i'to)

and Hhamor, the father of Shekhem, went out to Ya'aqov to speak with him,

7 and~SON~s (וּבְנֵי / uv'ney) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) they~did~COME(Verb) (בָּאוּ / ba'u) FROM (מִן / min) the~FIELD (הַשָּׂדֶה / ha'sa'deh) like~>~HEAR(Verb)~them(m) (כְּשָׁמְעָם / kê'sham'am) and~they(m)~will~self~DISTRESS(Verb) (וַיִּתְעַצְּבוּ / wai'yit'ats'vu) the~MAN~s (הָאֲנָשִׁים / ha'a'na'shim) and~he~will~FLARE.UP(Verb) (וַיִּחַר / wai'yi'hhar) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) MANY (מְאֹד / mê'od) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) FOLLY (נְבָלָה / nê'va'lah) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) in~Yisra'eyl (בְיִשְׂרָאֵל / vê'yis'ra'eyl) to~>~LIE.DOWN(Verb) (לִשְׁכַּב / lish'kav) AT (אֶת / et) DAUGHTER (בַּת / bat) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) and~SO (וְכֵן / wê'kheyn) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~be~DO(Verb) (יֵעָשֶׂה / yey'a'seh)

and the sons of Ya'aqov had come from the field and the men were distressed and they greatly flared up given the folly he did in Yisra'eyl, to lay down with the daughter of Ya'aqov and so he will not be done,

8 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) Hhamor (חֲמוֹר / hha'mor) AT~them(m) (אִתָּם / i'tam) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) Shekhem (שְׁכֶם / shê'khem) SON~me (בְּנִי / bê'ni) she~did~ATTACH(Verb) (חָשְׁקָה / hhash'qah) SOUL~him (נַפְשׁוֹ / naph'sho) in~DAUGHTER~them(m) (בְּבִתְּכֶם / bê'vit'khem) !(mp)~GIVE(Verb) (תְּנוּ / tê'nu) PLEASE (נָא / na) AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) to~WOMAN (לְאִשָּׁה / lê'i'shah)

and Hhamor spoke with them saying, Shekhem my son, his soul is attached with their daughter, please give her to him for a woman,

9 and~!(mp)~self~BE.AN.IN-LAW(Verb) (וְהִתְחַתְּנוּ / wê'hit'hhat'nu) AT~us (אֹתָנוּ / o'ta'nu) DAUGHTER~s~you(mp) (בְּנֹתֵיכֶם / bê'no'tey'khem) you(mp)~will~GIVE(Verb) (תִּתְּנוּ / tit'nu) to~~us (לָנוּ / la'nu) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) DAUGHTER~s~us (בְּנֹתֵינוּ / bê'no'tey'nu) you(mp)~will~TAKE(Verb) (תִּקְחוּ / tiq'hhu) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem)

and relate yourselves with us, you will give your daughters to us and you will take our daughters to you,

10 and~AT~us (וְאִתָּנוּ / wê'i'ta'nu) you(mp)~will~TURN.BACK(Verb) (תֵּשֵׁבוּ / tey'shey'vu) and~the~LAND (וְהָאָרֶץ / wê'ha'a'rets) she~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִּהְיֶה / tih'yeh) to~FACE~s~you(mp) (לִפְנֵיכֶם / liph'ney'khem) !(mp)~SETTLE(Verb) (שְׁבוּ / shê'vu) and~!(mp)~TRADE(Verb)~her (וּסְחָרוּהָ / us'hha'ru'ah) and~!(mp)~be~TAKE.HOLD(Verb) (וְהֵאָחֲזוּ / wê'hey'a'hha'zu) in~~her (בָּהּ / bah)

and you will turn back with us and the land will exist to your faces, settle and trade her and be held in her,

11 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Shekhem (שְׁכֶם / shê'khem) TO (אֶל / el) FATHER~her (אָבִיהָ / a'vi'ah) and~TO (וְאֶל / wê'el) BROTHER~s~her (אַחֶיהָ / a'hhey'ah) I~will~FIND(Verb) (אֶמְצָא / em'tsa) BEAUTY (חֵן / hheyn) in~EYE~s2~you(mp) (בְּעֵינֵיכֶם / bê'ey'ney'khem) and~WHICH (וַאֲשֶׁר / wa'a'sher) you(mp)~will~SAY(Verb) (תֹּאמְרוּ / tom'ru) TO~me (אֵלַי / ey'lai) I~will~GIVE(Verb) (אֶתֵּן / e'teyn)

and Shekhem said to her father and to her brothers, I will find beauty in your eyes and what you will say to me I will give.

12 !(mp)~make~INCREASE(Verb) (הַרְבּוּ / har'bu) UPON~me (עָלַי / a'lai) MANY (מְאֹד / mê'od) BRIDE.PRICE (מֹהַר / mo'har) and~GIFT (וּמַתָּן / u'ma'tan) and~I~will~GIVE(Verb)~& (וְאֶתְּנָה / wê'et'nah) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) you(mp)~will~SAY(Verb) (תֹּאמְרוּ / tom'ru) TO~me (אֵלָי / ey'lai) and~!(mp)~GIVE(Verb) (וּתְנוּ / ut'nu) to~~me (לִי / li) AT (אֶת / et) the~YOUNG.WOMAN (הַנַּעֲרָ / ha'na'a'ra) to~WOMAN (לְאִשָּׁה / lê'i'shah)

Make increase upon me a great bride price and gift and I will give just as you say to me and give to me the young woman for a woman,

13 and~they(m)~will~ANSWER(Verb) (וַיַּעֲנוּ / wai'ya'a'nu) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) AT (אֶת / et) Shekhem (שְׁכֶם / shê'khem) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) DONKEY (חֲמוֹר / hha'mor) FATHER~him (אָבִיו / a'viw) in~DECEIT (בְּמִרְמָה / bê'mir'mah) and~they(m)~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּרוּ / wa'ye'da'bey'ru) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~much~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (טִמֵּא / ti'mey) AT (אֵת / eyt) Dinah (דִּינָה / di'nah) SISTER~them(m) (אֲחֹתָם / a'hho'tam)

and the sons of Ya'aqov answered Shekhem and Hhamor his father in deceit and they spoke because he made Dinah their sister dirty,

14 and~they(m)~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמְרוּ / wai'yom'ru) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵיהֶם / a'ley'hem) NOT (לֹא / lo) we~will~BE.ABLE(Verb) (נוּכַל / nu'khal) to~>~DO(Verb) (לַעֲשׂוֹת / la'a'sot) the~WORD (הַדָּבָר / ha'da'var) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh) to~>~GIVE(Verb) (לָתֵת / la'teyt) AT (אֶת / et) SISTER~us (אֲחֹתֵנוּ / a'hho'tey'nu) to~MAN (לְאִישׁ / lê'ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) FORESKIN (עָרְלָה / ar'lah) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) DISGRACE (חֶרְפָּה / hher'pah) SHE (הִוא / hi) to~~us (לָנוּ / la'nu)

and they said to them, we will not be able to do this word, to give our sister to a man that has a foreskin given that she is a disgrace to us.

15 SURELY (אַךְ / akh) in~THIS (בְּזֹאת / bê'zot) we~will~be~AGREE(Verb) (נֵאוֹת / ney'ot) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) IF (אִם / im) you(mp)~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִּהְיוּ / tih'yu) like~THAT.ONE~us (כָמֹנוּ / kha'mo'nu) to~>~be~SNIP.OFF(Verb) (לְהִמֹּל / lê'hi'mol) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) ALL (כָּל / kol) MALE (זָכָר / za'khar)

Surely in this we will be agreed to you if you will exist like one of us, all males belonging to you to be snipped,

16 and~we~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַנּוּ / wê'na'ta'nu) AT (אֶת / et) DAUGHTER~s~us (בְּנֹתֵינוּ / bê'no'tey'nu) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) DAUGHTER~s~you(mp) (בְּנֹתֵיכֶם / bê'no'tey'khem) we~will~TAKE(Verb) (נִקַּח / ni'qahh) to~~us (לָנוּ / la'nu) and~we~did~SETTLE(Verb) (וְיָשַׁבְנוּ / wê'ya'shav'nu) AT~you(mp) (אִתְּכֶם / it'khem) and~we~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיִינוּ / wê'ha'yi'nu) to~PEOPLE (לְעַם / lê'am) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad)

and we will give our daughters to you and your daughters we will take for us and we will settle with you and we will exist for a people unit,

17 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~HEAR(Verb) (תִשְׁמְעוּ / tish'me'u) TO~us (אֵלֵינוּ / ey'ley'nu) to~>~be~SNIP.OFF(Verb) (לְהִמּוֹל / lê'hi'mol) and~we~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקַחְנוּ / wê'la'qahh'nu) AT (אֶת / et) DAUGHTER~us (בִּתֵּנוּ / bi'tey'nu) and~we~did~WALK(Verb) (וְהָלָכְנוּ / wê'ha'lakh'nu)

and if you will not listen to us, to be snipped, then we will take our daughter and we will walk,

18 and~they(m)~will~DO.WELL(Verb) (וַיִּיטְבוּ / wai'yi'te'vu) WORD~s~them(m) (דִבְרֵיהֶם / div'rey'hem) in~EYE~s2 (בְּעֵינֵי / bê'ey'ney) DONKEY (חֲמוֹר / hha'mor) and~in~EYE~s2 (וּבְעֵינֵי / uv'ey'ney) Shekhem (שְׁכֶם / shê'khem) SON (בֶּן / ben) DONKEY (חֲמוֹר / hha'mor)

and their words did well in the eyes of Hhamor and in the eyes of Shekhem, the son of Hhamor,

19 and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~did~much~DELAY(Verb) (אֵחַר / ey'hhar) the~YOUNG.MAN (הַנַּעַר / ha'na'ar) to~>~DO(Verb) (לַעֲשׂוֹת / la'a'sot) the~WORD (הַדָּבָר / ha'da'var) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~DELIGHT(Verb) (חָפֵץ / hha'pheyts) in~DAUGHTER (בְּבַת / bê'vat) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) and~HE (וְהוּא / wê'hu) be~BE.HEAVY(Verb)~ing(ms) (נִכְבָּד / nikh'bad) from~ALL (מִכֹּל / mi'kol) HOUSE (בֵּית / beyt) FATHER~him (אָבִיו / a'viw)

and the young man did not delay to do the words, given that he delighted in the daughter of Ya'aqov and he was a heavy one out of all the house of his father,

20 and~he~will~COME(Verb) (וַיָּבֹא / wai'ya'vo) DONKEY (חֲמוֹר / hha'mor) and~Shekhem (וּשְׁכֶם / ush'khem) SON~him (בְּנוֹ / bê'no) TO (אֶל / el) GATE (שַׁעַר / sha'ar) CITY~them(m) (עִירָם / i'ram) and~they(m)~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבְּרוּ / wa'ye'da'be'ru) TO (אֶל / el) MAN~s (אַנְשֵׁי / an'shey) CITY~them(m) (עִירָם / i'ram) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor)

and Hhamor came, and Shekhem his son, to the gate of their city and they spoke to the men of the city saying,

21 the~MAN~s (הָאֲנָשִׁים / ha'a'na'shim) the~THESE (הָאֵלֶּה / ha'ey'leh) COMPLETENESS~s (שְׁלֵמִים / shê'ley'mim) THEY(m) (הֵם / heym) AT~us (אִתָּנוּ / i'ta'nu) and~they(m)~will~SETTLE(Verb) (וְיֵשְׁבוּ / wê'yeysh'vu) in~the~LAND (בָאָרֶץ / va'a'rets) and~they(m)~will~TRADE(Verb) (וְיִסְחֲרוּ / wê'yis'hha'ru) AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) and~the~LAND (וְהָאָרֶץ / wê'ha'a'rets) LOOK (הִנֵּה / hin'neyh) WIDE (רַחֲבַת / ra'hha'vat) HAND~s2 (יָדַיִם / ya'da'yim) to~FACE~s~them(m) (לִפְנֵיהֶם / liph'ney'hem) AT (אֶת / et) DAUGHTER~s~them(m) (בְּנֹתָם / bê'no'tam) we~will~TAKE(Verb) (נִקַּח / ni'qahh) to~~us (לָנוּ / la'nu) to~WOMAN~s (לְנָשִׁים / lê'na'shim) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) DAUGHTER~s~us (בְּנֹתֵינוּ / bê'no'tey'nu) we~will~GIVE(Verb) (נִתֵּן / ni'teyn) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem)

These men are complete with us and they settled in the land and they traded with her and look, the land is wide of hands to their face, we will take their daughters for us for women and we will give our daughters to them.

22 SURELY (אַךְ / akh) in~THIS (בְּזֹאת / bê'zot) they(m)~will~AGREE(Verb) (יֵאֹתוּ / yey'o'tu) to~~us (לָנוּ / la'nu) the~MAN~s (הָאֲנָשִׁים / ha'a'na'shim) to~>~SETTLE(Verb) (לָשֶׁבֶת / la'she'vet) AT~us (אִתָּנוּ / i'ta'nu) to~>~EXIST(Verb) (לִהְיוֹת / lih'yot) to~PEOPLE (לְעַם / lê'am) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) in~>~be~SNIP.OFF(Verb) (בְּהִמּוֹל / bê'hi'mol) to~~us (לָנוּ / la'nu) ALL (כָּל / kol) MALE (זָכָר / za'khar) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) THEY(m) (הֵם / heym) be~SNIP.OFF(Verb)~ing(mp) (נִמֹּלִים / ni'mo'lim)

Surely in this, the men will agree with us to settle with us, to exist for a people unit, with all males belonging to us being snipped just as they are being snipped.
