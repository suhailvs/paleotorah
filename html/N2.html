Numbers Chapter 2

1 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) and~TO (וְאֶל / wê'el) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh and to Aharon saying,

2 MAN (אִישׁ / ish) UPON (עַל / al) BANNER~him (דִּגְלוֹ / dig'lo) in~SIGN~s (בְאֹתֹת / vê'o'tot) to~HOUSE (לְבֵית / lê'veyt) FATHER~s~them(m) (אֲבֹתָם / a'vo'tam) they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (יַחֲנוּ / ya'hha'nu) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) from~OPPOSITE (מִנֶּגֶד / mi'ne'ged) ALL.AROUND (סָבִיב / sa'viv) to~TENT (לְאֹהֶל / lê'o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (יַחֲנוּ / ya'hha'nu)

Each by his banner, with the signs according to the house of their fathers, the sons of Yisra'eyl will camp, opposite all around the appointed tent they will camp,

3 and~the~CAMP(Verb)~ing(mp) (וְהַחֹנִים / wê'ha'hho'nim) EAST~unto (קֵדְמָה / qeyd'mah) SUNRISE~unto (מִזְרָחָה / miz'ra'hhah) BANNER (דֶּגֶל / de'gel) CAMP (מַחֲנֵה / ma'hha'neyh) Yehudah (יְהוּדָה / yê'hu'dah) to~ARMY~s~them(m) (לְצִבְאֹתָם / lê'tsiv'o'tam) and~CAPTAIN (וְנָשִׂיא / wê'na'si) to~SON~s (לִבְנֵי / liv'ney) Yehudah (יְהוּדָה / yê'hu'dah) Nahhshon (נַחְשׁוֹן / nahh'shon) SON (בֶּן / ben) Amiynadav (עַמִּינָדָב / a'mi'na'dav)

and the ones camping to the east, toward the sunrise, is the banner of the camp of Yehudah, by their armies, and the captain for the sons of Yehudah is Nahhshon the son of Amiynadav,

4 and~ARMY~him (וּצְבָאוֹ / uts'va'o) and~REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp)~them(m) (וּפְקֻדֵיהֶם / uph'qu'dey'hem) FOUR (אַרְבָּעָה / ar'ba'ah) and~SEVEN~s (וְשִׁבְעִים / wê'shiv'im) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~SIX (וְשֵׁשׁ / wê'sheysh) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot)

and his army and their registered ones are seventy-four thousand and six hundred,

5 and~the~CAMP(Verb)~ing(mp) (וְהַחֹנִים / wê'ha'hho'nim) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) BRANCH (מַטֵּה / ma'teyh) Yis'sas'khar (יִשָּׂשכָר / yi'sas'khar) and~CAPTAIN (וְנָשִׂיא / wê'na'si) to~SON~s (לִבְנֵי / liv'ney) Yis'sas'khar (יִשָּׂשכָר / yi'sas'khar) Nataneyl (נְתַנְאֵל / nê'tan'eyl) SON (בֶּן / ben) Tso'ar (צוּעָר / tsu'ar)

and the ones camping by him, the branch of Yis'sas'khar, and the captain for the sons of Yis'sas'khar is Nataneyl the son of Tso'ar,

6 and~ARMY~him (וּצְבָאוֹ / uts'va'o) and~REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp)~him (וּפְקֻדָיו / uph'qu'daw) FOUR (אַרְבָּעָה / ar'ba'ah) and~FIVE~s (וַחֲמִשִּׁים / wa'hha'mi'shim) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~FOUR (וְאַרְבַּע / wê'ar'ba) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot)

and his army and his registered ones are fifty-four thousand and four hundred.

7 BRANCH (מַטֵּה / ma'teyh) Zevulun (זְבוּלֻן / zê'vu'lun) and~CAPTAIN (וְנָשִׂיא / wê'na'si) to~SON~s (לִבְנֵי / liv'ney) Zevulun (זְבוּלֻן / zê'vu'lun) Eli'av (אֱלִיאָב / e'li'av) SON (בֶּן / ben) Hheylon (חֵלֹן / hhey'lon)

The branch of Zevulun, and the captain for the sons of Zevulun is Eli'av the son of Hheylon,

8 and~ARMY~him (וּצְבָאוֹ / uts'va'o) and~REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp)~him (וּפְקֻדָיו / uph'qu'daw) SEVEN (שִׁבְעָה / shiv'ah) and~FIVE~s (וַחֲמִשִּׁים / wa'hha'mi'shim) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~FOUR (וְאַרְבַּע / wê'ar'ba) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot)

and his army and his registered ones are fifty-seven thousand and four hundred.

9 ALL (כָּל / kol) the~REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp) (הַפְּקֻדִים / hap'qu'dim) to~CAMP (לְמַחֲנֵה / lê'ma'hha'neyh) Yehudah (יְהוּדָה / yê'hu'dah) HUNDRED (מְאַת / mê'at) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~EIGHT~s (וּשְׁמֹנִים / u'she'mo'nim) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~SIX (וְשֵׁשֶׁת / wê'shey'shet) THOUSAND~s (אֲלָפִים / a'la'phim) and~FOUR (וְאַרְבַּע / wê'ar'ba) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot) to~ARMY~s~them(m) (לְצִבְאֹתָם / lê'tsiv'o'tam) FIRST (רִאשֹׁנָה / ri'sho'nah) they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (יִסָּעוּ / yi'sa'u)

All the registered ones for the camp of Yehudah are a hundred thousand and eighty thousand and six thousand and four hundred for their armies, they will journey first.

10 BANNER (דֶּגֶל / de'gel) CAMP (מַחֲנֵה / ma'hha'neyh) Re'uven (רְאוּבֵן / rê'u'veyn) SOUTHWARD~unto (תֵּימָנָה / tey'ma'nah) to~ARMY~s~them(m) (לְצִבְאֹתָם / lê'tsiv'o'tam) and~CAPTAIN (וְנָשִׂיא / wê'na'si) to~SON~s (לִבְנֵי / liv'ney) Re'uven (רְאוּבֵן / rê'u'veyn) Elitsur (אֱלִיצוּר / e'li'tsur) SON (בֶּן / ben) Shedeyur (שְׁדֵיאוּר / shê'dey'ur)

The banner of the camp of Re'uven is unto the south for their armies, and the captain for the sons of Re'uven is Elitsur the son of Shedeyur,

11 and~ARMY~him (וּצְבָאוֹ / uts'va'o) and~REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp)~him (וּפְקֻדָיו / uph'qu'daw) SIX (שִׁשָּׁה / shi'shah) and~FOUR~s (וְאַרְבָּעִים / wê'ar'ba'im) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~FIVE (וַחֲמֵשׁ / wa'hha'meysh) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot)

and his army and his registered ones is forty-six thousand and five hundred,

12 and~the~CAMP(Verb)~ing(mp) (וְהַחוֹנִם / wê'ha'hho'nim) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) BRANCH (מַטֵּה / ma'teyh) Shimon (שִׁמְעוֹן / shim'on) and~CAPTAIN (וְנָשִׂיא / wê'na'si) to~SON~s (לִבְנֵי / liv'ney) Shimon (שִׁמְעוֹן / shim'on) Shelumi'eyl (שְׁלֻמִיאֵל / shê'lu'mi'eyl) SON (בֶּן / ben) Tsurishaddai (צוּרִי-שַׁדָּי / tsu'ri)

and the ones camping by him is the branch of Shimon, and the captain for the sons of Shimon is Shelumi'eyl the son of Tsur of Shaddai,

13 and~ARMY~him (וּצְבָאוֹ / uts'va'o) and~REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp)~them(m) (וּפְקֻדֵיהֶם / uph'qu'dey'hem) NINE (תִּשְׁעָה / tish'ah) and~FIVE~s (וַחֲמִשִּׁים / wa'hha'mi'shim) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~THREE (וּשְׁלֹשׁ / ush'losh) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot)

and his army and their registered ones is fifty-nine thousand and three hundred,

14 and~BRANCH (וְמַטֵּה / wê'ma'teyh) Gad (גָּד / gad) and~CAPTAIN (וְנָשִׂיא / wê'na'si) to~SON~s (לִבְנֵי / liv'ney) Gad (גָד / gad) Elyasaph (אֶלְיָסָף / el'ya'saph) SON (בֶּן / ben) Re'u'eyl (רְעוּאֵל / rê'u'eyl)

and the branch of Gad, and the captain for the sons of Gad is Elyasaph the son of Re'u'eyl,

15 and~ARMY~him (וּצְבָאוֹ / uts'va'o) and~REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp)~them(m) (וּפְקֻדֵיהֶם / uph'qu'dey'hem) FIVE (חֲמִשָּׁה / hha'mi'shah) and~FOUR~s (וְאַרְבָּעִים / wê'ar'ba'im) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~SIX (וְשֵׁשׁ / wê'sheysh) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot) and~FIVE~s (וַחֲמִשִּׁים / wa'hha'mi'shim)

and his army, and their registered ones is forty-five thousand and six hundred and fifty.

16 ALL (כָּל / kol) the~REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp) (הַפְּקֻדִים / hap'qu'dim) to~CAMP (לְמַחֲנֵה / lê'ma'hha'neyh) Re'uven (רְאוּבֵן / rê'u'veyn) HUNDRED (מְאַת / mê'at) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~UNIT (וְאֶחָד / wê'e'hhad) and~FIVE~s (וַחֲמִשִּׁים / wa'hha'mi'shim) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~FOUR (וְאַרְבַּע / wê'ar'ba) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot) and~FIVE~s (וַחֲמִשִּׁים / wa'hha'mi'shim) to~ARMY~s~them(m) (לְצִבְאֹתָם / lê'tsiv'o'tam) and~SECOND (וּשְׁנִיִּם / ush'ni'yim) they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (יִסָּעוּ / yi'sa'u)

All the registered ones belonging to the camp of Yehudah are a hundred thousand and fifty-one thousand and four hundred and fifty for their armies, they will journey second,

17 and~he~did~JOURNEY(Verb) (וְנָסַע / wê'na'sa) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) CAMP (מַחֲנֵה / ma'hha'neyh) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim) in~MIDST (בְּתוֹךְ / bê'tokh) the~CAMP~s (הַמַּחֲנֹת / ha'ma'hha'not) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (יַחֲנוּ / ya'hha'nu) SO (כֵּן / keyn) they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (יִסָּעוּ / yi'sa'u) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) UPON (עַל / al) HAND~him (יָדוֹ / ya'do) to~BANNER~s~them(m) (לְדִגְלֵיהֶם / lê'dig'ley'hem)

and the appointed tent, the camp of the ones of Lewi, journeyed in the midst of the camps, just as they will camp, so they will journey, each according to his hand belonging to their banners.

18 BANNER (דֶּגֶל / de'gel) CAMP (מַחֲנֵה / ma'hha'neyh) Ephrayim (אֶפְרַיִם / eph'ra'yim) to~ARMY~s~them(m) (לְצִבְאֹתָם / lê'tsiv'o'tam) SEA~unto (יָמָּה / ya'mah) and~CAPTAIN (וְנָשִׂיא / wê'na'si) to~SON~s (לִבְנֵי / liv'ney) Ephrayim (אֶפְרַיִם / eph'ra'yim) Elishama (אֱלִישָׁמָע / e'li'sha'ma) SON (בֶּן / ben) Amihud (עַמִּיהוּד / a'mi'hud)

The banner of the camp of Ephrayim is unto the west for their armies, and the captain for the sons of Ephrayim is Elishama the son of Amihud,

19 and~ARMY~him (וּצְבָאוֹ / uts'va'o) and~REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp)~them(m) (וּפְקֻדֵיהֶם / uph'qu'dey'hem) FOUR~s (אַרְבָּעִים / ar'ba'im) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~FIVE (וַחֲמֵשׁ / wa'hha'meysh) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot)

and his army, and their registered ones is forty thousand and five hundred,

20 and~UPON~him (וְעָלָיו / wê'a'law) BRANCH (מַטֵּה / ma'teyh) Menasheh (מְנַשֶּׁה / mê'na'sheh) and~CAPTAIN (וְנָשִׂיא / wê'na'si) to~SON~s (לִבְנֵי / liv'ney) Menasheh (מְנַשֶּׁה / mê'na'sheh) Gamli'eyl (גַּמְלִיאֵל / gam'li'eyl) SON (בֶּן / ben) Pedatsur (פְּדָהצוּר / pê'dah'tsur)

and by him, the branch of Menasheh, and the captain of the sons of Menasheh is Gamli'eyl the son of Pedatsur,

21 and~ARMY~him (וּצְבָאוֹ / uts'va'o) and~REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp)~them(m) (וּפְקֻדֵיהֶם / uph'qu'dey'hem) TWO (שְׁנַיִם / shê'na'yim) and~THREE~s (וּשְׁלֹשִׁים / ush'lo'shim) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~HUNDRED~s2 (וּמָאתָיִם / u'ma'ta'yim)

and his army, and their registered ones is thirty-two thousand and two hundred,

22 and~BRANCH (וּמַטֵּה / u'ma'teyh) Binyamin (בִּנְיָמִן / bin'ya'min) and~CAPTAIN (וְנָשִׂיא / wê'na'si) to~SON~s (לִבְנֵי / liv'ney) Binyamin (בִנְיָמִן / vin'ya'min) Avidan (אֲבִידָן / a'vi'dan) SON (בֶּן / ben) Gidoni (גִּדְעֹנִי / gid'o'ni)

and the branch of Binyamin, and the captain for the sons of Binyamin is Avidan the son of Gidoni,

23 and~ARMY~him (וּצְבָאוֹ / uts'va'o) and~REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp)~them(m) (וּפְקֻדֵיהֶם / uph'qu'dey'hem) FIVE (חֲמִשָּׁה / hha'mi'shah) and~THREE~s (וּשְׁלֹשִׁים / ush'lo'shim) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~FOUR (וְאַרְבַּע / wê'ar'ba) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot)

and his army, and their registered ones if thirty-five thousand and four hundred.

24 ALL (כָּל / kol) the~REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp) (הַפְּקֻדִים / hap'qu'dim) to~CAMP (לְמַחֲנֵה / lê'ma'hha'neyh) Ephrayim (אֶפְרַיִם / eph'ra'yim) HUNDRED (מְאַת / mê'at) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~EIGHT (וּשְׁמֹנַת / ush'mo'nat) THOUSAND~s (אֲלָפִים / a'la'phim) and~HUNDRED (וּמֵאָה / u'mey'ah) to~ARMY~s~them(m) (לְצִבְאֹתָם / lê'tsiv'o'tam) and~THIRD~s (וּשְׁלִשִׁים / ush'li'shim) they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (יִסָּעוּ / yi'sa'u)

All the registered ones belonging to the camp of Ephrayim are a hundred thousand and eight thousand and a hundred for their armies, they will journey third.

25 BANNER (דֶּגֶל / de'gel) CAMP (מַחֲנֵה / ma'hha'neyh) Dan (דָן / dan) NORTH~unto (צָפֹנָה / tsa'pho'nah) to~ARMY~s~them(m) (לְצִבְאֹתָם / lê'tsiv'o'tam) and~CAPTAIN (וְנָשִׂיא / wê'na'si) to~SON~s (לִבְנֵי / liv'ney) Dan (דָן / dan) Ahhi'ezer (אֲחִיעֶזֶר / a'hhi'e'zer) SON (בֶּן / ben) Amishaddai (עַמִּישַׁדָּי / a'mi'sha'dai)

The banner of the camp of Dan is unto the north for their armies, and the captain for the sons of Dan is Ahhi'ezer the son of Amishaddai,

26 and~ARMY~him (וּצְבָאוֹ / uts'va'o) and~REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp)~them(m) (וּפְקֻדֵיהֶם / uph'qu'dey'hem) TWO (שְׁנַיִם / shê'na'yim) and~SIX~s (וְשִׁשִּׁים / wê'shi'shim) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~SEVEN (וּשְׁבַע / ush'va) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot)

and his army and their registered ones are sixty-two thousand and seven hundred,

27 and~the~CAMP(Verb)~ing(mp) (וְהַחֹנִים / wê'ha'hho'nim) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) BRANCH (מַטֵּה / ma'teyh) Asher (אָשֵׁר / a'sheyr) and~CAPTAIN (וְנָשִׂיא / wê'na'si) to~SON~s (לִבְנֵי / liv'ney) Asher (אָשֵׁר / a'sheyr) Pagi'eyl (פַּגְעִיאֵל / pag'i'eyl) SON (בֶּן / ben) Akhran (עָכְרָן / akh'ran)

and the ones camping by him are the branch of Asher, and the captain for the sons of Asher is Pagi'eyl the son of Akhran,

28 and~ARMY~him (וּצְבָאוֹ / uts'va'o) and~REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp)~them(m) (וּפְקֻדֵיהֶם / uph'qu'dey'hem) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) and~FOUR~s (וְאַרְבָּעִים / wê'ar'ba'im) THOUSAND (אֶלֶף / e'leph) and~FIVE (וַחֲמֵשׁ / wa'hha'meysh) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot)

and his army and their registered ones are forty-one thousand and five hundred,

29 and~BRANCH (וּמַטֵּה / u'ma'teyh) Naphtali (נַפְתָּלִי / naph'ta'li) and~CAPTAIN (וְנָשִׂיא / wê'na'si) to~SON~s (לִבְנֵי / liv'ney) Naphtali (נַפְתָּלִי / naph'ta'li) Ahhira (אֲחִירַע / a'hhi'ra) SON (בֶּן / ben) Eynan (עֵינָן / ey'nan)

and the branch of Naphtali, and the captain for the sons of Naphtali is Ahhira the son of Eynan,
