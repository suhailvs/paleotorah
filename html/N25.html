Numbers Chapter 25

1 and~he~will~SETTLE(Verb) (וַיֵּשֶׁב / wai'yey'shev) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) in~Shitiym (בַּשִּׁטִּים / ba'shi'tim) and~he~will~make~DRILL(Verb) (וַיָּחֶל / wai'ya'hhel) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) to~>~BE.A.HARLOT(Verb) (לִזְנוֹת / liz'not) TO (אֶל / el) DAUGHTER~s (בְּנוֹת / bê'not) Mo'av (מוֹאָב / mo'av)

and Yisra'eyl settled in the Shitiym, and the people began to be a harlot to the daughters of Mo'av,

2 and~they(f)~will~CALL.OUT(Verb) (וַתִּקְרֶאןָ / wa'tiq're'na) to~the~PEOPLE (לָעָם / la'am) to~SACRIFICE~s (לְזִבְחֵי / lê'zav'hhey) Elohiym~them(f) (אֱלֹהֵיהֶן / e'lo'hey'hen) and~he~will~EAT(Verb) (וַיֹּאכַל / wai'yo'khal) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) and~they(m)~will~self~BEND.DOWN(Verb) (וַיִּשְׁתַּחֲוּוּ / wai'yish'ta'hha'u'u) to~Elohiym~them(f) (לֵאלֹהֵיהֶן / ley'lo'hey'hen)

and they called out to the people for sacrifices of their Elohiym, and the people ate, and they bent themselves down to their Elohiym,

3 and~he~will~be~FASTEN(Verb) (וַיִּצָּמֶד / wai'yi'tsa'med) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) to~Ba'al-Pe'or (לְבַעַל-פְּעוֹר / lê'va'al) and~he~will~FLARE.UP(Verb) (וַיִּחַר / wai'yi'hhar) NOSE (אַף / aph) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) in~Yisra'eyl (בְּיִשְׂרָאֵל / bê'yis'ra'eyl)

and Yisra'eyl was fastened to Ba'al-Pe'or and YHWH flared up his nose with Yisra'eyl,

4 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) !(ms)~TAKE(Verb) (קַח / qahh) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) HEAD~s (רָאשֵׁי / ra'shey) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) and~!(ms)~make~DISLOCATE(Verb) (וְהוֹקַע / wê'ho'qa) AT~them(m) (אוֹתָם / o'tam) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) OPPOSITE (נֶגֶד / ne'ged) the~SUN (הַשָּׁמֶשׁ / ha'sha'mesh) and~he~will~TURN.BACK(Verb) (וְיָשֹׁב / wê'ya'shov) FLAMING.WRATH (חֲרוֹן / hha'ron) NOSE (אַף / aph) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) from~Yisra'eyl (מִיִּשְׂרָאֵל / mi'yis'ra'eyl)

and YHWH said to Mosheh, take all the heads of the people and hang them before YHWH opposite the sun, and the flaming wrath of the nose of YHWH was turned back from Yisra'eyl,

5 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) TO (אֶל / el) DECIDE(Verb)~ing(mp) (שֹׁפְטֵי / shoph'tey) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) !(mp)~KILL(Verb) (הִרְגוּ / hir'gu) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) MAN~s~him (אֲנָשָׁיו / a'na'shaw) the~be~FASTEN(Verb)~ing(mp) (הַנִּצְמָדִים / ha'nits'ma'dim) to~Ba'al-Pe'or (לְבַעַל-פְּעוֹר / lê'va'al)

and Mosheh said to the deciding ones of Yisra'eyl, kill each of his men, the ones being fastened to Ba'al-Pe'or,

6 and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) from~SON~s (מִבְּנֵי / mi'be'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) he~did~COME(Verb) (בָּא / ba) and~he~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וַיַּקְרֵב / wai'yaq'reyv) TO (אֶל / el) BROTHER~s~him (אֶחָיו / e'hhaw) AT (אֶת / et) the~Mid'yan~of (הַמִּדְיָנִית / ha'mid'ya'nit) [881] to~EYE~s2 (לְעֵינֵי / lê'ey'ney) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) and~to~EYE~s (וּלְעֵינֵי / ul'ey'ney) ALL (כָּל / kol) COMPANY (עֲדַת / a'dat) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~THEY(m) (וְהֵמָּה / wê'hey'mah) WEEP(Verb)~ing(mp) (בֹכִים / vo'khim) OPENING (פֶּתַח / pe'tahh) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd)

and look, a man from the sons of Yisra'eyl came, and he brought near to his brothers the one of Mid'yan to the eyes of Mosheh, and to the eyes of all the company of the sons of Yisra'eyl, and they were weeping at the opening of the appointed tent,

7 and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) Piynhhas (פִּינְחָס / pin'hhas) SON (בֶּן / ben) Elazar (אֶלְעָזָר / el'a'zar) SON (בֶּן / ben) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~he~will~RISE(Verb) (וַיָּקָם / wai'ya'qam) from~MIDST (מִתּוֹךְ / mi'tokh) the~COMPANY (הָעֵדָה / ha'ey'dah) and~he~will~TAKE(Verb) (וַיִּקַּח / wai'yi'qahh) SPEAR (רֹמַח / ro'mahh) in~HAND~him (בְּיָדוֹ / bê'ya'do)

and Piynhhas the son of Elazar, the son of Aharon the administrator, saw, and he rose from the midst of the company, and he took a spear in his hand,

8 and~he~will~COME(Verb) (וַיָּבֹא / wai'ya'vo) AFTER (אַחַר / a'hhar) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) TO (אֶל / el) the~HUT (הַקֻּבָּה / ha'qu'bah) and~he~will~PIERCE(Verb) (וַיִּדְקֹר / wai'yid'qor) AT (אֶת / et) TWO~them(m) (שְׁנֵיהֶם / shê'ney'hem) AT (אֵת / eyt) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~WOMAN (הָאִשָּׁה / ha'i'shah) TO (אֶל / el) STOMACH~her (קֳבָתָהּ / qa'va'tah) and~she~will~be~STOP(Verb) (וַתֵּעָצַר / wa'tey'a'tsar) the~PESTILENCE (הַמַּגֵּפָה / ha'ma'gey'phah) from~UPON (מֵעַל / mey'al) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl)

and he came after the man of Yisra'eyl to the hut, and he pierced the two of them, the man of Yisra'eyl, and the woman through her stomach, and the pestilence was stopped from upon the sons of Yisra'eyl,

9 and~they(m)~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיִּהְיוּ / wai'yih'yu) the~DIE(Verb)~ing(mp) (הַמֵּתִים / ha'mey'tim) in~the~PESTILENCE (בַּמַּגֵּפָה / ba'ma'gey'phah) FOUR (אַרְבָּעָה / ar'ba'ah) and~TEN~s (וְעֶשְׂרִים / wê'es'rim) THOUSAND (אָלֶף / a'leph)

and the ones dying in the pestilence were twenty-four thousand,

10 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

11 Piynhhas (פִּינְחָס / pin'hhas) SON (בֶּן / ben) Elazar (אֶלְעָזָר / el'a'zar) SON (בֶּן / ben) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) he~did~make~TURN.BACK(Verb) (הֵשִׁיב / hey'shiv) AT (אֶת / et) FURY~me (חֲמָתִי / hha'ma'ti) from~UPON (מֵעַל / mey'al) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) in~>~much~BE.ZEALOUS(Verb)~him (בְּקַנְאוֹ / bê'qan'o) AT (אֶת / et) ZEALOUSNESS~me (קִנְאָתִי / qin'a'ti) in~MIDST~them(m) (בְּתוֹכָם / bê'to'kham) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) I~did~much~much~FINISH(Verb) (כִלִּיתִי / khi'li'ti) AT (אֶת / et) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) in~ZEALOUSNESS~me (בְּקִנְאָתִי / bê'qin'a'ti)

Piynhhas the son of Elazar, the son of Aharon the administrator, had turned back my fury from upon the sons of Yisra'eyl, with his being zealous of my zealousness in their midst, and I did not finish the sons of Yisra'eyl with my zealousness.

12 to~SO (לָכֵן / la'kheyn) !(ms)~SAY(Verb) (אֱמֹר / e'mor) LOOK~me (הִנְנִי / hin'ni) GIVE(Verb)~ing(ms) (נֹתֵן / no'teyn) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) AT (אֶת / et) COVENANT~me (בְּרִיתִי / bê'ri'ti) COMPLETENESS (שָׁלוֹם / sha'lom)

Because of this say, look at me, giving to him my covenant of completeness,

13 and~she~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיְתָה / wê'hai'tah) to~~him (לּוֹ / lo) and~to~SEED~him (וּלְזַרְעוֹ / ul'zar'o) AFTER~him (אַחֲרָיו / a'hha'raw) COVENANT (בְּרִית / bê'rit) ADMINISTRATION (כְּהֻנַּת / kê'hu'nat) DISTANT (עוֹלָם / o'lam) UNDER (תַּחַת / ta'hhat) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~much~BE.ZEALOUS(Verb) (קִנֵּא / qi'ney) to~Elohiym~him (לֵאלֹהָיו / ley'lo'haw) and~he~will~much~COVER(Verb) (וַיְכַפֵּר / wa'ye'kha'peyr) UPON (עַל / al) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl)

and she existed for him, and to his seed after him, a covenant of a distant administration, because he was being zealous for his Elohiym, and he covered over the sons of Yisra'eyl,

14 and~TITLE (וְשֵׁם / wê'sheym) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) the~be~make~HIT(Verb)~ing(ms) (הַמֻּכֶּה / ha'mu'keh) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~be~make~HIT(Verb) (הֻכָּה / hu'kah) AT (אֶת / et) the~Mid'yan~of (הַמִּדְיָנִית / ha'mid'ya'nit) [882] Zimri (זִמְרִי / zim'ri) SON (בֶּן / ben) Salu (סָלוּא / sa'lu) CAPTAIN (נְשִׂיא / nê'si) HOUSE (בֵית / veyt) FATHER (אָב / av) to~Shimon~of (לַשִּׁמְעֹנִי / la'shim'o'ni)

and the title of the man of Yisra'eyl, the one being attacked, who was attacked with the one of Mid'yan, was Zimri the son of Salu, the captain of the house of the father belonging to the one of Shimon,

15 and~TITLE (וְשֵׁם / wê'sheym) the~WOMAN (הָאִשָּׁה / ha'i'shah) the~be~make~HIT(Verb)~ing(fs) (הַמֻּכָּה / ha'mu'kah) the~Mid'yan~of (הַמִּדְיָנִית / ha'mid'ya'nit) Kazbi (כָּזְבִּי / kaz'bi) DAUGHTER (בַת / vat) Tsur (צוּר / tsur) HEAD (רֹאשׁ / rosh) TRIBE~s (אֻמּוֹת / u'mot) HOUSE (בֵּית / beyt) FATHER (אָב / av) in~Mid'yan (בְּמִדְיָן / bê'mid'yan) HE (הוּא / hu)

and the title of the woman, the one being attacked, the one of Mid'yan was Kazbi, the daughter of Tsur, the head of the tribes of the father in Mid'yan is he,

16 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

17 >~PRESS.IN(Verb) (צָרוֹר / tsa'ror) AT (אֶת / et) the~Mid'yan~s (הַמִּדְיָנִים / ha'mid'ya'nim) and~you(mp)~did~make~HIT(Verb) (וְהִכִּיתֶם / wê'hi'ki'tem) AT~them(m) (אוֹתָם / o'tam)

Press in to the ones of Mid'yan, and you will attack them.

18 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) PRESS.IN(Verb)~ing(mp) (צֹרְרִים / tsor'rim) THEY(m) (הֵם / heym) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) in~CRAFTINESS~s~them(m) (בְּנִכְלֵיהֶם / bê'nikh'ley'hem) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they~did~much~BE.CRAFTY(Verb) (נִכְּלוּ / ni'ke'lu) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) UPON (עַל / al) WORD (דְּבַר / dê'var) Pe'or (פְּעוֹר / pê'or) and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) WORD (דְּבַר / dê'var) Kazbi (כָּזְבִּי / kaz'bi) DAUGHTER (בַת / vat) CAPTAIN (נְשִׂיא / nê'si) Mid'yan (מִדְיָן / mid'yan) SISTER~them(m) (אֲחֹתָם / a'hho'tam) the~be~make~HIT(Verb)~ing(fs) (הַמֻּכָּה / ha'mu'kah) in~DAY (בְיוֹם / vê'yom) the~PESTILENCE (הַמַּגֵּפָה / ha'ma'gey'phah) UPON (עַל / al) WORD (דְּבַר / dê'var) Pe'or (פְּעוֹר / pê'or)

Given that they are pressing in on you with their craftiness, because they were crafty toward you in the matter of Pe'or, and in the matter of Kazbi the daughter of the captain of Mid'yan, their sister, the one being attacked in the day of the pestilence, in the matter of Pe'or,

19 and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) the~PESTILENCE (הַמַּגֵּפָה / ha'ma'gey'phah) [883]

and it came to pass after the pestilence,
