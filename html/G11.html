Genesis Chapter 11

1 and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) ALL (כָל / khol) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) LIP (שָׂפָה / sa'phah) [83] UNIT (אֶחָת / e'hhat) and~WORD~s (וּדְבָרִים / ud'va'rim) UNIT~s (אֲחָדִים / a'hha'dim)

and all of the land existed as one lip and a unit of words,

2 and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) in~>~JOURNEY(Verb)~them(m) (בְּנָסְעָם / bê'nas'am) from~EAST (מִקֶּדֶם / mi'qe'dem) and~they(m)~will~FIND(Verb) (וַיִּמְצְאוּ / wai'yim'tse'u) LEVEL.VALLEY (בִקְעָה / viq'ah) in~LAND (בְּאֶרֶץ / bê'e'rets) Shinar (שִׁנְעָר / shin'ar) and~they(m)~will~SETTLE(Verb) (וַיֵּשְׁבוּ / wai'yeysh'vu) THERE (שָׁם / sham)

and it came to pass in their journey from the east, then they found a level valley in the land of Shinar, and they settled there,

3 and~they(m)~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמְרוּ / wai'yom'ru) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) TO (אֶל / el) COMPANION~him (רֵעֵהוּ / rey'ey'hu) !(ms)~PROVIDE(Verb)~& (הָבָה / ha'vah) we~will~MAKE.BRICKS(Verb) (נִלְבְּנָה / nil'be'nah) BRICK~s (לְבֵנִים / lê'vey'nim) and~we~will~CREMATE(Verb)~& (וְנִשְׂרְפָה / wê'nis're'phah) to~CREMATING (לִשְׂרֵפָה / lis'rey'phah) and~she~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַתְּהִי / wat'hi) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) the~BRICK (הַלְּבֵנָה / hal'vey'nah) to~STONE (לְאָבֶן / lê'a'ven) and~the~SLIME (וְהַחֵמָר / wê'ha'hhey'mar) he~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיָה / hai'yah) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) to~MORTAR (לַחֹמֶר / la'hho'mer)

and they said each to his companion, come, we will make bricks and we will cremate them, and the bricks existed to them for stone, and the slime existed for them for mortar,

4 and~they(m)~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמְרוּ / wai'yom'ru) !(ms)~PROVIDE(Verb)~& (הָבָה / ha'vah) we~will~BUILD(Verb) (נִבְנֶה / niv'neh) to~~us (לָּנוּ / la'nu) CITY (עִיר / ir) and~TOWER (וּמִגְדָּל / u'mig'dal) and~HEAD~him (וְרֹאשׁוֹ / wê'ro'sho) in~the~SKY~s2 (בַשָּׁמַיִם / va'sha'ma'yim) and~we~will~DO(Verb) (וְנַעֲשֶׂה / wê'na'a'seh) to~~us (לָּנוּ / la'nu) TITLE (שֵׁם / sheym) OTHERWISE (פֶּן / pen) we~will~SCATTER.ABROAD(Verb) (נָפוּץ / na'phuts) UPON (עַל / al) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) ALL (כָל / khol) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets)

and they said, come, we will build for us a city and a tower, and his head will be in the skies, and we will make for us a title, otherwise we will scatter abroad upon the face of all the land,

5 and~he~will~GO.DOWN(Verb) (וַיֵּרֶד / wai'yey'red) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) to~>~SEE(Verb) (לִרְאֹת / lir'ot) AT (אֶת / et) the~CITY (הָעִיר / ha'ir) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~TOWER (הַמִּגְדָּל / ha'mig'dal) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they~did~BUILD(Verb) (בָּנוּ / ba'nu) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) the~HUMAN (הָאָדָם / ha'a'dam)

and YHWH went down to see the city and the tower which the sons of the human built,

6 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) THOUGH (הֵן / heyn) PEOPLE (עַם / am) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) and~LIP (וְשָׂפָה / wê'sa'phah) [84] UNIT (אַחַת / a'hhat) to~ALL~them(m) (לְכֻלָּם / lê'khu'lam) and~THIS (וְזֶה / wê'zeh) >~make~DRILL(Verb)~them(m) (הַחִלָּם / ha'hhi'lam) to~>~DO(Verb) (לַעֲשׂוֹת / la'a'sot) and~NOW (וְעַתָּה / wê'a'tah) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~be~FENCE.IN(Verb) (יִבָּצֵר / yi'ba'tseyr) [85] from~~them(m) (מֵהֶם / mey'hem) ALL (כֹּל / kol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they(m)~will~PLOT(Verb) (יָזְמוּ / yaz'mu) to~>~DO(Verb) (לַעֲשׂוֹת / la'a'sot)

and YHWH said, though the people are a unit and to all of them is one lip, and this is what they will begin to do, and now not a thing will be fenced in from them, all which they will plot to do.

7 !(ms)~PROVIDE(Verb)~& (הָבָה / ha'vah) we~will~GO.DOWN(Verb)~& (נֵרְדָה / neyr'dah) and~we~will~MIX(Verb)~& (וְנָבְלָה / wê'nav'lah) THERE (שָׁם / sham) LIP~them(m) (שְׂפָתָם / sê'pha'tam) [86] WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) NOT (לֹא / lo) they(m)~will~HEAR(Verb) (יִשְׁמְעוּ / yish'me'u) [87] MAN (אִישׁ / ish) LIP (שְׂפַת / sê'phat) [88] COMPANION~him (רֵעֵהוּ / rey'ey'hu)

Come, we will go down and we will mix their lip there, that each will not hear the lip of his companion,

8 and~he~will~make~SCATTER.ABROAD(Verb) (וַיָּפֶץ / wai'ya'phets) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) from~THERE (מִשָּׁם / mi'sham) UPON (עַל / al) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) ALL (כָל / khol) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) and~they(m)~will~TERMINATE(Verb) (וַיַּחְדְּלוּ / wai'yahh'de'lu) to~>~BUILD(Verb) (לִבְנֹת / liv'not) the~CITY (הָעִיר / ha'ir)

and YHWH scattered them abroad from there upon the face of all the land, and they terminated to build the city.

9 UPON (עַל / al) SO (כֵּן / keyn) he~did~CALL.OUT(Verb) (קָרָא / qa'ra) TITLE~her (שְׁמָהּ / shê'mah) Bavel (בָּבֶל / ba'vel) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) THERE (שָׁם / sham) he~did~MIX(Verb) (בָּלַל / ba'lal) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) LIP (שְׂפַת / sê'phat) [89] ALL (כָּל / kol) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) and~from~THERE (וּמִשָּׁם / u'mi'sham) he~did~make~SCATTER.ABROAD(Verb)~them(m) (הֱפִיצָם / he'phi'tsam) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) UPON (עַל / al) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets)

Therefore he called out her title Bavel, given that there YHWH mixed the lip of all the land, and from there YHWH scatter them abroad upon the face of all the land.

10 THESE (אֵלֶּה / ey'leh) BIRTHING~s (תּוֹלְדֹת / tol'dot) Shem (שֵׁם / sheym) Shem (שֵׁם / sheym) SON (בֶּן / ben) [90] HUNDRED (מְאַת / mê'at) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) AT (אֶת / et) Arpakhshad (אַרְפַּכְשָׁד / ar'pakh'shad) YEAR~s2 (שְׁנָתַיִם / shê'na'ta'yim) AFTER (אַחַר / a'hhar) the~FLOOD (הַמַּבּוּל / ha'ma'bul)

These are the birthings of Shem, Shem was a son of a hundred years, and he caused to bring forth Arpakhshad two years after the flood,

11 and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וַיְחִי / wa'ye'hhi) Shem (שֵׁם / sheym) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) >~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb)~him (הוֹלִידוֹ / ho'li'do) AT (אֶת / et) Arpakhshad (אַרְפַּכְשָׁד / ar'pakh'shad) FIVE (חֲמֵשׁ / hha'meysh) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) SON~s (בָּנִים / ba'nim) and~DAUGHTER~s (וּבָנוֹת / u'va'not)

and Shem lived five hundred years after his causing to bring forth Arpakhshad, and he caused to bring forth sons and daughters,

12 and~Arpakhshad (וְאַרְפַּכְשַׁד / wê'ar'pakh'shad) he~did~LIVE(Verb) (חַי / hhai) FIVE (חָמֵשׁ / hha'meysh) and~THREE~s (וּשְׁלֹשִׁים / ush'lo'shim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) AT (אֶת / et) he~did~SEND(Verb) (שָׁלַח / sha'lahh)

and Arpakhshad had lived five and thirty years and he caused to bring forth Shelahh,

13 and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וַיְחִי / wa'ye'hhi) Arpakhshad (אַרְפַּכְשַׁד / ar'pakh'shad) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) >~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb)~him (הוֹלִידוֹ / ho'li'do) AT (אֶת / et) Shelahh (שֶׁלַח / she'lahh) THREE (שָׁלֹשׁ / sha'losh) YEAR~s (שָׁנִים / sha'nim) and~FOUR (וְאַרְבַּע / wê'ar'ba) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) SON~s (בָּנִים / ba'nim) and~DAUGHTER~s (וּבָנוֹת / u'va'not)

and Arpakhshad lived three and four hundred years after his causing to bring forth Shelahh, and he caused to bring forth sons and daughters,

14 and~Shelahh (וְשֶׁלַח / wê'she'lahh) he~did~LIVE(Verb) (חַי / hhai) THREE~s (שְׁלֹשִׁים / shê'lo'shim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) AT (אֶת / et) Ever (עֵבֶר / ey'ver)

and Shelahh lived thirty years and he caused to bring forth Ever,

15 and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וַיְחִי / wa'ye'hhi) Shelahh (שֶׁלַח / she'lahh) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) >~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb)~him (הוֹלִידוֹ / ho'li'do) AT (אֶת / et) Ever (עֵבֶר / ey'ver) THREE (שָׁלֹשׁ / sha'losh) YEAR~s (שָׁנִים / sha'nim) and~FOUR (וְאַרְבַּע / wê'ar'ba) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) SON~s (בָּנִים / ba'nim) and~DAUGHTER~s (וּבָנוֹת / u'va'not)

and Shelahh lived three and four hundred years after his causing to bring forth Ever, and he caused to bring forth sons and daughters,

16 and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וַיְחִי / wa'ye'hhi) Ever (עֵבֶר / ey'ver) FOUR (אַרְבַּע / ar'ba) and~THREE~s (וּשְׁלֹשִׁים / ush'lo'shim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) AT (אֶת / et) Peleg (פָּלֶג / pa'leg)

and Ever lived four and thirty years, and he caused to bring forth Peleg,

17 and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וַיְחִי / wa'ye'hhi) Ever (עֵבֶר / ey'ver) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) >~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb)~him (הוֹלִידוֹ / ho'li'do) AT (אֶת / et) Peleg (פֶּלֶג / pe'leg) THREE~s (שְׁלֹשִׁים / shê'lo'shim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~FOUR (וְאַרְבַּע / wê'ar'ba) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) SON~s (בָּנִים / ba'nim) and~DAUGHTER~s (וּבָנוֹת / u'va'not)

and Ever lived thirty and four hundred years after his causing to bring forth Peleg, and he caused to bring forth sons and daughters,

18 and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וַיְחִי / wa'ye'hhi) Peleg (פֶלֶג / phe'leg) THREE~s (שְׁלֹשִׁים / shê'lo'shim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) AT (אֶת / et) !(mp)~FEED(Verb) (רְעוּ / rê'u)

and Peleg lived thirty years and he caused to bring forth Re'u,

19 and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וַיְחִי / wa'ye'hhi) Peleg (פֶלֶג / phe'leg) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) >~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb)~him (הוֹלִידוֹ / ho'li'do) AT (אֶת / et) !(mp)~FEED(Verb) (רְעוּ / rê'u) NINE (תֵּשַׁע / tey'sha) YEAR~s (שָׁנִים / sha'nim) and~HUNDRED~s2 (וּמָאתַיִם / u'ma'ta'yim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) SON~s (בָּנִים / ba'nim) and~DAUGHTER~s (וּבָנוֹת / u'va'not)

and Peleg lived nine and two hundred years after his causing to bring forth Re'u, and he caused to bring forth sons and daughters,

20 and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וַיְחִי / wa'ye'hhi) !(mp)~FEED(Verb) (רְעוּ / rê'u) TWO (שְׁתַּיִם / shê'ta'yim) and~THREE~s (וּשְׁלֹשִׁים / ush'lo'shim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) AT (אֶת / et) Serug (שְׂרוּג / sê'rug)

and Re'u lived two and thirty years and he caused to bring forth Serug,

21 and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וַיְחִי / wa'ye'hhi) !(mp)~FEED(Verb) (רְעוּ / rê'u) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) >~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb)~him (הוֹלִידוֹ / ho'li'do) AT (אֶת / et) Serug (שְׂרוּג / sê'rug) SEVEN (שֶׁבַע / she'va) YEAR~s (שָׁנִים / sha'nim) and~HUNDRED~s2 (וּמָאתַיִם / u'ma'ta'yim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) SON~s (בָּנִים / ba'nim) and~DAUGHTER~s (וּבָנוֹת / u'va'not)

and Re'u lived seven and two hundred years after his causing to bring forth Serug, and he caused to bring forth sons and daughters,

22 and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וַיְחִי / wa'ye'hhi) Serug (שְׂרוּג / sê'rug) THREE~s (שְׁלֹשִׁים / shê'lo'shim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) AT (אֶת / et) Nahhor (נָחוֹר / na'hhor)

and Serug lived thirty years and he caused to bring forth Nahhor,

23 and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וַיְחִי / wa'ye'hhi) Serug (שְׂרוּג / sê'rug) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) >~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb)~him (הוֹלִידוֹ / ho'li'do) AT (אֶת / et) Nahhor (נָחוֹר / na'hhor) HUNDRED~s2 (מָאתַיִם / ma'ta'yim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) SON~s (בָּנִים / ba'nim) and~DAUGHTER~s (וּבָנוֹת / u'va'not)

and Serug lived two hundred years after his causing to bring forth Nahhor, and he caused to bring forth sons and daughters,

24 and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וַיְחִי / wa'ye'hhi) Nahhor (נָחוֹר / na'hhor) NINE (תֵּשַׁע / tey'sha) and~TEN~s (וְעֶשְׂרִים / wê'es'rim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) AT (אֶת / et) Terahh (תָּרַח / ta'rahh)

and Nahhor lived nine and twenty years and he caused to bring forth Terahh,

25 and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וַיְחִי / wa'ye'hhi) Nahhor (נָחוֹר / na'hhor) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) >~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb)~him (הוֹלִידוֹ / ho'li'do) AT (אֶת / et) Terahh (תֶּרַח / te'rahh) NINE (תְּשַׁע / tê'sha) TEN (עֶשְׂרֵה / es'reyh) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~HUNDRED (וּמְאַת / um'at) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) SON~s (בָּנִים / ba'nim) and~DAUGHTER~s (וּבָנוֹת / u'va'not)

and Nahhor lived nineteen and a hundred years after his causing to bring forth Terahh, and he caused to bring forth sons and daughters,

and Terahh lived seventy years and he caused to bring forth Avram, Nahhor and Haran,
