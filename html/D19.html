Deuteronomy Chapter 19

1 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~make~CUT(Verb) (יַכְרִית / yakh'rit) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) AT (אֶת / et) the~NATION~s (הַגּוֹיִם / ha'go'yim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) GIVE(Verb)~ing(ms) (נֹתֵן / no'teyn) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) AT (אֶת / et) LAND~them(m) (אַרְצָם / ar'tsam) and~you(ms)~did~POSSESS(Verb)~them(m) (וִירִשְׁתָּם / wi'rish'tam) and~you(ms)~did~SETTLE(Verb) (וְיָשַׁבְתָּ / wê'ya'shav'ta) in~CITY~s~them(m) (בְעָרֵיהֶם / vê'a'rey'hem) and~in~HOUSE~s~them(m) (וּבְבָתֵּיהֶם / uv'va'tey'hem)

Given that YHWH your Elohiym will cut the nations, their land which YHWH your Elohiym is giving to you, and you will possess them, and you will settle in their cities and in their houses.

2 THREE (שָׁלוֹשׁ / sha'losh) CITY~s (עָרִים / a'rim) you(ms)~will~make~SEPARATE(Verb) (תַּבְדִּיל / tav'dil) to~~you(fs) (לָךְ / lakh) in~MIDST (בְּתוֹךְ / bê'tokh) LAND~you(ms) (אַרְצְךָ / ar'tse'kha) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) GIVE(Verb)~ing(ms) (נֹתֵן / no'teyn) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) to~>~POSSESS(Verb)~her (לְרִשְׁתָּהּ / lê'rish'tah)

Three cities you will separate for you in the midst of your land which YHWH your Elohiym is giving to you to possess.

3 you(ms)~will~make~PREPARE(Verb) (תָּכִין / ta'khin) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) the~ROAD (הַדֶּרֶךְ / ha'de'rekh) and~you(ms)~did~much~BE.THREEFOLD(Verb) (וְשִׁלַּשְׁתָּ / wê'shi'lash'ta) [1007] AT (אֶת / et) BORDER (גְּבוּל / gê'vul) LAND~you(ms) (אַרְצְךָ / ar'tse'kha) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~make~INHERIT(Verb)~you(ms) (יַנְחִילְךָ / yan'hhil'kha) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) to~>~FLEE(Verb) (לָנוּס / la'nus) THERE~unto (שָׁמָּה / sha'mah) ALL (כָּל / kol) MURDER(Verb)~ing(ms) (רֹצֵחַ / ro'tsey'ahh)

You will prepare for you the road, and you will make the border of your land which YHWH your Elohiym will make you inherit threefold, and he will exist for fleeing unto there every murderer,

4 and~THIS (וְזֶה / wê'zeh) WORD (דְּבַר / dê'var) the~MURDER(Verb)~ing(ms) (הָרֹצֵחַ / ha'ro'tsey'ahh) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~FLEE(Verb) (יָנוּס / ya'nus) THERE~unto (שָׁמָּה / sha'mah) and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וָחָי / wa'hhai) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~make~HIT(Verb) (יַכֶּה / ya'keh) AT (אֶת / et) COMPANION~him (רֵעֵהוּ / rey'ey'hu) in~UNAWARE (בִּבְלִי / biv'li) DISCERNMENT (דַעַת / da'at) [1008] and~HE (וְהוּא / wê'hu) NOT (לֹא / lo) HATE(Verb)~ing(ms) (שֹׂנֵא / so'ney) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) from~YESTERDAY (מִתְּמֹל / mit'mol) THREE.DAYS.AGO (שִׁלְשֹׁם / shil'shom) [1009]

and this is the word of the murderer which will flee unto there that he will live, one who will attack his companion with unaware discernment, and he is not hating him previously,

5 and~WHICH (וַאֲשֶׁר / wa'a'sher) he~will~COME(Verb) (יָבֹא / ya'vo) AT (אֶת / et) COMPANION~him (רֵעֵהוּ / rey'ey'hu) in~the~FOREST (בַיַּעַר / vai'ya'ar) to~>~CARVE(Verb) (לַחְטֹב / lahh'tov) TREE~s (עֵצִים / ey'tsim) and~she~did~be~DRIVE.OUT(Verb) (וְנִדְּחָה / wê'nid'hhah) HAND~him (יָדוֹ / ya'do) in~the~AX (בַגַּרְזֶן / va'gar'zen) to~>~CUT(Verb) (לִכְרֹת / likh'rot) the~TREE (הָעֵץ / ha'eyts) and~he~did~CAST.OFF(Verb) (וְנָשַׁל / wê'na'shal) the~IRON (הַבַּרְזֶל / ha'bar'zel) FROM (מִן / min) the~TREE (הָעֵץ / ha'eyts) [1010] and~he~did~FIND(Verb) (וּמָצָא / u'ma'tsa) [1011] AT (אֶת / et) COMPANION~him (רֵעֵהוּ / rey'ey'hu) and~he~did~DIE(Verb) (וָמֵת / wa'meyt) HE (הוּא / hu) he~will~FLEE(Verb) (יָנוּס / ya'nus) TO (אֶל / el) UNIT (אַחַת / a'hhat) the~CITY~s (הֶעָרִים / he'a'rim) the~THESE (הָאֵלֶּה / ha'ey'leh) and~he~will~LIVE(Verb) (וָחָי / wa'hhai)

and when he will come with his companion in the forest to carve trees, and his hand will be driven out with the ax to cut the tree, and the iron is cast off from the wood, and he will find his companion, and he died, he will flee to one of these cities and he will live.

6 OTHERWISE (פֶּן / pen) he~will~PURSUE(Verb) (יִרְדֹּף / yir'doph) REDEEM(Verb)~ing(ms) (גֹּאֵל / go'eyl) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) the~MURDER(Verb)~ing(ms) (הָרֹצֵחַ / ha'ro'tsey'ahh) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~HEAT(Verb) (יֵחַם / yey'hham) HEART~him (לְבָבוֹ / lê'va'vo) and~he~did~make~OVERTAKE(Verb)~him (וְהִשִּׂיגוֹ / wê'hi'si'go) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~INCREASE(Verb) (יִרְבֶּה / yir'beh) the~ROAD (הַדֶּרֶךְ / ha'de'rekh) and~he~did~make~HIT(Verb)~him (וְהִכָּהוּ / wê'hi'ka'u) SOUL (נָפֶשׁ / na'phesh) and~to~~him (וְלוֹ / wê'lo) WITHOUT (אֵין / eyn) DECISION (מִשְׁפַּט / mish'pat) DEATH (מָוֶת / ma'wet) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) NOT (לֹא / lo) HATE(Verb)~ing(ms) (שֹׂנֵא / so'ney) HE (הוּא / hu) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) from~YESTERDAY (מִתְּמוֹל / mi'te'mol) THREE.DAYS.AGO (שִׁלְשׁוֹם / shil'shom) [1012]

Otherwise the redeemer of blood will pursue after the murderer, given that his heart is heated, and he will overtake him, given that the road is long, and he will attack him, the soul, and to him is without a decision of death, given that he was not hating him previously.

7 UPON (עַל / al) SO (כֵּן / keyn) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) much~DIRECT(Verb)~ing(ms)~you(ms) (מְצַוְּךָ / mê'tsa'we'kha) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) THREE (שָׁלֹשׁ / sha'losh) CITY~s (עָרִים / a'rim) you(ms)~will~make~SEPARATE(Verb) (תַּבְדִּיל / tav'dil) to~~you(fs) (לָךְ / lakh)

Therefore I am directing you to say, three cities you will separate for you,

8 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) he~will~make~WIDEN(Verb) (יַרְחִיב / yar'hhiv) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) AT (אֶת / et) BORDER~you(ms) (גְּבֻלְךָ / gê'vul'kha) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~be~SWEAR(Verb) (נִשְׁבַּע / nish'ba) to~FATHER~s~you(ms) (לַאֲבֹתֶיךָ / la'a'vo'tey'kha) and~he~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַן / wê'na'tan) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דִּבֶּר / di'ber) to~>~GIVE(Verb) (לָתֵת / la'teyt) to~FATHER~s~you(ms) (לַאֲבֹתֶיךָ / la'a'vo'tey'kha)

and if YHWH your Elohiym will widen your border just as he swore to your fathers, and to give to you all the land which he spoke to give to your fathers.

9 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(ms)~will~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (תִשְׁמֹר / tish'mor) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~DIRECTIVE (הַמִּצְוָה / ha'mits'wah) the~THIS (הַזֹּאת / ha'zot) to~>~DO(Verb)~her (לַעֲשֹׂתָהּ / la'a'so'tah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) much~DIRECT(Verb)~ing(ms)~you(ms) (מְצַוְּךָ / mê'tsa'we'kha) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) to~>~LOVE(Verb) (לְאַהֲבָה / lê'a'ha'vah) AT (אֶת / et) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) and~to~>~WALK(Verb) (וְלָלֶכֶת / wê'la'le'khet) in~ROAD~s~him (בִּדְרָכָיו / bid'ra'khaw) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~DAY~s (הַיָּמִים / hai'ya'mim) and~you(ms)~did~ADD(Verb) (וְיָסַפְתָּ / wê'ya'saph'ta) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) YET.AGAIN (עוֹד / od) THREE (שָׁלֹשׁ / sha'losh) CITY~s (עָרִים / a'rim) UPON (עַל / al) the~THREE (הַשָּׁלֹשׁ / ha'sha'losh) the~THESE (הָאֵלֶּה / ha'ey'leh)

Given that you will safeguard all these directives to do her, which I am directing you today, to love YHWH your Elohiym, and to walk in his road all the days, and you will add to you again three cities upon these three,

10 and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~will~be~POUR.OUT(Verb) (יִשָּׁפֵךְ / yi'sha'pheykh) BLOOD (דָּם / dam) INNOCENT (נָקִי / na'qi) in~INSIDE (בְּקֶרֶב / bê'qe'rev) LAND~you(ms) (אַרְצְךָ / ar'tse'kha) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) GIVE(Verb)~ing(ms) (נֹתֵן / no'teyn) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) INHERITANCE (נַחֲלָה / na'hha'lah) and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) UPON~you(ms) (עָלֶיךָ / a'ley'kha) BLOOD~s (דָּמִים / da'mim)

and the blood of innocent will not be poured out inside your land which YHWH your Elohiym is giving to you, an inheritance, and bloodshed will exist upon you,

11 and~GIVEN.THAT (וְכִי / wê'khi) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) HATE(Verb)~ing(ms) (שֹׂנֵא / so'ney) to~COMPANION~him (לְרֵעֵהוּ / lê'rey'ey'hu) and~he~did~AMBUSH(Verb) (וְאָרַב / wê'a'rav) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) and~he~did~RISE(Verb) (וְקָם / wê'qam) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) and~he~did~make~HIT(Verb)~him (וְהִכָּהוּ / wê'hi'ka'u) SOUL (נֶפֶשׁ / ne'phesh) and~he~did~DIE(Verb) (וָמֵת / wa'meyt) and~he~did~FLEE(Verb) (וְנָס / wê'nas) TO (אֶל / el) UNIT (אַחַת / a'hhat) the~CITY~s (הֶעָרִים / he'a'rim) the~THESE (הָאֵל / ha'eyl)

and given that a man will exist hating his companion, and he will ambush him, and he will rise upon him, and he will attack him, a soul, and he will die, then he will flee to one of these cities,

12 and~they~did~SEND(Verb) (וְשָׁלְחוּ / wê'shal'hhu) BEARD~s (זִקְנֵי / ziq'ney) CITY~him (עִירוֹ / i'ro) and~they~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקְחוּ / wê'laq'hhu) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) from~THERE (מִשָּׁם / mi'sham) and~they~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתְנוּ / wê'nat'nu) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) in~HAND (בְּיַד / bê'yad) REDEEM(Verb)~ing(ms) (גֹּאֵל / go'eyl) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam) and~he~did~DIE(Verb) (וָמֵת / wa'meyt)

and the bearded ones of his city will send, and they will take him from there, and they will give him in the hand of the redeemer of blood, and he will die.

13 NOT (לֹא / lo) she~will~SPARE(Verb) (תָחוֹס / ta'hhos) EYE~you(ms) (עֵינְךָ / eyn'kha) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) and~you(ms)~did~much~BURN(Verb) (וּבִעַרְתָּ / u'vi'ar'ta) BLOOD (דַם / dam) the~INNOCENT (הַנָּקִי / ha'na'qi) from~Yisra'eyl (מִיִּשְׂרָאֵל / mi'yis'ra'eyl) and~he~did~DO.WELL(Verb) (וְטוֹב / wê'tov) to~~you(fs) (לָךְ / lakh)

Your eye will not spare him, and you will burn the blood of the innocent from Yisra'eyl, and he will go well for you.

14 NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~make~OVERTAKE(Verb) (תַסִּיג / ta'sig) BORDER (גְּבוּל / gê'vul) COMPANION~you(ms) (רֵעֲךָ / rey'a'kha) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they~did~BOUND(Verb) (גָּבְלוּ / gav'lu) FIRST~s (רִאשֹׁנִים / ri'sho'nim) in~INHERITANCE~you(ms) (בְּנַחֲלָתְךָ / bê'na'hha'la'te'kha) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~will~INHERIT(Verb) (תִּנְחַל / tin'hhal) in~the~LAND (בָּאָרֶץ / ba'a'rets) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) GIVE(Verb)~ing(ms) (נֹתֵן / no'teyn) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) to~>~POSSESS(Verb)~her (לְרִשְׁתָּהּ / lê'rish'tah)

You will not overtake the border of your companion which they bound first in your inheritance, which you will inherit in the land which YHWH your Elohiym is giving to you to possess.

15 NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~RISE(Verb) (יָקוּם / ya'qum) WITNESS (עֵד / eyd) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) in~MAN (בְּאִישׁ / bê'ish) to~ALL (לְכָל / lê'khol) TWISTEDNESS (עָו‍ֹן / a'won) and~to~ALL (וּלְכָל / ul'khol) FAILURE (חַטָּאת / hha'tat) in~ALL (בְּכָל / bê'khol) FAILURE (חֵטְא / hheyt) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~FAIL(Verb) (יֶחֱטָא / ye'hhe'ta) UPON (עַל / al) MOUTH (פִּי / pi) TWO (שְׁנֵי / shê'ney) WITNESS~s (עֵדִים / ey'dim) OR (אוֹ / o) UPON (עַל / al) MOUTH (פִּי / pi) THREE (שְׁלֹשָׁה / shê'lo'shah) WITNESS~s (עֵדִים / ey'dim) he~will~RISE(Verb) (יָקוּם / ya'qum) WORD (דָּבָר / da'var)

A witness of one will not rise with a man for every twistedness and for every failure which he failed, according to the mouth of two witnesses or according to the mouth of three witnesses a word will rise.

16 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~RISE(Verb) (יָקוּם / ya'qum) WITNESS (עֵד / eyd) VIOLENCE (חָמָס / hha'mas) in~MAN (בְּאִישׁ / bê'ish) to~>~ANSWER(Verb) (לַעֲנוֹת / la'a'not) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo) TURNING.ASIDE (סָרָה / sa'rah)

If a witness of violence will rise with a man to answer in him a turning aside,

17 and~they~did~STAND(Verb) (וְעָמְדוּ / wê'am'du) TWO (שְׁנֵי / shê'ney) the~MAN~s (הָאֲנָשִׁים / ha'a'na'shim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) the~DISPUTE (הָרִיב / ha'riv) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) the~ADMINISTRATOR~s (הַכֹּהֲנִים / ha'ko'ha'nim) and~the~DECIDE(Verb)~ing(mp) (וְהַשֹּׁפְטִים / wê'ha'shoph'tim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they(m)~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיוּ / yih'yu) in~the~DAY~s (בַּיָּמִים / bai'ya'mim) the~THEY(m) (הָהֵם / ha'heym)

and the two men, which belong to them a dispute, will stand to the faces of the administrators and the deciders which exist in their days,
