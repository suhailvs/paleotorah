Exodus Chapter 29

1 and~THIS (וְזֶה / wê'zeh) the~WORD (הַדָּבָר / ha'da'var) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) to~>~much~SET.APART(Verb) (לְקַדֵּשׁ / lê'qa'deysh) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) to~>~much~ADORN(Verb) (לְכַהֵן / lê'kha'heyn) to~~me (לִי / li) !(ms)~TAKE(Verb) (לְקַח / lê'qahh) BULL (פַּר / par) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) SON (בֶּן / ben) CATTLE (בָּקָר / ba'qar) and~BUCK~s (וְאֵילִם / wê'ey'lim) TWO (שְׁנַיִם / shê'na'yim) WHOLE~s (תְּמִימִם / tê'mi'mim)

and this is the matter which you will do for them to set them apart to be adorned for me, take one bull, a son of the cattle, and two whole bucks,

2 and~BREAD (וְלֶחֶם / wê'le'hhem) UNLEAVENED.BREAD~s (מַצּוֹת / ma'tsot) and~PIERCED.BREAD~s (וְחַלֹּת / wê'hha'lot) UNLEAVENED.BREAD~s (מַצֹּת / ma'tsot) MIX(Verb)~ed(fp) (בְּלוּלֹת / bê'lu'lot) in~the~OIL (בַּשֶּׁמֶן / ba'she'men) and~THIN.BREAD~s (וּרְקִיקֵי / ur'qi'qey) UNLEAVENED.BREAD~s (מַצּוֹת / ma'tsot) SMEAR(Verb)~ed(mp) (מְשֻׁחִים / mê'shu'hhim) in~the~OIL (בַּשָּׁמֶן / ba'sha'men) FLOUR (סֹלֶת / so'let) WHEAT~s (חִטִּים / hhi'tim) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam)

and unleavened bread and unleavened pierced bread mixed in the oil, and unleavened thin bread smeared in the oil, from the flour of wheat you will make them,

3 and~you(ms)~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַתָּ / wê'na'ta'ta) AT~them(m) (אוֹתָם / o'tam) UPON (עַל / al) WICKER.BASKET (סַל / sal) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) and~you(ms)~did~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וְהִקְרַבְתָּ / wê'hiq'rav'ta) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) in~the~WICKER.BASKET (בַּסָּל / ba'sal) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) TWO (שְׁנֵי / shê'ney) the~BUCK~s (הָאֵילִם / ha'ey'lim)

and you will place them upon one basket, and you will make them come near with the basket and the bull and the two bucks,

4 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) SON~s~him (בָּנָיו / ba'naw) you(ms)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תַּקְרִיב / taq'riv) TO (אֶל / el) OPENING (פֶּתַח / pe'tahh) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) and~you(ms)~did~BATHE(Verb) (וְרָחַצְתָּ / wê'ra'hhats'ta) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) in~the~WATER~s2 (בַּמָּיִם / ba'ma'yim)

and you will make Aharon and his sons come near the opening of the appointed tent, and you will bathe them in the waters,

5 and~you(ms)~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקַחְתָּ / wê'la'qahh'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~GARMENT~s (הַבְּגָדִים / ha'be'ga'dim) and~you(ms)~did~make~WEAR(Verb) (וְהִלְבַּשְׁתָּ / wê'hil'bash'ta) AT (אֶת / et) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) AT (אֶת / et) the~TUNIC (הַכֻּתֹּנֶת / ha'ku'to'net) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) CLOAK (מְעִיל / mê'il) the~EPHOD (הָאֵפֹד / ha'ey'phod) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~EPHOD (הָאֵפֹד / ha'ey'phod) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~BREASTPLATE (הַחֹשֶׁן / ha'hho'shen) and~you(ms)~did~GIRD(Verb) (וְאָפַדְתָּ / wê'a'phad'ta) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) in~DECORATIVE.BAND (בְּחֵשֶׁב / bê'hhey'shev) the~EPHOD (הָאֵפֹד / ha'ey'phod)

and you will take the garments, and you will clothe Aharon with the tunic and with the cloak of the ephod and with the ephod and with the breastplate, and you will gird him with the decorative band of the ephod,

6 and~you(ms)~did~PLACE(Verb) (וְשַׂמְתָּ / wê'sam'ta) the~TURBAN (הַמִּצְנֶפֶת / ha'mits'ne'phet) UPON (עַל / al) HEAD~him (רֹאשׁוֹ / ro'sho) and~you(ms)~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַתָּ / wê'na'ta'ta) AT (אֶת / et) DEDICATION (נֵזֶר / ney'zer) [489] the~SPECIAL (הַקֹּדֶשׁ / ha'qo'desh) UPON (עַל / al) the~TURBAN (הַמִּצְנָפֶת / ha'mits'na'phet)

and you will place the turban upon his head, and you will place the special thing of dedication upon the turban,

7 and~you(ms)~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקַחְתָּ / wê'la'qahh'ta) AT (אֶת / et) OIL (שֶׁמֶן / she'men) the~OINTMENT (הַמִּשְׁחָה / ha'mish'hhah) and~you(ms)~did~POUR.DOWN(Verb) (וְיָצַקְתָּ / wê'ya'tsaq'ta) UPON (עַל / al) HEAD~him (רֹאשׁוֹ / ro'sho) and~you(ms)~did~SMEAR(Verb) (וּמָשַׁחְתָּ / u'ma'shahh'ta) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to)

and you will take the oil of ointment, and you will pour it down upon his head, and you will smear him,

8 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) SON~s~him (בָּנָיו / ba'naw) you(ms)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תַּקְרִיב / taq'riv) and~you(ms)~did~make~WEAR(Verb)~them(m) (וְהִלְבַּשְׁתָּם / wê'hil'bash'tam) TUNIC~s (כֻּתֳּנֹת / ku'ta'not)

and you will make his sons come near, and you will clothe them with tunics,

9 and~you(ms)~did~GIRD.UP(Verb) (וְחָגַרְתָּ / wê'hha'gar'ta) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) SASH (אַבְנֵט / av'neyt) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) and~SON~s~him (וּבָנָיו / u'va'naw) and~you(ms)~did~SADDLE(Verb) (וְחָבַשְׁתָּ / wê'hha'vash'ta) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) HEADDRESS~s (מִגְבָּעֹת / mig'ba'ot) and~she~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיְתָה / wê'hai'tah) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) ADMINISTRATION (כְּהֻנָּה / kê'hu'nah) to~CUSTOM (לְחֻקַּת / lê'hhu'qat) DISTANT (עוֹלָם / o'lam) and~you(ms)~did~much~FILL(Verb) (וּמִלֵּאתָ / u'mi'ley'ta) HAND (יַד / yad) [490] Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) and~HAND (וְיַד / wê'yad) SON~s~him (בָּנָיו / ba'naw)

and you will gird up Aharon and his sons with the sash, and you will saddle them with the headdresses, and the administration will exist for them, a ritual of a distant time, and you will fill the hand of Aharon and the hand of his sons,

10 and~you(ms)~did~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וְהִקְרַבְתָּ / wê'hiq'rav'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) and~he~did~SUPPORT(Verb) (וְסָמַךְ / wê'sa'makh) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) and~SON~s~him (וּבָנָיו / u'va'naw) AT (אֶת / et) HAND~s2~them(m) (יְדֵיהֶם / yê'dey'hem) UPON (עַל / al) HEAD (רֹאשׁ / rosh) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par)

and you will make the bull come near to the face of the appointed tent, and Aharon, and his sons, will support their hands upon the head of the bull,

11 and~you(ms)~did~SLAY(Verb) (וְשָׁחַטְתָּ / wê'sha'hhat'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) OPENING (פֶּתַח / pe'tahh) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd)

and you will slay the bull to the face of YHWH at the opening of the appointed place,

12 and~you(ms)~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקַחְתָּ / wê'la'qahh'ta) from~BLOOD (מִדַּם / mi'dam) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) and~you(ms)~did~GIVE(Verb)~& (וְנָתַתָּה / wê'na'ta'tah) UPON (עַל / al) HORN~s (קַרְנֹת / qar'not) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) in~FINGER~you(ms) (בְּאֶצְבָּעֶךָ / bê'ets'ba'e'kha) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam) you(ms)~will~POUR.OUT(Verb) (תִּשְׁפֹּךְ / tish'pokh) TO (אֶל / el) BOTTOM.BASE (יְסוֹד / yê'sod) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh)

and you will take from the blood of the bull and you will place it upon the horns of the altar with your finger, and you will pour out all the blood at the foundation of the altar,

13 and~you(ms)~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקַחְתָּ / wê'la'qahh'ta) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~FAT (הַחֵלֶב / ha'hhey'lev) the~much~COVER.OVER(Verb)~ing(ms) (הַמְכַסֶּה / ham'kha'seh) AT (אֶת / et) the~INSIDE (הַקֶּרֶב / ha'qe'rev) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) the~LOBE (הַיֹּתֶרֶת / hai'yo'te'ret) UPON (עַל / al) the~HEAVY (הַכָּבֵד / ha'ka'veyd) [491] and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) TWO (שְׁתֵּי / shê'tey) the~KIDNEY~s (הַכְּלָיֹת / hak'la'yot) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~FAT (הַחֵלֶב / ha'hhey'lev) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) UPON~them(f) (עֲלֵיהֶן / a'ley'hen) and~you(ms)~did~make~BURN.INCENSE(Verb) (וְהִקְטַרְתָּ / wê'hiq'tar'ta) [492] the~ALTAR~unto (הַמִּזְבֵּחָה / ha'miz'bey'hhah)

and you will take all the fat covering, the insides, and the lobe upon the heavy one, and the two kidneys, and the fat which is upon them, and you will burn it as incense unto the altar,

14 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) FLESH (בְּשַׂר / bê'sar) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) SKIN~him (עֹרוֹ / o'ro) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) DUNG~him (פִּרְשׁוֹ / pir'sho) you(ms)~will~CREMATE(Verb) (תִּשְׂרֹף / tis'roph) in~the~FIRE (בָּאֵשׁ / ba'eysh) from~OUTSIDE (מִחוּץ / mi'hhuts) to~the~CAMP (לַמַּחֲנֶה / la'ma'hha'neh) FAILURE (חַטָּאת / hha'tat) HE (הוּא / hu)

and you will cremate the flesh of the bull, and his skin, and his dung, in the fire outside the camp, he is a failure,

15 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~BUCK (הָאַיִל / ha'a'yil) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad) you(ms)~will~TAKE(Verb) (תִּקָּח / ti'qahh) and~they~did~SUPPORT(Verb) (וְסָמְכוּ / wê'sam'khu) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) and~SON~s~him (וּבָנָיו / u'va'naw) AT (אֶת / et) HAND~s2~them(m) (יְדֵיהֶם / yê'dey'hem) UPON (עַל / al) HEAD (רֹאשׁ / rosh) the~BUCK (הָאָיִל / ha'a'yil)

and you will take the one buck, and Aharon and his sons will support their hands upon the head of the buck,

16 and~you(ms)~did~SLAY(Verb) (וְשָׁחַטְתָּ / wê'sha'hhat'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~BUCK (הָאָיִל / ha'a'yil) and~you(ms)~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקַחְתָּ / wê'la'qahh'ta) AT (אֶת / et) BLOOD~him (דָּמוֹ / da'mo) and~you(ms)~did~SPRINKLE(Verb) (וְזָרַקְתָּ / wê'za'raq'ta) UPON (עַל / al) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) ALL.AROUND (סָבִיב / sa'viv)

and you will slay the buck, and you will take his blood and you will sprinkle it upon the altar all around,

17 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~BUCK (הָאַיִל / ha'a'yil) you(ms)~will~much~DIVIDE.INTO.PIECES(Verb) (תְּנַתֵּחַ / tê'na'tey'ahh) to~PIECE~s~him (לִנְתָחָיו / lin'ta'hhaw) [493] and~you(ms)~did~BATHE(Verb) (וְרָחַצְתָּ / wê'ra'hhats'ta) INSIDE~him (קִרְבּוֹ / qir'bo) and~LEG~s~him (וּכְרָעָיו / ukh'ra'aw) and~you(ms)~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַתָּ / wê'na'ta'ta) UPON (עַל / al) PIECE~s~him (נְתָחָיו / nê'ta'hhaw) and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) HEAD~him (רֹאשׁוֹ / ro'sho)

and you will divide the buck into pieces according to his pieces, and you will bathe his insides and his legs, and you will place them upon his pieces and upon his head,

18 and~you(ms)~did~make~BURN.INCENSE(Verb) (וְהִקְטַרְתָּ / wê'hiq'tar'ta) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~BUCK (הָאַיִל / ha'a'yil) the~ALTAR~unto (הַמִּזְבֵּחָה / ha'miz'bey'hhah) ASCENSION.OFFERING (עֹלָה / o'lah) HE (הוּא / hu) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) AROMA (רֵיחַ / rey'ahh) SWEET (נִיחוֹחַ / ni'hho'ahh) FIRE.OFFERING (אִשֶּׁה / i'sheh) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) HE (הוּא / hu)

and you will burn as incense all of the buck unto the altar, he is a ascension offering for YHWH, a sweet aroma, he is a fire offering for YHWH,

19 and~you(ms)~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקַחְתָּ / wê'la'qahh'ta) AT (אֵת / eyt) the~BUCK (הָאַיִל / ha'a'yil) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִי / ha'shey'ni) and~he~did~SUPPORT(Verb) (וְסָמַךְ / wê'sa'makh) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) and~SON~s~him (וּבָנָיו / u'va'naw) AT (אֶת / et) HAND~s2~them(m) (יְדֵיהֶם / yê'dey'hem) UPON (עַל / al) HEAD (רֹאשׁ / rosh) the~BUCK (הָאָיִל / ha'a'yil)

and you will take the second buck, and Aharon, and his sons, will support their hands upon the head of the buck,
