Genesis Chapter 6

1 and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~make~DRILL(Verb) (הֵחֵל / hey'hheyl) the~HUMAN (הָאָדָם / ha'a'dam) to~>~INCREASE.IN.NUMBER(Verb) (לָרֹב / la'rov) UPON (עַל / al) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) the~GROUND (הָאֲדָמָה / ha'a'da'mah) and~DAUGHTER~s (וּבָנוֹת / u'va'not) BRING.FORTH(Verb)~ed(mp) (יֻלְּדוּ / yul'du) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem)

and it came to pass that the human began to increase in number upon the face of the ground, and daughters were brought forth for them,

2 and~they(m)~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיִּרְאוּ / wai'yir'u) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) the~Elohiym (הָאֱלֹהִים / ha'e'lo'him) AT (אֶת / et) DAUGHTER~s (בְּנוֹת / bê'not) the~HUMAN (הָאָדָם / ha'a'dam) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) FUNCTIONAL~s (טֹבֹת / to'vot) THEY(f) (הֵנָּה / heyn'nah) and~they(m)~will~TAKE(Verb) (וַיִּקְחוּ / wai'yiq'hhu) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) WOMAN~s (נָשִׁים / na'shim) from~ALL (מִכֹּל / mi'kol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they~did~CHOOSE(Verb) (בָּחָרוּ / ba'hha'ru)

and the sons of the Elohiym saw the daughters of the human, that they were functional, and took for them women from all which they chose,

3 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~MODERATE(Verb) (יָדוֹן / ya'don) [49] WIND~me (רוּחִי / ru'hhi) in~the~HUMAN (בָאָדָם / va'a'dam) to~DISTANT (לְעֹלָם / lê'o'lam) in~which~ALSO (בְּשַׁגַּם / bê'sha'gam) HE (הוּא / hu) FLESH (בָשָׂר / va'sar) and~they~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיוּ / wê'hai'u) DAY~s~him (יָמָיו / ya'maw) HUNDRED (מֵאָה / mey'ah) and~TEN~s (וְעֶשְׂרִים / wê'es'rim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah)

and YHWH said, my wind will not moderate in the human for a distant time, whereas he is flesh and his days will exist a hundred and twenty years.

4 the~Nephilim~s (הַנְּפִלִים / han'phi'lim) they~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיוּ / hai'u) in~the~LAND (בָאָרֶץ / va'a'rets) in~the~DAY~s (בַּיָּמִים / bai'ya'mim) the~THEY(m) (הָהֵם / ha'heym) and~ALSO (וְגַם / wê'gam) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) SO (כֵן / kheyn) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they(m)~will~COME(Verb) (יָבֹאוּ / ya'vo'u) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) the~Elohiym (הָאֱלֹהִים / ha'e'lo'him) TO (אֶל / el) DAUGHTER~s (בְּנוֹת / bê'not) the~HUMAN (הָאָדָם / ha'a'dam) and~they(m)~will~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וְיָלְדוּ / wê'yal'du) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) THEY(m) (הֵמָּה / hey'mah) the~COURAGEOUS~s (הַגִּבֹּרִים / ha'gi'bo'rim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) from~DISTANT (מֵעוֹלָם / mey'o'lam) [50] MAN~s (אַנְשֵׁי / an'shey) the~TITLE (הַשֵּׁם / ha'sheym) [51]

The Nephilim existed in the land in those days and also afterward, when the sons of the Elohiym came to the daughters of the human, and they brought forth for them, they are the courageous ones which are from a distant time, men of the title,

5 and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) ABUNDANT (רַבָּה / ra'bah) DYSFUNCTIONAL (רָעַת / ra'at) the~HUMAN (הָאָדָם / ha'a'dam) in~the~LAND (בָּאָרֶץ / ba'a'rets) and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) THOUGHT (יֵצֶר / yey'tser) INVENTION~s (מַחְשְׁבֹת / mahh'she'vot) HEART~him (לִבּוֹ / li'bo) ONLY (רַק / raq) DYSFUNCTIONAL (רַע / ra) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom)

and YHWH saw that the dysfunctions of the human in the land was abundant, and all the thoughts of inventions of his heart was only dysfunctional every day,

6 and~he~will~be~COMFORT(Verb) (וַיִּנָּחֶם / wai'yi'na'hhem) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) AT (אֶת / et) the~HUMAN (הָאָדָם / ha'a'dam) in~the~LAND (בָּאָרֶץ / ba'a'rets) and~he~will~self~DISTRESS(Verb) (וַיִּתְעַצֵּב / wai'yit'a'tseyv) TO (אֶל / el) HEART~him (לִבּוֹ / li'bo)

and YHWH repented that he made the human in the land, and he was distressed within his heart,

7 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) I~will~WIPE.AWAY(Verb) (אֶמְחֶה / em'hheh) AT (אֶת / et) the~HUMAN (הָאָדָם / ha'a'dam) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I~did~SHAPE(Verb) (בָּרָאתִי / ba'ra'ti) from~UPON (מֵעַל / mey'al) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) the~GROUND (הָאֲדָמָה / ha'a'da'mah) from~HUMAN (מֵאָדָם / mey'a'dam) UNTIL (עַד / ad) BEAST (בְּהֵמָה / bê'hey'mah) UNTIL (עַד / ad) TREADER (רֶמֶשׂ / re'mes) and~UNTIL (וְעַד / wê'ad) FLYER (עוֹף / oph) the~SKY~s2 (הַשָּׁמָיִם / ha'sha'ma'yim) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I~did~be~COMFORT(Verb) (נִחַמְתִּי / ni'hham'ti) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I~did~DO(Verb)~them(m) (עֲשִׂיתִם / a'si'tim)

and YHWH said, I will wipe away the human which I shaped from upon the face of the ground, from the human as well as the beast as well as the treader and as well as the flyer of the skies, given that I repented that I made them,

8 and~No'ahh (וְנֹחַ / wê'no'ahh) he~did~FIND(Verb) (מָצָא / ma'tsa) BEAUTY (חֵן / hheyn) in~EYE~s2 (בְּעֵינֵי / bê'ey'ney) [52] YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and No'ahh found beauty in the eyes of YHWH.

9 THESE (אֵלֶּה / ey'leh) BIRTHING~s (תּוֹלְדֹת / tol'dot) No'ahh (נֹחַ / no'ahh) No'ahh (נֹחַ / no'ahh) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) STEADFAST.ONE (צַדִּיק / tsa'diq) WHOLE (תָּמִים / ta'mim) he~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיָה / hai'yah) in~GENERATION~s~him (בְּדֹרֹתָיו / bê'do'ro'taw) AT (אֶת / et) the~Elohiym (הָאֱלֹהִים / ha'e'lo'him) he~did~self~WALK(Verb) (הִתְהַלֶּךְ / hit'ha'lekh) No'ahh (נֹחַ / no'ahh)

These are the birthings of No'ahh, No'ahh was a steadfast one and mature man in his generations, No'ahh walked himself with the Elohiym,

10 and~he~will~make~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וַיּוֹלֶד / wai'yo'led) No'ahh (נֹחַ / no'ahh) THREE (שְׁלֹשָׁה / shê'lo'shah) SON~s (בָנִים / va'nim) AT (אֶת / et) Shem (שֵׁם / sheym) AT (אֶת / et) Hham (חָם / hham) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) Yaphet (יָפֶת / ya'phet)

and No'ahh caused to bring forth three sons, Shem, Hham and Yaphet,

11 and~she~will~be~DAMAGE(Verb) (וַתִּשָּׁחֵת / wa'ti'sha'hheyt) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) [53] the~Elohiym (הָאֱלֹהִים / ha'e'lo'him) and~she~will~be~FILL(Verb) (וַתִּמָּלֵא / wa'ti'ma'ley) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) VIOLENCE (חָמָס / hha'mas) [54]

and the land was damaged to the face of the Elohiym and the land was filled with violence,

12 and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) AT (אֶת / et) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) she~did~be~DAMAGE(Verb) (נִשְׁחָתָה / nish'hha'tah) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~make~DAMAGE(Verb) (הִשְׁחִית / hish'hhit) ALL (כָּל / kol) FLESH (בָּשָׂר / ba'sar) AT (אֶת / et) ROAD~him (דַּרְכּוֹ / dar'ko) [55] UPON (עַל / al) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets)

and Elohiym saw the land and look, she was damaged given that all the flesh destroyed his road upon the land,

13 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) to~No'ahh (לְנֹחַ / lê'no'ahh) CONCLUSION (קֵץ / qeyts) ALL (כָּל / kol) FLESH (בָּשָׂר / ba'sar) he~did~COME(Verb) (בָּא / ba) to~FACE~s~me (לְפָנַי / lê'pha'nai) [56] GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) she~did~FILL(Verb) (מָלְאָה / mal'ah) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) VIOLENCE (חָמָס / hha'mas) from~FACE~s~them(m) (מִפְּנֵיהֶם / mi'pe'ney'hem) [57] and~LOOK~me (וְהִנְנִי / wê'hin'ni) make~DAMAGE(Verb)~ing(ms)~them(m) (מַשְׁחִיתָם / mash'hhi'tam) AT (אֶת / et) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets)

and Elohiym said to No'ahh, a conclusion of all the flesh has come to my face, given that the land of violence was filled from their face, and look at me, I am destroying them with the land.

14 !(ms)~DO(Verb) (עֲשֵׂה / a'seyh) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) VESSEL (תֵּבַת / tey'vat) TREE~s (עֲצֵי / a'tsey) GOPHER (גֹפֶר / go'pher) NEST~s (קִנִּים / qi'nim) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) AT (אֶת / et) the~VESSEL (הַתֵּבָה / ha'tey'vah) and~you(ms)~did~COVER(Verb) (וְכָפַרְתָּ / wê'kha'phar'ta) AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) from~HOUSE (מִבַּיִת / mi'ba'yit) and~from~OUTSIDE (וּמִחוּץ / u'mi'hhuts) in~the~COVERING (בַּכֹּפֶר / ba'ko'pher)

Make for yourself a vessel of gopher wood, you will make nests for the vessel, and you will cover her from the inside and the outside with the covering,

15 and~THIS (וְזֶה / wê'zeh) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) THREE (שְׁלֹשׁ / shê'losh) HUNDRED~s (מֵאוֹת / mey'ot) FOREARM (אַמָּה / a'mah) LENGTH (אֹרֶךְ / o'rekh) the~VESSEL (הַתֵּבָה / ha'tey'vah) FIVE~s (חֲמִשִּׁים / hha'mi'shim) FOREARM (אַמָּה / a'mah) WIDTH~her (רָחְבָּהּ / rahh'bah) and~THREE~s (וּשְׁלֹשִׁים / ush'lo'shim) FOREARM (אַמָּה / a'mah) HEIGHT~her (קוֹמָתָהּ / qo'ma'tah)

and this is how you will make her, three hundred forearms is the length of the vessel, fifty forearms is her width, and thirty forearms is her height.

16 GLISTENING (צֹהַר / tso'har) [58] you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) to~the~VESSEL (לַתֵּבָה / la'tey'vah) and~TO (וְאֶל / wê'el) FOREARM (אַמָּה / a'mah) you(ms)~will~much~FINISH(Verb)~her (תְּכַלֶּנָּה / tê'kha'le'nah) from~to~UPWARD~her (מִלְמַעְלָה / mi'le'ma'lah) [59] and~OPENING (וּפֶתַח / u'phe'tahh) the~VESSEL (הַתֵּבָה / ha'tey'vah) in~SIDE~her (בְּצִדָּהּ / bê'tsi'dah) you(ms)~will~PLACE(Verb) (תָּשִׂים / ta'sim) LOWER.PART~s (תַּחְתִּיִּם / tahh'ti'yim) SECOND~s (שְׁנִיִּם / shê'ni'yim) and~THIRD~s (וּשְׁלִשִׁים / ush'li'shim) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb)~her (תַּעֲשֶׂהָ / ta'a'se'ah)

You will make a glistening for the vessel, and to a forearm you will finish her above, and you will place an opening of the vessel in her side, you will make her with lower parts, second ones and third ones,

17 and~I (וַאֲנִי / wa'ani) LOOK~me (הִנְנִי / hin'ni) make~COME(Verb)~ing(ms) (מֵבִיא / me'vi) AT (אֶת / et) the~FLOOD (הַמַּבּוּל / ha'ma'bul) WATER~s2 (מַיִם / ma'yim) UPON (עַל / al) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) to~>~much~DAMAGE(Verb) (לְשַׁחֵת / lê'sha'hheyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) FLESH (בָּשָׂר / ba'sar) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo) WIND (רוּחַ / ru'ahh) LIVING~s (חַיִּים / hhai'yim) from~UNDER (מִתַּחַת / mi'ta'hhat) the~SKY~s2 (הַשָּׁמָיִם / ha'sha'ma'yim) ALL (כֹּל / kol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~the~LAND (בָּאָרֶץ / ba'a'rets) he~will~EXPIRE(Verb) (יִגְוָע / yig'wa)

and look at me, I am bringing the flood of waters upon the land to damage all the flesh, which has in him the wind of life, below the skies, all which are in the land will expire,

18 and~I~did~make~RISE(Verb) (וַהֲקִמֹתִי / wa'ha'qi'mo'ti) AT (אֶת / et) COVENANT~me (בְּרִיתִי / bê'ri'ti) AT~you(fs) (אִתָּךְ / i'takh) and~you(ms)~did~COME(Verb) (וּבָאתָ / u'va'ta) TO (אֶל / el) the~VESSEL (הַתֵּבָה / ha'tey'vah) YOU(MS) (אַתָּה / a'tah) and~SON~s~you(ms) (וּבָנֶיךָ / u'va'ney'kha) and~WOMAN~you(ms) (וְאִשְׁתְּךָ / wê'ish'te'kha) and~WOMAN~s (וּנְשֵׁי / un'shey) SON~s~you(ms) (בָנֶיךָ / va'ney'kha) AT~you(fs) (אִתָּךְ / i'takh)

and I will make my covenant rise with you and you will come to the vessel, you and your sons and your woman and the women of your sons with you,

19 and~from~ALL (וּמִכָּל / u'mi'kol) the~LIVING (הָחַי / ha'hhai) from~ALL (מִכָּל / mi'kol) FLESH (בָּשָׂר / ba'sar) TWO (שְׁנַיִם / shê'na'yim) from~ALL (מִכֹּל / mi'kol) you(ms)~will~make~COME(Verb) (תָּבִיא / ta'vi) TO (אֶל / el) the~VESSEL (הַתֵּבָה / ha'tey'vah) to~>~make~LIVE(Verb) (לְהַחֲיֹת / lê'ha'hha'yot) AT~you(fs) (אִתָּךְ / i'takh) MALE (זָכָר / za'khar) and~FEMALE (וּנְקֵבָה / un'qey'vah) they(m)~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיוּ / yih'yu)

and from all of the living ones, from all the flesh, two from all you will bring to the vessel to live with you, male and female will exist.

20 from~the~FLYER (מֵהָעוֹף / mey'ha'oph) to~KIND~him (לְמִינֵהוּ / lê'mi'ney'hu) and~FROM (וּמִן / u'min) the~BEAST (הַבְּהֵמָה / ha'be'hey'mah) to~KIND~her (לְמִינָהּ / lê'mi'nah) from~ALL (מִכֹּל / mi'kol) TREADER (רֶמֶשׂ / re'mes) the~GROUND (הָאֲדָמָה / ha'a'da'mah) to~KIND~him (לְמִינֵהוּ / lê'mi'ney'hu) TWO (שְׁנַיִם / shê'na'yim) from~ALL (מִכֹּל / mi'kol) they(m)~will~COME(Verb) (יָבֹאוּ / ya'vo'u) TO~you(ms) (אֵלֶיךָ / ey'ley'kha) to~>~make~LIVE(Verb) (לְהַחֲיוֹת / lê'ha'hha'yot)

From the flyer to his kind, and from the beast to her kind, from all of the treaders of the ground to his kind, two from all will come to you to live,

21 and~YOU(MS) (וְאַתָּה / wê'a'tah) !(ms)~TAKE(Verb) (קַח / qahh) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) from~ALL (מִכָּל / mi'kol) NOURISHMENT (מַאֲכָל / ma'a'khal) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~be~EAT(Verb) (יֵאָכֵל / yey'a'kheyl) and~you(ms)~did~GATHER(Verb) (וְאָסַפְתָּ / wê'a'saph'ta) TO~you(ms) (אֵלֶיךָ / ey'ley'kha) and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) and~to~~them(m) (וְלָהֶם / wê'la'hem) to~FOOD (לְאָכְלָה / lê'akh'lah)

and you, take for you from all the nourishment which will be eaten, and you will gather for you, and he will exist for you and for them for food,

22 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) No'ahh (נֹחַ / no'ahh) like~ALL (כְּכֹל / kê'khol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~much~DIRECT(Verb) (צִוָּה / tsi'wah) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) SO (כֵּן / keyn) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah)

and No'ahh did everything Elohiym directed him, so he did,
