Numbers Chapter 16

1 and~he~will~TAKE(Verb) (וַיִּקַּח / wai'yi'qahh) Qorahh (קֹרַח / qo'rahh) SON (בֶּן / ben) Yits'har (יִצְהָר / yits'har) SON (בֶּן / ben) Qehat (קְהָת / qê'hat) SON (בֶּן / ben) Lewi (לֵוִי / ley'wi) and~Datan (וְדָתָן / wê'da'tan) and~Aviram (וַאֲבִירָם / wa'a'vi'ram) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Eli'av (אֱלִיאָב / e'li'av) and~On (וְאוֹן / wê'on) SON (בֶּן / ben) Pelet (פֶּלֶת / pe'let) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Re'uven (רְאוּבֵן / rê'u'veyn)

and Qorahh the son of Qehat the son of Lewi took, and Datan and Aviram the sons of Eli'av, and On the son of Pelet, the sons of Re'uven,

2 and~they(m)~will~RISE(Verb) (וַיָּקֻמוּ / wai'ya'qu'mu) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) and~MAN~s (וַאֲנָשִׁים / wa'a'na'shim) from~SON~s (מִבְּנֵי / mi'be'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) FIVE~s (חֲמִשִּׁים / hha'mi'shim) and~HUNDRED~s2 (וּמָאתָיִם / u'ma'ta'yim) CAPTAIN~s (נְשִׂיאֵי / nê'si'ey) WITNESS (עֵדָה / ey'dah) SELECTED~s (קְרִאֵי / qê'ri'ey) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) MAN~s (אַנְשֵׁי / an'shey) TITLE (שֵׁם / sheym)

and they rose to the face of Mosheh and the men of the sons of Yisra'eyl, two hundred and fifty, captains of the witness, selected ones of the appointed, men of the title,

3 and~they(m)~will~be~ASSEMBLE(Verb) (וַיִּקָּהֲלוּ / wai'yi'qa'ha'lu) UPON (עַל / al) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) and~they(m)~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמְרוּ / wai'yom'ru) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) ABUNDANT (רַב / rav) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) ALL (כָל / khol) the~COMPANY (הָעֵדָה / ha'ey'dah) ALL~them(m) (כֻּלָּם / ku'lam) UNIQUE~s (קְדֹשִׁים / qê'do'shim) and~in~MIDST~them(m) (וּבְתוֹכָם / uv'to'kham) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~WHY (וּמַדּוּעַ / u'ma'du'a) you(mp)~will~self~LIFT.UP(Verb) (תִּתְנַשְּׂאוּ / tit'nas'u) UPON (עַל / al) ASSEMBLY (קְהַל / qê'hal) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and they were assembled over Mosheh and over Aharon, and they said to them, it is abundant for you given that all the company, all of them, are unique ones, and in their midst is YHWH, so why do you lift yourself up over the assembly of YHWH,

4 and~he~will~HEAR(Verb) (וַיִּשְׁמַע / wai'yish'ma) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) and~he~will~FALL(Verb) (וַיִּפֹּל / wai'yi'pol) UPON (עַל / al) FACE~s~him (פָּנָיו / pa'naw)

and Mosheh heard, and he fell upon his face,

5 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) TO (אֶל / el) Qorahh (קֹרַח / qo'rahh) and~TO (וְאֶל / wê'el) ALL (כָּל / kol) COMPANY~him (עֲדָתוֹ / a'da'to) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) MORNING (בֹּקֶר / bo'qer) [834] and~he~will~make~KNOW(Verb) (וְיֹדַע / wê'yo'da) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT (אֶת / et) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~UNIQUE (הַקָּדוֹשׁ / ha'qa'dosh) and~he~did~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וְהִקְרִיב / wê'hiq'riv) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~CHOOSE(Verb) (יִבְחַר / yiv'hhar) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo) he~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (יַקְרִיב / yaq'riv) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law)

and he spoke to Qorahh and to all his company saying, morning, and YHWH will make known who is for him and who is unique, and he will bring him near and who he will choose with him, he will bring near to him.

6 THIS (זֹאת / zot) !(mp)~DO(Verb) (עֲשׂוּ / a'su) !(mp)~TAKE(Verb) (קְחוּ / qê'hhu) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) FIRE.PAN~s (מַחְתּוֹת / mahh'tot) Qorahh (קֹרַח / qo'rahh) and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) COMPANY~him (עֲדָתוֹ / a'da'to)

Do this, take for yourself firepans, Qorahh and all his company,

7 and~!(mp)~GIVE(Verb) (וּתְנוּ / ut'nu) in~~them(f) (בָהֵן / va'heyn) FIRE (אֵשׁ / eysh) and~they~did~PLACE(Verb) (וְשִׂימוּ / wê'si'mu) UPON~them(f) (עֲלֵיהֶן / a'ley'hen) INCENSE.SMOKE (קְטֹרֶת / qê'to'ret) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TOMORROW (מָחָר / ma'hhar) and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) the~MAN (הָאִישׁ / ha'ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~CHOOSE(Verb) (יִבְחַר / yiv'hhar) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) HE (הוּא / hu) the~UNIQUE (הַקָּדוֹשׁ / ha'qa'dosh) ABUNDANT (רַב / rav) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Lewi (לֵוִי / ley'wi)

and place in them fire, and they will place upon them incense smoke to the face of YHWH tomorrow, and the man that YHWH will choose will exist, he is the unique one, abundant for you sons of Lewi,

8 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) TO (אֶל / el) Qorahh (קֹרַח / qo'rahh) !(mp)~HEAR(Verb) (שִׁמְעוּ / shim'u) PLEASE (נָא / na) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Lewi (לֵוִי / ley'wi)

and Mosheh said to Qorahh, sons of Lewi please hear.

9 ?~SMALL.AMOUNT (הַמְעַט / ha'me'at) from~~you(mp) (מִכֶּם / mi'kem) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~make~SEPARATE(Verb) (הִבְדִּיל / hav'dil) Elohiym (אֱלֹהֵי / e'lo'hey) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem) from~COMPANY (מֵעֲדַת / mey'a'dat) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) to~>~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (לְהַקְרִיב / lê'haq'riv) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) to~>~SERVE(Verb) (לַעֲבֹד / la'a'vod) AT (אֶת / et) SERVICE (עֲבֹדַת / a'vo'dat) DWELLING (מִשְׁכַּן / mish'kan) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~to~>~STAND(Verb) (וְלַעֲמֹד / wê'la'a'mod) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) the~COMPANY (הָעֵדָה / ha'ey'dah) to~>~much~MINISTER(Verb)~them(m) (לְשָׁרְתָם / lê'shar'tam)

Is it a small thing for you given that Elohiym caused you to be separated from the company of Yisra'eyl, to bring you near to him to serve in the service of the dwelling of YHWH, and to stand to the face of the company to minister them,

10 and~he~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וַיַּקְרֵב / wai'yaq'reyv) AT~you(ms) (אֹתְךָ / ot'kha) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) ALL (כָּל / kol) BROTHER~s~you(ms) (אַחֶיךָ / a'hhey'kha) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Lewi (לֵוִי / ley'wi) AT~you(fs) (אִתָּךְ / i'takh) [835] and~you(mp)~did~much~SEARCH.OUT(Verb) (וּבִקַּשְׁתֶּם / u'vi'qash'tem) ALSO (גַּם / gam) ADMINISTRATION (כְּהֻנָּה / kê'hu'nah)

and he brought you near and all your brothers, the sons of Lewi with you, and you are searching out also the administration.

11 to~SO (לָכֵן / la'kheyn) YOU(MS) (אַתָּה / a'tah) and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) COMPANY~you(ms) (עֲדָתְךָ / a'dat'kha) the~be~APPOINT(Verb)~ing(mp) (הַנֹּעָדִים / ha'no'a'dim) UPON (עַל / al) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~Aharon (וְאַהֲרֹן / wê'a'ha'ron) WHAT (מַה / mah) HE (הוּא / hu) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(mp)~will~be~MURMUR(Verb) (תִלּוֹנוּ / ti'lo'nu) [836] UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law)

Because of this, you and all your company, the ones meeting against YHWH, and Aharon, what of him, given that you will be murmuring against him,

12 and~he~will~SEND(Verb) (וַיִּשְׁלַח / wai'yish'lahh) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~CALL.OUT(Verb) (לִקְרֹא / liq'ro) to~Datan (לְדָתָן / lê'da'tan) and~to~Aviram (וְלַאֲבִירָם / wê'la'a'vi'ram) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Eli'av (אֱלִיאָב / e'li'av) and~they(m)~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמְרוּ / wai'yom'ru) NOT (לֹא / lo) we~will~GO.UP(Verb)~& (נַעֲלֶה / na'a'leh)

and Mosheh sent to call out to Datan and to Aviram, the sons of Eli'av, and they said, we will not go up.

13 ?~SMALL.AMOUNT (הַמְעַט / ha'me'at) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(ms)~did~make~GO.UP(Verb)~us (הֶעֱלִיתָנוּ / he'e'li'ta'nu) from~LAND (מֵאֶרֶץ / mey'e'rets) ISSUE(Verb)~ing(fs) (זָבַת / za'vat) FAT (חָלָב / hha'lav) and~HONEY (וּדְבַשׁ / ud'vash) to~>~make~DIE(Verb)~us (לַהֲמִיתֵנוּ / la'ha'mi'tey'nu) in~the~WILDERNESS (בַּמִּדְבָּר / ba'mid'bar) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(ms)~will~self~TURN.ASIDE(Verb) (תִשְׂתָּרֵר / tis'ta'reyr) UPON~us (עָלֵינוּ / a'ley'nu) ALSO (גַּם / gam) >~self~TURN.ASIDE(Verb) (הִשְׂתָּרֵר / his'ta'reyr)

Is it a small thing, given that you will bring us up from the land issuing fat and honey to kill us in the wilderness, given that you are surely making yourself ruler over us also.

14 MOREOVER (אַף / aph) NOT (לֹא / lo) TO (אֶל / el) LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) ISSUE(Verb)~ing(fs) (זָבַת / za'vat) FAT (חָלָב / hha'lav) and~HONEY (וּדְבַשׁ / ud'vash) you(ms)~did~make~COME(Verb)~us (הֲבִיאֹתָנוּ / ha'vi'o'ta'nu) and~you(ms)~will~GIVE(Verb) (וַתִּתֶּן / wa'ti'ten) to~~us (לָנוּ / la'nu) INHERITANCE (נַחֲלַת / na'hha'lat) FIELD (שָׂדֶה / sa'deh) and~VINEYARD (וָכָרֶם / wa'kha'rem) ?~EYE~s2 (הַעֵינֵי / ha'ey'ney) the~MAN~s (הָאֲנָשִׁים / ha'a'na'shim) the~THEY(m) (הָהֵם / ha'heym) you(ms)~will~much~PICK.OUT(Verb) (תְּנַקֵּר / tê'na'qeyr) NOT (לֹא / lo) we~will~GO.UP(Verb)~& (נַעֲלֶה / na'a'leh)

Moreover, you did not bring us to a land issuing fat and honey, or given to us an inheritance of the field and vineyard, will you pick out the eyes of those men, we will not go up,

15 and~he~will~FLARE.UP(Verb) (וַיִּחַר / wai'yi'hhar) to~Mosheh (לְמֹשֶׁה / lê'mo'sheh) MANY (מְאֹד / mê'od) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) TO (אֶל / el) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) DO.NOT (אַל / al) you(ms)~will~TURN(Verb) (תֵּפֶן / tey'phen) TO (אֶל / el) DEPOSIT~them(m) (מִנְחָתָם / min'hha'tam) NOT (לֹא / lo) DONKEY (חֲמוֹר / hha'mor) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) from~~them(m) (מֵהֶם / mey'hem) I~did~LIFT.UP(Verb) (נָשָׂאתִי / na'sa'ti) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) I~did~make~BE.DYSFUNCTIONAL(Verb) (הֲרֵעֹתִי / ha'rey'o'ti) AT (אֶת / et) UNIT (אַחַד / a'hhad) from~~them(m) (מֵהֶם / mey'hem)

and for Mosheh, he greatly flared up, and he said to YHWH, do not turn to their deposit, I did not lift up one donkey from them, and I will not make one from them be dysfunctional,

16 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) TO (אֶל / el) Qorahh (קֹרַח / qo'rahh) YOU(MS) (אַתָּה / a'tah) and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) COMPANY~you(ms) (עֲדָתְךָ / a'dat'kha) !(mp)~EXIST(Verb) (הֱיוּ / he'yu) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) YOU(MS) (אַתָּה / a'tah) and~THEY(m) (וָהֵם / wa'heym) and~Aharon (וְאַהֲרֹן / wê'a'ha'ron) TOMORROW (מָחָר / ma'hhar)

and Mosheh said to Qorahh, you and all your company, exist to the face of YHWH, you and they and Aharon tomorrow,

17 and~!(mp)~TAKE(Verb) (וּקְחוּ / uq'hhu) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) FIRE.PAN~him (מַחְתָּתוֹ / mahh'ta'to) and~you(mp)~did~GIVE(Verb) (וּנְתַתֶּם / un'ta'tem) UPON~them(m) (עֲלֵיהֶם / a'ley'hem) INCENSE.SMOKE (קְטֹרֶת / qê'to'ret) and~you(mp)~did~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וְהִקְרַבְתֶּם / wê'hiq'rav'tem) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) FIRE.PAN~him (מַחְתָּתוֹ / mahh'ta'to) FIVE~s (חֲמִשִּׁים / hha'mi'shim) and~HUNDRED~s2 (וּמָאתַיִם / u'ma'ta'yim) FIRE.PAN~s (מַחְתֹּת / mahh'tot) and~YOU(MS) (וְאַתָּה / wê'a'tah) and~Aharon (וְאַהֲרֹן / wê'a'ha'ron) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) FIRE.PAN~him (מַחְתָּתוֹ / mahh'ta'to)

and each will take his fire pan, and you will place upon them incense smoke, and you will bring it near to the face of YHWH each his fire pan, two hundred and fifty, and you and Aharon, each his fire pan,

18 and~they(m)~will~TAKE(Verb) (וַיִּקְחוּ / wai'yiq'hhu) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) FIRE.PAN~him (מַחְתָּתוֹ / mahh'ta'to) and~they(m)~will~GIVE(Verb) (וַיִּתְּנוּ / wai'yit'nu) UPON~them(m) (עֲלֵיהֶם / a'ley'hem) FIRE (אֵשׁ / eysh) and~they(m)~will~PLACE(Verb) (וַיָּשִׂימוּ / wai'ya'si'mu) UPON~them(m) (עֲלֵיהֶם / a'ley'hem) INCENSE.SMOKE (קְטֹרֶת / qê'to'ret) and~they(m)~will~STAND(Verb) (וַיַּעַמְדוּ / wai'ya'am'du) OPENING (פֶּתַח / pe'tahh) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) and~Mosheh (וּמֹשֶׁה / u'mo'sheh) and~Aharon (וְאַהֲרֹן / wê'a'ha'ron)

and each will take his fire pan, and they will place upon them fire, and they will place upon them incense smoke, and they will stand at the opening of the appointed tent, and Mosheh and Aharon,

19 and~he~will~ASSEMBLE(Verb) (וַיַּקְהֵל / wai'yaq'heyl) UPON~them(m) (עֲלֵיהֶם / a'ley'hem) Qorahh (קֹרַח / qo'rahh) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~COMPANY (הָעֵדָה / ha'ey'dah) TO (אֶל / el) OPENING (פֶּתַח / pe'tahh) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) and~he~will~be~SEE(Verb) (וַיֵּרָא / wai'yey'ra) ARMAMENT (כְבוֹד / khê'vod) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~COMPANY (הָעֵדָה / ha'ey'dah)

and Qorahh will assemble upon them all the company to the opening of the appointed tent, and the armament of YHWH appeared to all the company,

20 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) and~TO (וְאֶל / wê'el) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh and to Aharon saying,
