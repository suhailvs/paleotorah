Numbers Chapter 8

1 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

2 !(ms)~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דַּבֵּר / da'beyr) TO (אֶל / el) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) and~you(ms)~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמַרְתָּ / wê'a'mar'ta) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) in~>~make~GO.UP(Verb)~you(ms) (בְּהַעֲלֹתְךָ / bê'ha'a'lot'kha) [785] AT (אֶת / et) the~LAMP~s (הַנֵּרֹת / ha'ney'rot) TO (אֶל / el) FOREFRONT (מוּל / mul) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) the~LAMPSTAND (הַמְּנוֹרָה / ham'no'rah) they(m)~will~make~LIGHT(Verb) (יָאִירוּ / ya'i'ru) SEVEN (שִׁבְעַת / shiv'at) the~LAMP~s (הַנֵּרוֹת / ha'ney'rot)

Speak to Aharon, and you will say to him, in your making the lamps go up, to the forefront of the face of the lampstand, the seven lamps will make light,

3 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) SO (כֵּן / keyn) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) TO (אֶל / el) FOREFRONT (מוּל / mul) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) the~LAMPSTAND (הַמְּנוֹרָה / ham'no'rah) he~did~make~GO.UP(Verb) (הֶעֱלָה / he'e'lah) [786] LAMP~s~her (נֵרֹתֶיהָ / ney'ro'te'yah) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~much~DIRECT(Verb) (צִוָּה / tsi'wah) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT (אֶת / et) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh)

and Aharon will do so to the forefront of the face of the lampstand, and he will make her light go up, just as YHWH directed Mosheh,

4 and~THIS (וְזֶה / wê'zeh) WORK (מַעֲשֵׂה / ma'a'seyh) the~LAMPSTAND (הַמְּנֹרָה / ham'no'rah) BEATEN.WORK (מִקְשָׁה / miq'shah) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) UNTIL (עַד / ad) MIDSECTION~her (יְרֵכָהּ / yê'rey'khah) UNTIL (עַד / ad) BUD~her (פִּרְחָהּ / pir'hhah) BEATEN.WORK (מִקְשָׁה / miq'shah) SHE (הִוא / hi) like~the~APPEARANCE (כַּמַּרְאֶה / ka'mar'eh) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~make~SEE(Verb) (הֶרְאָה / her'ah) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT (אֶת / et) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) SO (כֵּן / keyn) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) AT (אֶת / et) the~LAMPSTAND (הַמְּנֹרָה / ham'no'rah)

and this is the work of the lampstand, a beaten work of gold, unto her midsection, unto her bud, she is a beaten work, like the appearance that YHWH made Mosheh see, so he did do with the lampstand,

5 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

6 !(ms)~TAKE(Verb) (קַח / qahh) AT (אֶת / et) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim) from~MIDST (מִתּוֹךְ / mi'tokh) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~you(ms)~did~much~BE.CLEAN(Verb) (וְטִהַרְתָּ / wê'ti'har'ta) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam)

Take the ones of Lewi from the midst of the sons of Yisra'eyl, and you will declare them clean,

7 and~IN.THIS.WAY (וְכֹה / wê'khoh) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) to~>~much~BE.CLEAN(Verb)~them(m) (לְטַהֲרָם / lê'ta'ha'ram) !(ms)~make~SPATTER(Verb) (הַזֵּה / ha'zeyh) UPON~them(m) (עֲלֵיהֶם / a'ley'hem) WATER~s2 (מֵי / mey) FAILURE (חַטָּאת / hha'tat) and~they~did~make~CROSS.OVER(Verb) (וְהֶעֱבִירוּ / wê'he'e'vi'ru) RAZOR (תַעַר / ta'ar) UPON (עַל / al) ALL (כָּל / kol) FLESH~them(m) (בְּשָׂרָם / bê'sa'ram) and~they~did~much~WASH(Verb) (וְכִבְּסוּ / wê'khi'be'su) GARMENT~s~them(m) (בִגְדֵיהֶם / vig'dey'hem) and~they~did~be~make~BE.CLEAN(Verb) (וְהִטֶּהָרוּ / wê'hi'te'ha'ru)

and in this way you will do for them, to declare them clean, spatter the waters of failure upon them, and they will make a razor cross over upon all their flesh, and they will wash their garments, and they will be declared clean,

8 and~they~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקְחוּ / wê'laq'hhu) BULL (פַּר / par) SON (בֶּן / ben) CATTLE (בָּקָר / ba'qar) and~DEPOSIT~him (וּמִנְחָתוֹ / u'min'hha'to) FLOUR (סֹלֶת / so'let) MIX(Verb)~ed(fs) (בְּלוּלָה / bê'lu'lah) in~the~OIL (בַשָּׁמֶן / va'sha'men) and~BULL (וּפַר / u'phar) SECOND (שֵׁנִי / shey'ni) SON (בֶן / ven) CATTLE (בָּקָר / ba'qar) you(ms)~will~TAKE(Verb) (תִּקַּח / ti'qahh) to~FAILURE (לְחַטָּאת / lê'hha'tat)

and they will take a bull, a son of the cattle, and his deposit of flour mixed in the oil, and a second bull, a son of the cattle, you will take for a failure,

9 and~you(ms)~did~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וְהִקְרַבְתָּ / wê'hiq'rav'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) and~you(ms)~did~make~ASSEMBLE(Verb) (וְהִקְהַלְתָּ / wê'hiq'hal'ta) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) COMPANY (עֲדַת / a'dat) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl)

and you will bring the ones of Lewi near to the face of the appointed tent, and you will assemble all the company of the sons of Yisra'eyl,

10 and~you(ms)~did~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וְהִקְרַבְתָּ / wê'hiq'rav'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~they~did~SUPPORT(Verb) (וְסָמְכוּ / wê'sam'khu) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) AT (אֶת / et) HAND~s2~them(m) (יְדֵיהֶם / yê'dey'hem) UPON (עַל / al) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim)

and you will bring the ones of Lewi near to the face of YHWH, and the sons of Yisra'eyl will support their hands upon the ones of Lewi,

11 and~he~did~WAVE(Verb) (וְהֵנִיף / wê'hey'niph) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) AT (אֶת / et) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim) WAVING (תְּנוּפָה / tê'nu'phah) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) from~AT (מֵאֵת / mey'eyt) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~they~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיוּ / wê'hai'u) to~>~SERVE(Verb) (לַעֲבֹד / la'a'vod) AT (אֶת / et) SERVICE (עֲבֹדַת / a'vo'dat) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and Aharon will wave the ones of Lewi, a waving to the face of YHWH, from the sons of Yisra'eyl, and they will exist to serve the service of YHWH,

12 and~the~Lewi~s (וְהַלְוִיִּם / wê'hal'wi'yim) they(m)~will~SUPPORT(Verb) (יִסְמְכוּ / yis'me'khu) AT (אֶת / et) HAND~s2~them(m) (יְדֵיהֶם / yê'dey'hem) UPON (עַל / al) HEAD (רֹאשׁ / rosh) the~BULL~s (הַפָּרִים / ha'pa'rim) and~!(ms)~DO(Verb) (וַעֲשֵׂה / wa'a'seyh) AT (אֶת / et) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad) FAILURE (חַטָּאת / hha'tat) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad) ASCENSION.OFFERING (עֹלָה / o'lah) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) to~>~much~COVER(Verb) (לְכַפֵּר / lê'kha'peyr) UPON (עַל / al) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim)

and the ones of Lewi will support their hands upon the head of the bulls, and do the one of failure, and the one of a ascension offering, to YHWH to make a covering upon the ones of Lewi,

13 and~you(ms)~did~make~STAND(Verb) (וְהַעֲמַדְתָּ / wê'ha'a'mad'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) and~to~FACE~s (וְלִפְנֵי / wê'liph'ney) SON~s~him (בָנָיו / va'naw) and~you(ms)~did~make~WAVE(Verb) (וְהֵנַפְתָּ / wê'hey'naph'ta) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) WAVING (תְּנוּפָה / tê'nu'phah) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

and you will make the ones of Lewi stand to the face of Aharon and to the faces of his sons, and you will wave them, a waving to YHWH,

14 and~you(ms)~did~make~SEPARATE(Verb) (וְהִבְדַּלְתָּ / wê'hiv'dal'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim) from~MIDST (מִתּוֹךְ / mi'tokh) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~they~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיוּ / wê'hai'u) to~~me (לִי / li) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim)

and you will make the ones of Lewi separate from the midst of the sons of Yisra'eyl, and the ones of Lewi will exist for me,

15 and~AFTER (וְאַחֲרֵי / wê'a'hha'rey) SO (כֵן / kheyn) they(m)~will~COME(Verb) (יָבֹאוּ / ya'vo'u) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim) to~>~SERVE(Verb) (לַעֲבֹד / la'a'vod) AT (אֶת / et) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) and~you(ms)~did~much~BE.CLEAN(Verb) (וְטִהַרְתָּ / wê'ti'har'ta) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) and~you(ms)~did~make~WAVE(Verb) (וְהֵנַפְתָּ / wê'hey'naph'ta) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) WAVING (תְּנוּפָה / tê'nu'phah)

and after this, the ones of Lewi will come to serve the appointed tent, and you will declare them clean, and you will make them wave a waving.

16 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) GIVE(Verb)~ed(mp) (נְתֻנִים / nê'tu'nim) GIVE(Verb)~ed(mp) (נְתֻנִים / nê'tu'nim) THEY(m) (הֵמָּה / hey'mah) to~~me (לִי / li) from~MIDST (מִתּוֹךְ / mi'tokh) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) UNDER (תַּחַת / ta'hhat) BURSTING (פִּטְרַת / pit'rat) ALL (כָּל / kol) BOWELS (רֶחֶם / re'hhem) [787] FIRSTBORN (בְּכוֹר / bê'khor) ALL (כֹּל / kol) from~SON~s (מִבְּנֵי / mi'be'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) I~did~TAKE(Verb) (לָקַחְתִּי / la'qahh'ti) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) to~~me (לִי / li)

Given that each of them were given to me from the midst of the sons of Yisra'eyl, in place of the bursting of all the bowels of the firstborn from all the sons of Yisra'eyl, I will take them for me.

17 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) to~~me (לִי / li) ALL (כָל / khol) FIRSTBORN (בְּכוֹר / bê'khor) in~SON~s (בִּבְנֵי / biv'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) in~the~HUMAN (בָּאָדָם / ba'a'dam) and~in~the~BEAST (וּבַבְּהֵמָה / u'va'be'hey'mah) in~DAY (בְּיוֹם / bê'yom) >~make~HIT(Verb)~me (הַכֹּתִי / ha'ko'ti) ALL (כָל / khol) FIRSTBORN (בְּכוֹר / bê'khor) in~LAND (בְּאֶרֶץ / bê'e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) I~did~make~SET.APART(Verb) (הִקְדַּשְׁתִּי / hiq'dash'ti) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) to~~me (לִי / li)

Given that all the firstborn in the sons of Yisra'eyl belong to me, among the human and among the beast, in the day I hit the firstborn in the land of Mits'rayim, I set them apart for me,

18 and~I~will~TAKE(Verb) (וָאֶקַּח / wa'e'qahh) AT (אֶת / et) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim) UNDER (תַּחַת / ta'hhat) ALL (כָּל / kol) FIRSTBORN (בְּכוֹר / bê'khor) in~SON~s (בִּבְנֵי / biv'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl)

and I will take the ones of Lewi in place of all the firstborn in the sons of Yisra'eyl,

19 and~I~will~GIVE(Verb) (וָאֶתְּנָה / wa'et'nah) AT (אֶת / et) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim) GIVE(Verb)~ed(mp) (נְתֻנִים / nê'tu'nim) to~Aharon (לְאַהֲרֹן / lê'a'ha'ron) and~to~SON~s~him (וּלְבָנָיו / ul'va'naw) from~MIDST (מִתּוֹךְ / mi'tokh) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) to~>~SERVE(Verb) (לַעֲבֹד / la'a'vod) AT (אֶת / et) SERVICE (עֲבֹדַת / a'vo'dat) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) in~TENT (בְּאֹהֶל / bê'o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) and~to~>~much~COVER(Verb) (וּלְכַפֵּר / ul'kha'peyr) UPON (עַל / al) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) in~SON~s (בִּבְנֵי / biv'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) STRIKING (נֶגֶף / ne'geph) [788] in~>~DRAW.NEAR(Verb) (בְּגֶשֶׁת / bê'ge'shet) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) TO (אֶל / el) the~SPECIAL (הַקֹּדֶשׁ / ha'qo'desh)

and I gave the ones of Lewi, given to Aharon and to his sons from the midst of the sons of Yisra'eyl, to serve the service of the sons of Yisra'eyl in the appointed tent, and to make a covering upon the sons of Yisra'eyl, and a striking will not exist in the sons of Yisra'eyl with the sons of Yisra'eyl drawing near to the special place,

20 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) and~Aharon (וְאַהֲרֹן / wê'a'ha'ron) and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) COMPANY (עֲדַת / a'dat) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) to~Lewi~s (לַלְוִיִּם / la'le'wi'yim) like~ALL (כְּכֹל / kê'khol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~much~DIRECT(Verb) (צִוָּה / tsi'wah) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT (אֶת / et) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~Lewi~s (לַלְוִיִּם / la'le'wi'yim) SO (כֵּן / keyn) they~did~DO(Verb) (עָשׂוּ / a'su) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl)

and Mosheh, and Aharon and all the company of the sons of Yisra'eyl, did to the ones of Lewi everything that YHWH directed Mosheh concerning the ones of Lewi, so the sons of Yisra'eyl did to them,

21 and~they(m)~he~will~self~FAIL(Verb) (וַיִּתְחַטְּאוּ / wai'yit'hhat'u) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim) and~they(m)~will~much~WASH(Verb) (וַיְכַבְּסוּ / wai'kha'be'su) GARMENT~s~them(m) (בִּגְדֵיהֶם / big'dey'hem) and~he~will~make~WAVE(Verb) (וַיָּנֶף / wai'ya'neph) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) WAVING (תְּנוּפָה / tê'nu'phah) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~he~will~much~COVER(Verb) (וַיְכַפֵּר / wa'ye'kha'peyr) UPON~them(m) (עֲלֵיהֶם / a'ley'hem) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) to~>~much~BE.CLEAN(Verb)~them(m) (לְטַהֲרָם / lê'ta'ha'ram)

and the ones of Lewi purified themselves, and they washed their garments, and Aharon made a waving of them, waving to the face of YHWH, and Aharon made a covering upon them to make them clean,

and after this, the ones of Lewi came to serve their service in the appointed tent, to the face of Aharon and to the faces of his sons, just as YHWH directed Mosheh upon the ones of Lewi, so they did to them,

23 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

24 THIS (זֹאת / zot) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) to~Lewi~s (לַלְוִיִּם / la'le'wi'yim) from~SON (מִבֶּן / mi'ben) FIVE (חָמֵשׁ / hha'meysh) and~TEN~s (וְעֶשְׂרִים / wê'es'rim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~UPWARD~unto (וָמַעְלָה / wa'ma'lah) he~will~COME(Verb) (יָבוֹא / ya'vo) to~>~MUSTER(Verb) (לִצְבֹא / lits'vo) ARMY (צָבָא / tsa'va) in~the~SERVICE (בַּעֲבֹדַת / ba'a'vo'dat) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd)

This is what belongs to the ones of Lewi, from a son of five and twenty years and upward, he will come to muster the army in the service of the appointed tent,

25 and~from~SON (וּמִבֶּן / u'mi'ben) FIVE~s (חֲמִשִּׁים / hha'mi'shim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) he~will~TURN.BACK(Verb) (יָשׁוּב / ya'shuv) from~ARMY (מִצְּבָא / mits'va) the~SERVICE (הָעֲבֹדָה / ha'a'vo'dah) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~will~SERVE(Verb) (יַעֲבֹד / ya'a'vod) YET.AGAIN (עוֹד / od)

and from a son of fifty years, he will turn back from the army of the service and he will not serve again,

26 and~he~did~much~MINISTER(Verb) (וְשֵׁרֵת / wê'shey'reyt) AT (אֶת / et) BROTHER~s~him (אֶחָיו / e'hhaw) in~TENT (בְּאֹהֶל / bê'o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) to~>~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (לִשְׁמֹר / lish'mor) CHARGE (מִשְׁמֶרֶת / mish'me'ret) and~SERVICE (וַעֲבֹדָה / wa'a'vo'dah) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~SERVE(Verb) (יַעֲבֹד / ya'a'vod) like~IN.THIS.WAY (כָּכָה / ka'khah) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) to~Lewi~s (לַלְוִיִּם / la'le'wi'yim) in~CUSTODY~s~them(m) (בְּמִשְׁמְרֹתָם / bê'mish'me'ro'tam)

and he will minister to his brothers in the appointed tent to safeguard the charge, and he will not serve a service, like this way you will do to the ones of Lewi concerning their custodies,
