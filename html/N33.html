Numbers Chapter 33

1 THESE (אֵלֶּה / ey'leh) JOURNEY~s (מַסְעֵי / mas'ey) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they~did~GO.OUT(Verb) (יָצְאוּ / yats'u) from~LAND (מֵאֶרֶץ / mey'e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) to~ARMY~s~them(m) (לְצִבְאֹתָם / lê'tsiv'o'tam) in~HAND (בְּיַד / bê'yad) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) and~Aharon (וְאַהֲרֹן / wê'a'ha'ron)

These are the journeys of the sons of Yisra'eyl who went out from the land of Mits'rayim by their armies by the hand of Mosheh and Aharon,

2 and~he~will~WRITE(Verb) (וַיִּכְתֹּב / wai'yikh'tov) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) AT (אֶת / et) GOING.OUT~s~them(m) (מוֹצָאֵיהֶם / mo'tsa'ey'hem) to~JOURNEY~s~them(m) (לְמַסְעֵיהֶם / lê'mas'ey'hem) UPON (עַל / al) MOUTH (פִּי / pi) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~THESE (וְאֵלֶּה / wê'ey'leh) JOURNEY~s~them(m) (מַסְעֵיהֶם / mas'ey'hem) to~GOING.OUT~s~them(m) (לְמוֹצָאֵיהֶם / lê'mo'tsa'ey'hem)

and Mosheh wrote about their goings out, by their journeys, according to the mouth of YHWH, and these are their journeys by their goings out,

3 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Ra'meses (מֵרַעְמְסֵס / mey'ra'me'seys) in~the~NEW.MOON (בַּחֹדֶשׁ / ba'hho'desh) the~FIRST (הָרִאשׁוֹן / ha'ri'shon) in~the~FIVE (בַּחֲמִשָּׁה / ba'hha'mi'shah) TEN (עָשָׂר / a'sar) DAY (יוֹם / yom) to~the~NEW.MOON (לַחֹדֶשׁ / la'hho'desh) the~FIRST (הָרִאשׁוֹן / ha'ri'shon) from~MORROW (מִמָּחֳרַת / mi'ma'hha'rat) the~Pesahh (הַפֶּסַח / ha'pe'sahh) they~did~GO.OUT(Verb) (יָצְאוּ / yats'u) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) in~HAND (בְּיָד / bê'yad) RAISED~ing(fs) (רָמָה / ra'mah) to~EYE~s2 (לְעֵינֵי / lê'ey'ney) ALL (כָּל / kol) Mits'rayim (מִצְרָיִם / mits'ra'yim)

and they journeyed from Ra'meses in the first new moon, on the fifteenth day of the first new moon, from the morrow of the Pesahh the sons of Yisra'eyl went out, by the hand raising, to the eyes of all Mits'rayim,

4 and~Mits'rayim (וּמִצְרַיִם / u'mits'ra'yim) much~BURY(Verb)~ing(mp) (מְקַבְּרִים / mê'qa'be'rim) AT (אֵת / eyt) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~make~HIT(Verb) (הִכָּה / hi'kah) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) in~~them(m) (בָּהֶם / ba'hem) ALL (כָּל / kol) FIRSTBORN (בְּכוֹר / bê'khor) and~in~Elohiym~them(m) (וּבֵאלֹהֵיהֶם / u'vey'lo'hey'hem) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) JUDGMENT~s (שְׁפָטִים / shê'pha'tim)

and Mits'rayim is burying those among them who were attacked by YHWH, all the firstborn, and YHWH did judgments on their Elohiym,

5 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) from~Ra'meses (מֵרַעְמְסֵס / mey'ra'me'seys) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Suk'kot (בְּסֻכֹּת / bê'su'kot)

and the sons of Yisra'eyl journeyed from Ra'meses, and they camped in Suk'kot,

6 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Suk'kot (מִסֻּכֹּת / mi'su'kot) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Eytam (בְאֵתָם / ve'ey'tam) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~EXTREMITY (בִּקְצֵה / biq'tseyh) the~WILDERNESS (הַמִּדְבָּר / ha'mid'bar)

and they journeyed from Suk'kot, and they camped in Eytam, which is in the extremity of the wilderness,

7 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Eytam (מֵאֵתָם / mey'ey'tam) and~he~will~TURN.BACK(Verb) (וַיָּשָׁב / wai'ya'shav) [914] UPON (עַל / al) Piy-Hahhiyrot (פִּי־הַחִירֹת / pi-ha'hhi'rot) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) UPON (עַל / al) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) Ba'al-Tsephon (בַּעַל צְפֹן / ba'al-tse'phon) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) Migdol (מִגְדֹּל / mig'dol)

and they journeyed from Eytam, and they turned back upon Piy-Hahhiyrot, which is upon the face of Ba'al-Tsephon, and they camped to the face of Migdol,

8 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~FACE~s (מִפְּנֵי / mip'ney) the~Hhirot (הַחִירֹת / ha'hhi'rot) and~they(m)~will~CROSS.OVER(Verb) (וַיַּעַבְרוּ / wai'ya'av'ru) in~MIDST (בְתוֹךְ / ve'tokh) the~SEA (הַיָּם / hai'yam) the~WILDERNESS~unto (הַמִּדְבָּרָה / ha'mid'ba'rah) and~they(m)~will~WALK(Verb) (וַיֵּלְכוּ / wai'yeyl'khu) ROAD (דֶּרֶךְ / de'rekh) THREE (שְׁלֹשֶׁת / shê'lo'shet) DAY~s (יָמִים / ya'mim) in~WILDERNESS (בְּמִדְבַּר / bê'mid'bar) Eytam (אֵתָם / ey'tam) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Marah (בְּמָרָה / bê'ma'rah)

and they journeyed from the face of Hhirot, and they crossed over in the midst of the sea, unto the wilderness, and they walked a road three days in the wilderness of Eytam, and they camped in Marah,

9 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Marah (מִמָּרָה / mi'ma'rah) and~they(m)~will~COME(Verb) (וַיָּבֹאוּ / wai'ya'vo'u) Eyliym~unto (אֵילִמָה / ey'li'mah) and~in~Eyliym (וּבְאֵילִם / uv'ey'lim) TWO (שְׁתֵּים / shê'teym) TEN (עֶשְׂרֵה / es'reyh) EYE~s (עֵינֹת / ey'not) [915] WATER~s2 (מַיִם / ma'yim) and~SEVEN~s (וְשִׁבְעִים / wê'shiv'im) DATE.PALM~s (תְּמָרִים / tê'ma'rim) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) THERE (שָׁם / sham)

and they journeyed form Marah, and they came unto Eyliym, and in Eyliym were twelve eyes of waters and seventy date palms, and they camped there,

10 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Eyliym (מֵאֵילִם / mey'ey'lim) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) UPON (עַל / al) SEA (יַם / yam) REEDS (סוּף / suph) [916]

and they journeyed from Eyliym, and they camped upon the sea of reeds,

11 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~SEA (מִיַּם / mi'yam) REEDS (סוּף / suph) [917] and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~WILDERNESS (בְּמִדְבַּר / bê'mid'bar) Sin (סִין / sin)

and they journeyed from the sea of reeds, and they camped in the wilderness of Sin,

12 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~WILDERNESS (מִמִּדְבַּר / mi'mid'bar) Sin (סִין / sin) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Daphqah (בְּדָפְקָה / bê'daph'qah)

and they journeyed from the wilderness of Sin, and they camped in Daphqah,

13 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Daphqah (מִדָּפְקָה / mi'daph'qah) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Alush (בְּאָלוּשׁ / bê'a'lush)

and they journeyed from Daphqah, and they camped in Alush,

14 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Alush (מֵאָלוּשׁ / mey'a'lush) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Rephiydiym (בִּרְפִידִם / bir'phi'dim) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיָה / hai'yah) THERE (שָׁם / sham) WATER~s2 (מַיִם / ma'yim) to~the~PEOPLE (לָעָם / la'am) to~>~GULP(Verb) (לִשְׁתּוֹת / lish'tot)

and they journeyed from Alush, and they camped in Rephiydiym, and waters did not exist there for the people to gulp,

15 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Rephiydiym (מֵרְפִידִם / mey're'phi'dim) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~WILDERNESS (בְּמִדְבַּר / bê'mid'bar) Sinai (סִינָי / si'nai)

and they journeyed from Rephiydiym, and they camped in the wilderness of Sinai,

16 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~WILDERNESS (מִמִּדְבַּר / mi'mid'bar) Sinai (סִינָי / si'nai) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Qivrot-Hata'awah (בְּקִבְרֹת-הַתַּאֲוָה / bê'qiv'rot)

and they journeyed from the wilderness of Sinai, and they camped in Qivrot-Hata'awah,

17 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Qivrot-Hata'awah (מִקִּבְרֹת-הַתַּאֲוָה / mi'qiv'rot) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Hhatsarot (בַּחֲצֵרֹת / ba'hha'tsey'rot)

and they journeyed from Qivrot-Hata'awah, and they camped in Hhatsarot,

18 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Hhatsarot (מֵחֲצֵרֹת / mey'hha'tsey'rot) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Ritmah (בְּרִתְמָה / bê'rit'mah)

and they journeyed from Hhatsarot, and they camped in Ritmah,

19 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Ritmah (מֵרִתְמָה / mey'rit'mah) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Rimon-Perets (בְּרִמֹּן פָּרֶץ / bê'ri'mon perets)

and they journeyed from Ritmah, and they camped in Rimon-Perets,

20 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Rimon-Perets (מֵרִמֹּן פָּרֶץ / mey'ri'mon perets) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Livnah (בְּלִבְנָה / bê'liv'nah)

and they journeyed from Rimon-Perets, and they camped in Lavan,

21 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Livnah (מִלִּבְנָה / mi'liv'nah) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Risah (בְּרִסָּה / bê'ri'sah)

and they journeyed from Livnah, and they camped in Risah,

22 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Risah (מֵרִסָּה / mey'ri'sah) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Qe'hey'latah (בִּקְהֵלָתָה / biq'hey'la'tah)

and they journeyed from Risah, and they camped in Qe'hey'latah,

23 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Qe'hey'latah (מִקְּהֵלָתָה / mi'qe'hey'la'tah) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~HILL (בְּהַר / bê'har) Shapher (שָׁפֶר / sha'pher)

and they journeyed from Qe'hey'latah, and they camped in the hill of Shapher,

24 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~HILL (מֵהַר / me'har) Shapher (שָׁפֶר / sha'pher) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Hharadah (בַּחֲרָדָה / ba'hha'ra'dah)

and they journeyed from the hill of Shapher, and they camped in Hharadah,

25 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Hharadah (מֵחֲרָדָה / mey'hha'ra'dah) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Maqheylot (בְּמַקְהֵלֹת / bê'maq'hey'lot)

and they journeyed from Hharadah, and they camped in Maqheylot,

26 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Maqheylot (מִמַּקְהֵלֹת / mi'maq'hey'lot) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Tahhat (בְּתָחַת / bê'ta'hhat)

and they journeyed from Maqheylot, and they camped in Tahhat,

27 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Tahhat (מִתָּחַת / mi'ta'hhat) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Terahh (בְּתָרַח / bê'ta'rahh)

and they journeyed from Tahhat, and they camped in Terahh,

28 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Terahh (מִתָּרַח / mi'ta'rahh) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Mitqah (בְּמִתְקָה / bê'mit'qah)

and they journeyed from Terahh, and they camped in Mitqah,

29 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Mitqah (מִמִּתְקָה / mi'mit'qah) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Hhashmonah (בְּחַשְׁמֹנָה / bê'hhash'mo'nah)

and they journeyed from Mitqah, and they camped in Hhashmonah,

30 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Hhashmonah (מֵחַשְׁמֹנָה / mey'hhash'mo'nah) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Moseyrot (בְּמֹסֵרוֹת / bê'mo'sey'rot)

and they journeyed from Hhashmonah, and they camped in Moseyrot,

31 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Moseyrot (מִמֹּסֵרוֹת / mi'mo'sey'rot) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~B'ney-Ya'aqan (בִּבְנֵי יַעֲקָן / biv'ney-ya'a'qan)

and they journeyed from Moseyrot, and they camped in B'ney-Ya'aqan,

32 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~B'ney-Ya'aqan (מִבְּנֵי יַעֲקָן / mi'be'ney-ya'a'qan) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Hhor-Hagidgad (בְּחֹר הַגִּדְגָּד / bê'hhor ha'gid'gad)

and they journeyed from B'ney-Ya'aqan, and they camped in Hhor-Hagidgad,

33 and~they(m)~will~JOURNEY(Verb) (וַיִּסְעוּ / wai'yis'u) from~Hhor-Hagidgad (מֵחֹר הַגִּדְגָּד / mey'hhor ha'gid'gad) and~they(m)~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיַּחֲנוּ / wai'ya'hha'nu) in~Yatvatah (בְּיָטְבָתָה / bê'yat'va'tah)

and they journeyed from Hhor-Hagidgad, and they camped in Yatvatah,
