Deuteronomy Chapter 11

1 and~you(ms)~did~LOVE(Verb) (וְאָהַבְתָּ / wê'a'hav'ta) AT (אֵת / eyt) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) and~you(ms)~did~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (וְשָׁמַרְתָּ / wê'sha'mar'ta) CHARGE~him (מִשְׁמַרְתּוֹ / mish'mar'to) and~CUSTOM~s~him (וְחֻקֹּתָיו / wê'hhu'qo'taw) and~DECISION~s~him (וּמִשְׁפָּטָיו / u'mish'pa'taw) and~DIRECTIVE~s~him (וּמִצְוֹתָיו / u'mits'o'taw) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~DAY~s (הַיָּמִים / hai'ya'mim)

and you will love YHWH your Elohiym, and you will safeguard his charge, and his decisions, and his directives all the days,

2 and~you(mp)~did~KNOW(Verb) (וִידַעְתֶּם / wi'da'tem) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) NOT (לֹא / lo) AT (אֶת / et) SON~s~you(mp) (בְּנֵיכֶם / bê'ney'khem) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) NOT (לֹא / lo) they~did~KNOW(Verb) (יָדְעוּ / yad'u) and~WHICH (וַאֲשֶׁר / wa'a'sher) NOT (לֹא / lo) they~did~SEE(Verb) (רָאוּ / ra'u) AT (אֶת / et) DISCIPLINE (מוּסַר / mu'sar) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem) AT (אֶת / et) MAGNIFICENCE~him (גָּדְלוֹ / gad'lo) AT (אֶת / et) HAND~him (יָדוֹ / ya'do) the~FORCEFUL (הַחֲזָקָה / ha'hha'za'qah) and~ARM~him (וּזְרֹעוֹ / uz'ro'u) the~EXTEND(Verb)~ed(fs) (הַנְּטוּיָה / han'tu'yah)

and you will know today, that not with your sons who did not know and did not see the discipline of YHWH your Elohiym, his magnificence, his forceful hand and his extended arm,

3 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) SIGN~s~him (אֹתֹתָיו / o'to'taw) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) WORK~s~him (מַעֲשָׂיו / ma'a'saw) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) in~MIDST (בְּתוֹךְ / bê'tokh) Mits'rayim (מִצְרָיִם / mits'ra'yim) to~Paroh (לְפַרְעֹה / lê'phar'oh) KING (מֶלֶךְ / me'lekh) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) and~to~ALL (וּלְכָל / ul'khol) LAND~him (אַרְצוֹ / ar'tso)

and his signs, and his works, which he did in the midst of Mits'rayim to Paroh the king of Mits'rayim and to all his land,

4 and~WHICH (וַאֲשֶׁר / wa'a'sher) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) to~FORCE (לְחֵיל / lê'hheyl) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) to~HORSE~s~him (לְסוּסָיו / lê'su'saw) and~to~VEHICLE~him (וּלְרִכְבּוֹ / ul'rikh'bo) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~make~FLOAT(Verb) (הֵצִיף / hey'tsiph) AT (אֶת / et) WATER~s2 (מֵי / mey) SEA (יַם / yam) REEDS (סוּף / suph) [974] UPON (עַל / al) FACE~s~them(m) (פְּנֵיהֶם / pê'ney'hem) in~>~PURSUE(Verb)~them(m) (בְּרָדְפָם / bê'rad'pham) AFTER~you(mp) (אַחֲרֵיכֶם / a'hha'rey'khem) and~he~will~much~PERISH(Verb)~them(m) (וַיְאַבְּדֵם / wa'ye'a'be'deym) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) UNTIL (עַד / ad) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh)

and what he did to the forces of Mits'rayim, to his horses and to his vehicles, when he made the waters of the sea of reeds float upon their faces in their pursuing after you, and YHWH caused them to perish until this day,

5 and~WHICH (וַאֲשֶׁר / wa'a'sher) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) in~the~WILDERNESS (בַּמִּדְבָּר / ba'mid'bar) UNTIL (עַד / ad) in~>~COME(Verb)~you(mp) (בֹּאֲכֶם / bo'a'khem) UNTIL (עַד / ad) the~AREA (הַמָּקוֹם / ha'ma'qom) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh)

and what he did to you in the wilderness until your coming unto this area,

6 and~WHICH (וַאֲשֶׁר / wa'a'sher) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) to~Datan (לְדָתָן / lê'da'tan) and~to~Aviram (וְלַאֲבִירָם / wê'la'a'vi'ram) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Eli'av (אֱלִיאָב / e'li'av) SON (בֶּן / ben) Re'uven (רְאוּבֵן / rê'u'veyn) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) she~did~PART(Verb) (פָּצְתָה / pats'tah) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) AT (אֶת / et) MOUTH~her (פִּיהָ / pi'ah) and~she~will~SWALLOW(Verb)~them(m) (וַתִּבְלָעֵם / wa'tiv'la'eym) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) HOUSE~s~them(m) (בָּתֵּיהֶם / ba'tey'hem) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) TENT~s~them(m) (אָהֳלֵיהֶם / a'ha'ley'hem) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~SUBSTANCE (הַיְקוּם / hai'qum) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~FOOT~s~them(m) (בְּרַגְלֵיהֶם / bê'rag'ley'hem) in~INSIDE (בְּקֶרֶב / bê'qe'rev) ALL (כָּל / kol) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl)

and what he did to Datan and to Aviram, the sons of Eli'av the son of Re'uven, when the land parted her mouth and she swallowed them, and with their houses and their tents, and all the substance which was at their feet, in the inside of all Yisra'eyl.

7 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) EYE~s2~you(mp) (עֵינֵיכֶם / ey'ney'khem) the~SEE(Verb)~ing(fp) (הָרֹאֹת / ha'ro'ot) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) WORK (מַעֲשֵׂה / ma'a'seyh) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) the~GREAT (הַגָּדֹל / ha'ga'dol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah)

Given that your eyes are seeing all the great work of YHWH which he did,

8 and~you(mp)~did~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (וּשְׁמַרְתֶּם / ush'mar'tem) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~DIRECTIVE (הַמִּצְוָה / ha'mits'wah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) much~DIRECT(Verb)~ing(ms)~you(ms) (מְצַוְּךָ / mê'tsa'we'kha) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) to~THAT (לְמַעַן / lê'ma'an) you(mp)~will~SEIZE(Verb) (תֶּחֶזְקוּ / te'hhez'qu) and~you(mp)~did~COME(Verb) (וּבָאתֶם / u'va'tem) and~you(mp)~did~POSSESS(Verb) (וִירִשְׁתֶּם / wi'rish'tem) AT (אֶת / et) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YOU(MP) (אַתֶּם / a'tem) CROSS.OVER(Verb)~ing(mp) (עֹבְרִים / ov'rim) THERE~unto (שָׁמָּה / sha'mah) to~>~POSSESS(Verb)~her (לְרִשְׁתָּהּ / lê'rish'tah)

and you will safeguard all the directives which I am directing you today, so that you will seize, and you will come, and you will possess the land which you are crossing over unto to possess her,

9 and~to~THAT (וּלְמַעַן / ul'ma'an) you(mp)~will~make~PROLONG(Verb) (תַּאֲרִיכוּ / ta'a'ri'khu) DAY~s (יָמִים / ya'mim) UPON (עַל / al) the~GROUND (הָאֲדָמָה / ha'a'da'mah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~be~SWEAR(Verb) (נִשְׁבַּע / nish'ba) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) to~FATHER~s~you(mp) (לַאֲבֹתֵיכֶם / la'a'vo'tey'khem) to~>~GIVE(Verb) (לָתֵת / la'teyt) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) and~to~SEED~them(m) (וּלְזַרְעָם / ul'zar'am) LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) ISSUE(Verb)~ing(fs) (זָבַת / za'vat) FAT (חָלָב / hha'lav) and~HONEY (וּדְבָשׁ / ud'vash)

and so that you will prolong the days upon the ground, which YHWH swore to your fathers to give them and to their seed, a land issuing fat and honey.

10 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YOU(MS) (אַתָּה / a'tah) he~did~COME(Verb) (בָא / va) THERE~unto (שָׁמָּה / sha'mah) to~>~POSSESS(Verb)~her (לְרִשְׁתָּהּ / lê'rish'tah) NOT (לֹא / lo) like~LAND (כְאֶרֶץ / khê'e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) SHE (הִוא / hi) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(mp)~did~GO.OUT(Verb) (יְצָאתֶם / yê'tsa'tem) from~THERE (מִשָּׁם / mi'sham) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~will~SOW(Verb) (תִּזְרַע / tiz'ra) AT (אֶת / et) SEED~you(ms) (זַרְעֲךָ / zar'a'kha) and~you(ms)~did~make~DRINK(Verb) (וְהִשְׁקִיתָ / wê'hish'qi'ta) in~FOOT~you(ms) (בְרַגְלְךָ / vê'rag'le'kha) like~GARDEN (כְּגַן / kê'gan) the~GREEN (הַיָּרָק / hai'ya'raq)

Given that the land which you came unto there to possess her, she is not like the land of Mits'rayim which you went out from there, where you sowed your seed and you made drink with your foot, like the green garden,

11 and~the~LAND (וְהָאָרֶץ / wê'ha'a'rets) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YOU(MP) (אַתֶּם / a'tem) CROSS.OVER(Verb)~ing(mp) (עֹבְרִים / ov'rim) THERE~unto (שָׁמָּה / sha'mah) to~>~POSSESS(Verb)~her (לְרִשְׁתָּהּ / lê'rish'tah) LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) HILL~s (הָרִים / ha'rim) and~LEVEL.VALLEY~s (וּבְקָעֹת / uv'qa'ot) to~PRECIPITATION (לִמְטַר / lim'tar) the~SKY~s2 (הַשָּׁמַיִם / ha'sha'ma'yim) you(ms)~will~GULP(Verb) (תִּשְׁתֶּה / tish'teh) WATER~s2 (מָּיִם / ma'yim)

and the land which you are crossing over unto there to possess her, a land of hills and level valleys, by the precipitation of the skies you will gulp waters.

12 LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) SEEK(Verb)~ing(ms) (דֹּרֵשׁ / do'reysh) AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) CONTINUALLY (תָּמִיד / ta'mid) EYE~s2 (עֵינֵי / ey'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) in~~her (בָּהּ / bah) from~ORIGIN (מֵרֵשִׁית / mey'rey'shit) the~YEAR (הַשָּׁנָה / ha'sha'nah) and~UNTIL (וְעַד / wê'ad) END (אַחֲרִית / a'hha'rit) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah)

A land which YHWH your Elohiym is seeking her, continually the eyes of YHWH your Elohiym are on her, from the origin of the year and until the end of the year,

and it will come to pass, if you will certainly listen to my directives which I directed you today, to love YHWH your Elohiym and to serve him with all your heart and with all your soul,

14 and~I~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַתִּי / wê'na'ta'ti) PRECIPITATION (מְטַר / mê'tar) LAND~you(mp) (אַרְצְכֶם / ar'tse'khem) in~APPOINTED.TIME~him (בְּעִתּוֹ / bê'i'to) FIRST.RAIN (יוֹרֶה / yo'reh) and~LATE.RAIN (וּמַלְקוֹשׁ / u'mal'qosh) and~you(ms)~did~GATHER(Verb) (וְאָסַפְתָּ / wê'a'saph'ta) CEREAL~you(ms) (דְגָנֶךָ / dê'ga'ne'kha) and~FRESH.WINE~you(ms) (וְתִירֹשְׁךָ / wê'ti'rosh'kha) and~FRESH.OIL~you(ms) (וְיִצְהָרֶךָ / wê'yits'ha're'kha)

and I will give your land precipitation in his appointed time, the first rain and the late rain, and you will gather your cereal, and your fresh wine and your fresh oil,

15 and~I~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַתִּי / wê'na'ta'ti) HERB (עֵשֶׂב / ey'sev) in~FIELD~you(ms) (בְּשָׂדְךָ / bê'sad'kha) to~BEAST~you(ms) (לִבְהֶמְתֶּךָ / liv'hem'te'kha) and~you(ms)~did~EAT(Verb) (וְאָכַלְתָּ / wê'a'khal'ta) and~you(ms)~did~BE.SATISFIED(Verb) (וְשָׂבָעְתָּ / wê'sa'va'ta)

and I will give herbs in your field for your beast, and you will eat and you will be satisfied.

16 !(mp)~be~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (הִשָּׁמְרוּ / hi'sham'ru) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) OTHERWISE (פֶּן / pen) he~will~SPREAD.WIDE(Verb) (יִפְתֶּה / yiph'teh) HEART~you(mp) (לְבַבְכֶם / lê'va've'khem) and~you(mp)~did~TURN.ASIDE(Verb) (וְסַרְתֶּם / wê'sar'tem) and~you(mp)~will~SERVE(Verb) (וַעֲבַדְתֶּם / wa'a'vad'tem) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) OTHER~s (אֲחֵרִים / a'hhey'rim) and~you(mp)~did~self~BEND.DOWN(Verb) (וְהִשְׁתַּחֲוִיתֶם / wê'hish'ta'hha'wi'tem) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem)

Safeguard yourselves, otherwise your heart will spread wide, and you will turn aside, and you will serve other Elohiym, and you will bend yourselves down to them,

17 and~he~did~FLARE.UP(Verb) (וְחָרָה / wê'hha'rah) NOSE (אַף / aph) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) in~~you(mp) (בָּכֶם / ba'khem) and~he~did~STOP(Verb) (וְעָצַר / wê'a'tsar) AT (אֶת / et) the~SKY~s2 (הַשָּׁמַיִם / ha'sha'ma'yim) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) PRECIPITATION (מָטָר / ma'tar) and~the~GROUND (וְהָאֲדָמָה / wê'ha'a'da'mah) NOT (לֹא / lo) she~will~GIVE(Verb) (תִתֵּן / ti'teyn) AT (אֶת / et) PRODUCT~her (יְבוּלָהּ / yê'vu'lah) and~you(mp)~did~PERISH(Verb) (וַאֲבַדְתֶּם / wa'a'vad'tem) QUICKLY (מְהֵרָה / mê'hey'rah) from~UPON (מֵעַל / mey'al) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) the~FUNCTIONAL (הַטֹּבָה / ha'to'vah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) GIVE(Verb)~ing(ms) (נֹתֵן / no'teyn) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem)

and the nose of YHWH flared up with you, and he will stop the skies and precipitation will not exist, and the ground will not give her produce, and you will perish quickly from upon the functional land which YHWH is giving to you,
