Exodus Chapter 8

1 (5) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) !(ms)~SAY(Verb) (אֱמֹר / e'mor) TO (אֶל / el) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) !(ms)~EXTEND(Verb) (נְטֵה / nê'teyh) AT (אֶת / et) HAND~you(ms) (יָדְךָ / yad'kha) in~BRANCH~you(ms) (בְּמַטֶּךָ / bê'ma'te'kha) UPON (עַל / al) the~RIVER~s (הַנְּהָרֹת / han'ha'rot) UPON (עַל / al) the~STREAM~s (הַיְאֹרִים / hai'o'rim) and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) the~POOL~s (הָאֲגַמִּים / ha'a'ga'mim) and~!(ms)~make~GO.UP(Verb) (וְהַעַל / wê'ha'al) AT (אֶת / et) the~FROG~s (הַצְפַרְדְּעִים / ha'tse'phar'de'im) UPON (עַל / al) LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרָיִם / mits'ra'yim)

and YHWH said to Mosheh, say to Aharon, extend your hand, with your branch, upon the rivers, upon the streams and upon the pools, and make the frogs upon the land of Mits'rayim go up,

2 (6) and~he~will~EXTEND(Verb) (וַיֵּט / wai'yeyt) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) AT (אֶת / et) HAND~him (יָדוֹ / ya'do) UPON (עַל / al) WATER~s2 (מֵימֵי / mey'mey) Mits'rayim (מִצְרָיִם / mits'ra'yim) and~she~will~GO.UP(Verb) (וַתַּעַל / wa'ta'al) the~FROG (הַצְּפַרְדֵּעַ / hats'phar'dey'a) and~she~will~much~COVER.OVER(Verb) (וַתְּכַס / wat'khas) AT (אֶת / et) LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרָיִם / mits'ra'yim)

and Aharon extended his hand upon the waters of Mits'rayim, and the frogs went up and she covered over the land of Mits'rayim,

3 (7) and~they(m)~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעֲשׂוּ / wai'ya'a'su) SO (כֵן / kheyn) the~MAGICIAN~s (הַחַרְטֻמִּים / ha'hhar'tu'mim) in~SECRET~s~them(m) (בְּלָטֵיהֶם / bê'la'tey'hem) and~they(m)~will~GO.UP(Verb) (וַיַּעֲלוּ / wai'ya'a'lu) [273] AT (אֶת / et) the~FROG~s (הַצְפַרְדְּעִים / ha'tse'phar'de'im) UPON (עַל / al) LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרָיִם / mits'ra'yim)

and the magicians did so with their secrets, and frogs went up upon the land of Mits'rayim,

4 (8) and~he~will~CALL.OUT(Verb) (וַיִּקְרָא / wai'yiq'ra) Paroh (פַרְעֹה / phar'oh) to~Mosheh (לְמֹשֶׁה / lê'mo'sheh) and~to~Aharon (וּלְאַהֲרֹן / ul'a'ha'ron) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) !(mp)~make~INTERCEDE(Verb) (הַעְתִּירוּ / ha'ti'ru) TO (אֶל / el) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~he~will~make~TURN.ASIDE(Verb) (וְיָסֵר / wê'ya'seyr) the~FROG~s (הַצְפַרְדְּעִים / ha'tse'phar'de'im) FROM~me (מִמֶּנִּי / mi'me'ni) and~from~PEOPLE~me (וּמֵעַמִּי / u'mey'a'mi) and~I~will~much~SEND(Verb)~& (וַאֲשַׁלְּחָה / wa'a'shal'hhah) AT (אֶת / et) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) and~they(m)~will~SACRIFICE(Verb) (וְיִזְבְּחוּ / wê'yiz'be'hhu) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

and Paroh called out to Mosheh and to Aharon and he said, intercede to YHWH and he will make the frogs turn aside from me and from my people, and I will send the people and they will sacrifice to YHWH,

5 (9) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~Paroh (לְפַרְעֹה / lê'phar'oh) !(ms)~self~DECORATE(Verb) (הִתְפָּאֵר / hit'pa'eyr) UPON~me (עָלַי / a'lai) [274] to~HOW.LONG (לְמָתַי / lê'ma'tai) I~will~make~INTERCEDE(Verb) (אַעְתִּיר / a'tir) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) and~to~SERVANT~s~you(ms) (וְלַעֲבָדֶיךָ / wê'la'a'va'dey'kha) and~to~PEOPLE~you(ms) (וּלְעַמְּךָ / ul'am'kha) to~>~make~CUT(Verb) (לְהַכְרִית / lê'hakh'rit) the~FROG~s (הַצְפַרְדְּעִים / ha'tse'phar'de'im) FROM~you(ms) (מִמְּךָ / mim'kha) and~from~HOUSE~s~you(ms) (וּמִבָּתֶּיךָ / u'mi'ba'tey'kha) ONLY (רַק / raq) in~the~STREAM (בַּיְאֹר / bai'or) they(f)~will~be~REMAIN(Verb) (תִּשָּׁאַרְנָה / ti'sha'ar'nah)

and Mosheh said to Paroh, decorate yourself upon me for how long I will intercede for you and for your servants and for your people, to make the frogs cut from you and from your houses, only in the streams will they remain,

6 (10) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) to~TOMORROW (לְמָחָר / lê'ma'hhar) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) like~WORD~you(ms) (כִּדְבָרְךָ / kid'var'kha) to~THAT (לְמַעַן / lê'ma'an) you(ms)~will~KNOW(Verb) (תֵּדַע / tey'da) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) WITHOUT (אֵין / eyn) like~YHWH (כַּיהוָה / ka'YHWH) Elohiym~us (אֱלֹהֵינוּ / e'lo'hey'nu)

and he said, tomorrow, and he said, it will be like your word so that you will know that there is none like YHWH our Elohiym,

7 (11) and~they~did~TURN.ASIDE(Verb) (וְסָרוּ / wê'sa'ru) the~FROG~s (הַצְפַרְדְּעִים / ha'tse'phar'de'im) FROM~you(ms) (מִמְּךָ / mim'kha) and~from~HOUSE~s~you(ms) (וּמִבָּתֶּיךָ / u'mi'ba'tey'kha) and~from~SERVANT~s~you(ms) (וּמֵעֲבָדֶיךָ / u'mey'a'va'dey'kha) and~from~PEOPLE~you(ms) (וּמֵעַמֶּךָ / u'mey'a'me'kha) ONLY (רַק / raq) in~the~STREAM (בַּיְאֹר / bai'or) they(f)~will~be~REMAIN(Verb) (תִּשָּׁאַרְנָה / ti'sha'ar'nah)

and the frogs will turn aside from you and from your houses and from your servants and from your people, only in the stream will they remain,

8 (12) and~he~will~GO.OUT(Verb) (וַיֵּצֵא / wai'yey'tsey) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) and~Aharon (וְאַהֲרֹן / wê'a'ha'ron) from~WITH (מֵעִם / mey'im) Paroh (פַּרְעֹה / par'oh) and~he~will~CRY.OUT(Verb) (וַיִּצְעַק / wai'yits'aq) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) TO (אֶל / el) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) UPON (עַל / al) WORD (דְּבַר / dê'var) [275] the~FROG~s (הַצְפַרְדְּעִים / ha'tse'phar'de'im) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~PLACE(Verb) (שָׂם / sam) to~Paroh (לְפַרְעֹה / lê'phar'oh)

and Mosheh went out, and Aharon, away from Paroh, and Mosheh cried out to YHWH because of the word of the frogs which he placed for Paroh,

9 (13) and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) like~WORD (כִּדְבַר / kid'var) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) and~they(m)~will~DIE(Verb) (וַיָּמֻתוּ / wai'ya'mu'tu) the~FROG~s (הַצְפַרְדְּעִים / ha'tse'phar'de'im) FROM (מִן / min) the~HOUSE~s (הַבָּתִּים / ha'ba'tim) FROM (מִן / min) the~COURTYARD~s (הַחֲצֵרֹת / ha'hha'tsey'rot) and~FROM (וּמִן / u'min) the~FIELD~s (הַשָּׂדֹת / ha'sa'dot)

and YHWH did just like the word of Mosheh, and the frogs died, from the houses, from the courtyards, and from the fields,

10 (14) and~they(m)~will~PILE.UP(Verb) (וַיִּצְבְּרוּ / wai'yits'be'ru) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) SLIME~s (חֳמָרִם / hha'ma'rim) SLIME~s (חֳמָרִם / hha'ma'rim) [276] and~she~will~STINK(Verb) (וַתִּבְאַשׁ / wa'tiv'ash) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets)

and they piled them up as much slime, and the land stank,

11 (15) and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) Paroh (פַּרְעֹה / par'oh) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) she~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיְתָה / hai'tah) the~RESPITE (הָרְוָחָה / har'wa'hhah) and~>~make~BE.HEAVY(Verb) (וְהַכְבֵּד / wê'hakh'beyd) AT (אֶת / et) HEART~him (לִבּוֹ / li'bo) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~did~HEAR(Verb) (שָׁמַע / sha'ma) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דִּבֶּר / di'ber) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and Paroh saw that the respite existed, and his heart was made heavy, and he did not listen to them just as YHWH spoke,

12 (16) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) !(ms)~SAY(Verb) (אֱמֹר / e'mor) TO (אֶל / el) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) !(ms)~EXTEND(Verb) (נְטֵה / nê'teyh) AT (אֶת / et) BRANCH~you(ms) (מַטְּךָ / mat'kha) and~!(ms)~make~HIT(Verb) (וְהַךְ / wê'hakh) AT (אֶת / et) DIRT (עֲפַר / a'phar) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) to~GNAT~s (לְכִנִּם / lê'khi'nim) in~ALL (בְּכָל / bê'khol) LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרָיִם / mits'ra'yim)

and YHWH said to Mosheh, say to Aharon, extend your branch and attack the dirt of the land and he will exist as gnats in all the land of Mits'rayim,

13 (17) and~they(m)~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעֲשׂוּ / wai'ya'a'su) SO (כֵן / kheyn) and~he~will~EXTEND(Verb) (וַיֵּט / wai'yeyt) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) AT (אֶת / et) HAND~him (יָדוֹ / ya'do) in~BRANCH~him (בְמַטֵּהוּ / vê'ma'tey'hu) and~he~will~make~HIT(Verb) (וַיַּךְ / wai'yakh) AT (אֶת / et) DIRT (עֲפַר / a'phar) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) and~she~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַתְּהִי / wat'hi) the~GNAT~s (הַכִּנָּם / ha'ki'nam) [277] in~the~HUMAN (בָּאָדָם / ba'a'dam) and~in~the~BEAST (וּבַבְּהֵמָה / u'va'be'hey'mah) ALL (כָּל / kol) DIRT (עֲפַר / a'phar) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) he~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיָה / hai'yah) GNAT~s (כִנִּים / khi'nim) in~ALL (בְּכָל / bê'khol) LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרָיִם / mits'ra'yim)

and they did so, and Aharon extended his hand, with his branch, and he attacked the dirt of the land and the gnats existed in the human and in the beast, all the dirt of the land had existed as gnats in all the land of Mits'rayim,

14 (18) and~they(m)~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעֲשׂוּ / wai'ya'a'su) SO (כֵן / kheyn) the~MAGICIAN~s (הַחַרְטֻמִּים / ha'hhar'tu'mim) in~SECRET~s~them(m) (בְּלָטֵיהֶם / bê'la'tey'hem) to~>~make~GO.OUT(Verb) (לְהוֹצִיא / lê'ho'tsi) AT (אֶת / et) the~GNAT~s (הַכִּנִּים / ha'ki'nim) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) they~did~BE.ABLE(Verb) (יָכֹלוּ / ya'kho'lu) and~she~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַתְּהִי / wat'hi) the~GNAT~s (הַכִּנָּם / ha'ki'nam) [278] in~the~HUMAN (בָּאָדָם / ba'a'dam) and~in~the~BEAST (וּבַבְּהֵמָה / u'va'be'hey'mah)

and the magicians did so with their secrets, to make the gnats go out, and they were not able, and the gnats existed in the human and in the beast,

15 (19) and~they(m)~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמְרוּ / wai'yom'ru) the~MAGICIAN~s (הַחַרְטֻמִּם / ha'hhar'tu'mim) TO (אֶל / el) Paroh (פַּרְעֹה / par'oh) FINGER (אֶצְבַּע / ets'ba) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) SHE (הִוא / hi) and~he~will~SEIZE(Verb) (וַיֶּחֱזַק / wa'ye'hhe'zaq) HEART (לֵב / leyv) [279] Paroh (פַּרְעֹה / par'oh) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~did~HEAR(Verb) (שָׁמַע / sha'ma) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דִּבֶּר / di'ber) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and the magicians said to Paroh, this is the finger of Elohiym, and he seized the heart of Paroh and he did not listen to them, just as YHWH spoke,

16 (20) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) !(mp)~make~DEPART.EARLY(Verb) (הַשְׁכֵּם / hash'keym) in~the~MORNING (בַּבֹּקֶר / ba'bo'qer) and~!(ms)~self~STAND.UP(Verb) (וְהִתְיַצֵּב / wê'hit'ya'tseyv) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) Paroh (פַרְעֹה / phar'oh) LOOK (הִנֵּה / hin'neyh) GO.OUT(Verb)~ing(ms) (יוֹצֵא / yo'tsey) the~WATER~s2~unto (הַמָּיְמָה / ha'mai'mah) and~you(ms)~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמַרְתָּ / wê'a'mar'ta) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) IN.THIS.WAY (כֹּה / koh) he~did~SAY(Verb) (אָמַר / a'mar) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) !(ms)~much~SEND(Verb) (שַׁלַּח / sha'lahh) PEOPLE~me (עַמִּי / a'mi) and~they(m)~will~SERVE(Verb)~me (וְיַעַבְדֻנִי / wê'ya'av'du'ni)

and YHWH said to Mosheh, depart early in the morning and stand yourself up to the face of Paroh, look, he is going out unto the waters, and you will say to him in this way, YHWH said, send my people and they will serve me.

17 (21) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) IF (אִם / im) WITHOUT~you(ms) (אֵינְךָ / eyn'kha) much~SEND(Verb)~ing(ms) (מְשַׁלֵּחַ / mê'sha'ley'ahh) AT (אֶת / et) PEOPLE~me (עַמִּי / a'mi) LOOK~me (הִנְנִי / hin'ni) make~SEND(Verb)~ing(ms) (מַשְׁלִיחַ / mash'li'ahh) in~~you(ms) (בְּךָ / bê'kha) and~in~SERVANT~s~you(ms) (וּבַעֲבָדֶיךָ / u'va'a'va'dey'kha) and~in~PEOPLE~you(ms) (וּבְעַמְּךָ / uv'am'kha) and~in~HOUSE~s~you(ms) (וּבְבָתֶּיךָ / uv'va'tey'kha) AT (אֶת / et) the~HORDE (הֶעָרֹב / he'a'rov) and~they~did~FILL(Verb) (וּמָלְאוּ / u'mal'u) HOUSE~s (בָּתֵּי / ba'tey) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) AT (אֶת / et) the~HORDE (הֶעָרֹב / he'a'rov) and~ALSO (וְגַם / wê'gam) the~GROUND (הָאֲדָמָה / ha'a'da'mah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) THEY(m) (הֵם / heym) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah)

Instead you are not sending my people, look at me, I am sending the horde in you and in your servants and in your people and in your houses, and the houses of Mits'rayim will be filled with the horde, and also the ground which they are upon,
