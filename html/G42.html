Genesis Chapter 42

1 and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) THERE.IS (יֶשׁ / yesh) GRAIN.SEEDS (שֶׁבֶר / she'ver) in~Mits'rayim (בְּמִצְרָיִם / bê'mits'ra'yim) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) to~SON~s~him (לְבָנָיו / lê'va'naw) to~WHAT (לָמָּה / la'mah) you(mp)~will~self~SEE(Verb) (תִּתְרָאוּ / tit'ra'u)

and Ya'aqov saw that there was barley in Mits'rayim and Ya'aqov said to his sons, why do you look at yourselves,

2 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) LOOK (הִנֵּה / hin'neyh) I~did~HEAR(Verb) (שָׁמַעְתִּי / sha'ma'ti) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) THERE.IS (יֶשׁ / yesh) GRAIN.SEEDS (שֶׁבֶר / she'ver) in~Mits'rayim (בְּמִצְרָיִם / bê'mits'ra'yim) !(mp)~GO.DOWN(Verb) (רְדוּ / rê'du) THERE~unto (שָׁמָּה / sha'mah) and~!(mp)~EXCHANGE(Verb) (וְשִׁבְרוּ / wê'shiv'ru) to~~us (לָנוּ / la'nu) from~THERE (מִשָּׁם / mi'sham) and~we~will~LIVE(Verb) (וְנִחְיֶה / wê'nihh'yeh) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) we~will~DIE(Verb) (נָמוּת / na'mut)

and he said, look, I heard that there is barley in Mits'rayim, go down unto there and exchange for us from there and we will live and we will not die,

3 and~they(m)~will~GO.DOWN(Verb) (וַיֵּרְדוּ / wai'yeyr'du) BROTHER~s (אֲחֵי / a'hhey) Yoseph (יוֹסֵף / yo'seyph) TEN (עֲשָׂרָה / a'sa'rah) to~>~EXCHANGE(Verb) (לִשְׁבֹּר / lish'bor) GRAIN (בָּר / bar) from~Mits'rayim (מִמִּצְרָיִם / mi'mits'ra'yim)

and the ten brothers of Yoseph went down to exchange grain from Mits'rayim,

4 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) Binyamin (בִּנְיָמִין / bin'ya'min) BROTHER~of (אֲחִי / a'hhi) Yoseph (יוֹסֵף / yo'seyph) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~did~SEND(Verb) (שָׁלַח / sha'lahh) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) AT (אֶת / et) BROTHER~s~him (אֶחָיו / e'hhaw) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~SAY(Verb) (אָמַר / a'mar) OTHERWISE (פֶּן / pen) he~will~MEET(Verb)~us (יִקְרָאֶנּוּ / yiq'ra'e'nu) HARM (אָסוֹן / a'son)

and Binyamin, brother of Yoseph, Ya'aqov did not send with his brothers given that he said, otherwise harm will meet us,

5 and~they(m)~will~COME(Verb) (וַיָּבֹאוּ / wai'ya'vo'u) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) to~>~EXCHANGE(Verb) (לִשְׁבֹּר / lish'bor) in~MIDST (בְּתוֹךְ / bê'tokh) the~COME(Verb)~ing(mp) (הַבָּאִים / ha'ba'im) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיָה / hai'yah) the~HUNGER (הָרָעָב / ha'ra'av) in~LAND (בְּאֶרֶץ / bê'e'rets) Kena'an (כְּנָעַן / kê'na'an)

and the sons of Yisra'eyl came to exchange in the midst of the ones coming given that the hunger existed in the land of Kena'an,

6 and~Yoseph (וְיוֹסֵף / wê'yo'seyph) HE (הוּא / hu) the~GOVERNOR (הַשַּׁלִּיט / ha'sha'lit) UPON (עַל / al) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) HE (הוּא / hu) the~make~EXCHANGE(Verb)~ing(ms) (הַמַּשְׁבִּיר / ha'mash'bir) to~ALL (לְכָל / lê'khol) PEOPLE (עַם / am) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) and~they(m)~will~COME(Verb) (וַיָּבֹאוּ / wai'ya'vo'u) BROTHER~s (אֲחֵי / a'hhey) Yoseph (יוֹסֵף / yo'seyph) and~they(m)~will~self~BEND.DOWN(Verb) (וַיִּשְׁתַּחֲווּ / wai'yish'ta'hha'wu) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) NOSE~s2 (אַפַּיִם / a'pa'yim) LAND~unto (אָרְצָה / ar'tsah)

and Yoseph was the governor upon the land making exchange to all the people of the land and the brothers of Yoseph came and they bent themselves down to him, nostrils unto the land,

7 and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) Yoseph (יוֹסֵף / yo'seyph) AT (אֶת / et) BROTHER~s~him (אֶחָיו / e'hhaw) and~he~will~make~RECOGNIZE(Verb)~them(m) (וַיַּכִּרֵם / wai'yi'ki'reym) and~he~will~self~RECOGNIZE(Verb) (וַיִּתְנַכֵּר / wai'yit'na'keyr) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵיהֶם / a'ley'hem) and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) AT~them(m) (אִתָּם / i'tam) HARD~s (קָשׁוֹת / qa'shot) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) from~WITHOUT (מֵאַיִן / mey'a'yin) you(mp)~did~COME(Verb) (בָּאתֶם / ba'tem) and~they(m)~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמְרוּ / wai'yom'ru) from~LAND (מֵאֶרֶץ / mey'e'rets) Kena'an (כְּנַעַן / kê'na'an) to~>~EXCHANGE(Verb) (לִשְׁבָּר / lish'bar) FOODSTUFF (אֹכֶל / o'khel)

and Yoseph saw his brothers and he recognized them and he made himself unrecognizable to them and he spoke to them hard, from where did you come, and they said, from the land of Kena'an to exchange foodstuff,

8 and~he~will~make~RECOGNIZE(Verb) (וַיַּכֵּר / wai'ya'keyr) Yoseph (יוֹסֵף / yo'seyph) AT (אֶת / et) BROTHER~s~him (אֶחָיו / e'hhaw) and~THEY(m) (וְהֵם / wê'heym) NOT (לֹא / lo) they~will~RECOGNIZE(Verb)~him (הִכִּרֻהוּ / hi'ki'ru'hu)

and Yoseph recognized his brothers and they did not recognize him,

9 and~he~will~REMEMBER(Verb) (וַיִּזְכֹּר / wai'yiz'kor) Yoseph (יוֹסֵף / yo'seyph) AT (אֵת / eyt) the~DREAM~s (הַחֲלֹמוֹת / ha'hha'lo'mot) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~DREAM(Verb) (חָלַם / hha'lam) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) much~TREAD.ABOUT(Verb)~ing(mp) (מְרַגְּלִים / mê'rag'lim) YOU(MP) (אַתֶּם / a'tem) to~>~SEE(Verb) (לִרְאוֹת / lir'ot) AT (אֶת / et) NAKEDNESS (עֶרְוַת / er'wat) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) you(mp)~did~COME(Verb) (בָּאתֶם / ba'tem)

and Yoseph remembered the dreams which he dreamed to them and he said to them, you are spies, you came to see the nakedness of the land,

10 and~they(m)~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמְרוּ / wai'yom'ru) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) NOT (לֹא / lo) LORD~me (אֲדֹנִי / a'do'ni) and~SERVANT~s~you(ms) (וַעֲבָדֶיךָ / wa'a'va'dey'kha) they~did~COME(Verb) (בָּאוּ / ba'u) to~>~EXCHANGE(Verb) (לִשְׁבָּר / lish'bar) FOODSTUFF (אֹכֶל / o'khel)

and they said to him, no my lord and your servants had come to exchange foodstuff.

11 ALL~us (כֻּלָּנוּ / ku'la'nu) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) WE (נָחְנוּ / nahh'nu) BASE~s (כֵּנִים / key'nim) WE (אֲנַחְנוּ / a'nahh'nu) NOT (לֹא / lo) they~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיוּ / hai'u) SERVANT~s~you(ms) (עֲבָדֶיךָ / a'va'dey'kha) much~TREAD.ABOUT(Verb)~ing(mp) (מְרַגְּלִים / mê'rag'lim)

All of us are the sons of one man, we are bases, your servants do not exist as spies,

12 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) NOT (לֹא / lo) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) NAKEDNESS (עֶרְוַת / er'wat) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) you(mp)~did~COME(Verb) (בָּאתֶם / ba'tem) to~>~SEE(Verb) (לִרְאוֹת / lir'ot)

and he said to them, no, but the nakedness of the land you came to see,

13 and~they(m)~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמְרוּ / wai'yom'ru) TWO (שְׁנֵים / shê'neym) TEN (עָשָׂר / a'sar) SERVANT~s~you(ms) (עֲבָדֶיךָ / a'va'dey'kha) BROTHER~s (אַחִים / a'hhim) WE (אֲנַחְנוּ / a'nahh'nu) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) in~LAND (בְּאֶרֶץ / bê'e'rets) Kena'an (כְּנָעַן / kê'na'an) and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) the~SMALL (הַקָּטֹן / ha'qa'ton) AT (אֶת / et) FATHER~us (אָבִינוּ / a'vi'nu) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) and~the~UNIT (וְהָאֶחָד / wê'ha'e'hhad) WITHOUT~him (אֵינֶנּוּ / ey'ne'nu)

and they said, we are twelve of your servants, we are brothers, sons of one man in the land of Kena'an and look, the small one is with our father today and the one is not with us,

14 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) Yoseph (יוֹסֵף / yo'seyph) HE (הוּא / hu) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I~did~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דִּבַּרְתִּי / di'bar'ti) TO~you(mp) (אֲלֵכֶם / a'ley'khem) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) much~TREAD.ABOUT(Verb)~ing(mp) (מְרַגְּלִים / mê'rag'lim) YOU(MP) (אַתֶּם / a'tem)

and Yoseph said to them, that is what I spoke to you saying, you are spies.

15 in~THIS (בְּזֹאת / bê'zot) you(mp)~will~be~WATCH.OVER(Verb) (תִּבָּחֵנוּ / ti'ba'hhey'nu) LIVING (חֵי / hhey) Paroh (פַרְעֹה / phar'oh) IF (אִם / im) you(mp)~will~GO.OUT(Verb) (תֵּצְאוּ / teyts'u) from~THIS (מִזֶּה / mi'zeh) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) IF (אִם / im) >~COME(Verb) (בְּבוֹא / bê'vo) BROTHER~you(mp) (אֲחִיכֶם / a'hhi'khem) the~SMALL (הַקָּטֹן / ha'qa'ton) TO.THIS.POINT (הֵנָּה / heyn'nah)

In this, you will be watched over, the life of Paroh if you go out from this unless your small brother comes here.

16 !(mp)~SEND(Verb) (שִׁלְחוּ / shil'hhu) from~~you(mp) (מִכֶּם / mi'kem) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) and~he~will~TAKE(Verb) (וְיִקַּח / wê'yi'qahh) AT (אֶת / et) BROTHER~you(mp) (אֲחִיכֶם / a'hhi'khem) and~YOU(MP) (וְאַתֶּם / wê'a'tem) !(mp)~be~TIE.UP(Verb) (הֵאָסְרוּ / hey'as'ru) and~they(m)~will~be~WATCH.OVER(Verb) (וְיִבָּחֲנוּ / wê'yi'ba'hha'nu) WORD~s~you(mp) (דִּבְרֵיכֶם / div'rey'khem) ?~TRUTH (הַאֱמֶת / ha'e'met) AT~you(mp) (אִתְּכֶם / it'khem) and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) NOT (לֹא / lo) LIVING (חֵי / hhey) Paroh (פַרְעֹה / phar'oh) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) much~TREAD.ABOUT(Verb)~ing(mp) (מְרַגְּלִים / mê'rag'lim) YOU(MP) (אַתֶּם / a'tem)

Send from you one and he will take your brother and you will be tied up and your words will be watched over, is truth with you, and if not, the life of Paroh that you are spies,

17 and~he~will~GATHER(Verb) (וַיֶּאֱסֹף / wai'ye'e'soph) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) TO (אֶל / el) CUSTODY (מִשְׁמָר / mi'she'mar) THREE (שְׁלֹשֶׁת / shê'lo'shet) DAY~s (יָמִים / ya'mim)

and he gathered them for a custody of three days,

18 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) Yoseph (יוֹסֵף / yo'seyph) in~the~DAY (בַּיּוֹם / ba'yom) the~THIRD (הַשְּׁלִישִׁי / hash'li'shi) THIS (זֹאת / zot) !(mp)~DO(Verb) (עֲשׂוּ / a'su) and~!(mp)~LIVE(Verb) (וִחְיוּ / wihh'yu) AT (אֶת / et) the~Elohiym (הָאֱלֹהִים / ha'e'lo'him) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) he~did~FEAR(Verb) (יָרֵא / ya'rey)

and Yoseph said to them in the third day, this do and live, I am fearing the Elohiym.

19 IF (אִם / im) BASE~s (כֵּנִים / key'nim) YOU(MP) (אַתֶּם / a'tem) BROTHER~you(mp) (אֲחִיכֶם / a'hhi'khem) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) he~will~be~TIE.UP(Verb) (יֵאָסֵר / yey'a'seyr) in~HOUSE (בְּבֵית / bê'veyt) CUSTODY~you(mp) (מִשְׁמַרְכֶם / mish'mar'khem) and~YOU(MP) (וְאַתֶּם / wê'a'tem) !(mp)~WALK(Verb) (לְכוּ / lê'khu) !(mp)~make~COME(Verb) (הָבִיאוּ / ha'vi'u) GRAIN.SEEDS (שֶׁבֶר / she'ver) FAMINE (רַעֲבוֹן / ra'a'von) [200] HOUSE~s~you(mp) (בָּתֵּיכֶם / ba'tey'khem)

If you are bases, your one brother will be tied up in the house of your custody and you will walk, bring barley to the famine of your house,

20 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) BROTHER~you(mp) (אֲחִיכֶם / a'hhi'khem) the~SMALL (הַקָּטֹן / ha'qa'ton) you(mp)~will~make~COME(Verb) (תָּבִיאוּ / ta'vi'u) TO~me (אֵלַי / ey'lai) and~they(m)~will~be~SECURE(Verb) (וְיֵאָמְנוּ / wê'yey'am'nu) WORD~s~you(mp) (דִבְרֵיכֶם / div'rey'khem) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(mp)~will~DIE(Verb) (תָמוּתוּ / ta'mu'tu) and~they(m)~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעֲשׂוּ / wai'ya'a'su) SO (כֵן / kheyn)

and you will bring to me your small brother than your words will be firm and you will not die and they did so,

21 and~they(m)~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמְרוּ / wai'yom'ru) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) TO (אֶל / el) BROTHER~him (אָחִיו / a'hhiw) NEVERTHELESS (אֲבָל / a'val) GUILT~s (אֲשֵׁמִים / a'shey'mim) WE (אֲנַחְנוּ / a'nahh'nu) UPON (עַל / al) BROTHER~us (אָחִינוּ / a'hhi'nu) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) we~did~SEE(Verb) (רָאִינוּ / ra'i'nu) PERSECUTION (צָרַת / tsa'rat) SOUL~him (נַפְשׁוֹ / naph'sho) in~>~self~PROVIDE.PROTECTION(Verb)~him (בְּהִתְחַנְנוֹ / bê'hit'hhan'no) TO~us (אֵלֵינוּ / ey'ley'nu) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) we~did~HEAR(Verb) (שָׁמָעְנוּ / sha'ma'nu) UPON (עַל / al) SO (כֵּן / keyn) she~did~COME(Verb) (בָּאָה / ba'ah) TO~us (אֵלֵינוּ / ey'ley'nu) the~PERSECUTION (הַצָּרָה / ha'tsa'rah) the~THIS (הַזֹּאת / ha'zot)

and they said each to his brother, nevertheless we are guilty about our brother because we saw the trouble of his soul in his beseeching to us and we did not hear, therefore this trouble came to us,

22 and~he~will~ANSWER(Verb) (וַיַּעַן / wai'ya'an) Re'uven (רְאוּבֵן / rê'u'veyn) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) ?~NOT (הֲלוֹא / ha'lo) I~did~SAY(Verb) (אָמַרְתִּי / a'mar'ti) TO~you(mp) (אֲלֵיכֶם / a'ley'khem) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) DO.NOT (אַל / al) you(mp)~will~FAIL(Verb) (תֶּחֶטְאוּ / te'hhet'u) in~the~BOY (בַיֶּלֶד / va'ye'led) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(mp)~did~HEAR(Verb) (שְׁמַעְתֶּם / shê'ma'tem) and~ALSO (וְגַם / wê'gam) BLOOD~him (דָּמוֹ / da'mo) LOOK (הִנֵּה / hin'neyh) be~SEEK(Verb)~ing(ms) (נִדְרָשׁ / nid'rash)

and Re'uven answered them saying, did I not say to you saying, you will not fail with the boy and you did not hear and also look, his blood is required,
