Deuteronomy Chapter 21

1 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~be~FIND(Verb) (יִמָּצֵא / yi'ma'tsey) DRILLED (חָלָל / hha'lal) in~GROUND (בָּאֲדָמָה / ba'a'da'mah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) GIVE(Verb)~ing(ms) (נֹתֵן / no'teyn) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) to~>~POSSESS(Verb)~her (לְרִשְׁתָּהּ / lê'rish'tah) FALL(Verb)~ing(ms) (נֹפֵל / no'pheyl) in~the~FIELD (בַּשָּׂדֶה / ba'sa'deh) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~did~be~KNOW(Verb) (נוֹדַע / no'da) WHO (מִי / mi) he~did~make~HIT(Verb)~him (הִכָּהוּ / hi'ka'hu)

If one will be found drilled in the ground, which YHWH your Elohiym is giving to you to possess her, falling in the field, it is not known who attacked him,

2 and~they~did~GO.OUT(Verb) (וְיָצְאוּ / wê'yats'u) BEARD~s~you(ms) (זְקֵנֶיךָ / zê'qey'ney'kha) and~DECIDE(Verb)~ing(mp)~you(ms) (וְשֹׁפְטֶיךָ / wê'shoph'tey'kha) and~they~did~MEASURE(Verb) (וּמָדְדוּ / u'mad'du) TO (אֶל / el) the~CITY~s (הֶעָרִים / he'a'rim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) ALL.AROUND~s (סְבִיבֹת / sê'vi'vot) the~DRILLED (הֶחָלָל / he'hha'lal)

then the bearded ones will go out, and your deciders, and they will measure to the cities which are all around the drilled one,

3 and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) the~CITY (הָעִיר / ha'ir) the~NEAR (הַקְּרֹבָה / haq'ro'vah) TO (אֶל / el) the~DRILLED (הֶחָלָל / he'hha'lal) and~they~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקְחוּ / wê'laq'hhu) BEARD~s (זִקְנֵי / ziq'ney) the~CITY (הָעִיר / ha'ir) the~SHE (הַהִוא / ha'hi) HEIFER (עֶגְלַת / eg'lat) CATTLE (בָּקָר / ba'qar) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) NOT (לֹא / lo) SERVE(Verb)~ed(ms) (עֻבַּד / u'bad) in~~her (בָּהּ / bah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) NOT (לֹא / lo) she~did~DRAW(Verb) (מָשְׁכָה / mash'khah) in~YOKE (בְּעֹל / bê'ol)

and it will come to pass, the nearest city to the drilled one, and the bearded ones of that city will take a heifer of the cattle which has not served, which did not draw in a yoke,

4 and~they~did~make~GO.DOWN(Verb) (וְהוֹרִדוּ / wê'ho'ri'du) BEARD~s (זִקְנֵי / ziq'ney) the~CITY (הָעִיר / ha'ir) the~SHE (הַהִוא / ha'hi) AT (אֶת / et) the~HEIFER (הָעֶגְלָה / ha'eg'lah) TO (אֶל / el) WADI (נַחַל / na'hhal) CONSISTENCY (אֵיתָן / ey'tan) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~be~SERVE(Verb) (יֵעָבֵד / yey'a'veyd) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~will~be~SOW(Verb) (יִזָּרֵעַ / yi'za'rey'a) and~they~did~BEHEAD(Verb) (וְעָרְפוּ / wê'ar'phu) THERE (שָׁם / sham) AT (אֶת / et) the~HEIFER (הָעֶגְלָה / ha'eg'lah) in~the~WADI (בַּנָּחַל / ba'na'hhal)

and the bearded ones of that city will bring down the heifer to a consistent wadi, which was not served and which was not sown, and they will behead there the heifer in the wadi,

5 and~they~did~be~DRAW.NEAR(Verb) (וְנִגְּשׁוּ / wê'nig'shu) the~ADMINISTRATOR~s (הַכֹּהֲנִים / ha'ko'ha'nim) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Lewi (לֵוִי / ley'wi) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) in~~them(m) (בָם / vam) he~did~CHOOSE(Verb) (בָּחַר / ba'hhar) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) to~>~much~MINISTER(Verb)~him (לְשָׁרְתוֹ / lê'shar'to) and~to~>~much~KNEEL(Verb) (וּלְבָרֵךְ / ul'va'reykh) in~TITLE (בְּשֵׁם / bê'sheym) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) MOUTH~them(m) (פִּיהֶם / pi'hem) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) ALL (כָּל / kol) DISPUTE (רִיב / riv) and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) TOUCH (נָגַע / na'ga)

and the administrators, the sons of Lewi, will be drawn near, given that in them YHWH your Elohiym chose to minister him, and to respect in the title of YHWH, and upon their mouth every dispute and every touch will exist,

6 and~ALL (וְכֹל / wê'khol) BEARD~s (זִקְנֵי / ziq'ney) the~CITY (הָעִיר / ha'ir) the~SHE (הַהִוא / ha'hi) the~NEAR~s (הַקְּרֹבִים / haq'ro'vim) TO (אֶל / el) the~DRILLED (הֶחָלָל / he'hha'lal) they(m)~will~BATHE(Verb) (יִרְחֲצוּ / yir'hha'tsu) AT (אֶת / et) HAND~s2~them(m) (יְדֵיהֶם / yê'dey'hem) UPON (עַל / al) the~HEIFER (הָעֶגְלָה / ha'eg'lah) the~BEHEAD(Verb)~ed(fs) (הָעֲרוּפָה / ha'a'ru'phah) in~the~WADI (בַנָּחַל / va'na'hhal)

and all the bearded ones of that city, the ones near to the drilled one, they will bathe their hands upon the heifer, the one beheaded in the wadi,

7 and~they~did~ANSWER(Verb) (וְעָנוּ / wê'a'nu) and~they~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמְרוּ / wê'am'ru) HAND~s2~us (יָדֵינוּ / ya'dey'nu) NOT (לֹא / lo) she~did~POUR.OUT(Verb) (שָׁפְכָה / shaph'khah) [1018] AT (אֶת / et) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh) and~EYE~s2~us (וְעֵינֵינוּ / wê'ey'ney'nu) NOT (לֹא / lo) they~did~SEE(Verb) (רָאוּ / ra'u)

and they will answer, and they will say, our hands did not pour out this blood, and our eyes did not see.

8 !(ms)~much~COVER(Verb) (כַּפֵּר / ka'peyr) to~PEOPLE~you(ms) (לְעַמְּךָ / lê'am'kha) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~did~RANSOM(Verb) (פָּדִיתָ / pa'di'ta) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~DO.NOT (וְאַל / wê'al) you(ms)~will~GIVE(Verb) (תִּתֵּן / ti'teyn) BLOOD (דָּם / dam) INNOCENT (נָקִי / na'qi) in~INSIDE (בְּקֶרֶב / bê'qe'rev) PEOPLE~you(ms) (עַמְּךָ / am'kha) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~he~did~self~COVER(Verb) (וְנִכַּפֵּר / wê'ni'ka'peyr) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam)

A covering to your people of Yisra'eyl, who you, YHWH, ransomed, and you will not give innocent blood in among your people of Yisra'eyl, and the blood will cover for them,

9 and~YOU(MS) (וְאַתָּה / wê'a'tah) you(ms)~will~much~BURN(Verb) (תְּבַעֵר / tê'va'eyr) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam) the~INNOCENT (הַנָּקִי / ha'na'qi) from~INSIDE~you(ms) (מִקִּרְבֶּךָ / mi'qir'be'kha) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) the~STRAIGHT (הַיָּשָׁר / hai'ya'shar) in~EYE~s2 (בְּעֵינֵי / bê'ey'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and you, you will burn the innocent blood from inside you, given that you will do the straight thing in the eyes of YHWH.

10 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(ms)~will~GO.OUT(Verb) (תֵצֵא / tey'tsey) to~the~BATTLE (לַמִּלְחָמָה / la'mil'hha'mah) UPON (עַל / al) ATTACK(Verb)~ing(mp)~s~you(ms) (אֹיְבֶיךָ / oi'vey'kha) and~he~did~GIVE(Verb)~him (וּנְתָנוֹ / un'ta'no) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) in~HAND~you(ms) (בְּיָדֶךָ / bê'ya'de'kha) and~you(ms)~did~CAPTURE(Verb) (וְשָׁבִיתָ / wê'sha'vi'ta) CAPTIVE~him (שִׁבְיוֹ / shiv'yo)

Given that you will go out to the battle upon your attackers, and YHWH your Elohiym will give him in your hands, and you will capture his captives,

11 and~you(ms)~did~SEE(Verb) (וְרָאִיתָ / wê'ra'i'ta) in~the~CAPTIVE (בַּשִּׁבְיָה / ba'shiv'yah) WOMAN (אֵשֶׁת / ey'shet) BEAUTIFUL (יְפַת / yê'phat) FORM (תֹּאַר / to'ar) and~you(ms)~did~ATTACH(Verb) (וְחָשַׁקְתָּ / wê'hha'shaq'ta) in~~her (בָהּ / vah) and~you(ms)~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקַחְתָּ / wê'la'qahh'ta) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) to~WOMAN (לְאִשָּׁה / lê'i'shah)

and you will see in the captives a woman of beauty of form, and you will attach with her, and you will take for you for a woman,

12 and~you(ms)~did~make~COME(Verb)~her (וַהֲבֵאתָהּ / wa'ha'vey'tah) TO (אֶל / el) MIDST (תּוֹךְ / tokh) HOUSE~you(ms) (בֵּיתֶךָ / bey'te'kha) and~she~did~much~SHAVE(Verb) (וְגִלְּחָה / wê'gil'hhah) AT (אֶת / et) HEAD~her (רֹאשָׁהּ / ro'shah) and~she~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשְׂתָה / wê'as'tah) AT (אֶת / et) POINT~s~her (צִפָּרְנֶיהָ / tsi'par'ney'ah) [1019]

and you will bring her to the midst of your house, and she will shave her head, and she will do her points,

13 and~she~did~make~TURN.ASIDE(Verb) (וְהֵסִירָה / wê'hey'si'rah) AT (אֶת / et) APPAREL (שִׂמְלַת / sim'lat) CAPTIVE~her (שִׁבְיָהּ / shiv'yah) from~UPON~her (מֵעָלֶיהָ / mey'a'ley'ah) and~she~did~SETTLE(Verb) (וְיָשְׁבָה / wê'yash'vah) in~HOUSE~you(ms) (בְּבֵיתֶךָ / bê'vey'te'kha) and~she~did~WEEP(Verb) (וּבָכְתָה / u'vakh'tah) AT (אֶת / et) FATHER~her (אָבִיהָ / a'vi'ah) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) MOTHER~her (אִמָּהּ / i'mah) MOON (יֶרַח / ye'rahh) DAY~s (יָמִים / ya'mim) and~AFTER (וְאַחַר / wê'a'hhar) SO (כֵּן / keyn) you(ms)~will~COME(Verb) (תָּבוֹא / ta'vo) TO~her (אֵלֶיהָ / ey'ley'ah) and~you(ms)~did~MARRY(Verb)~her (וּבְעַלְתָּהּ / uv'al'tah) and~she~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיְתָה / wê'hai'tah) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) to~WOMAN (לְאִשָּׁה / lê'i'shah)

and she will turn aside the apparel of her captivity from upon her, and she will settle in your house, and she will weep for her father and her mother a moon of days, and after so you will come to her, and you will marry her, and she will exist for you for a woman,

14 and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) IF (אִם / im) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~did~DELIGHT(Verb) (חָפַצְתָּ / hha'phats'ta) in~~her (בָּהּ / bah) and~you(ms)~did~SEND(Verb)~her (וְשִׁלַּחְתָּהּ / wê'shi'lahh'tah) to~SOUL~her (לְנַפְשָׁהּ / lê'naph'shah) and~>~SELL(Verb) (וּמָכֹר / u'ma'khor) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~SELL(Verb)~her (תִמְכְּרֶנָּה / tim'ke're'nah) in~SILVER (בַּכָּסֶף / ba'ka'seph) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~self~BUNDLE(Verb) (תִתְעַמֵּר / tit'a'meyr) in~~her (בָּהּ / bah) UNDER (תַּחַת / ta'hhat) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(ms)~did~much~AFFLICT(Verb)~her (עִנִּיתָהּ / i'ni'tah)

and it will come to pass, if you did not delight in her, and you will send her to her soul, and you will surely not sell her with silver, you will not bundle her under, because you afflicted her.

15 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) they(f)~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִהְיֶיןָ / tih'yey'na) to~MAN (לְאִישׁ / lê'ish) TWO (שְׁתֵּי / shê'tey) WOMAN~s (נָשִׁים / na'shim) the~UNIT (הָאַחַת / ha'a'hhat) LOVE(Verb)~ed(fs) (אֲהוּבָה / a'hu'vah) and~the~UNIT (וְהָאַחַת / wê'ha'a'hhat) HATE(Verb)~ed(fs) (שְׂנוּאָה / sê'nu'ah) and~they(m)~will~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וְיָלְדוּ / wê'yal'du) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) SON~s (בָנִים / va'nim) the~LOVE(Verb)~ed(fs) (הָאֲהוּבָה / ha'a'hu'vah) and~the~HATE(Verb)~ed(fs) (וְהַשְּׂנוּאָה / wê'has'nu'ah) and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) the~SON (הַבֵּן / ha'beyn) the~FIRSTBORN (הַבְּכוֹר / ha'be'khor) to~the~HATED (לַשְּׂנִיאָה / la'se'ni'ah)

If two women will exist for a man, the one is loved and the one is hated, and they will bring forth for him sons, the loved one and the hated one, and the firstborn son will exist to the hated one,

16 and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) in~DAY (בְּיוֹם / bê'yom) >~make~INHERIT(Verb)~him (הַנְחִילוֹ / han'hhi'lo) AT (אֶת / et) SON~s~him (בָּנָיו / ba'naw) AT (אֵת / eyt) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~BE.ABLE(Verb) (יוּכַל / yu'khal) to~>~much~BE.FIRSTBORN(Verb) (לְבַכֵּר / lê'va'keyr) AT (אֶת / et) SON (בֶּן / ben) the~LOVE(Verb)~ed(fs) (הָאֲהוּבָה / ha'a'hu'vah) UPON (עַל / al) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) SON (בֶן / ven) the~HATE(Verb)~ed(fs) (הַשְּׂנוּאָה / has'nu'ah) the~FIRSTBORN (הַבְּכֹר / ha'be'khor)

and it will come to pass in the day of making his sons which will exist for him to inherit him, he will not be able to make the son of the loved one be the firstborn over the face of the son of the hated one, the firstborn,

17 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) AT (אֶת / et) the~FIRSTBORN (הַבְּכֹר / ha'be'khor) SON (בֶּן / ben) the~HATE(Verb)~ed(fs) (הַשְּׂנוּאָה / has'nu'ah) he~will~make~RECOGNIZE(Verb) (יַכִּיר / ya'kir) to~>~GIVE(Verb) (לָתֶת / la'tet) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) MOUTH (פִּי / pi) TWO (שְׁנַיִם / shê'na'yim) [1020] in~ALL (בְּכֹל / bê'khol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~be~FIND(Verb) (יִמָּצֵא / yi'ma'tsey) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) HE (הוּא / hu) ORIGIN (רֵאשִׁית / rey'shit) VIGOR~him (אֹנוֹ / o'no) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) DECISION (מִשְׁפַּט / mish'pat) the~BIRTHRIGHT (הַבְּכֹרָה / ha'be'kho'rah)

but he will recognize the firstborn son of the hated one, to give to him a mouth of two in all which will be found for him, given that he is the origin of his vigor, for him is the decision of the birthright.

18 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) to~MAN (לְאִישׁ / lê'ish) SON (בֵּן / beyn) BE.STUBBORN(Verb)~ing(ms) (סוֹרֵר / so'reyr) and~DISOBEY(Verb)~ing(ms) (וּמוֹרֶה / u'mo'reh) WITHOUT~him (אֵינֶנּוּ / ey'ne'nu) HEAR(Verb)~ing(ms) (שֹׁמֵעַ / sho'mey'a) in~VOICE (בְּקוֹל / bê'qol) FATHER~him (אָבִיו / a'viw) and~in~VOICE (וּבְקוֹל / uv'qol) MOTHER~him (אִמּוֹ / i'mo) and~they~did~much~CORRECT(Verb) (וְיִסְּרוּ / wê'yis'ru) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~will~HEAR(Verb) (יִשְׁמַע / yish'ma) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵיהֶם / a'ley'hem)

When a son will exist for a man, being stubborn and disobeying, he is not hearing the voice of his father or the voice of his mother, and they correct him and he will not listen to them,

then his father and his mother will seize hold of him, and bring him out to the bearded ones of his city and to the gate of his area,
