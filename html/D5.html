Deuteronomy Chapter 5

1 and~he~will~CALL.OUT(Verb) (וַיִּקְרָא / wai'yiq'ra) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) TO (אֶל / el) ALL (כָּל / kol) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) !(ms)~HEAR(Verb) (שְׁמַע / shê'ma) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) AT (אֶת / et) the~CUSTOM~s (הַחֻקִּים / ha'hhu'qim) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~DECISION~s (הַמִּשְׁפָּטִים / ha'mish'pa'tim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) SPEAK(Verb)~ing(ms) (דֹּבֵר / do'veyr) in~EAR~s2~you(mp) (בְּאָזְנֵיכֶם / bê'az'ney'khem) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) and~you(mp)~did~LEARN(Verb) (וּלְמַדְתֶּם / ul'mad'tem) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) and~you(mp)~did~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (וּשְׁמַרְתֶּם / ush'mar'tem) to~>~DO(Verb)~them(m) (לַעֲשֹׂתָם / la'a'so'tam)

and Mosheh called out to all of Yisra'eyl, and he said to them, hear Yisra'eyl the customs and the decisions which I am speaking in your ears today, and you will learn them, and you will safeguard them.

2 YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~us (אֱלֹהֵינוּ / e'lo'hey'nu) he~did~CUT(Verb) (כָּרַת / ka'rat) WITH~us (עִמָּנוּ / i'ma'nu) COVENANT (בְּרִית / bê'rit) in~Hhorev (בְּחֹרֵב / bê'hho'reyv)

YHWH is our Elohiym, he cut with us a covenant in Hhorev.

3 NOT (לֹא / lo) AT (אֶת / et) FATHER~s~us (אֲבֹתֵינוּ / a'vo'tey'nu) he~did~CUT(Verb) (כָּרַת / ka'rat) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT (אֶת / et) the~COVENANT (הַבְּרִית / ha'be'rit) the~THIS (הַזֹּאת / ha'zot) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) AT~us (אִתָּנוּ / i'ta'nu) WE (אֲנַחְנוּ / a'nahh'nu) THESE (אֵלֶּה / ey'leh) HERE (פֹה / phoh) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) ALL~us (כֻּלָּנוּ / ku'la'nu) LIVING~s (חַיִּים / hhai'yim)

YHWH did not cut this covenant with our fathers but with us, we these here today, all us living ones.

4 FACE~s (פָּנִים / pa'nim) in~FACE~s (בְּפָנִים / bê'pha'nim) he~did~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דִּבֶּר / di'ber) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) WITH~you(mp) (עִמָּכֶם / i'ma'khem) in~the~HILL (בָּהָר / ba'har) from~MIDST (מִתּוֹךְ / mi'tokh) the~FIRE (הָאֵשׁ / ha'eysh)

Face to face YHWH spoke with you on the hill from the midst of the fire.

5 I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) STAND(Verb)~ing(ms) (עֹמֵד / o'meyd) BETWEEN (בֵּין / beyn) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~BETWEEN~you(mp) (וּבֵינֵיכֶם / u'vey'ney'khem) in~the~APPOINTED.TIME (בָּעֵת / ba'eyt) the~SHE (הַהִוא / ha'hi) to~>~make~BE.FACE.TO.FACE(Verb) (לְהַגִּיד / lê'ha'gid) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) AT (אֶת / et) WORD (דְּבַר / dê'var) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(mp)~did~FEAR(Verb) (יְרֵאתֶם / yê'rey'tem) from~FACE~s (מִפְּנֵי / mip'ney) the~FIRE (הָאֵשׁ / ha'eysh) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(mp)~did~make~GO.UP(Verb) (עֲלִיתֶם / a'li'tem) in~the~HILL (בָּהָר / ba'har) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor)

I am standing between YHWH and you in that appointed time to tell you the word of YHWH, given that you feared from the face of the fire, and you did not go up on the hill, saying,

6 I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I~did~make~GO.OUT(Verb)~you(ms) (הוֹצֵאתִיךָ / ho'tsey'ti'kha) from~LAND (מֵאֶרֶץ / mey'e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) from~HOUSE (מִבֵּית / mi'beyt) SERVANT~s (עֲבָדִים / a'va'dim)

I am YHWH your Elohiym, which I brought you out from the land of Mits'rayim, from the house of servants.

7 NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) OTHER~s (אֲחֵרִים / a'hhey'rim) UPON (עַל / al) FACE~s~me (פָּנָיַ / pa'na'ya)

Other Elohiym will not exist for you upon my face.

8 NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) SCULPTURE (פֶסֶל / phe'sel) ALL (כָּל / kol) RESEMBLANCE (תְּמוּנָה / tê'mu'nah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~the~SKY~s2 (בַּשָּׁמַיִם / ba'sha'ma'yim) from~UPWARD (מִמַּעַל / mi'ma'al) and~WHICH (וַאֲשֶׁר / wa'a'sher) in~the~LAND (בָּאָרֶץ / ba'a'rets) from~UNDER (מִתָּחַת / mi'ta'hhat) and~WHICH (וַאֲשֶׁר / wa'a'sher) in~the~WATER~s2 (בַּמַּיִם / ba'ma'yim) from~UNDER (מִתַּחַת / mi'ta'hhat) to~the~LAND (לָאָרֶץ / la'a'rets)

You will not do for yourselves a sculpture, any resemblance which is in the skies above and which is on the land below and which is in the waters below the land.

9 NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~self~BEND.DOWN(Verb) (תִשְׁתַּחֲוֶה / tish'ta'hha'weh) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~be~make~SERVE(Verb)~them(m) (תָעָבְדֵם / ta'av'deym) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) MIGHTY.ONE (אֵל / eyl) ZEALOUS (קַנָּא / qa'na) REGISTER(Verb)~ing(ms) (פֹּקֵד / po'qeyd) TWISTEDNESS (עֲו‍ֹן / a'won) FATHER~s (אָבוֹת / a'vot) UPON (עַל / al) SON~s (בָּנִים / ba'nim) and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) THIRD.GENERATION~s (שִׁלֵּשִׁים / shi'ley'shim) and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) FOURTH.GENERATION~s (רִבֵּעִים / ri'bey'im) to~HATE(Verb)~ing(mp)~me (לְשֹׂנְאָי / lê'son'ai)

You will not bend yourself down to them, and you will not serve them, given that I am YHWH your Elohiym, a mighty one, a zealous one, registering twistedness of the fathers upon the sons and upon the third generation, and upon the fourth generation to the ones hating me,

10 and~DO(Verb)~ing(ms) (וְעֹשֶׂה / wê'o'seh) KINDNESS (חֶסֶד / hhe'sed) to~the~THOUSAND~s (לַאֲלָפִים / la'a'la'phim) to~LOVE(Verb)~ing(mp)~me (לְאֹהֲבַי / lê'o'ha'vai) and~to~SAFEGUARD(Verb)~ing(mp) (וּלְשֹׁמְרֵי / ul'shom'rey) DIRECTIVE~s~him (מִצְוֹתוֹ / mits'o'to) [958]

and do kindness to the thousands to the ones loving me and safeguarding my directives.

11 NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~LIFT.UP(Verb) (תִשָּׂא / ti'sa) AT (אֶת / et) TITLE (שֵׁם / sheym) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) to~the~FALSENESS (לַשָּׁוְא / la'sha'we) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~much~ACQUIT(Verb) (יְנַקֶּה / yê'na'qeh) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT (אֵת / eyt) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~LIFT.UP(Verb) (יִשָּׂא / yi'sa) AT (אֶת / et) TITLE~him (שְׁמוֹ / shê'mo) to~the~FALSENESS (לַשָּׁוְא / la'sha'we)

You will not lift up the title of YHWH your Elohiym for falseness, given that YHWH will not acquit who will lift up his title to falseness.

12 >~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (שָׁמוֹר / sha'mor) AT (אֶת / et) DAY (יוֹם / yom) the~CEASING (הַשַּׁבָּת / ha'sha'bat) to~>~much~SET.APART(Verb)~him (לְקַדְּשׁוֹ / lê'qad'sho) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~much~DIRECT(Verb)~you(ms) (צִוְּךָ / tsi'we'kha) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha)

Safeguard the day of ceasing, to set him apart just as YHWH your Elohiym directed you.

13 SIX (שֵׁשֶׁת / shey'shet) DAY~s (יָמִים / ya'mim) you(ms)~will~SERVE(Verb) (תַּעֲבֹד / ta'a'vod) and~you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשִׂיתָ / wê'a'si'ta) ALL (כָּל / kol) BUSINESS~you(ms) (מְלַאכְתֶּךָ / mê'lakh'te'kha)

Six days you will serve and you will do your business,

14 and~DAY (וְיוֹם / wê'yom) the~SEVENTH (הַשְּׁבִיעִי / hash'vi'i) CEASING (שַׁבָּת / sha'bat) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) ALL (כָל / khol) BUSINESS (מְלָאכָה / mê'la'khah) YOU(MS) (אַתָּה / a'tah) and~SON~you(ms) (וּבִנְךָ / u'vin'kha) and~DAUGHTER~you(ms) (וּבִתֶּךָ / u'vi'te'kha) and~SERVANT~you(ms) (וְעַבְדְּךָ / wê'av'de'kha) and~BONDWOMAN~you(ms) (וַאֲמָתֶךָ / wa'a'ma'te'kha) and~OX~you(ms) (וְשׁוֹרְךָ / wê'shor'kha) and~DONKEY~you(ms) (וַחֲמֹרְךָ / wa'hha'mor'kha) and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) BEAST~you(ms) (בְּהֶמְתֶּךָ / bê'hem'te'kha) and~IMMIGRANT~you(ms) (וְגֵרְךָ / wê'ger'kha) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~GATE~s~you(ms) (בִּשְׁעָרֶיךָ / bish'a'rey'kha) to~THAT (לְמַעַן / lê'ma'an) he~will~REST(Verb) (יָנוּחַ / ya'nu'ahh) SERVANT~you(ms) (עַבְדְּךָ / av'de'kha) and~BONDWOMAN~you(ms) (וַאֲמָתְךָ / wa'a'mat'kha) like~THAT.ONE~you(ms) (כָּמוֹךָ / ka'mo'kha)

and the seventh day is a ceasing for YHWH your Elohiym, you will not do business, you and your sons and your daughters and your servants and your bondwomen and your ox and your donkey and all your beasts and your immigrants that are in your gates, so that your servant will rest, and your bondwomen, like you,

15 and~you(ms)~did~REMEMBER(Verb) (וְזָכַרְתָּ / wê'za'khar'ta) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) SERVANT (עֶבֶד / e'ved) you(ms)~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיִיתָ / ha'yi'ta) in~LAND (בְּאֶרֶץ / bê'e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) and~he~will~make~GO.OUT(Verb)~you(ms) (וַיֹּצִאֲךָ / wai'yo'tsi'a'kha) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) from~THERE (מִשָּׁם / mi'sham) in~HAND (בְּיָד / bê'yad) FORCEFUL (חֲזָקָה / hha'za'qah) and~in~ARM (וּבִזְרֹעַ / u'viz'ro'a) EXTEND(Verb)~ed(fs) (נְטוּיָה / nê'tu'yah) UPON (עַל / al) SO (כֵּן / keyn) he~did~much~DIRECT(Verb)~you(ms) (צִוְּךָ / tsi'we'kha) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) to~>~DO(Verb) (לַעֲשׂוֹת / la'a'sot) AT (אֶת / et) DAY (יוֹם / yom) the~CEASING (הַשַּׁבָּת / ha'sha'bat)

and you will remember that you existed as a servant in the land of Mits'rayim, and YHWH your Elohiym brought you out from there with a forceful hand and with an extended arm, therefore YHWH your Elohiym directed you to do the day of ceasing.

16 !(ms)~much~BE.HEAVY(Verb) (כַּבֵּד / ka'beyd) AT (אֶת / et) FATHER~you(ms) (אָבִיךָ / a'vi'kha) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) MOTHER~you(ms) (אִמֶּךָ / i'me'kha) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~much~DIRECT(Verb)~you(ms) (צִוְּךָ / tsi'we'kha) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) to~THAT (לְמַעַן / lê'ma'an) they(m)~will~make~PROLONG(Verb)~must (יַאֲרִיכֻן / ya'a'ri'khun) DAY~s~you(ms) (יָמֶיךָ / ya'mey'kha) and~to~THAT (וּלְמַעַן / ul'ma'an) he~will~DO.WELL(Verb) (יִיטַב / yi'tav) to~~you(fs) (לָךְ / lakh) UPON (עַל / al) the~GROUND (הָאֲדָמָה / ha'a'da'mah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) GIVE(Verb)~ing(ms) (נֹתֵן / no'teyn) to~~you(fs) (לָךְ / lakh)

Honor your father and your mother just as YHWH your Elohiym directed you, so that your days will certainly be prolonged, and so that he will go well for you upon the ground which YHWH your Elohiym is giving to you.

17 NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~MURDER(Verb) (תִּרְצָח / tir'tsahh)

You will not murder,

18 and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~COMMIT.ADULTERY(Verb) (תִּנְאָף / tin'aph)

and you will not commit adultery,

19 and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~STEAL(Verb) (תִּגְנֹב / tig'nov)

and you will not steal,

20 and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~AFFLICT(Verb) (תַעֲנֶה / ta'a'neh) in~COMPANION~you(ms) (בְרֵעֲךָ / vê'rey'a'kha) WITNESS (עֵד / eyd) FALSENESS (שָׁוְא / sha'we)

and you will not afflict a witness of falseness on your companion,

21 and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~CRAVE(Verb) (תַחְמֹד / tahh'mod) WOMAN (אֵשֶׁת / ey'shet) COMPANION~you(ms) (רֵעֶךָ / rey'e'kha) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~self~YEARN(Verb) (תִתְאַוֶּה / tit'a'weh) HOUSE (בֵּית / beyt) COMPANION~you(ms) (רֵעֶךָ / rey'e'kha) FIELD~him (שָׂדֵהוּ / sa'dey'hu) and~SERVANT~him (וְעַבְדּוֹ / wê'av'do) and~BONDWOMAN~him (וַאֲמָתוֹ / wa'a'ma'to) OX~him (שׁוֹרוֹ / sho'ro) and~DONKEY~him (וַחֲמֹרוֹ / wa'hha'mo'ro) and~ALL (וְכֹל / wê'khol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) to~COMPANION~you(ms) (לְרֵעֶךָ / lê'rey'e'kha)

and you will not crave the woman of your companion, and you will not yearn for the house of your companion, his field and his servant and his bondwoman, his ox and his donkey and all that belongs to your companion.
