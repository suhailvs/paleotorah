Numbers Chapter 5

1 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

2 !(ms)~much~DIRECT(Verb) (צַו / tsaw) AT (אֶת / et) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~they(m)~will~much~SEND(Verb) (וִישַׁלְּחוּ / wi'shal'hhu) FROM (מִן / min) the~CAMP (הַמַּחֲנֶה / ha'ma'hha'neh) ALL (כָּל / kol) INFECT(Verb)~ed(ms) (צָרוּעַ / tsa'ru'a) and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) ISSUE(Verb)~ing(ms) (זָב / zav) and~ALL (וְכֹל / wê'khol) DIRTY (טָמֵא / ta'mey) to~the~SOUL (לָנָפֶשׁ / la'na'phesh) [776]

Direct the sons of Yisra'eyl, and they will send from the camp all the infected all the ones issuing, and every dirty one to the soul.

3 from~the~MALE (מִזָּכָר / mi'za'khar) UNTIL (עַד / ad) FEMALE (נְקֵבָה / nê'qey'vah) you(mp)~will~much~SEND(Verb) (תְּשַׁלֵּחוּ / tê'shal'hhu) TO (אֶל / el) from~OUTSIDE (מִחוּץ / mi'hhuts) to~the~CAMP (לַמַּחֲנֶה / la'ma'hha'neh) you(mp)~will~much~SEND(Verb)~them(m) (תְּשַׁלְּחוּם / tê'shal'hhum) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) they~will~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (יְטַמְּאוּ / yê'tam'u) AT (אֶת / et) CAMP~s~them(m) (מַחֲנֵיהֶם / ma'hha'ney'hem) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) DWELL(Verb)~ing(ms) (שֹׁכֵן / sho'kheyn) in~MIDST~them(m) (בְּתוֹכָם / bê'to'kham)

From the male until the female you will send, to the outside of the camp you will send them, and they will not dirty their camps, which I am dwelling in the midst of them,

4 and~they(m)~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעֲשׂוּ / wai'ya'a'su) SO (כֵן / kheyn) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~they(m)~will~much~SEND(Verb) (וַיְשַׁלְּחוּ / wa'ye'shal'hhu) AT~them(m) (אוֹתָם / o'tam) TO (אֶל / el) from~OUTSIDE (מִחוּץ / mi'hhuts) to~the~CAMP (לַמַּחֲנֶה / la'ma'hha'neh) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דִּבֶּר / di'ber) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) SO (כֵּן / keyn) they~did~DO(Verb) (עָשׂוּ / a'su) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl)

and the sons of Yisra'eyl will do so, and they sent them to the outside of the camp, just as YHWH spoke to Mosheh, so the sons of Yisra'eyl did do,

5 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

6 !(ms)~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דַּבֵּר / da'beyr) TO (אֶל / el) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) OR (אוֹ / o) WOMAN (אִשָּׁה / i'shah) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) they(m)~will~DO(Verb) (יַעֲשׂוּ / ya'a'su) from~ALL (מִכָּל / mi'kol) FAILURE~s (חַטֹּאת / hha'tot) the~HUMAN (הָאָדָם / ha'a'dam) to~>~TRANSGRESS(Verb) (לִמְעֹל / lim'ol) TRANSGRESSION (מַעַל / ma'al) in~YHWH (בַּיהוָה / ba'YHWH) and~she~did~BE.GUILTY(Verb) (וְאָשְׁמָה / wê'ash'mah) the~SOUL (הַנֶּפֶשׁ / ha'ne'phesh) the~SHE (הַהִוא / ha'hi)

Speak to the sons of Yisra'eyl, a man or a woman that will do from any of the failures of the human to transgress a transgression in YHWH, then that soul will be guilty,

7 and~they~did~self~THROW.THE.HAND(Verb) (וְהִתְוַדּוּ / wê'hit'wa'du) AT (אֶת / et) FAILURE~them(m) (חַטָּאתָם / hha'ta'tam) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they~did~DO(Verb) (עָשׂוּ / a'su) and~he~did~make~TURN.BACK(Verb) (וְהֵשִׁיב / wê'hey'shiv) AT (אֶת / et) GUILT~him (אֲשָׁמוֹ / a'sha'mo) in~HEAD~him (בְּרֹאשׁוֹ / bê'ro'sho) and~FIFTH~him (וַחֲמִישִׁתוֹ / wa'hha'mi'shi'to) he~will~make~ADD(Verb) (יֹסֵף / yo'seyph) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) and~he~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַן / wê'na'tan) to~the~WHICH (לַאֲשֶׁר / la'a'sheyr) he~did~BE.GUILTY(Verb) (אָשַׁם / a'sham) to~~him (לוֹ / lo)

and they will confess their failures that they did, and they will turn back his guilt in his head, and a fifth of him he will add upon him, and he will give it to him he did the guilt,

8 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) WITHOUT (אֵין / eyn) to~the~MAN (לָאִישׁ / la'ish) REDEEM(Verb)~ing(ms) (גֹּאֵל / go'eyl) to~>~make~TURN.BACK(Verb) (לְהָשִׁיב / lê'ha'shiv) the~GUILT (הָאָשָׁם / ha'a'sham) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) the~GUILT (הָאָשָׁם / ha'a'sham) the~be~make~TURN.BACK(Verb)~ing(ms) (הַמּוּשָׁב / ha'mu'shav) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) to~the~ADMINISTRATOR (לַכֹּהֵן / la'ko'heyn) from~to~STRAND (מִלְּבַד / mi'le'vad) [777] BUCK (אֵיל / eyl) the~ATONEMENT~s (הַכִּפֻּרִים / ha'ki'pu'rim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~much~COVER(Verb) (יְכַפֶּר / yê'kha'per) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law)

and if the man is without a redeemer to turn back the guilt to him, the guilt to be turned back belongs to YHWH, belongs to the administrator, apart from the buck of the atonement that will make a covering for him upon him,

9 and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) OFFERING (תְּרוּמָה / tê'ru'mah) to~ALL (לְכָל / lê'khol) SPECIAL~s (קָדְשֵׁי / qad'shey) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they(m)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (יַקְרִיבוּ / yaq'ri'vu) to~the~ADMINISTRATOR (לַכֹּהֵן / la'ko'heyn) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh)

and every offerings for all the special things of the sons of Yisra'eyl, which they will bring near to the administrator will exist for him,

10 and~MAN (וְאִישׁ / wê'ish) AT (אֶת / et) SPECIAL~s~him (קֳדָשָׁיו / qa'da'shaw) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) they(m)~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיוּ / yih'yu) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~GIVE(Verb) (יִתֵּן / yi'teyn) to~the~ADMINISTRATOR (לַכֹּהֵן / la'ko'heyn) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh)

and a man, his special things will exist for him, a man which will give to the administrator will exist for him,

11 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

12 !(ms)~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דַּבֵּר / da'beyr) TO (אֶל / el) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~you(ms)~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמַרְתָּ / wê'a'mar'ta) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) she~will~GO.ASIDE(Verb) (תִשְׂטֶה / tis'teh) WOMAN~him (אִשְׁתּוֹ / ish'to) and~she~did~TRANSGRESS(Verb) (וּמָעֲלָה / u'ma'a'lah) in~~him (בוֹ / vo) TRANSGRESSION (מָעַל / ma'al)

Speak to the sons of Yisra'eyl, and you will say to them, each man whose woman will go aside and she will transgress with him a transgression,

13 and~he~did~LIE.DOWN(Verb) (וְשָׁכַב / wê'sha'khav) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) LYING.DOWN (שִׁכְבַת / shikh'vat) SEED (זֶרַע / ze'ra) and~he~did~be~BE.OUT.OF.SIGHT(Verb) (וְנֶעְלַם / wê'ne'lam) from~EYE~s2 (מֵעֵינֵי / mey'ey'ney) MAN~her (אִישָׁהּ / i'shah) and~she~did~be~HIDE(Verb) (וְנִסְתְּרָה / wê'nis'te'rah) and~SHE (וְהִיא / wê'hi) she~did~be~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (נִטְמָאָה / nit'ma'ah) and~WITNESS (וְעֵד / wê'eyd) WITHOUT (אֵין / eyn) in~~her (בָּהּ / bah) and~SHE (וְהִוא / wê'hi) NOT (לֹא / lo) she~did~be~SEIZE.HOLD(Verb) (נִתְפָּשָׂה / nit'pa'sah)

and a man will lay her down a lying down of seed, and he will be out of sight from the eyes of her man, and she was hidden, and she, she was dirty and without a witness for her, and she, she was not seized,

14 and~he~did~CROSS.OVER(Verb) (וְעָבַר / wê'a'var) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) WIND (רוּחַ / ru'ahh) ZEALOUSNESS (קִנְאָה / qin'ah) and~he~did~much~BE.ZEALOUS(Verb) (וְקִנֵּא / wê'qi'ney) AT (אֶת / et) WOMAN~him (אִשְׁתּוֹ / ish'to) and~SHE (וְהִוא / wê'hi) she~did~be~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (נִטְמָאָה / nit'ma'ah) OR (אוֹ / o) he~did~CROSS.OVER(Verb) (עָבַר / a'var) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) WIND (רוּחַ / ru'ahh) ZEALOUSNESS (קִנְאָה / qin'ah) and~he~did~much~BE.ZEALOUS(Verb) (וְקִנֵּא / wê'qi'ney) AT (אֶת / et) WOMAN~him (אִשְׁתּוֹ / ish'to) and~SHE (וְהִיא / wê'hi) NOT (לֹא / lo) she~did~be~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (נִטְמָאָה / nit'ma'ah)

and a wind of zealousness crossed over upon him, and he was zealous with his woman, and she, she was dirty or a wind of zealousness crossed over upon him, and he was zealous with his woman, and she, she was not dirty,

15 and~he~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבִיא / wê'hey'vi) the~MAN (הָאִישׁ / ha'ish) AT (אֶת / et) WOMAN~him (אִשְׁתּוֹ / ish'to) TO (אֶל / el) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~he~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבִיא / wê'hey'vi) AT (אֶת / et) DONATION~her (קָרְבָּנָהּ / qar'ba'nah) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah) TENTH (עֲשִׂירִת / a'si'rit) the~EYPHAH (הָאֵיפָה / ha'ey'phah) GRAIN.FLOUR (קֶמַח / qe'mahh) BARLEY~s (שְׂעֹרִים / sê'o'rim) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~POUR.DOWN(Verb) (יִצֹק / yi'tsoq) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) OIL (שֶׁמֶן / she'men) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~will~GIVE(Verb) (יִתֵּן / yi'teyn) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) FRANKINCENSE (לְבֹנָה / lê'vo'nah) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) DEPOSIT (מִנְחַת / mi'ne'hhat) ZEALOUSNESS~s (קְנָאֹת / qê'na'ot) HE (הוּא / hu) DEPOSIT (מִנְחַת / mi'ne'hhat) REMEMBRANCE (זִכָּרוֹן / zi'ka'ron) make~REMEMBER(Verb)~ing(fs) (מַזְכֶּרֶת / maz'ke'ret) TWISTEDNESS (עָו‍ֹן / a'won)

then the man will bring his woman to the administrator, and he brought her donation upon her, a tenth eyphah of grain flour of barley, he will not pour down upon him the oil, and he will not place upon him frankincense, given that it is a deposit of zealousness, a deposit of remembrance, making a remembrance of twistedness,

16 and~he~did~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וְהִקְרִיב / wê'hiq'riv) AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~he~did~make~STAND(Verb)~her (וְהֶעֱמִדָהּ / wê'he'e'mi'dah) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and he will bring her near the administrator, and he will make her stand to the face of YHWH,

17 and~he~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקַח / wê'la'qahh) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) WATER~s2 (מַיִם / ma'yim) UNIQUE~s (קְדֹשִׁים / qê'do'shim) in~UTENSIL (בִּכְלִי / bikh'li) CLAY (חָרֶשׂ / hha'res) and~FROM (וּמִן / u'min) the~DIRT (הֶעָפָר / he'a'phar) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) in~BOTTOM (בְּקַרְקַע / bê'qar'qa) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan) he~will~TAKE(Verb) (יִקַּח / yi'qahh) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~he~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַן / wê'na'tan) TO (אֶל / el) the~WATER~s2 (הַמָּיִם / ha'ma'yim)

and the administrator will take unique waters in a utensil of clay, and from the dirt that exists in the bottom of the dwelling, the administrator will take and he will give to the waters,

18 and~he~did~make~STAND(Verb) (וְהֶעֱמִיד / wê'he'e'mid) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) AT (אֶת / et) the~WOMAN (הָאִשָּׁה / ha'i'shah) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~he~did~LOOSE(Verb) (וּפָרַע / u'pha'ra) [778] AT (אֶת / et) HEAD (רֹאשׁ / rosh) the~WOMAN (הָאִשָּׁה / ha'i'shah) and~he~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַן / wê'na'tan) UPON (עַל / al) PALM~s2~her (כַּפֶּיהָ / ka'pey'ah) AT (אֵת / eyt) DEPOSIT (מִנְחַת / mi'ne'hhat) the~REMEMBRANCE (הַזִּכָּרוֹן / ha'zi'ka'ron) DEPOSIT (מִנְחַת / mi'ne'hhat) ZEALOUSNESS~s (קְנָאֹת / qê'na'ot) SHE (הִוא / hi) and~in~HAND (וּבְיַד / uv'yad) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) they(m)~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיוּ / yih'yu) WATER~s2 (מֵי / mey) the~BITTER~s (הַמָּרִים / ha'ma'rim) the~much~SPIT.UPON(Verb)~ing(mp) (הַמְאָרֲרִים / ham'a'ra'rim) [779]

and the administrator will make the woman stand to the face of YHWH, and he will loose the head of the woman, and he will place upon her palms the deposit of remembrance, she is a deposit of zealousness, and in the hand of the administrator will exist the bitter waters that causes the spitting upon,

19 and~he~did~make~SWEAR(Verb) (וְהִשְׁבִּיעַ / wê'hish'bi'a) AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~he~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמַר / wê'a'mar) TO (אֶל / el) the~WOMAN (הָאִשָּׁה / ha'i'shah) IF (אִם / im) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~did~LIE.DOWN(Verb) (שָׁכַב / sha'khav) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) AT~you(fs) (אֹתָךְ / o'takh) and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(fs)~did~GO.ASIDE(Verb) (שָׂטִית / sa'tit) DIRTY (טֻמְאָה / tum'ah) UNDER (תַּחַת / ta'hhat) MAN~you(fs) (אִישֵׁךְ / i'sheykh) !(fs)~be~ACQUIT(Verb) (הִנָּקִי / hi'na'qi) from~WATER~s2 (מִמֵּי / mi'mey) the~BITTER~s (הַמָּרִים / ha'ma'rim) the~much~SPIT.UPON(Verb)~ing(mp) (הַמְאָרֲרִים / ham'a'ra'rim) [780] the~THESE (הָאֵלֶּה / ha'ey'leh)

and the administrator will make her swear, and he will say to the woman, if a man did not lay down with you, and if you did not go aside to dirtiness in place of your man, you will be acquitted from this bitter waters causing the spitting upon,
