Leviticus Chapter 19

1 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

2 !(ms)~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דַּבֵּר / da'beyr) TO (אֶל / el) ALL (כָּל / kol) COMPANY (עֲדַת / a'dat) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~you(ms)~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמַרְתָּ / wê'a'mar'ta) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) UNIQUE~s (קְדֹשִׁים / qê'do'shim) you(mp)~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִּהְיוּ / tih'yu) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) UNIQUE (קָדוֹשׁ / qa'dosh) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem)

Speak to all the company of the sons of Yisra'eyl and you will say to them, you will exist as unique ones, given that I, YHWH your Elohiym, am unique.

3 MAN (אִישׁ / ish) MOTHER~him (אִמּוֹ / i'mo) and~FATHER~him (וְאָבִיו / wê'a'viw) you(mp)~will~FEAR(Verb) (תִּירָאוּ / ti'ra'u) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) CEASING~s~me (שַׁבְּתֹתַי / sha'be'to'tai) you(mp)~will~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (תִּשְׁמֹרוּ / tish'moru) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem)

Each of you will fear his mother and his father, you will safeguard my ceasings, I am YHWH your Elohiym.

4 DO.NOT (אַל / al) you(mp)~will~TURN(Verb) (תִּפְנוּ / tiph'nu) TO (אֶל / el) the~WORTHLESS~s (הָאֱלִילִים / ha'e'li'lim) and~Elohiym (וֵאלֹהֵי / wey'lo'hey) CAST.IMAGE (מַסֵּכָה / ma'sey'khah) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~DO(Verb) (תַעֲשׂוּ / ta'a'su) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem)

You will not turn to the worthless ones and you will not make an Elohiym of a cast image for yourself, I am YHWH your Elohiym,

5 and~GIVEN.THAT (וְכִי / wê'khi) you(ms)~will~SACRIFICE(Verb)~him (תִזְבְּחוּ / tiz'be'hhu) SACRIFICE (זֶבַח / ze'vahh) OFFERING.OF.RESTITUTION~s (שְׁלָמִים / shê'la'mim) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) to~SELF-WILL~you(mp) (לִרְצֹנְכֶם / lir'tson'khem) you(mp)~will~SACRIFICE(Verb)~him (תִּזְבָּחֻהוּ / tiz'ba'hhu'hu)

and given that you sacrifice him as a sacrifice of offering of restitutions to YHWH, by the will of yourself you will sacrifice him.

6 in~DAY (בְּיוֹם / bê'yom) SACRIFICE~you(mp) (זִבְחֲכֶם / ziv'hha'khem) he~will~be~EAT(Verb) (יֵאָכֵל / yey'a'kheyl) and~from~MORROW (וּמִמָּחֳרָת / u'mi'ma'hha'rat) and~the~be~LEAVE.BEHIND(Verb)~ing(ms) (וְהַנּוֹתָר / wê'ha'no'tar) UNTIL (עַד / ad) DAY (יוֹם / yom) the~THIRD (הַשְּׁלִישִׁי / hash'li'shi) in~the~FIRE (בָּאֵשׁ / ba'eysh) he~will~be~CREMATE(Verb) (יִשָּׂרֵף / yi'sa'reyph)

In the day of your sacrifice he will be eaten, and on the morrow, and what is being left behind until the third day will be cremated in the fire,

7 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) the~>~be~EAT(Verb) (הֵאָכֹל / hey'a'khol) he~will~be~EAT(Verb) (יֵאָכֵל / yey'a'kheyl) in~the~DAY (בַּיּוֹם / ba'yom) the~THIRD (הַשְּׁלִישִׁי / hash'li'shi) FOUL (פִּגּוּל / pi'gul) HE (הוּא / hu) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~be~ACCEPT(Verb) (יֵרָצֶה / yey'ra'tseh)

and if he will surely be eaten in the third day, he is foul, he will not be accepted,

8 and~EAT(Verb)~ing(mp)~him (וְאֹכְלָיו / wê'okh'law) TWISTEDNESS~him (עֲוֹנוֹ / a'o'no) he~will~LIFT.UP(Verb) (יִשָּׂא / yi'sa) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) AT (אֶת / et) SPECIAL (קֹדֶשׁ / qo'desh) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) he~did~much~DRILL(Verb) (חִלֵּל / hhi'leyl) and~she~did~be~CUT(Verb) (וְנִכְרְתָה / wê'nikh're'tah) the~SOUL (הַנֶּפֶשׁ / ha'ne'phesh) the~SHE (הַהִוא / ha'hi) from~PEOPLE~s~her (מֵעַמֶּיהָ / mey'a'mey'ah)

and the ones eating him will have his twistedness lifted up, given that he defiled the specialness of YHWH, that soul will be cut from her peoples,

9 and~in~>~SEVER(Verb)~you(mp) (וּבְקֻצְרְכֶם / uv'quts're'khem) [727] AT (אֶת / et) HARVEST (קְצִיר / qê'tsir) LAND~you(mp) (אַרְצְכֶם / ar'tse'khem) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~much~FINISH(Verb) (תְכַלֶּה / tê'kha'leh) EDGE (פְּאַת / pê'at) FIELD~you(ms) (שָׂדְךָ / sad'kha) to~>~SEVER(Verb) (לִקְצֹר / liq'tsor) and~GLEANINGS (וְלֶקֶט / wê'le'qet) HARVEST~you(ms) (קְצִירְךָ / qê'tsir'kha) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~much~PICK.UP(Verb) (תְלַקֵּט / tê'la'qeyt)

and with your severing of the harvest in your land, you will not finish the edge of your field to sever it, and the gleanings of your harvest you will not pick up,

10 and~VINEYARD~you(ms) (וְכַרְמְךָ / wê'khar'me'kha) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~much~ROLL(Verb) (תְעוֹלֵל / tê'o'leyl) and~FALLEN.GRAPE (וּפֶרֶט / u'phe'ret) VINEYARD~you(ms) (כַּרְמְךָ / kar'me'kha) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~much~PICK.UP(Verb) (תְלַקֵּט / tê'la'qeyt) to~AFFLICTION (לֶעָנִי / le'a'ni) and~to~IMMIGRANT (וְלַגֵּר / wê'la'geyr) you(ms)~will~LEAVE(Verb) (תַּעֲזֹב / ta'a'zov) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem)

and your vineyard you will not glean, and the fallen grapes of your vineyard you will not pick up, you will leave them for the afflicted and for the immigrant, I am YHWH your Elohiym.

11 NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~STEAL(Verb) (תִּגְנֹבוּ / tig'no'vu) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(mp)~will~much~DENY(Verb) (תְכַחֲשׁוּ / tê'kha'hha'shu) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(mp)~will~much~DEAL.FALSELY(Verb) (תְשַׁקְּרוּ / tê'shaq'ru) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) in~the~NEIGHBOR~him (בַּעֲמִיתוֹ / ba'a'mi'to)

You will not steal and you will not lie and you will not deal falsely a man with his neighbor,

12 and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(mp)~will~be~SWEAR(Verb) (תִשָּׁבְעוּ / ti'sha'va'u) in~TITLE~me (בִשְׁמִי / vish'mi) to~the~FALSE (לַשָּׁקֶר / la'sha'qer) and~you(ms)~did~much~DRILL(Verb) (וְחִלַּלְתָּ / wê'hhi'lal'ta) AT (אֶת / et) TITLE (שֵׁם / sheym) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and you will not swear with my title to falseness, and you will not defile my title Elohiym, I am YHWH.

13 NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~OPPRESS(Verb) (תַעֲשֹׁק / ta'a'shoq) AT (אֶת / et) COMPANION~you(ms) (רֵעֲךָ / rey'a'kha) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~PLUCK.AWAY(Verb) (תִגְזֹל / tig'zol) [728] NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~STAY.THE.NIGHT(Verb) (תָלִין / ta'lin) [729] MAKE(Verb)~ed(fs) (פְּעֻלַּת / pê'u'lat) [730] HIRELING (שָׂכִיר / sa'khir) AT~you(ms) (אִתְּךָ / it'kha) UNTIL (עַד / ad) MORNING (בֹּקֶר / bo'qer)

You will not oppress your companion and you will not pluck away, you will not stay the night what is made by your hireling until morning.

14 NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~much~BELITTLE(Verb) (תְקַלֵּל / tê'qa'leyl) SILENT (חֵרֵשׁ / hhey'reysh) and~to~FACE~s (וְלִפְנֵי / wê'liph'ney) BLIND (עִוֵּר / i'weyr) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~GIVE(Verb) (תִתֵּן / ti'teyn) STUMBLING.BLOCK (מִכְשֹׁל / mikh'shol) and~you(ms)~did~FEAR(Verb) (וְיָרֵאתָ / wê'ya'rey'ta) from~Elohiym~you(ms) (מֵּאֱלֹהֶיךָ / mey'e'lo'hey'kha) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

You will not belittle a silent one, and to the face of blind you will not place a stumbling block, and you will fear your Elohiym, I am YHWH.

15 NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~DO(Verb) (תַעֲשׂוּ / ta'a'su) WICKED (עָוֶל / a'wel) in~the~DECISION (בַּמִּשְׁפָּט / ba'mish'pat) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~LIFT.UP(Verb) (תִשָּׂא / ti'sa) FACE~s (פְנֵי / phê'ney) HELPLESS (דָל / dal) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~GIVE.HONOR(Verb) (תֶהְדַּר / teh'dar) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) GREAT (גָדוֹל / ga'dol) in~STEADFAST (בְּצֶדֶק / bê'tse'deq) you(ms)~will~DECIDE(Verb) (תִּשְׁפֹּט / tish'pot) NEIGHBOR~you(ms) (עֲמִיתֶךָ / a'mi'te'kha)

You will not do wickedness in the decision, you will not lift up the face of the helpless and you will not give honor to the face of the great one, with steadfastness you will decide your neighbor.

16 NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~WALK(Verb) (תֵלֵךְ / tey'leykh) TALEBEARER (רָכִיל / ra'khil) in~PEOPLE~s~you(ms) (בְּעַמֶּיךָ / bê'a'mey'kha) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~STAND(Verb) (תַעֲמֹד / ta'a'mod) UPON (עַל / al) BLOOD (דַּם / dam) COMPANION~you(ms) (רֵעֶךָ / rey'e'kha) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

You will not walk as a talebearer with your people, you will not stand upon the blood of your companion, I am YHWH.

17 NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~HATE(Verb) (תִשְׂנָא / tis'na) AT (אֶת / et) BROTHER~you(ms) (אָחִיךָ / a'hhi'kha) in~HEART~you(ms) (בִּלְבָבֶךָ / bil'va've'kha) >~make~CONVICT(Verb) (הוֹכֵחַ / ho'khey'ahh) you(ms)~will~make~CONVICT(Verb) (תּוֹכִיחַ / to'khi'ahh) AT (אֶת / et) NEIGHBOR~you(ms) (עֲמִיתֶךָ / a'mi'te'kha) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~LIFT.UP(Verb) (תִשָּׂא / ti'sa) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) FAILURE (חֵטְא / hheyt)

You will not hate your brother in your heart, you will certainly make a conviction of your neighbor, and you will not lift up upon him failure,

18 NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~AVENGE(Verb) (תִקֹּם / ti'qom) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~KEEP(Verb) (תִטֹּר / ti'tor) [731] AT (אֶת / et) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) PEOPLE~you(ms) (עַמֶּךָ / a'me'kha) and~you(ms)~did~LOVE(Verb) (וְאָהַבְתָּ / wê'a'hav'ta) to~COMPANION~you(ms) (לְרֵעֲךָ / lê'rey'a'kha) like~THAT.ONE~you(ms) (כָּמוֹךָ / ka'mo'kha) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and you will not avenge and you will not keep the sons of your people, and you will love your companion like one of you, I am YHWH.

19 AT (אֶת / et) CUSTOM~s~me (חֻקֹּתַי / hhu'qo'tai) you(mp)~will~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (תִּשְׁמֹרוּ / tish'moru) BEAST~you(ms) (בְּהֶמְתְּךָ / bê'hem'te'kha) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~make~BE.SQUARE(Verb) (תַרְבִּיעַ / tar'bi'a) [732] DIVERSE.KIND~s2 (כִּלְאַיִם / kil'a'yim) FIELD~you(ms) (שָׂדְךָ / sad'kha) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~SOW(Verb) (תִזְרַע / tiz'ra) DIVERSE.KIND~s2 (כִּלְאָיִם / kil'a'yim) and~GARMENT (וּבֶגֶד / u've'ged) DIVERSE.KIND~s2 (כִּלְאַיִם / kil'a'yim) LINSEY-WOOLSEY (שַׁעַטְנֵז / sha'at'neyz) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~GO.UP(Verb) (יַעֲלֶה / ya'a'leh) UPON~you(ms) (עָלֶיךָ / a'ley'kha) [733]

My customs you will safeguard, your beasts you will not cause to be squared with diverse kinds, your fields you will not sow with diverse kinds, and garments of diverse kinds of linsey-woolsey you will not go up upon you.,

20 and~MAN (וְאִישׁ / wê'ish) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~LIE.DOWN(Verb) (יִשְׁכַּב / yish'kav) AT (אֶת / et) WOMAN (אִשָּׁה / i'shah) LYING.DOWN (שִׁכְבַת / shikh'vat) SEED (זֶרַע / ze'ra) and~SHE (וְהִוא / wê'hi) MAID (שִׁפְחָה / shiph'hhah) be~CONSORT(Verb)~ing(fs) (נֶחֱרֶפֶת / ne'hhe're'phet) to~MAN (לְאִישׁ / lê'ish) and~>~be~much~RANSOM(Verb) (וְהָפְדֵּה / wê'haph'deyh) NOT (לֹא / lo) she~did~be~RANSOM(Verb) (נִפְדָּתָה / niph'da'tah) OR (אוֹ / o) FREEDOM (חֻפְשָׁה / hhuph'shah) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~did~be~GIVE(Verb) (נִתַּן / ni'tan) to~~her (לָהּ / lah) PUNISHMENT (בִּקֹּרֶת / bi'qo'ret) she~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִּהְיֶה / tih'yeh) NOT (לֹא / lo) they~will~be~make~DIE(Verb) (יוּמְתוּ / yu'me'tu) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) NOT (לֹא / lo) she~did~be~much~FREE(Verb) (חֻפָּשָׁה / hhu'pa'shah)

and a man that lies down with a woman for the laying down of seed, and she is a maid, being a consort of a man and certainly not ransomed, freedom will not be given her, there will be punishment, they will not be made to die given that she was not free,

21 and~he~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבִיא / wê'hey'vi) AT (אֶת / et) GUILT~him (אֲשָׁמוֹ / a'sha'mo) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) OPENING (פֶּתַח / pe'tahh) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) BUCK (אֵיל / eyl) GUILT (אָשָׁם / a'sham)

and he will bring his guilt to YHWH to the opening of the appointed tent, a buck of guilt,

22 and~he~did~much~COVER(Verb) (וְכִפֶּר / wê'khi'per) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) in~BUCK (בְּאֵיל / bê'eyl) the~GUILT (הָאָשָׁם / ha'a'sham) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) UPON (עַל / al) FAILURE~him (חַטָּאתוֹ / hha'ta'to) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~FAIL(Verb) (חָטָא / hha'ta) and~he~did~be~FORGIVE(Verb) (וְנִסְלַח / wê'nis'lahh) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) from~FAILURE~him (מֵחַטָּאתוֹ / mey'hha'ta'to) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~FAIL(Verb) (חָטָא / hha'ta)

and the administrator will make restitution upon him with the buck of guilt to the face of YHWH, concerning his failure which he failed, and he will be forgiven for him from his failure which he failed,

23 and~GIVEN.THAT (וְכִי / wê'khi) you(mp)~will~COME(Verb) (תָבֹאוּ / ta'vo'u) TO (אֶל / el) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) and~you(mp)~did~PLANT(Verb) (וּנְטַעְתֶּם / un'ta'tem) ALL (כָּל / kol) TREE (עֵץ / eyts) NOURISHMENT (מַאֲכָל / ma'a'khal) and~you(mp)~did~CONSIDERED.UNCIRCUMCISED(Verb) (וַעֲרַלְתֶּם / wa'a'ral'tem) FORESKIN~him (עָרְלָתוֹ / ar'la'to) AT (אֶת / et) PRODUCE~him (פִּרְיוֹ / pir'yo) THREE (שָׁלֹשׁ / sha'losh) YEAR~s (שָׁנִים / sha'nim) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) FORESKIN~s (עֲרֵלִים / a'rey'lim) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~be~EAT(Verb) (יֵאָכֵל / yey'a'kheyl)

and given that you will come to the land, and you will plant every tree of nourishment, and you will consider uncircumcised his foreskin, his produce, three years he will exist to you as foreskin, he will not be eaten,

24 and~in~the~YEAR (וּבַשָּׁנָה / u'va'sha'nah) the~FOURTH (הָרְבִיעִת / har'vi'it) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) ALL (כָּל / kol) PRODUCE~him (פִּרְיוֹ / pir'yo) SPECIAL (קֹדֶשׁ / qo'desh) SHINING~s (הִלּוּלִים / hi'lu'lim) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

and in the fourth year all his produce will exist as special, shining things to YHWH,
