Leviticus Chapter 2

1 and~SOUL (וְנֶפֶשׁ / wê'ne'phesh) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) she~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תַקְרִיב / taq'riv) DONATION (קָרְבַּן / qar'ban) DEPOSIT (מִנְחָה / min'hhah) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) FLOUR (סֹלֶת / so'let) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) DONATION~him (קָרְבָּנוֹ / qar'ba'no) and~he~did~POUR.DOWN(Verb) (וְיָצַק / wê'ya'tsaq) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah) OIL (שֶׁמֶן / she'men) and~he~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַן / wê'na'tan) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah) FRANKINCENSE (לְבֹנָה / lê'vo'nah)

and a soul that will bring near a donation of deposit for YHWH, flour will exist as his donation, and he will pour down upon her oil, and he will give upon her frankincense,

2 and~he~did~make~COME(Verb)~her (וֶהֱבִיאָהּ / we'he'vi'ah) TO (אֶל / el) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) the~ADMINISTRATOR~s (הַכֹּהֲנִים / ha'ko'ha'nim) and~he~did~GRASP(Verb) (וְקָמַץ / wê'qa'mats) from~THERE (מִשָּׁם / mi'sham) FILLING (מְלֹא / mê'lo) HANDFUL~him (קֻמְצוֹ / qum'tso) from~FLOUR~her (מִסָּלְתָּהּ / mi'sal'tah) and~from~OIL (וּמִשַּׁמְנָהּ / u'mi'sham'nah) UPON (עַל / al) ALL (כָּל / kol) to~FRANKINCENSE~her (לְבֹנָתָהּ / lê'vo'na'tah) and~he~did~make~BURN.INCENSE(Verb) (וְהִקְטִיר / wê'hiq'tir) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) AT (אֶת / et) MEMORIAL~her (אַזְכָּרָתָהּ / az'ka'ra'tah) the~ALTAR~unto (הַמִּזְבֵּחָה / ha'miz'bey'hhah) FIRE.OFFERING (אִשֵּׁה / i'sheyh) AROMA (רֵיחַ / rey'ahh) SWEET (נִיחֹחַ / ni'hho'ahh) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

and he will bring her to the sons of Aharon, the administrators, and he will grasp from there, filling his handful from her flour and from the oil upon all for her frankincense, and the administrator will burn her memorial as incense upon the altar, a fire offering, a sweet aroma for YHWH,

3 and~the~be~LEAVE.BEHIND(Verb)~ing(fs) (וְהַנּוֹתֶרֶת / wê'ha'no'te'ret) FROM (מִן / min) the~DEPOSIT (הַמִּנְחָה / ha'min'hhah) to~Aharon (לְאַהֲרֹן / lê'a'ha'ron) and~to~SON~s~him (וּלְבָנָיו / ul'va'naw) SPECIAL (קֹדֶשׁ / qo'desh) SPECIAL~s (קָדָשִׁים / qa'da'shim) [651] from~FIRE.OFFERING~s (מֵאִשֵּׁי / mey'I'shey) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and from the deposit being left behind, it belongs to Aharon and to his sons, it is a special thing of special things from the fire offerings of YHWH,

4 and~GIVEN.THAT (וְכִי / wê'khi) you(ms)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תַקְרִב / taq'riv) DONATION (קָרְבַּן / qar'ban) DEPOSIT (מִנְחָה / min'hhah) BAKED (מַאֲפֵה / ma'a'pheyh) OVEN (תַנּוּר / ta'nur) FLOUR (סֹלֶת / so'let) PIERCED.BREAD~s (חַלּוֹת / hha'lot) UNLEAVENED.BREAD~s (מַצֹּת / ma'tsot) MIX(Verb)~ed(fp) (בְּלוּלֹת / bê'lu'lot) in~the~OIL (בַּשֶּׁמֶן / ba'she'men) and~THIN.BREAD~s (וּרְקִיקֵי / ur'qi'qey) UNLEAVENED.BREAD~s (מַצּוֹת / ma'tsot) SMEAR(Verb)~ed(mp) (מְשֻׁחִים / mê'shu'hhim) in~the~OIL (בַּשָּׁמֶן / ba'sha'men)

and given that you will bring near a donation of deposit, oven baked, flour of unleavened pierced breads mixed with the oil and unleavened thin breads smeared with the oil,

5 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) DEPOSIT (מִנְחָה / min'hhah) UPON (עַל / al) the~PAN (הַמַּחֲבַת / ha'ma'hha'vat) DONATION~you(ms) (קָרְבָּנֶךָ / qar'ba'ne'kha) FLOUR (סֹלֶת / so'let) MIX(Verb)~ed(fs) (בְּלוּלָה / bê'lu'lah) in~the~OIL (בַשֶּׁמֶן / va'she'men) UNLEAVENED.BREAD (מַצָּה / ma'tsah) she~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִהְיֶה / tih'yeh)

and if your donation is a deposit upon the pan, flour mixed with the oil, she will be unleavened bread.

6 >~CRUMBLE(Verb) (פָּתוֹת / pa'tot) AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) FRAGMENT~s (פִּתִּים / pi'tim) and~you(ms)~did~POUR.DOWN(Verb) (וְיָצַקְתָּ / wê'ya'tsaq'ta) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah) OIL (שָׁמֶן / sha'men) DEPOSIT (מִנְחָה / min'hhah) SHE (הִוא / hi)

Crumble her fragments, and you will pour down upon her oil, she is a deposit,

7 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) DEPOSIT (מִנְחַת / mi'ne'hhat) BOILING.POT (מַרְחֶשֶׁת / mar'hhe'shet) DONATION~you(ms) (קָרְבָּנֶךָ / qar'ba'ne'kha) FLOUR (סֹלֶת / so'let) in~the~OIL (בַּשֶּׁמֶן / ba'she'men) she~will~be~DO(Verb) (תֵּעָשֶׂה / tey'a'seh)

and if your donation is a deposit of the boiling pot, the flour will be made with the oil,

8 and~you(ms)~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבֵאתָ / wê'hey'vey'ta) AT (אֶת / et) the~DEPOSIT (הַמִּנְחָה / ha'min'hhah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~be~DO(Verb) (יֵעָשֶׂה / yey'a'seh) from~THESE (מֵאֵלֶּה / mey'ey'leh) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) and~he~did~make~COME.NEAR(Verb)~her (וְהִקְרִיבָהּ / wê'hiq'ri'vah) TO (אֶל / el) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~he~did~make~DRAW.NEAR(Verb)~her (וְהִגִּישָׁהּ / wê'hi'gi'shah) TO (אֶל / el) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh)

and you will bring the deposit, which he will make from these, to YHWH, and he will bring it near to the administrator, and he will draw her near to the altar,

9 and~he~did~make~RAISE.UP(Verb) (וְהֵרִים / wê'hey'rim) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) FROM (מִן / min) the~DEPOSIT (הַמִּנְחָה / ha'min'hhah) AT (אֶת / et) MEMORIAL~her (אַזְכָּרָתָהּ / az'ka'ra'tah) and~he~did~make~BURN.INCENSE(Verb) (וְהִקְטִיר / wê'hiq'tir) the~ALTAR~unto (הַמִּזְבֵּחָה / ha'miz'bey'hhah) FIRE.OFFERING (אִשֵּׁה / i'sheyh) AROMA (רֵיחַ / rey'ahh) SWEET (נִיחֹחַ / ni'hho'ahh) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

and the administrator will raise her memorial up from the deposit, and he will burn incense upon the altar, a fire offering, a sweet aroma for YHWH,

10 and~the~be~LEAVE.BEHIND(Verb)~ing(fs) (וְהַנּוֹתֶרֶת / wê'ha'no'te'ret) FROM (מִן / min) the~DEPOSIT (הַמִּנְחָה / ha'min'hhah) to~Aharon (לְאַהֲרֹן / lê'a'ha'ron) and~to~SON~s~him (וּלְבָנָיו / ul'va'naw) SPECIAL (קֹדֶשׁ / qo'desh) SPECIAL~s (קָדָשִׁים / qa'da'shim) [652] from~FIRE.OFFERING~s (מֵאִשֵּׁי / mey'I'shey) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and from the deposit being left behind, it belongs to Aharon and to his sons, it is a special thing of special things from the fire offerings of YHWH.

11 ALL (כָּל / kol) the~DEPOSIT (הַמִּנְחָה / ha'min'hhah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(mp)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תַּקְרִיבוּ / taq'ri'vu) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תֵעָשֶׂה / tey'a'seh) LEAVENED.BREAD (חָמֵץ / hha'meyts) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) ALL (כָל / khol) LEAVEN (שְׂאֹר / sê'or) and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) HONEY (דְּבַשׁ / dê'vash) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~make~BURN.INCENSE(Verb) (תַקְטִירוּ / taq'ti'ru) FROM~him (מִמֶּנּוּ / mi'me'nu) FIRE.OFFERING (אִשֶּׁה / i'sheh) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

All the deposit, which you will bring near to YHWH, you will not make leavened bread, given that you will not burn any leaven or any honey as incense from him, a fire offering for YHWH.

12 DONATION (קָרְבַּן / qar'ban) ORIGIN (רֵאשִׁית / rey'shit) [653] you(mp)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תַּקְרִיבוּ / taq'ri'vu) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) and~TO (וְאֶל / wê'el) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) NOT (לֹא / lo) they(m)~will~GO.UP(Verb) (יַעֲלוּ / ya'a'lu) [654] to~AROMA (לְרֵיחַ / lê'rey'ahh) SWEET (נִיחֹחַ / ni'hho'ahh)

The donation of the origin, you will bring them near to YHWH and to the altar, they will not go up for a sweet aroma,

13 and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) DONATION (קָרְבַּן / qar'ban) DEPOSIT~you(ms) (מִנְחָתְךָ / min'hha'te'kha) in~the~SALT (בַּמֶּלַח / ba'me'lahh) you(ms)~will~SEASON(Verb) (תִּמְלָח / tim'lahh) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~make~CEASE(Verb) (תַשְׁבִּית / tash'bit) SALT (מֶלַח / me'lahh) COVENANT (בְּרִית / bê'rit) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) from~UPON (מֵעַל / mey'al) DEPOSIT~you(ms) (מִנְחָתֶךָ / min'hha'te'kha) UPON (עַל / al) ALL (כָּל / kol) DONATION~you(ms) (קָרְבָּנְךָ / qar'ban'kha) you(ms)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תַּקְרִיב / taq'riv) SALT (מֶלַח / me'lahh)

and all the donations of your deposit you will season with salt, and you will not cease the salt of the covenant of your Elohiym from upon your deposit, upon all your donations you will bring near with salt,

14 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) you(ms)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תַּקְרִיב / taq'riv) DEPOSIT (מִנְחַת / mi'ne'hhat) FIRSTFRUIT~s (בִּכּוּרִים / bi'ku'rim) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) GREEN.GRAIN (אָבִיב / a'viv) DRY(Verb)~ed(ms) (קָלוּי / qa'lui) in~the~FIRE (בָּאֵשׁ / ba'eysh) BEATEN.GRAIN (גֶּרֶשׂ / ge'res) PLANTATION (כַּרְמֶל / kar'mel) you(ms)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תַּקְרִיב / taq'riv) AT (אֵת / eyt) DEPOSIT (מִנְחַת / mi'ne'hhat) FIRSTFRUIT~s~you(ms) (בִּכּוּרֶיךָ / bi'ku'rey'kha)

and if you will bring near a deposit of the firstfruits for YHWH, it will be green grain dried by the fire, beaten grain of the plantation, you will bring near the deposit of your firstfruits,

15 and~you(ms)~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַתָּ / wê'na'ta'ta) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah) OIL (שֶׁמֶן / she'men) and~you(ms)~did~PLACE(Verb) (וְשַׂמְתָּ / wê'sam'ta) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah) FRANKINCENSE (לְבֹנָה / lê'vo'nah) DEPOSIT (מִנְחָה / min'hhah) SHE (הִוא / hi)

and you will give upon her oil, and you will place upon her frankincense, she is a deposit,

16 and~he~did~make~BURN.INCENSE(Verb) (וְהִקְטִיר / wê'hiq'tir) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) AT (אֶת / et) MEMORIAL~her (אַזְכָּרָתָהּ / az'ka'ra'tah) from~BEATEN.GRAIN~her (מִגִּרְשָׂהּ / mi'gir'sah) and~from~OIL (וּמִשַּׁמְנָהּ / u'mi'sham'nah) UPON (עַל / al) ALL (כָּל / kol) to~FRANKINCENSE~her (לְבֹנָתָהּ / lê'vo'na'tah) FIRE.OFFERING (אִשֶּׁה / i'sheh) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

and the administrator will make incense with her memorial, from her beaten grain and from the oil, with all of her frankincense, a fire offering for YHWH,
