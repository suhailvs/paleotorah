Leviticus Chapter 4

1 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

2 !(ms)~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דַּבֵּר / da'beyr) TO (אֶל / el) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) SOUL (נֶפֶשׁ / ne'phesh) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) she~will~FAIL(Verb) (תֶחֱטָא / te'hhe'ta) in~ERROR (בִשְׁגָגָה / vish'ga'gah) from~ALL (מִכֹּל / mi'kol) DIRECTIVE~s (מִצְוֹת / mits'ot) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) NOT (לֹא / lo) they(f)~will~be~DO(Verb) (תֵעָשֶׂינָה / tey'a'sey'nah) and~he~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשָׂה / wê'a'sah) from~UNIT (מֵאַחַת / mey'a'hhat) from~THEY(f) (מֵהֵנָּה / mey'hey'nah)

Speak to the sons of Yisra'eyl saying, a soul that will fail with an error from any of the directives of YHWH, which were not done, and he will do from one of them.

3 IF (אִם / im) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) the~SMEARED (הַמָּשִׁיחַ / ha'ma'shi'ahh) he~will~FAIL(Verb) (יֶחֱטָא / ye'hhe'ta) to~GUILTINESS (לְאַשְׁמַת / lê'ash'mat) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) and~he~did~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וְהִקְרִיב / wê'hiq'riv) UPON (עַל / al) FAILURE~him (חַטָּאתוֹ / hha'ta'to) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~FAIL(Verb) (חָטָא / hha'ta) BULL (פַּר / par) SON (בֶּן / ben) CATTLE (בָּקָר / ba'qar) WHOLE (תָּמִים / ta'mim) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) to~FAILURE (לְחַטָּאת / lê'hha'tat)

If the smeared administrator will fail, it will be guiltiness of the people, and he will bring near, for his failure because he failed, a bull son, a whole cattle to YHWH for the failure,

4 and~he~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבִיא / wê'hey'vi) AT (אֶת / et) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) TO (אֶל / el) OPENING (פֶּתַח / pe'tahh) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~he~did~SUPPORT(Verb) (וְסָמַךְ / wê'sa'makh) AT (אֶת / et) HAND~him (יָדוֹ / ya'do) UPON (עַל / al) HEAD (רֹאשׁ / rosh) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) and~he~did~SLAY(Verb) (וְשָׁחַט / wê'sha'hhat) AT (אֶת / et) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and he will bring the bull to the opening of the appointed tent, to the face of YHWH, and he will support his hand upon the head of the bull, and he will slay the bull to the face of YHWH,

5 and~he~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקַח / wê'la'qahh) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) the~SMEARED (הַמָּשִׁיחַ / ha'ma'shi'ahh) from~BLOOD (מִדַּם / mi'dam) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) and~he~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבִיא / wê'hey'vi) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) TO (אֶל / el) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd)

and the smeared administrator will take from the blood of the bull and he will bring him to the appointed tent,

6 and~he~did~DIP(Verb) (וְטָבַל / wê'ta'val) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) AT (אֶת / et) FINGER~him (אֶצְבָּעוֹ / ets'ba'o) in~the~BLOOD (בַּדָּם / ba'dam) and~he~did~make~SPATTER(Verb) (וְהִזָּה / wê'hi'zah) FROM (מִן / min) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam) SEVEN (שֶׁבַע / she'va) FOOTSTEP~s (פְּעָמִים / pê'a'mim) [656] to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT (אֶת / et) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) TENT.CURTAIN (פָּרֹכֶת / pa'ro'khet) the~SPECIAL (הַקֹּדֶשׁ / ha'qo'desh)

and the administrator will dip his finger in the blood and he will spatter from the blood seven times to the face of YHWH, at the face of the special tent curtain,

7 and~he~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַן / wê'na'tan) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) FROM (מִן / min) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam) UPON (עַל / al) HORN~s (קַרְנוֹת / qar'not) ALTAR (מִזְבַּח / miz'bahh) INCENSE.SMOKE (קְטֹרֶת / qê'to'ret) the~AROMATIC.SPICE~s (הַסַּמִּים / ha'sa'mim) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~TENT (בְּאֹהֶל / bê'o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) BLOOD (דַּם / dam) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) he~will~POUR.OUT(Verb) (יִשְׁפֹּךְ / yish'pokh) TO (אֶל / el) BOTTOM.BASE (יְסוֹד / yê'sod) ALTAR (מִזְבַּח / miz'bahh) the~ASCENSION.OFFERING (הָעֹלָה / ha'o'lah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) OPENING (פֶּתַח / pe'tahh) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd)

and the administrator will place from the blood upon the horns of the altar of incense smoke of the aromatic spices to the face of YHWH, which is in the appointed tent, and he will pour out all the blood of the bull to the bottom base of the altar of the ascension offering, which is at the opening of the appointed tent,

8 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) ALL (כָּל / kol) FAT (חֵלֶב / hhey'lev) BULL (פַּר / par) the~FAILURE (הַחַטָּאת / ha'hha'tat) he~will~make~RAISE.UP(Verb) (יָרִים / ya'rim) FROM~him (מִמֶּנּוּ / mi'me'nu) AT (אֶת / et) the~FAT (הַחֵלֶב / ha'hhey'lev) the~much~COVER.OVER(Verb)~ing(ms) (הַמְכַסֶּה / ham'kha'seh) UPON (עַל / al) the~INSIDE (הַקֶּרֶב / ha'qe'rev) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~FAT (הַחֵלֶב / ha'hhey'lev) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) UPON (עַל / al) the~INSIDE (הַקֶּרֶב / ha'qe'rev)

and he will raise up all the fat of the bull of the failure, the fat of the covering upon the insides and all the fat which is upon the insides,

9 and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) TWO (שְׁתֵּי / shê'tey) the~KIDNEY~s (הַכְּלָיֹת / hak'la'yot) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~FAT (הַחֵלֶב / ha'hhey'lev) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) UPON~them(f) (עֲלֵיהֶן / a'ley'hen) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) UPON (עַל / al) the~HIP~s (הַכְּסָלִים / hak'sa'lim) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~LOBE (הַיֹּתֶרֶת / hai'yo'te'ret) UPON (עַל / al) the~HEAVY (הַכָּבֵד / ha'ka'veyd) [657] UPON (עַל / al) the~KIDNEY~s (הַכְּלָיוֹת / hak'la'yot) he~will~make~TURN.ASIDE(Verb)~her (יְסִירֶנָּה / yê'si're'nah)

and the two kidneys and the fat, which is upon them, which is upon the hips, and the lobe upon the liver and upon the kidneys he will remove.

10 like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) they(m)~will~make~RAISE.UP(Verb) (יוּרַם / yu'ram) from~OX (מִשּׁוֹר / mi'shor) SACRIFICE (זֶבַח / ze'vahh) the~OFFERING.OF.RESTITUTION~s (הַשְּׁלָמִים / ha'she'la'mim) and~he~did~make~BURN.INCENSE(Verb)~them(m) (וְהִקְטִירָם / wê'hiq'ti'ram) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) UPON (עַל / al) ALTAR (מִזְבַּח / miz'bahh) the~ASCENSION.OFFERING (הָעֹלָה / ha'o'lah)

Just as they were raised up from the ox of the sacrifice of the offerings of restitution, and the administrator will burn them as incense upon the altar of the ascension offering,

11 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) SKIN (עוֹר / or) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) ALL (כָּל / kol) FLESH~him (בְּשָׂרוֹ / bê'sar'o) UPON (עַל / al) HEAD~him (רֹאשׁוֹ / ro'sho) and~UPON (וְעַל / wê'al) LEG~s~him (כְּרָעָיו / kê'ra'aw) and~INSIDE~him (וְקִרְבּוֹ / wê'qir'bo) and~DUNG~him (וּפִרְשׁוֹ / u'phir'sho)

and the skin of the bull and all his flesh upon his head and upon his legs and his inside and his dung,

12 and~he~did~make~GO.OUT(Verb) (וְהוֹצִיא / wê'ho'tsi) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) TO (אֶל / el) from~OUTSIDE (מִחוּץ / mi'hhuts) to~the~CAMP (לַמַּחֲנֶה / la'ma'hha'neh) TO (אֶל / el) AREA (מָקוֹם / ma'qom) CLEAN (טָהוֹר / ta'hor) TO (אֶל / el) POUR.OUT(Verb)~ing(ms) (שֶׁפֶךְ / she'phekh) the~FATNESS (הַדֶּשֶׁן / ha'de'shen) and~he~did~CREMATE(Verb) (וְשָׂרַף / wê'sa'raph) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) UPON (עַל / al) TREE~s (עֵצִים / ey'tsim) in~the~FIRE (בָּאֵשׁ / ba'eysh) UPON (עַל / al) POUR.OUT(Verb)~ing(ms) (שֶׁפֶךְ / she'phekh) the~FATNESS (הַדֶּשֶׁן / ha'de'shen) he~will~be~CREMATE(Verb) (יִשָּׂרֵף / yi'sa'reyph)

and he will bring out all of the bull to the outside of the camp, to the clean area for pouring out the fatness, and he will cremate him upon the wood with the fire, upon the pouring out of the fatness he will be cremated,

13 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) ALL (כָּל / kol) COMPANY (עֲדַת / a'dat) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) they(m)~will~GO.ASTRAY(Verb) (יִשְׁגּוּ / yish'gu) and~he~did~be~BE.OUT.OF.SIGHT(Verb) (וְנֶעְלַם / wê'ne'lam) WORD (דָּבָר / da'var) [658] from~EYE~s2 (מֵעֵינֵי / mey'ey'ney) the~ASSEMBLY (הַקָּהָל / ha'qa'hal) and~they~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשׂוּ / wê'a'su) UNIT (אַחַת / a'hhat) from~ALL (מִכָּל / mi'kol) DIRECTIVE~s (מִצְוֹת / mits'ot) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) NOT (לֹא / lo) they(f)~will~be~DO(Verb) (תֵעָשֶׂינָה / tey'a'sey'nah) [659] and~they~did~BE.GUILTY(Verb) (וְאָשֵׁמוּ / wê'a'shey'mu)

and if all the company of Yisra'eyl will go astray, and a word was out of sight from the eyes of the assembly, and they did anyone of the directives of YHWH, which was not to be done, then they will be guilty,

14 and~she~did~be~KNOW(Verb) (וְנוֹדְעָה / wê'nod'ah) the~FAILURE (הַחַטָּאת / ha'hha'tat) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they~did~FAIL(Verb) (חָטְאוּ / hhat'u) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah) and~they~did~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וְהִקְרִיבוּ / wê'hiq'ri'vu) the~ASSEMBLY (הַקָּהָל / ha'qa'hal) BULL (פַּר / par) SON (בֶּן / ben) CATTLE (בָּקָר / ba'qar) to~FAILURE (לְחַטָּאת / lê'hha'tat) and~they~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבִיאוּ / wê'hey'vi'u) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd)

and the failure, which they failed, will be known upon her, and the assembly will bring near a son of a bull of the cattle for the failure, and they will bring him to the face of the appointed tent,

15 and~they~did~SUPPORT(Verb) (וְסָמְכוּ / wê'sam'khu) BEARD~s (זִקְנֵי / ziq'ney) the~COMPANY (הָעֵדָה / ha'ey'dah) AT (אֶת / et) HAND~s2~them(m) (יְדֵיהֶם / yê'dey'hem) UPON (עַל / al) HEAD (רֹאשׁ / rosh) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~he~did~SLAY(Verb) (וְשָׁחַט / wê'sha'hhat) AT (אֶת / et) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and the bearded ones will support the company with their hands upon the head of the bull to the face of YHWH, and they will slay the bull to the face of YHWH,

16 and~he~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבִיא / wê'hey'vi) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) the~SMEARED (הַמָּשִׁיחַ / ha'ma'shi'ahh) from~BLOOD (מִדַּם / mi'dam) the~BULL (הַפָּר / ha'par) TO (אֶל / el) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd)

and the smeared administrator will bring the blood from the bull to the appointed tent,

17 and~he~did~DIP(Verb) (וְטָבַל / wê'ta'val) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) FINGER~him (אֶצְבָּעוֹ / ets'ba'o) FROM (מִן / min) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam) and~he~did~make~SPATTER(Verb) (וְהִזָּה / wê'hi'zah) SEVEN (שֶׁבַע / she'va) FOOTSTEP~s (פְּעָמִים / pê'a'mim) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT (אֵת / eyt) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) the~TENT.CURTAIN (הַפָּרֹכֶת / ha'pa'ro'khet)

and the administrator will dip his finger in the blood and he will spatter it seven times to the face of YHWH, at the face of the tent curtain,

18 and~FROM (וּמִן / u'min) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam) he~will~GIVE(Verb) (יִתֵּן / yi'teyn) [660] UPON (עַל / al) HORN~s (קַרְנֹת / qar'not) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~TENT (בְּאֹהֶל / bê'o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~BLOOD (הַדָּם / ha'dam) he~will~POUR.OUT(Verb) (יִשְׁפֹּךְ / yish'pokh) TO (אֶל / el) BOTTOM.BASE (יְסוֹד / yê'sod) ALTAR (מִזְבַּח / miz'bahh) the~ASCENSION.OFFERING (הָעֹלָה / ha'o'lah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) OPENING (פֶּתַח / pe'tahh) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd)

and from the blood, he will place it upon the horns of the altar, which is to the face of YHWH, which is in the appointed tent, and he will pour out all the blood to the bottom base of the altar of the ascension offering, which is at the opening of the appointed tent,

19 and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) FAT~him (חֶלְבּוֹ / hhel'bo) he~will~make~RAISE.UP(Verb) (יָרִים / ya'rim) FROM~him (מִמֶּנּוּ / mi'me'nu) and~he~did~make~BURN.INCENSE(Verb) (וְהִקְטִיר / wê'hiq'tir) the~ALTAR~unto (הַמִּזְבֵּחָה / ha'miz'bey'hhah)

and he will raise up all his fat from him, and he will burn it as incense upon the altar,

20 and~he~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשָׂה / wê'a'sah) to~the~BULL (לַפָּר / la'par) like~WHICH (כַּאֲשֶׁר / ka'a'sheyr) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) to~BULL (לְפַר / lê'phar) the~FAILURE (הַחַטָּאת / ha'hha'tat) SO (כֵּן / keyn) he~will~DO(Verb) (יַעֲשֶׂה / ya'a'seh) to~~him (לּוֹ / lo) and~he~did~much~COVER(Verb) (וְכִפֶּר / wê'khi'per) UPON~them(m) (עֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~he~did~be~FORGIVE(Verb) (וְנִסְלַח / wê'nis'lahh) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem)

and he will do to the bull just as he did to the bull of the failure, so he will do to him, and the administrator will make a covering upon them and he will be forgiven for them,

and he will bring out the bull to the outside of the camp, and he will cremate him just as he cremated the first bull, he is the failure of the assembly.
