Exodus Chapter 37

1 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) Betsaleyl (בְּצַלְאֵל / bê'tsal'eyl) AT (אֶת / et) the~BOX (הָאָרֹן / ha'a'ron) TREE~s (עֲצֵי / a'tsey) ACACIA~s (שִׁטִּים / shi'tim) FOREARM~s2 (אַמָּתַיִם / a'ma'ta'yim) and~HALF (וָחֵצִי / wa'hhey'tsi) LENGTH~him (אָרְכּוֹ / ar'ko) and~FOREARM (וְאַמָּה / wê'a'mah) and~HALF (וָחֵצִי / wa'hhey'tsi) WIDTH~him (רָחְבּוֹ / rahh'bo) and~FOREARM (וְאַמָּה / wê'a'mah) and~HALF (וָחֵצִי / wa'hhey'tsi) HEIGHT~him (קֹמָתוֹ / qo'ma'to)

and Betsaleyl made the box of acacia wood, two forearms and a half is his length, and a forearm and a half is his width, and a forearm and a half is his height,

2 and~he~will~much~OVERLAY(Verb)~him (וַיְצַפֵּהוּ / wai'tsa'pey'hu) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) CLEAN (טָהוֹר / ta'hor) from~HOUSE (מִבַּיִת / mi'ba'yit) and~from~OUTSIDE (וּמִחוּץ / u'mi'hhuts) and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) MOLDING (זֵר / zeyr) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) ALL.AROUND (סָבִיב / sa'viv)

and he overlaid him with clean gold, from the inside and from the outside, and he made for him a molding of gold all around,

3 and~he~will~POUR.DOWN(Verb) (וַיִּצֹק / wai'yi'tsoq) [611] to~~him (לוֹ / lo) FOUR (אַרְבַּע / ar'ba) RING~s (טַבְּעֹת / ta'be'ot) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) UPON (עַל / al) FOUR (אַרְבַּע / ar'ba) FOOTSTEP~s~him (פַּעֲמֹתָיו / pa'a'mo'taw) [612] and~TWO (וּשְׁתֵּי / ush'tey) RING~s (טַבָּעֹת / ta'ba'ot) UPON (עַל / al) RIB~him (צַלְעוֹ / tsal'o) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָת / ha'e'hhat) and~TWO (וּשְׁתֵּי / ush'tey) RING~s (טַבָּעֹות / ta'ba'ot) UPON (עַל / al) RIB~him (צַלְעוֹ / tsal'o) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִית / ha'shey'nit)

and he poured down for him four rings of gold upon his four footsteps and two rings upon his one rib, and two rings upon his second rib,

4 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) STRAND~s (בַּדֵּי / ba'dey) TREE~s (עֲצֵי / a'tsey) ACACIA~s (שִׁטִּים / shi'tim) and~he~will~much~OVERLAY(Verb) (וַיְצַף / wai'tsaph) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav)

and he made strands of acacia wood, and he overlaid them with gold,

5 and~he~will~make~COME(Verb) (וַיָּבֵא / wai'ya'vey) AT (אֶת / et) the~STRAND~s (הַבַּדִּים / ha'ba'dim) in~the~RING~s (בַּטַּבָּעֹת / ba'ta'ba'ot) UPON (עַל / al) RIB~s (צַלְעֹת / tsal'ot) the~BOX (הָאָרֹן / ha'a'ron) to~>~LIFT.UP(Verb) (לָשֵׂאת / la'seyt) AT (אֶת / et) the~BOX (הָאָרֹן / ha'a'ron)

and he brought the strands in the rings upon the ribs of the box to lift up the box,

6 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) LID (כַּפֹּרֶת / ka'po'ret) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) CLEAN (טָהוֹר / ta'hor) FOREARM~s2 (אַמָּתַיִם / a'ma'ta'yim) and~HALF (וָחֵצִי / wa'hhey'tsi) LENGTH~her (אָרְכָּהּ / ar'kah) and~FOREARM (וְאַמָּה / wê'a'mah) and~HALF (וָחֵצִי / wa'hhey'tsi) WIDTH~her (רָחְבָּהּ / rahh'bah)

and he made a lid of clean gold, two forearms and a half is her length, and a forearm and a half is her width,

7 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) TWO (שְׁנֵי / shê'ney) KERUV~s (כְרֻבִים / khê'ru'vim) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) BEATEN.WORK (מִקְשָׁה / miq'shah) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) from~TWO (מִשְּׁנֵי / mish'ney) EXTREMITY~s (קְצוֹת / qê'tsot) the~LID (הַכַּפֹּרֶת / ha'ka'po'ret)

and he made two keruvs of gold, a beaten work, he made them from the two extremities of the lid.

8 KERUV (כְּרוּב / kê'ruv) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) from~EXTREMITY (מִקָּצָה / mi'qa'tsah) from~THIS (מִזֶּה / mi'zeh) and~KERUV (וּכְרוּב / ukh'ruv) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) from~EXTREMITY (מִקָּצָה / mi'qa'tsah) from~THIS (מִזֶּה / mi'zeh) FROM (מִן / min) the~LID (הַכַּפֹּרֶת / ha'ka'po'ret) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) AT (אֶת / et) the~KERUV~s (הַכְּרֻבִים / hak'ru'vim) from~TWO (מִשְּׁנֵי / mish'ney) EXTREMITY~s~him (קִצְווֹתֹו / qits'wo'to) [613]

One keruv from this extremity, and the other keruv from that extremity, from the lid he made the keruvs, from the two ends of his extremities,

9 and~they(m)~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיִּהְיוּ / wai'yih'yu) the~KERUV~s (הַכְּרֻבִים / hak'ru'vim) SPREAD.OUT(Verb)~ing(mp) (פֹּרְשֵׂי / por'sey) WING~s2 (כְנָפַיִם / khê'na'pha'yim) to~UPWARD~unto (לְמַעְלָה / lê'ma'lah) FENCE.AROUND(Verb)~ing(mp) (סֹכְכִים / sokh'khim) in~WING~s~them(m) (בְּכַנְפֵיהֶם / bê'khan'phey'hem) UPON (עַל / al) the~LID (הַכַּפֹּרֶת / ha'ka'po'ret) and~FACE~s~them(m) (וּפְנֵיהֶם / uph'ney'hem) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) TO (אֶל / el) BROTHER~him (אָחִיו / a'hhiw) TO (אֶל / el) the~LID (הַכַּפֹּרֶת / ha'ka'po'ret) they~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיוּ / hai'u) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) the~KERUV~s (הַכְּרֻבִים / hak'ru'vim)

and the keruvs were spreading out the wings above, fencing around with their wings upon the lid, and their faces each to his brother, the faces of the keruvs will exist toward the lid,

10 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) AT (אֶת / et) the~TABLE (הַשֻּׁלְחָן / ha'shul'hhan) TREE~s (עֲצֵי / a'tsey) ACACIA~s (שִׁטִּים / shi'tim) FOREARM~s2 (אַמָּתַיִם / a'ma'ta'yim) LENGTH~him (אָרְכּוֹ / ar'ko) and~FOREARM (וְאַמָּה / wê'a'mah) WIDTH~him (רָחְבּוֹ / rahh'bo) and~FOREARM (וְאַמָּה / wê'a'mah) and~HALF (וָחֵצִי / wa'hhey'tsi) HEIGHT~him (קֹמָתוֹ / qo'ma'to)

and he made the table of acacia wood, two forearms is his length, and a forearm is his width, and a forearm and a half is his height,

11 and~he~will~much~OVERLAY(Verb) (וַיְצַף / wai'tsaph) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) CLEAN (טָהוֹר / ta'hor) and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) MOLDING (זֵר / zeyr) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) ALL.AROUND (סָבִיב / sa'viv)

and he overlaid him with clean gold, and he made for him a molding of gold all around,

12 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) RIM (מִסְגֶּרֶת / mis'ge'ret) HAND.SPAN (טֹפַח / to'phahh) ALL.AROUND (סָבִיב / sa'viv) and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) MOLDING (זֵר / zeyr) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) to~RIM~him (לְמִסְגַּרְתּוֹ / lê'mis'gar'to) ALL.AROUND (סָבִיב / sa'viv)

and he made for him a rim a hand span all around, and he made a molding of gold for him all around,

13 and~he~will~POUR.DOWN(Verb) (וַיִּצֹק / wai'yi'tsoq) [614] to~~him (לוֹ / lo) FOUR (אַרְבַּע / ar'ba) RING~s (טַבְּעֹת / ta'be'ot) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) and~he~will~GIVE(Verb) (וַיִּתֵּן / wai'yi'teyn) AT (אֶת / et) the~RING~s (הַטַּבָּעֹת / ha'ta'ba'ot) UPON (עַל / al) FOUR (אַרְבַּע / ar'ba) the~EDGE~s (הַפֵּאֹת / ha'pey'ot) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) to~FOUR (לְאַרְבַּע / lê'ar'ba) FOOT~s2~him (רַגְלָיו / rag'law)

and he poured down for him four rings of gold, and he placed the rings upon the four edges which is for his four feet.

14 to~ALONGSIDE (לְעֻמַּת / lê'u'mat) the~RIM (הַמִּסְגֶּרֶת / ha'mis'ge'ret) they~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיוּ / hai'u) the~RING~s (הַטַּבָּעֹת / ha'ta'ba'ot) HOUSE~s (בָּתִּים / ba'tim) [615] to~the~STRAND~s (לַבַּדִּים / la'ba'dim) to~>~LIFT.UP(Verb) (לָשֵׂאת / la'seyt) AT (אֶת / et) the~TABLE (הַשֻּׁלְחָן / ha'shul'hhan)

The rings existed alongside the rim, houses for the strands to lift up the table,

15 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) AT (אֶת / et) the~STRAND~s (הַבַּדִּים / ha'ba'dim) TREE~s (עֲצֵי / a'tsey) ACACIA~s (שִׁטִּים / shi'tim) and~he~will~much~OVERLAY(Verb) (וַיְצַף / wai'tsaph) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) to~>~LIFT.UP(Verb) (לָשֵׂאת / la'seyt) AT (אֶת / et) the~TABLE (הַשֻּׁלְחָן / ha'shul'hhan)

and he made the strands of acacia wood, and he overlaid them with gold to lift up the table,

16 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) AT (אֶת / et) the~UTENSIL~s (הַכֵּלִים / ha'key'lim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) UPON (עַל / al) the~TABLE (הַשֻּׁלְחָן / ha'shul'hhan) AT (אֶת / et) PLATTER~s~him (קְעָרֹתָיו / qê'a'ro'taw) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) PALM~s~him (כַּפֹּתָיו / ka'po'taw) [616] and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) SACRIFICIAL.BOWL~s~him (מְנַקִּיֹּתָיו / mê'na'qi'yo'taw) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~JUG~s (הַקְּשָׂו‍ֹת / haq'sa'ot) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~be~make~POUR(Verb) (יֻסַּךְ / yu'sakh) in~~them(f) (בָּהֵן / ba'heyn) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) CLEAN (טָהוֹר / ta'hor) [617]

and he made the utensils which are upon the table, his platters, and his palms and his sacrificial bowls and his jugs, which will be for pouring with them, and he made them with clean gold,

17 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) AT (אֶת / et) the~LAMPSTAND (הַמְּנֹרָה / ham'no'rah) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) CLEAN (טָהוֹר / ta'hor) BEATEN.WORK (מִקְשָׁה / miq'shah) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) AT (אֶת / et) the~LAMPSTAND (הַמְּנֹרָה / ham'no'rah) MIDSECTION~her (יְרֵכָהּ / yê'rey'khah) and~STALK~her (וְקָנָהּ / wê'qa'nah) BOWL~s~her (גְּבִיעֶיהָ / gê'vi'ey'ah) KNOB~s~her (כַּפְתֹּרֶיהָ / kaph'to'rey'ah) and~BUD~s~her (וּפְרָחֶיהָ / uph'ra'hhey'ah) FROM~her (מִמֶּנָּה / mi'me'nah) [618] they~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיוּ / hai'u)

and he made the lampstand with clean gold, a beaten work, and he made the lampstand, her midsection, and her stalk, her bowls, her knobs and her buds existed out of her,

18 and~SIX (וְשִׁשָּׁה / wê'shi'shah) STALK~s (קָנִים / qa'nim) GO.OUT(Verb)~ing(mp) (יֹצְאִים / yots'im) from~SIDE~s~her (מִצִּדֶּיהָ / mi'tsi'dey'ah) THREE (שְׁלֹשָׁה / shê'lo'shah) STALK~s (קְנֵי / qê'ney) LAMPSTAND (מְנֹרָה / mê'no'rah) from~SIDE~her (מִצִּדָּהּ / mi'tsi'dah) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad) and~THREE (וּשְׁלֹשָׁה / ush'lo'shah) STALK~s (קְנֵי / qê'ney) LAMPSTAND (מְנֹרָה / mê'no'rah) from~SIDE~her (מִצִּדָּהּ / mi'tsi'dah) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִי / ha'shey'ni)

and six stalks are going out from her sides, three stalks of the lampstand out of her one side, and three stalks of the lampstand out of her second side.

19 THREE (שְׁלֹשָׁה / shê'lo'shah) BOWL~s (גְבִעִים / gê'vi'im) be~much~BE.ALMOND.SHAPED(Verb)~ing(mp) (מְשֻׁקָּדִים / mê'shu'qa'dim) in~the~STALK (בַּקָּנֶה / ba'qa'neh) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad) KNOB (כַּפְתֹּר / kaph'tor) and~BUD (וָפֶרַח / wa'phe'rahh) and~THREE (וּשְׁלֹשָׁה / ush'lo'shah) BOWL~s (גְבִעִים / gê'vi'im) be~much~BE.ALMOND.SHAPED(Verb)~ing(mp) (מְשֻׁקָּדִים / mê'shu'qa'dim) in~STALK (בְּקָנֶה / bê'qa'neh) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) KNOB (כַּפְתֹּר / kaph'tor) and~BUD (וָפָרַח / wa'pha'rahh) SO (כֵּן / keyn) to~SIX (לְשֵׁשֶׁת / lê'shey'shet) the~STALK~s (הַקָּנִים / ha'qa'nim) the~GO.OUT(Verb)~ing(mp) (הַיֹּצְאִים / hai'yots'im) FROM (מִן / min) the~LAMPSTAND (הַמְּנֹרָה / ham'no'rah)

Three bowls, being almond shaped in the stalk of the one with a knob and a bud, and three bowls, being almond shaped in the stalk of the other with a knob and a bud, so it is for the six stalks going out from the lampstand,

20 and~in~the~LAMPSTAND (וּבַמְּנֹרָה / u'vam'no'rah) FOUR (אַרְבָּעָה / ar'ba'ah) BOWL~s (גְבִעִים / gê'vi'im) be~much~BE.ALMOND.SHAPED(Verb)~ing(mp) (מְשֻׁקָּדִים / mê'shu'qa'dim) KNOB~s~her (כַּפְתֹּרֶיהָ / kaph'to'rey'ah) and~BUD~s~her (וּפְרָחֶיהָ / uph'ra'hhey'ah)

and in the lampstand are four bowls being almond shaped with her knobs and her buds,

21 and~KNOB (וְכַפְתֹּר / wê'khaph'tor) UNDER (תַּחַת / ta'hhat) TWO (שְׁנֵי / shê'ney) the~STALK~s (הַקָּנִים / ha'qa'nim) FROM~her (מִמֶּנָּה / mi'me'nah) and~KNOB (וְכַפְתֹּר / wê'khaph'tor) UNDER (תַּחַת / ta'hhat) TWO (שְׁנֵי / shê'ney) the~STALK~s (הַקָּנִים / ha'qa'nim) FROM~her (מִמֶּנָּה / mi'me'nah) and~KNOB (וְכַפְתֹּר / wê'khaph'tor) UNDER (תַּחַת / ta'hhat) TWO (שְׁנֵי / shê'ney) the~STALK~s (הַקָּנִים / ha'qa'nim) FROM~her (מִמֶּנָּה / mi'me'nah) to~SIX (לְשֵׁשֶׁת / lê'shey'shet) the~STALK~s (הַקָּנִים / ha'qa'nim) the~GO.OUT(Verb)~ing(mp) (הַיֹּצְאִים / hai'yots'im) FROM~her (מִמֶּנָּה / mi'me'nah)

and a knob under two of the stalks out of her, and a knob under two of the stalks out of her, and a knob under two of the stalks out of her, for the six stalks going out from her.

22 KNOB~s~them(m) (כַּפְתֹּרֵיהֶם / kaph'to'rey'hem) and~STALK~s~them(m) (וּקְנֹתָם / uq'no'tam) FROM~her (מִמֶּנָּה / mi'me'nah) they~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיוּ / hai'u) ALL~her (כֻּלָּהּ / ku'lah) BEATEN.WORK (מִקְשָׁה / miq'shah) UNIT (אַחַת / a'hhat) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) CLEAN (טָהוֹר / ta'hor)

Their knobs and their stalks out of her, all of her will exist as one beaten work of clean gold,

23 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) AT (אֶת / et) LAMP~s~her (נֵרֹתֶיהָ / ney'ro'te'yah) SEVEN (שִׁבְעָה / shiv'ah) and~TONG~s~her (וּמַלְקָחֶיהָ / u'mal'qa'hhey'ah) and~FIRE.PAN~s~her (וּמַחְתֹּתֶיהָ / u'mahh'to'tey'ah) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) CLEAN (טָהוֹר / ta'hor)

and he made her seven lamps, and her tongs, and her fire pans, with clean gold.

24 KIKAR (כִּכָּר / ki'kar) GOLD (זָהָב / za'hav) CLEAN (טָהוֹר / ta'hor) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) UTENSIL~s~her (כֵּלֶיהָ / key'ley'ah)

A kikar of clean gold he made her and all her utensils,
