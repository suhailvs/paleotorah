Numbers Chapter 28

1 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

2 !(ms)~much~DIRECT(Verb) (צַו / tsaw) AT (אֶת / et) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~you(ms)~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמַרְתָּ / wê'a'mar'ta) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) AT (אֶת / et) DONATION~me (קָרְבָּנִי / qar'ba'ni) BREAD~me (לַחְמִי / lahh'mi) to~FIRE.OFFERING~s~me (לְאִשַּׁי / lê'i'shai) AROMA (רֵיחַ / rey'ahh) SWEET~me (נִיחֹחִי / ni'hho'hhi) you(mp)~will~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (תִּשְׁמְרוּ / tish'me'ru) to~>~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (לְהַקְרִיב / lê'haq'riv) to~~me (לִי / li) in~APPOINTED~him (בְּמוֹעֲדוֹ / bê'mo'a'do)

Direct the sons of Yisra'eyl, and you will say to them, my donation, my bread, for my fire offerings, my sweet aroma, you will safeguard to bring near to me in his appointed time,

3 and~you(ms)~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמַרְתָּ / wê'a'mar'ta) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) THIS (זֶה / zeh) the~FIRE.OFFERING (הָאִשֶּׁה / ha'i'sheh) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) you(mp)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תַּקְרִיבוּ / taq'ri'vu) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) SHEEP~s (כְּבָשִׂים / kê'va'sim) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) WHOLE~s (תְמִימִם / tê'mi'mim) TWO (שְׁנַיִם / shê'na'yim) to~the~DAY (לַיּוֹם / lai'yom) ASCENSION.OFFERING (עֹלָה / o'lah) CONTINUALLY (תָמִיד / ta'mid)

and you will say to them, this is the fire offering which you will bring near to YHWH, two sheep, sons of a year, whole ones, daily, a ascension offering continually.

4 AT (אֶת / et) the~SHEEP (הַכֶּבֶשׂ / ha'ke'ves) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) in~the~MORNING (בַבֹּקֶר / va'bo'qer) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) the~SHEEP (הַכֶּבֶשׂ / ha'ke'ves) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִי / ha'shey'ni) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) BETWEEN (בֵּין / beyn) the~EVENING~s2 (הָעַרְבָּיִם / ha'ar'ba'yim) [893]

One of the sheep you will do in the morning and the second sheep you will do between the evenings,

5 and~TENTH (וַעֲשִׂירִית / wa'a'si'rit) the~EYPHAH (הָאֵיפָה / ha'ey'phah) FLOUR (סֹלֶת / so'let) to~DEPOSIT (לְמִנְחָה / lê'min'hhah) MIX(Verb)~ed(fs) (בְּלוּלָה / bê'lu'lah) in~OIL (בְּשֶׁמֶן / bê'she'men) SMASHED (כָּתִית / ka'tit) FOURTH (רְבִיעִת / rê'vi'it) the~HIYN (הַהִין / ha'hin)

and a tenth of the eyphah of flour for a deposit, mixed in smashed oil of a fourth of the hiyn.

6 ASCENSION.OFFERING (עֹלַת / o'lat) CONTINUALLY (תָּמִיד / ta'mid) the~DO(Verb)~ed(fs) (הָעֲשֻׂיָה / ha'a'su'yah) in~HILL (בְּהַר / bê'har) Sinai (סִינַי / si'nai) to~AROMA (לְרֵיחַ / lê'rey'ahh) SWEET (נִיחֹחַ / ni'hho'ahh) FIRE.OFFERING (אִשֶּׁה / i'sheh) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

A continual ascension offering, the one done in the hill of Sinai, for a sweet aroma, a fire offering to YHWH,

7 and~POURING~him (וְנִסְכּוֹ / wê'nis'ko) FOURTH (רְבִיעִת / rê'vi'it) the~HIYN (הַהִין / ha'hin) to~the~SHEEP (לַכֶּבֶשׂ / la'ke'ves) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad) in~the~SPECIAL (בַּקֹּדֶשׁ / ba'qo'desh) !(ms)~make~POUR(Verb) (הַסֵּךְ / ha'seykh) POURING (נֶסֶךְ / ne'sekh) LIQUOR (שֵׁכָר / shey'khar) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

and his pouring of the fourth of the hiyn for the one sheep in the special place, make the pouring of liquor pour to YHWH,

8 and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) the~SHEEP (הַכֶּבֶשׂ / ha'ke'ves) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִי / ha'shey'ni) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) BETWEEN (בֵּין / beyn) the~EVENING~s2 (הָעַרְבָּיִם / ha'ar'ba'yim) like~DEPOSIT (כְּמִנְחַת / kê'min'hhat) the~MORNING (הַבֹּקֶר / ha'bo'qer) and~like~POURING~him (וּכְנִסְכּוֹ / ukh'nis'ko) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) FIRE.OFFERING (אִשֵּׁה / i'sheyh) AROMA (רֵיחַ / rey'ahh) SWEET (נִיחֹחַ / ni'hho'ahh) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

and the second sheep you will do between the evenings, like the morning deposit, and like his pouring you will do the fire offering, a sweet aroma to YHWH,

9 and~in~DAY (וּבְיוֹם / uv'yom) the~CEASING (הַשַּׁבָּת / ha'sha'bat) TWO (שְׁנֵי / shê'ney) SHEEP~s (כְבָשִׂים / khê'va'sim) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) WHOLE~s (תְּמִימִם / tê'mi'mim) and~TWO (וּשְׁנֵי / ush'ney) ONE.TENTH~s (עֶשְׂרֹנִים / es'ro'nim) FLOUR (סֹלֶת / so'let) DEPOSIT (מִנְחָה / min'hhah) MIX(Verb)~ed(fs) (בְּלוּלָה / bê'lu'lah) in~the~OIL (בַשֶּׁמֶן / va'she'men) and~POURING~him (וְנִסְכּוֹ / wê'nis'ko)

and the day of ceasing, two sheep, sons of a year, whole ones, and two-tenths of flour, a deposit of mixed in the oil and his pouring.

10 ASCENSION.OFFERING (עֹלַת / o'lat) CEASING (שַׁבַּת / sha'bat) in~CEASING~him (בְּשַׁבַּתּוֹ / bê'sha'ba'to) UPON (עַל / al) ASCENSION.OFFERING (עֹלַת / o'lat) the~CONTINUALLY (הַתָּמִיד / ha'ta'mid) and~POURING~her (וְנִסְכָּהּ / wê'nis'kah)

A ascension offering of ceasing in his ceasing, in addition to the continual ascension offering and her pouring,

11 and~in~HEAD~s (וּבְרָאשֵׁי / uv'ra'shey) NEW.MOON~s~you(mp) (חָדְשֵׁיכֶם / hhad'shey'khem) you(mp)~will~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תַּקְרִיבוּ / taq'ri'vu) ASCENSION.OFFERING (עֹלָה / o'lah) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) BULL~s (פָּרִים / pa'rim) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) CATTLE (בָקָר / va'qar) TWO (שְׁנַיִם / shê'na'yim) and~BUCK (וְאַיִל / wê'a'yil) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) SHEEP~s (כְּבָשִׂים / kê'va'sim) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) SEVEN (שִׁבְעָה / shiv'ah) WHOLE~s (תְּמִימִם / tê'mi'mim)

and in the heads of your new moons you will bring near a ascension offering to YHWH, two bulls, sons of the cattle, and one buck, seven sheep, sons of a year, whole ones,

12 and~THREE (וּשְׁלֹשָׁה / ush'lo'shah) ONE.TENTH~s (עֶשְׂרֹנִים / es'ro'nim) FLOUR (סֹלֶת / so'let) DEPOSIT (מִנְחָה / min'hhah) MIX(Verb)~ed(fs) (בְּלוּלָה / bê'lu'lah) in~the~OIL (בַשֶּׁמֶן / va'she'men) to~the~BULL (לַפָּר / la'par) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad) and~TWO (וּשְׁנֵי / ush'ney) ONE.TENTH~s (עֶשְׂרֹנִים / es'ro'nim) FLOUR (סֹלֶת / so'let) DEPOSIT (מִנְחָה / min'hhah) MIX(Verb)~ed(fs) (בְּלוּלָה / bê'lu'lah) in~the~OIL (בַשֶּׁמֶן / va'she'men) to~the~BUCK (לָאַיִל / la'a'yil) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad)

and three-tenths of flour of the deposit mixed in the oil for the one bull, and two-tenths of flour of the deposit mixed in the oil for the one buck,

13 and~ONE.TENTH (וְעִשָּׂרֹן / wê'i'sa'ron) ONE.TENTH (עִשָּׂרוֹן / i'sa'ron) FLOUR (סֹלֶת / so'let) DEPOSIT (מִנְחָה / min'hhah) MIX(Verb)~ed(fs) (בְּלוּלָה / bê'lu'lah) in~the~OIL (בַשֶּׁמֶן / va'she'men) to~the~SHEEP (לַכֶּבֶשׂ / la'ke'ves) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad) ASCENSION.OFFERING (עֹלָה / o'lah) AROMA (רֵיחַ / rey'ahh) SWEET (נִיחֹחַ / ni'hho'ahh) FIRE.OFFERING (אִשֶּׁה / i'sheh) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

and one-tenth of flour of a deposit mixed in the oil for the one sheep, a ascension offering, a sweet aroma, a fire offering to YHWH,

14 and~POURING~s~them(m) (וְנִסְכֵּיהֶם / wê'nis'key'hem) HALF (חֲצִי / hha'tsi) the~HIYN (הַהִין / ha'hin) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) to~the~BULL (לַפָּר / la'par) and~THIRD (וּשְׁלִישִׁת / ush'li'shit) the~HIYN (הַהִין / ha'hin) to~the~BUCK (לָאַיִל / la'a'yil) and~FOURTH (וּרְבִיעִת / ur'vi'it) the~HIYN (הַהִין / ha'hin) to~the~SHEEP (לַכֶּבֶשׂ / la'ke'ves) WINE (יָיִן / ya'yin) THIS (זֹאת / zot) ASCENSION.OFFERING (עֹלַת / o'lat) NEW.MOON (חֹדֶשׁ / hho'desh) in~NEW.MOON~him (בְּחָדְשׁוֹ / bê'hhad'sho) to~NEW.MOON~s (לְחָדְשֵׁי / lê'hhad'shey) the~YEAR (הַשָּׁנָה / ha'sha'nah)

and their pourings is half the hiyn of wine, he will exist for the bull, and a third of the hiyn for the buck, and a fourth of the hiyn for the sheep, this is the ascension offering of the new moon in his new moon, for the new moons of the year,

15 and~HAIRY.GOAT (וּשְׂעִיר / us'ir) GOAT~s (עִזִּים / i'zim) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) to~FAILURE (לְחַטָּאת / lê'hha'tat) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) UPON (עַל / al) ASCENSION.OFFERING (עֹלַת / o'lat) the~CONTINUALLY (הַתָּמִיד / ha'ta'mid) he~will~be~DO(Verb) (יֵעָשֶׂה / yey'a'seh) and~POURING~him (וְנִסְכּוֹ / wê'nis'ko)

and one hairy goat for failure to YHWH, besides the continual ascension offering that will be done and his pouring,

16 and~in~the~NEW.MOON (וּבַחֹדֶשׁ / u'va'hho'desh) the~FIRST (הָרִאשׁוֹן / ha'ri'shon) in~FOUR (בְּאַרְבָּעָה / bê'ar'ba'ah) TEN (עָשָׂר / a'sar) DAY (יוֹם / yom) to~the~NEW.MOON (לַחֹדֶשׁ / la'hho'desh) Pesahh (פֶּסַח / pe'sahh) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

and in the first new moon on the fourteenth day of the new moon is the Pesahh for YHWH,

17 and~in~the~FIVE (וּבַחֲמִשָּׁה / u'va'hha'mi'shah) TEN (עָשָׂר / a'sar) DAY (יוֹם / yom) to~the~NEW.MOON (לַחֹדֶשׁ / la'hho'desh) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh) FEAST (חָג / hhag) SEVEN (שִׁבְעַת / shiv'at) DAY~s (יָמִים / ya'mim) UNLEAVENED.BREAD~s (מַצּוֹת / ma'tsot) he~will~be~EAT(Verb) (יֵאָכֵל / yey'a'kheyl)

and on the fifteenth day of this new moon is a feast of seven days, unleavened breads will be eaten.

18 in~the~DAY (בַּיּוֹם / ba'yom) the~FIRST (הָרִאשׁוֹן / ha'ri'shon) MEETING (מִקְרָא / miq'ra) SPECIAL (קֹדֶשׁ / qo'desh) ALL (כָּל / kol) BUSINESS (מְלֶאכֶת / mê'le'khet) SERVICE (עֲבֹדָה / a'vo'dah) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~DO(Verb) (תַעֲשׂוּ / ta'a'su)

On the first day is a special meeting, you will not do any business of service,

19 and~you(mp)~did~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וְהִקְרַבְתֶּם / wê'hiq'rav'tem) FIRE.OFFERING (אִשֶּׁה / i'sheh) ASCENSION.OFFERING (עֹלָה / o'lah) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) BULL~s (פָּרִים / pa'rim) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) CATTLE (בָקָר / va'qar) TWO (שְׁנַיִם / shê'na'yim) and~BUCK (וְאַיִל / wê'a'yil) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) and~SEVEN (וְשִׁבְעָה / wê'shiv'ah) SHEEP~s (כְבָשִׂים / khê'va'sim) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) WHOLE~s (תְּמִימִם / tê'mi'mim) they(m)~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיוּ / yih'yu) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem)

and you will bring near a fire offering to YHWH, two bulls of the sons of cattle, and one buck, and seven sheep, sons of a year, whole ones, they will exist for you,

20 and~DEPOSIT~them(m) (וּמִנְחָתָם / u'min'hha'tam) FLOUR (סֹלֶת / so'let) MIX(Verb)~ed(fs) (בְּלוּלָה / bê'lu'lah) in~the~OIL (בַשָּׁמֶן / va'sha'men) THREE (שְׁלֹשָׁה / shê'lo'shah) ONE.TENTH~s (עֶשְׂרֹנִים / es'ro'nim) to~the~BULL (לַפָּר / la'par) and~TWO (וּשְׁנֵי / ush'ney) ONE.TENTH~s (עֶשְׂרֹנִים / es'ro'nim) to~the~BUCK (לָאַיִל / la'a'yil) you(mp)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשׂוּ / ta'a'su)

and their deposit of flour mixed in the oil, three-tenths for the bull and two-tenths for the buck you will do.

21 ONE.TENTH (עִשָּׂרוֹן / i'sa'ron) ONE.TENTH (עִשָּׂרוֹן / i'sa'ron) you(ms)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשֶׂה / ta'a'seh) to~the~SHEEP (לַכֶּבֶשׂ / la'ke'ves) the~UNIT (הָאֶחָד / ha'e'hhad) to~SEVEN (לְשִׁבְעַת / lê'shiv'at) the~SHEEP~s (הַכְּבָשִׂים / hak'va'sim)

One-tenth you will do for each sheep of the seven sheep,

22 and~HAIRY.GOAT (וּשְׂעִיר / us'ir) FAILURE (חַטָּאת / hha'tat) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) to~>~much~COVER(Verb) (לְכַפֵּר / lê'kha'peyr) UPON~you(mp) (עֲלֵיכֶם / a'ley'khem)

and one hairy goat, a failure to cover over you.

23 from~to~STRAND (מִלְּבַד / mi'le'vad) [894] ASCENSION.OFFERING (עֹלַת / o'lat) the~MORNING (הַבֹּקֶר / ha'bo'qer) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) to~ASCENSION.OFFERING (לְעֹלַת / lê'o'lat) the~CONTINUALLY (הַתָּמִיד / ha'ta'mid) you(mp)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשׂוּ / ta'a'su) AT (אֶת / et) THESE (אֵלֶּה / ey'leh)

Apart from the ascension offering of the morning, which is for the continual ascension offering, you will do these.

24 like~THESE (כָּאֵלֶּה / ka'ey'leh) you(mp)~will~DO(Verb) (תַּעֲשׂוּ / ta'a'su) to~the~DAY (לַיּוֹם / lai'yom) SEVEN (שִׁבְעַת / shiv'at) DAY~s (יָמִים / ya'mim) BREAD (לֶחֶם / le'hhem) FIRE.OFFERING (אִשֵּׁה / i'sheyh) AROMA (רֵיחַ / rey'ahh) SWEET (נִיחֹחַ / ni'hho'ahh) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) UPON (עַל / al) ASCENSION.OFFERING (עוֹלַת / o'lat) the~CONTINUALLY (הַתָּמִיד / ha'ta'mid) he~will~be~DO(Verb) (יֵעָשֶׂה / yey'a'seh) and~POURING~him (וְנִסְכּוֹ / wê'nis'ko)

Like these you will do daily, seven days, the bread of the fire offering is a sweet aroma to YHWH, besides the continual ascension offering that will be done, and his pouring,
