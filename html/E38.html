Exodus Chapter 38

1 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) AT (אֶת / et) ALTAR (מִזְבַּח / miz'bahh) the~ASCENSION.OFFERING (הָעֹלָה / ha'o'lah) TREE~s (עֲצֵי / a'tsey) ACACIA~s (שִׁטִּים / shi'tim) FIVE (חָמֵשׁ / hha'meysh) FOREARM~s (אַמּוֹת / a'mot) LENGTH~him (אָרְכּוֹ / ar'ko) and~FIVE (וְחָמֵשׁ / wê'hha'meysh) FOREARM~s (אַמּוֹת / a'mot) WIDTH~him (רָחְבּוֹ / rahh'bo) BE.SQUARE(Verb)~ed(ms) (רָבוּעַ / ra'vu'a) and~THREE (וְשָׁלֹשׁ / wê'sha'losh) FOREARM~s (אַמּוֹת / a'mot) HEIGHT~him (קֹמָתוֹ / qo'ma'to)

and he made the altar of the ascension offering of acacia wood, five forearms is his length, and five forearms is his width, it is square, and three forearms is his height,

2 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) HORN~s~him (קַרְנֹתָיו / qar'no'taw) UPON (עַל / al) FOUR (אַרְבַּע / ar'ba) CORNER~s~him (פִּנֹּתָיו / pi'no'taw) FROM~him (מִמֶּנּוּ / mi'me'nu) they~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיוּ / hai'u) HORN~s~him (קַרְנֹתָיו / qar'no'taw) and~he~will~much~OVERLAY(Verb) (וַיְצַף / wai'tsaph) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) COPPER (נְחֹשֶׁת / nê'hho'shet)

and he made his horns upon his four corners, out of him his horns will exist, and he overlaid him with copper,

3 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) UTENSIL~s (כְּלֵי / kê'ley) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) AT (אֶת / et) the~POT~s (הַסִּירֹת / ha'si'rot) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~SHOVEL~s (הַיָּעִים / hai'ya'im) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~SPRINKLING.BASIN~s (הַמִּזְרָקֹת / ha'miz'ra'qot) AT (אֶת / et) the~FORK~s (הַמִּזְלָגֹת / ha'miz'la'got) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~FIRE.PAN~s (הַמַּחְתֹּת / ha'mahh'tot) ALL (כָּל / kol) UTENSIL~s~him (כֵּלָיו / key'law) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) COPPER (נְחֹשֶׁת / nê'hho'shet)

and he made all the utensils of the altar, the pots, and the shovels, and the sprinkling basins, the forks, and the fire pans, he made all his utensils with copper,

4 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) to~the~ALTAR (לַמִּזְבֵּחַ / la'miz'bey'ahh) GRATE (מִכְבָּר / mikh'bar) WORK (מַעֲשֵׂה / ma'a'seyh) NETTING (רֶשֶׁת / re'shet) COPPER (נְחֹשֶׁת / nê'hho'shet) UNDER (תַּחַת / ta'hhat) OUTER.RIM~him (כַּרְכֻּבּוֹ / kar'ku'bo) from~to~BENEATH (מִלְּמַטָּה / mil'ma'tah) UNTIL (עַד / ad) HALF~him (חֶצְיוֹ / hhets'yo)

and he made for the altar a grate work of netting with copper under his outer rim, from beneath unto his half,

5 and~he~will~POUR.DOWN(Verb) (וַיִּצֹק / wai'yi'tsoq) [621] FOUR (אַרְבַּע / ar'ba) RING~s (טַבָּעֹת / ta'ba'ot) in~FOUR (בְּאַרְבַּע / bê'ar'ba) the~EXTREMITY~s (הַקְּצָו‍ֹת / haq'tsa'ot) to~GRATE (לְמִכְבַּר / lê'mikh'bar) the~COPPER (הַנְּחֹשֶׁת / han'hho'shet) HOUSE~s (בָּתִּים / ba'tim) [622] to~the~STRAND~s (לַבַּדִּים / la'ba'dim)

and he poured down four rings in four extremities for the copper grate, houses for the strands,

6 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) AT (אֶת / et) the~STRAND~s (הַבַּדִּים / ha'ba'dim) TREE~s (עֲצֵי / a'tsey) ACACIA~s (שִׁטִּים / shi'tim) and~he~will~much~OVERLAY(Verb) (וַיְצַף / wai'tsaph) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) COPPER (נְחֹשֶׁת / nê'hho'shet)

and he made the strands of acacia wood, and he overlaid them with copper,

7 and~he~will~make~COME(Verb) (וַיָּבֵא / wai'ya'vey) AT (אֶת / et) the~STRAND~s (הַבַּדִּים / ha'ba'dim) in~the~RING~s (בַּטַּבָּעֹת / ba'ta'ba'ot) UPON (עַל / al) RIB~s (צַלְעֹת / tsal'ot) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) to~>~LIFT.UP(Verb) (לָשֵׂאת / la'seyt) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) in~~them(m) (בָּהֶם / ba'hem) BORE.OUT(Verb)~ed(ms) (נְבוּב / nê'vuv) SLAB~s (לֻחֹת / lu'hhot) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to)

and he brought the strands in the rings upon the ribs of the altar to lift him up with them, he made him with hollowed out slabs,

8 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) AT (אֵת / eyt) the~CAULDRON (הַכִּיּוֹר / ha'ki'yor) COPPER (נְחֹשֶׁת / nê'hho'shet) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) BASE~him (כַּנּוֹ / ka'no) COPPER (נְחֹשֶׁת / nê'hho'shet) in~REFLECTION~s (בְּמַרְאֹת / bê'mar'ot) the~MUSTER(Verb)~ing(fp) (הַצֹּבְאֹת / ha'tsov'ot) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they~did~MUSTER(Verb) (צָבְאוּ / tsav'u) OPENING (פֶּתַח / pe'tahh) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd)

and he made the cauldron with copper, and his base with copper, with reflections of the musterers who muster at the opening of the appointed tent,

9 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) AT (אֶת / et) the~COURTYARD (הֶחָצֵר / he'hha'tseyr) to~EDGE (לִפְאַת / liph'at) SOUTH (נֶגֶב / ne'gev) SOUTHWARD~unto (תֵּימָנָה / tey'ma'nah) SLING~s (קַלְעֵי / qal'ey) the~COURTYARD (הֶחָצֵר / he'hha'tseyr) LINEN (שֵׁשׁ / sheysh) be~make~TWIST.TOGETHER(Verb)~ing(ms) (מָשְׁזָר / mash'zar) HUNDRED (מֵאָה / mey'ah) in~the~FOREARM (בָּאַמָּה / ba'a'mah)

and he made the courtyard to the edge, unto the south of the south, slings of the courtyard are of twisted linen, a hundred by the forearm.

10 PILLAR~s~them(m) (עַמּוּדֵיהֶם / a'mu'dey'hem) TEN~s (עֶשְׂרִים / es'rim) and~FOOTING~s~them(m) (וְאַדְנֵיהֶם / wê'ad'ney'hem) TEN~s (עֶשְׂרִים / es'rim) COPPER (נְחֹשֶׁת / nê'hho'shet) and~PEG~s (וָוֵי / wa'wey) the~PILLAR~s (הָעַמֻּדִים / ha'a'mu'dim) and~BINDER~s~them(m) (וַחֲשֻׁקֵיהֶם / wa'hha'shu'qey'hem) SILVER (כָּסֶף / ka'seph)

Their twenty pillars and their twenty footings of copper, the pegs of the pillars and their binders of silver,

11 and~to~EDGE (וְלִפְאַת / wê'liph'at) NORTH (צָפוֹן / tsa'phon) HUNDRED (מֵאָה / mey'ah) in~the~FOREARM (בָאַמָּה / va'a'mah) PILLAR~s~them(m) (עַמּוּדֵיהֶם / a'mu'dey'hem) TEN~s (עֶשְׂרִים / es'rim) and~FOOTING~s~them(m) (וְאַדְנֵיהֶם / wê'ad'ney'hem) TEN~s (עֶשְׂרִים / es'rim) COPPER (נְחֹשֶׁת / nê'hho'shet) and~PEG~s (וָוֵי / wa'wey) the~PILLAR~s (הָעַמּוּדִים / ha'amu'dim) and~BINDER~s~them(m) (וַחֲשֻׁקֵיהֶם / wa'hha'shu'qey'hem) SILVER (כָּסֶף / ka'seph)

and to the north edge, a hundred by the forearm, their twenty pillars and their twenty footings of copper, the pegs of the pillars and their binders of silver,

12 and~to~EDGE (וְלִפְאַת / wê'liph'at) SEA (יָם / yam) [623] SLING~s (קְלָעִים / qê'la'im) FIVE~s (חֲמִשִּׁים / hha'mi'shim) in~the~FOREARM (בָּאַמָּה / ba'a'mah) PILLAR~s~them(m) (עַמּוּדֵיהֶם / a'mu'dey'hem) TEN (עֲשָׂרָה / a'sa'rah) and~FOOTING~s~them(m) (וְאַדְנֵיהֶם / wê'ad'ney'hem) TEN (עֲשָׂרָה / a'sa'rah) and~PEG~s (וָוֵי / wa'wey) the~PILLAR~s (הָעַמֻּדִים / ha'a'mu'dim) and~BINDER~s~them(m) (וַחֲשׁוּקֵיהֶם / wa'hha'shu'qey'hem) SILVER (כָּסֶף / ka'seph)

and to the edge of the sea, fifty slings by the forearm, their ten pillars and their ten footings, the pegs of the pillars and their silver binders,

13 and~to~EDGE (וְלִפְאַת / wê'liph'at) EAST~unto (קֵדְמָה / qeyd'mah) SUNRISE~unto (מִזְרָחָה / miz'ra'hhah) FIVE~s (חֲמִשִּׁים / hha'mi'shim) FOREARM (אַמָּה / a'mah)

and to the edge unto the east, unto the sunrise, it is fifty forearms.

14 SLING~s (קְלָעִים / qê'la'im) FIVE (חֲמֵשׁ / hha'meysh) TEN (עֶשְׂרֵה / es'reyh) FOREARM (אַמָּה / a'mah) TO (אֶל / el) the~SHOULDER.PIECE (הַכָּתֵף / ha'ka'teyph) PILLAR~s~them(m) (עַמּוּדֵיהֶם / a'mu'dey'hem) THREE (שְׁלֹשָׁה / shê'lo'shah) and~FOOTING~s~them(m) (וְאַדְנֵיהֶם / wê'ad'ney'hem) THREE (שְׁלֹשָׁה / shê'lo'shah)

The slings are fifteen forearms for the shoulder piece, their three pillars and their three footings,

15 and~to~the~SHOULDER.PIECE (וְלַכָּתֵף / wê'la'ka'teyph) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִית / ha'shey'nit) from~THIS (מִזֶּה / mi'zeh) and~from~THIS (וּמִזֶּה / u'mi'zeh) to~GATE (לְשַׁעַר / lê'sha'ar) the~COURTYARD (הֶחָצֵר / he'hha'tseyr) SLING~s (קְלָעִים / qê'la'im) FIVE (חֲמֵשׁ / hha'meysh) TEN (עֶשְׂרֵה / es'reyh) FOREARM (אַמָּה / a'mah) PILLAR~s~them(m) (עַמֻּדֵיהֶם / a'mu'dey'hem) THREE (שְׁלֹשָׁה / shê'lo'shah) and~FOOTING~s~them(m) (וְאַדְנֵיהֶם / wê'ad'ney'hem) THREE (שְׁלֹשָׁה / shê'lo'shah)

and for the second shoulder piece, from this one and from that one, for the gate of the courtyard are slings, fifteen forearms, their three pillars and their three footings.

16 ALL (כָּל / kol) SLING~s (קַלְעֵי / qal'ey) the~COURTYARD (הֶחָצֵר / he'hha'tseyr) ALL.AROUND (סָבִיב / sa'viv) LINEN (שֵׁשׁ / sheysh) be~make~TWIST.TOGETHER(Verb)~ing(ms) (מָשְׁזָר / mash'zar)

All the slings of the courtyard all around are of twisted linen,

17 and~the~FOOTING~s (וְהָאֲדָנִים / wê'ha'a'da'nim) to~the~PILLAR~s (לָעַמֻּדִים / la'a''mu'dim) COPPER (נְחֹשֶׁת / nê'hho'shet) and~PEG~s (וָוֵי / wa'wey) the~PILLAR~s (הָעַמּוּדִים / ha'amu'dim) and~BINDER~s~them(m) (וַחֲשׁוּקֵיהֶם / wa'hha'shu'qey'hem) SILVER (כֶּסֶף / ke'seph) and~METAL.PLATING (וְצִפּוּי / wê'tsi'pui) HEAD~s~them(m) (רָאשֵׁיהֶם / ra'shey'hem) SILVER (כָּסֶף / ka'seph) and~THEY(m) (וְהֵם / wê'heym) be~much~ATTACH(Verb)~ing(mp) (מְחֻשָּׁקִים / mê'hhu'sha'qim) SILVER (כֶּסֶף / ke'seph) ALL (כֹּל / kol) PILLAR~s (עַמֻּדֵי / a'mu'dey) the~COURTYARD (הֶחָצֵר / he'hha'tseyr)

and the footings for the pillars of copper, the pegs of the pillars and their binders of silver, and the metal plating of their heads of silver, and all the pillars of the courtyard are being attached with silver,

18 and~CANOPY (וּמָסַךְ / u'ma'sakh) GATE (שַׁעַר / sha'ar) the~COURTYARD (הֶחָצֵר / he'hha'tseyr) WORK (מַעֲשֵׂה / ma'a'seyh) EMBROIDER(Verb)~ing(ms) (רֹקֵם / ro'qeym) [624] BLUE (תְּכֵלֶת / tê'khey'let) and~PURPLE (וְאַרְגָּמָן / wê'ar'ga'man) and~KERMES (וְתוֹלַעַת / wê'to'la'at) SCARLET (שָׁנִי / sha'ni) and~LINEN (וְשֵׁשׁ / wê'sheysh) be~make~TWIST.TOGETHER(Verb)~ing(ms) (מָשְׁזָר / mash'zar) and~TEN~s (וְעֶשְׂרִים / wê'es'rim) FOREARM (אַמָּה / a'mah) LENGTH (אֹרֶךְ / o'rekh) and~HEIGHT (וְקוֹמָה / wê'qo'mah) in~WIDTH (בְרֹחַב / vê'ro'hhav) FIVE (חָמֵשׁ / hha'meysh) FOREARM~s (אַמּוֹת / a'mot) to~ALONGSIDE (לְעֻמַּת / lê'u'mat) SLING~s (קַלְעֵי / qal'ey) the~COURTYARD (הֶחָצֵר / he'hha'tseyr)

and the canopy of the gate of the courtyard is a work of embroidering with blue, and purple, and kermes of scarlet, and twisted linen, and twenty forearms is the length, and the height with the width is five forearms alongside the slings of the courtyard,

19 and~PILLAR~s~them(m) (וְעַמֻּדֵיהֶם / wê'a'mu'dey'hem) FOUR (אַרְבָּעָה / ar'ba'ah) and~FOOTING~s~them(m) (וְאַדְנֵיהֶם / wê'ad'ney'hem) FOUR (אַרְבָּעָה / ar'ba'ah) COPPER (נְחֹשֶׁת / nê'hho'shet) and~PEG~s~them(m) (וָוֵיהֶם / wa'wey'hem) SILVER (כֶּסֶף / ke'seph) and~METAL.PLATING (וְצִפּוּי / wê'tsi'pui) HEAD~s~them(m) (רָאשֵׁיהֶם / ra'shey'hem) and~BINDER~s~them(m) (וַחֲשֻׁקֵיהֶם / wa'hha'shu'qey'hem) SILVER (כָּסֶף / ka'seph)

and their four pillars and their four footings of copper, their pegs of silver, and the metal plating of their heads and their binders of silver,

20 and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) the~TENT.PEG~s (הַיְתֵדֹת / hai'tey'dot) to~the~DWELLING (לַמִּשְׁכָּן / la'mish'kan) and~to~COURTYARD (וְלֶחָצֵר / wê'le'hha'tser) ALL.AROUND (סָבִיב / sa'viv) COPPER (נְחֹשֶׁת / nê'hho'shet)

and all the tent pegs for the dwelling and for the courtyard all around of copper.

21 THESE (אֵלֶּה / ey'leh) REGISTER(Verb)~ed(mp) (פְקוּדֵי / phê'qudey) the~DWELLING (הַמִּשְׁכָּן / ha'mish'kan) DWELLING (מִשְׁכַּן / mish'kan) the~EVIDENCE (הָעֵדֻת / ha'ey'dut) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~be~much~REGISTER(Verb) (פֻּקַּד / pu'qad) UPON (עַל / al) MOUTH (פִּי / pi) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) SERVICE (עֲבֹדַת / a'vo'dat) the~Lewi~s (הַלְוִיִּם / hal'wi'yim) in~HAND (בְּיַד / bê'yad) Iytamar (אִיתָמָר / i'ta'mar) SON (בֶּן / ben) Aharon (אַהֲרֹן / a'ha'ron) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn)

These are registered of the dwelling, the dwelling of the evidence which he registered by the mouth of Mosheh, the service of the ones of Lewi, by the hand of Iytamar, the son of Aharon, the administrator,

22 and~Betsaleyl (וּבְצַלְאֵל / uv'tsal'eyl) SON (בֶּן / ben) Uriy (אוּרִי / u'ri) SON (בֶן / ven) Hhur (חוּר / hhur) to~BRANCH (לְמַטֵּה / lê'ma'teyh) Yehudah (יְהוּדָה / yê'hu'dah) he~did~DO(Verb) (עָשָׂה / a'sah) AT (אֵת / eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~much~DIRECT(Verb) (צִוָּה / tsi'wah) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) AT (אֶת / et) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh)

and Betsaleyl, the son of Uriy, the son of Hhur, belonging to the branch of Yehudah, had made all which YHWH directed Mosheh,
