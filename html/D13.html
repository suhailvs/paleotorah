Deuteronomy Chapter 13

1 (32) AT (אֵת / eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~WORD (הַדָּבָר / ha'da'var) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) much~DIRECT(Verb)~ing(ms) (מְצַוֶּה / mê'tsa'weh) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) you(mp)~will~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (תִשְׁמְרוּ / tish'me'ru) to~>~DO(Verb) (לַעֲשׂוֹת / la'a'sot) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~make~ADD(Verb) (תֹסֵף / to'seyph) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~TAKE.AWAY(Verb) (תִגְרַע / tig'ra) FROM~him (מִמֶּנּוּ / mi'me'nu)

All the words which I am directing you, him you will safeguard to do, you will not add upon him and you will not take away from him.

2 (1) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~RISE(Verb) (יָקוּם / ya'qum) in~INSIDE~you(ms) (בְּקִרְבְּךָ / bê'qir'be'kha) ANNOUNCER (נָבִיא / na'vi) OR (אוֹ / o) DREAM(Verb)~ing(ms) (חֹלֵם / hho'leym) DREAM (חֲלוֹם / hha'lom) and~he~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַן / wê'na'tan) TO~you(ms) (אֵלֶיךָ / ey'ley'kha) SIGN (אוֹת / ot) OR (אוֹ / o) WONDER (מוֹפֵת / mo'pheyt)

When an announcer or a dreamer of dreams will rise from in among you, and he will give to you a sign or a wonder,

3 (2) and~he~did~COME(Verb) (וּבָא / u'va) the~SIGN (הָאוֹת / ha'ot) and~the~WONDER (וְהַמּוֹפֵת / wê'ha'mo'pheyt) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דִּבֶּר / di'ber) TO~you(ms) (אֵלֶיךָ / ey'ley'kha) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) we~will~WALK(Verb)~& (נֵלְכָה / neyl'khah) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) OTHER~s (אֲחֵרִים / a'hhey'rim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~did~KNOW(Verb)~them(m) (יְדַעְתָּם / yê'da'tam) and~we~will~be~make~SERVE(Verb)~them(m) (וְנָעָבְדֵם / wê'na'av'deym)

and the sign or the wonder will come, which he spoke to you, saying, we will walk after other Elohiym which you did not know and we will serve them.

4 (3) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~HEAR(Verb) (תִשְׁמַע / tish'ma) TO (אֶל / el) WORD~s (דִּבְרֵי / div'rey) the~ANNOUNCER (הַנָּבִיא / ha'na'vi) the~HE (הַהוּא / ha'hu) OR (אוֹ / o) TO (אֶל / el) DREAM(Verb)~ing(ms) (חוֹלֵם / hho'leym) the~DREAM (הַחֲלוֹם / ha'hha'lom) the~HE (הַהוּא / ha'hu) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) much~TEST(Verb)~ing(ms) (מְנַסֶּה / mê'na'seh) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem) to~>~KNOW(Verb) (לָדַעַת / la'da'at) ?~THERE.IS~you(mp) (הֲיִשְׁכֶם / ha'yish'khem) LOVE(Verb)~ing(mp) (אֹהֲבִים / o'ha'vim) AT (אֶת / et) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem) in~ALL (בְּכָל / bê'khol) HEART~you(mp) (לְבַבְכֶם / lê'va've'khem) and~in~ALL (וּבְכָל / uv'khol) SOUL~you(ms) (נַפְשְׁכֶם / naph'she'khem)

You will not listen to the words of that announcer or to that dreamer of dreams, given that YHWH your Elohiym is testing you to know if you are loving YHWH your Elohiym with all your heart and with all your soul.

5 (4) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem) you(mp)~will~WALK(Verb) (תֵּלֵכוּ / tey'ley'khu) and~AT~him (וְאֹתוֹ / wê'o'to) you(mp)~will~FEAR(Verb) (תִירָאוּ / ti'ra'u) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) DIRECTIVE~s~him (מִצְוֹתָיו / mits'o'taw) you(mp)~will~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (תִּשְׁמֹרוּ / tish'moru) and~in~VOICE~him (וּבְקֹלוֹ / uv'qo'lo) you(mp)~will~HEAR(Verb) (תִשְׁמָעוּ / tish'ma'u) and~AT~him (וְאֹתוֹ / wê'o'to) you(mp)~will~SERVE(Verb) (תַעֲבֹדוּ / ta'a'vo'du) and~in~~him (וּבוֹ / u'vo) you(mp)~will~ADHERE(Verb)~must (תִדְבָּקוּן / tid'ba'qun)

You will walk after YHWH your Elohiym, and him you will fear, and his directives you will safeguard, and in his voice you will hear, and him you will serve, and in him you must adhere,

6 (5) and~the~ANNOUNCER (וְהַנָּבִיא / wê'ha'na'vi) the~HE (הַהוּא / ha'hu) OR (אוֹ / o) DREAM(Verb)~ing(ms) (חֹלֵם / hho'leym) the~DREAM (הַחֲלוֹם / ha'hha'lom) the~HE (הַהוּא / ha'hu) he~will~be~make~DIE(Verb) (יוּמָת / yu'mat) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דִבֶּר / di'ber) TURNING.ASIDE (סָרָה / sa'rah) UPON (עַל / al) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem) the~make~GO.OUT(Verb)~ing(ms) (הַמּוֹצִיא / ha'mo'tsi) AT~you(mp) (אֶתְכֶם / et'khem) from~LAND (מֵאֶרֶץ / mey'e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) and~the~RANSOM(Verb)~ing(ms)~you(ms) (וְהַפֹּדְךָ / wê'ha'pod'kha) from~HOUSE (מִבֵּית / mi'beyt) SERVANT~s (עֲבָדִים / a'va'dim) to~>~make~DRIVE.OUT(Verb)~you(ms) (לְהַדִּיחֲךָ / lê'ha'di'hha'kha) FROM (מִן / min) the~ROAD (הַדֶּרֶךְ / ha'de'rekh) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~much~DIRECT(Verb)~you(ms) (צִוְּךָ / tsi'we'kha) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) to~>~WALK(Verb) (לָלֶכֶת / la'le'khet) in~~her (בָּהּ / bah) and~you(ms)~did~much~BURN(Verb) (וּבִעַרְתָּ / u'vi'ar'ta) the~DYSFUNCTIONAL (הָרָע / ha'ra) from~INSIDE~you(ms) (מִקִּרְבֶּךָ / mi'qir'be'kha)

and that announcer or that dreamer of dreams, will be killed, given that he spoke of turning aside from YHWH your Elohiym, the one bringing you out from the land of Mits'rayim, and the one ransoming you from the house of servants, to drive you out from the road which YHWH your Elohiym directed you to walk in her, and you will burn the dysfunctional one from inside you.

7 (6) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~will~make~PERSUADE(Verb)~you(ms) (יְסִיתְךָ / yê'si'te'kha) BROTHER~you(ms) (אָחִיךָ / a'hhi'kha) SON (בֶן / ven) MOTHER~you(ms) (אִמֶּךָ / i'me'kha) OR (אוֹ / o) SON~you(ms) (בִנְךָ / vin'kha) OR (אוֹ / o) DAUGHTER~you(ms) (בִתְּךָ / vit'kha) OR (אוֹ / o) WOMAN (אֵשֶׁת / ey'shet) BOSOM~you(ms) (חֵיקֶךָ / hhey'qe'kha) OR (אוֹ / o) COMPANION~you(ms) (רֵעֲךָ / rey'a'kha) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) like~SOUL~you(ms) (כְּנַפְשְׁךָ / kê'naph'she'kha) in~the~HIDING (בַּסֵּתֶר / ba'sey'ter) [986] to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) we~will~WALK(Verb)~& (נֵלְכָה / neyl'khah) and~we~will~SERVE(Verb)~& (וְנַעַבְדָה / wê'na'av'dah) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) OTHER~s (אֲחֵרִים / a'hhey'rim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~did~KNOW(Verb) (יָדַעְתָּ / ya'da'ta) YOU(MS) (אַתָּה / a'tah) and~FATHER~s~you(ms) (וַאֲבֹתֶיךָ / wa'a'vo'tey'kha)

Given that your brother, the son of your mother, or your son, or your daughter, or the woman of your bosom, or your companion which is like your soul, will persuade you in hiding saying, we will walk and we will serve other Elohiym, which you did not know, you and your fathers.

8 (7) from~Elohiym (מֵאֱלֹהֵי / mey'e'lo'hey) the~PEOPLE~s (הָעַמִּים / ha'a'mim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) ALL.AROUND~s~you(mp) (סְבִיבֹתֵיכֶם / sê'vi'vo'tey'khem) the~NEAR~s (הַקְּרֹבִים / haq'ro'vim) TO~you(ms) (אֵלֶיךָ / ey'ley'kha) OR (אוֹ / o) the~DISTANCE~s (הָרְחֹקִים / har'hho'qim) FROM~you(ms) (מִמֶּךָּ / mi'me'ka) from~EXTREMITY (מִקְצֵה / mi'qe'tseyh) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) and~UNTIL (וְעַד / wê'ad) EXTREMITY (קְצֵה / qê'tseyh) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets)

From the Elohiym of the people, which are all around you, the ones near to you, or the ones distant from you, from the extremity of the land and until the extremity of the land.

9 (8) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~CONSENT(Verb) (תֹאבֶה / to'veh) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~HEAR(Verb) (תִשְׁמַע / tish'ma) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) she~will~SPARE(Verb) (תָחוֹס / ta'hhos) EYE~you(ms) (עֵינְךָ / eyn'kha) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~SHOW.PITY(Verb) (תַחְמֹל / tahh'mol) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(ms)~will~much~COVER.OVER(Verb) (תְכַסֶּה / tê'kha'seh) [987] UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law)

You will not consent to him, and you will not listen to him, and your eye will not spare him, and you will not show pity, and you will not cover him over.

10 (9) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) >~KILL(Verb) (הָרֹג / ha'rog) you(ms)~will~KILL(Verb)~him (תַּהַרְגֶנּוּ / ta'har'ge'nu) HAND~you(ms) (יָדְךָ / yad'kha) she~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִּהְיֶה / tih'yeh) in~~him (בּוֹ / bo) in~the~FIRST (בָרִאשׁוֹנָה / va'ri'sho'nah) to~>~make~DIE(Verb)~him (לַהֲמִיתוֹ / la'ha'mi'to) and~HAND (וְיַד / wê'yad) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) in~the~LAST (בָּאַחֲרֹנָה / ba'a'hha'ro'nah)

Given that you will surely kill him, your hand will exist in him first to kill him, and the hand of all the people last,

11 (10) and~you(ms)~did~STONE(Verb)~him (וּסְקַלְתּוֹ / us'qal'to) in~the~STONE~s (בָאֲבָנִים / va'a'va'nim) and~he~did~DIE(Verb) (וָמֵת / wa'meyt) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~much~SEARCH.OUT(Verb) (בִקֵּשׁ / vi'qeysh) to~>~make~DRIVE.OUT(Verb)~you(ms) (לְהַדִּיחֲךָ / lê'ha'di'hha'kha) from~UPON (מֵעַל / mey'al) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) the~make~GO.OUT(Verb)~ing(ms)~you(ms) (הַמּוֹצִיאֲךָ / ha'mo'tsi'a'kha) from~LAND (מֵאֶרֶץ / mey'e'rets) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) from~HOUSE (מִבֵּית / mi'beyt) SERVANT~s (עֲבָדִים / a'va'dim)

and you will stone him with stones, and he will die, given that he searched out to drive you out from upon YHWH your Elohiym, the one bringing you out from the land of Mits'rayim, from the house of servants,

12 (11) and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) they(m)~will~HEAR(Verb) (יִשְׁמְעוּ / yish'me'u) and~they(m)~will~SEE(Verb)~must (וְיִרָאוּן / wê'yi'ra'un) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) they(m)~will~make~ADD(Verb) (יוֹסִפוּ / yo'si'phu) to~>~DO(Verb) (לַעֲשׂוֹת / la'a'sot) like~the~WORD (כַּדָּבָר / ka'da'var) the~DYSFUNCTIONAL (הָרָע / ha'ra) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh) in~INSIDE~you(ms) (בְּקִרְבֶּךָ / bê'qir'be'kha)

and all Yisra'eyl will hear, and they must see, and they will not again do like this dysfunctional word in among you.

13 (12) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(ms)~will~HEAR(Verb) (תִשְׁמַע / tish'ma) in~UNIT (בְּאַחַת / bê'a'ahhat) CITY~s~you(ms) (עָרֶיךָ / a'rey'kha) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(ms) (אֱלֹהֶיךָ / e'lo'hey'kha) GIVE(Verb)~ing(ms) (נֹתֵן / no'teyn) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) to~>~SETTLE(Verb) (לָשֶׁבֶת / la'she'vet) THERE (שָׁם / sham) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor)

Given that you heard in one of your cities which YHWH your Elohiym is giving to you to settle there, saying,

14 (13) they~did~GO.OUT(Verb) (יָצְאוּ / yats'u) MAN~s (אֲנָשִׁים / a'na'shim) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Beli'ya'al (בְלִיַּעַל / vê'li'ya'al) from~INSIDE~you(ms) (מִקִּרְבֶּךָ / mi'qir'be'kha) and~they(m)~will~make~DRIVE.OUT(Verb) (וַיַּדִּיחוּ / wai'ya'di'hhu) AT (אֶת / et) SETTLE(Verb)~ing(mp) (יֹשְׁבֵי / yosh'vey) CITY~them(m) (עִירָם / i'ram) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) we~will~WALK(Verb)~& (נֵלְכָה / neyl'khah) and~we~will~SERVE(Verb)~& (וְנַעַבְדָה / wê'na'av'dah) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) OTHER~s (אֲחֵרִים / a'hhey'rim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~did~KNOW(Verb) (יְדַעְתֶּם / yê'da'tem)

The men, the sons of Beli'ya'al, went out from inside you, and they drove the settlers of their city out saying, we will walk and we will serve other Elohiym which you did not know,

15 (14) and~you(ms)~did~SEEK(Verb) (וְדָרַשְׁתָּ / wê'da'rash'ta) and~you(ms)~did~EXAMINE(Verb) (וְחָקַרְתָּ / wê'hha'qar'ta) and~you(ms)~did~INQUIRE(Verb) (וְשָׁאַלְתָּ / wê'sha'al'ta) >~make~DO.WELL(Verb) (הֵיטֵב / hey'teyv) and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) TRUTH (אֱמֶת / e'met) be~PREPARE(Verb)~ing(ms) (נָכוֹן / na'khon) the~WORD (הַדָּבָר / ha'da'var) she~did~be~DO(Verb) (נֶעֶשְׂתָה / ne'es'tah) the~DISGUSTING (הַתּוֹעֵבָה / ha'to'ey'vah) the~THIS (הַזֹּאת / ha'zot) in~INSIDE~you(ms) (בְּקִרְבֶּךָ / bê'qir'be'kha)

and you will seek, and you will examine, and you will inquire thoroughly, and look, truth, the thing is being prepared, this disgusting thing is being done in among you.

16 (15) >~make~HIT(Verb) (הַכֵּה / ha'keyh) you(ms)~will~make~HIT(Verb) (תַכֶּה / ta'keh) AT (אֶת / et) SETTLE(Verb)~ing(mp) (יֹשְׁבֵי / yosh'vey) the~CITY (הָעִיר / ha'ir) the~HE (הַהוּא / ha'hu) [988] to~MOUTH (לְפִי / lê'phi) [989] SWORD (חָרֶב / hha'rev) >~make~PERFORATE(Verb) (הַחֲרֵם / ha'hha'reym) AT~her (אֹתָהּ / o'tah) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) ALL (כָּל / kol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~~her (בָּהּ / bah) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) BEAST~her (בְּהֶמְתָּהּ / bê'hem'tah) to~MOUTH (לְפִי / lê'phi) SWORD (חָרֶב / hha'rev)

You will certainly attack the settlers of that city by the mouth of the sword, perforate her and all which are in her, and her beast by the mouth of the sword,
