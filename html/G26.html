Genesis Chapter 26

1 and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) HUNGER (רָעָב / ra'av) in~the~LAND (בָּאָרֶץ / ba'a'rets) from~to~STRAND (מִלְּבַד / mi'le'vad) [156] the~HUNGER (הָרָעָב / ha'ra'av) the~FIRST (הָרִאשׁוֹן / ha'ri'shon) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיָה / hai'yah) in~DAY~s (בִּימֵי / bi'mey) Avraham (אַבְרָהָם / av'ra'ham) and~he~will~WALK(Verb) (וַיֵּלֶךְ / wai'yey'lekh) Yits'hhaq (יִצְחָק / yits'hhaq) TO (אֶל / el) Aviymelekh (אֲבִימֶלֶךְ / a'vi'me'lekh) KING (מֶלֶךְ / me'lekh) Peleshet~s (פְּלִשְׁתִּים / pê'lish'tim) Gerar~unto (גְּרָרָה / gê'ra'rah)

and hunger existed in the land apart from the first hunger which existed in the days of Avraham and Yits'hhaq walked to Aviymelekh king of the ones of Peleshet unto Gerar,

2 and~he~will~be~SEE(Verb) (וַיֵּרָא / wai'yey'ra) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) DO.NOT (אַל / al) you(ms)~will~GO.DOWN(Verb) (תֵּרֵד / tey'reyd) Mits'rayim~unto (מִצְרָיְמָה / mits'rai'mah) !(ms)~DWELL(Verb) (שְׁכֹן / shê'khon) in~the~LAND (בָּאָרֶץ / ba'a'rets) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I~will~SAY(Verb) (אֹמַר / o'mar) TO~you(ms) (אֵלֶיךָ / ey'ley'kha)

and YHWH appeared to him and he said, you will not go down unto Mits'rayim, dwell in the land which I will say to you.

3 !(ms)~IMMIGRATE(Verb) (גּוּר / gur) in~the~LAND (בָּאָרֶץ / ba'a'rets) the~THIS (הַזֹּאת / ha'zot) and~I~will~EXIST(Verb) (וְאֶהְיֶה / wê'eh'yeh) WITH~you(ms) (עִמְּךָ / im'kha) and~I~will~much~KNEEL(Verb)~you(ms) (וַאֲבָרְכֶךָּ / wa'a'var'khe'ka) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) and~to~SEED~you(ms) (וּלְזַרְעֲךָ / ul'zar'a'kha) I~will~GIVE(Verb) (אֶתֵּן / e'teyn) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~LAND~s (הָאֲרָצֹת / ha'a'ra'tsot) the~THESE (הָאֵל / ha'eyl) and~I~did~make~RISE(Verb) (וַהֲקִמֹתִי / wa'ha'qi'mo'ti) AT (אֶת / et) the~SWEARING (הַשְּׁבֻעָה / hash'vu'ah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I~did~be~SWEAR(Verb) (נִשְׁבַּעְתִּי / nish'ba'ti) to~Avraham (לְאַבְרָהָם / lê'av'ra'ham) FATHER~you(ms) (אָבִיךָ / a'vi'kha)

Immigrate in this land and I will exist with you and I will exalt you given that to you and to your seed I will give all these lands and I will make rise the swearing which I was sworn to Avraham your father,

4 and~I~did~make~INCREASE(Verb) (וְהִרְבֵּיתִי / wê'hir'bey'ti) AT (אֶת / et) SEED~you(ms) (זַרְעֲךָ / zar'a'kha) like~STAR~s (כְּכוֹכְבֵי / kê'khokh'vey) the~SKY~s2 (הַשָּׁמַיִם / ha'sha'ma'yim) and~I~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַתִּי / wê'na'ta'ti) to~SEED~you(ms) (לְזַרְעֲךָ / lê'zar'a'kha) AT (אֵת / eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~LAND~s (הָאֲרָצֹת / ha'a'ra'tsot) the~THESE (הָאֵל / ha'eyl) and~they~did~self~KNEEL(Verb) (וְהִתְבָּרְכוּ / wê'hit'bar'khu) in~SEED~you(ms) (בְזַרְעֲךָ / vê'zar'a'kha) ALL (כֹּל / kol) NATION~s (גּוֹיֵי / go'yey) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets)

and I will make your seed increase like the stars of the skies and I will give to your seed all these lands and all the nations of the land will exalt themselves with your seed.

5 SINCE (עֵקֶב / ey'qev) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) [157] he~did~HEAR(Verb) (שָׁמַע / sha'ma) Avraham (אַבְרָהָם / av'ra'ham) in~VOICE~me (בְּקֹלִי / bê'qo'li) and~he~will~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (וַיִּשְׁמֹר / wai'yish'mor) CHARGE~me (מִשְׁמַרְתִּי / mish'ma're'ti) DIRECTIVE~s~me (מִצְו‍ֹתַי / mits'o'tai) CUSTOM~s~me (חֻקּוֹתַי / hhu'qo'tai) and~TEACHING~s~me (וְתוֹרֹתָי / wê'to'ro'tai)

Because Avraham heard in my voice and he guarded my charge, my directives, my customs and my teachings,

6 and~he~will~SETTLE(Verb) (וַיֵּשֶׁב / wai'yey'shev) Yits'hhaq (יִצְחָק / yits'hhaq) in~Gerar (בִּגְרָר / big'rar)

and Yits'hhaq settled in Gerar,

7 and~they(m)~will~INQUIRE(Verb) (וַיִּשְׁאֲלוּ / wai'yish'a'lu) MAN~s (אַנְשֵׁי / an'shey) the~AREA (הַמָּקוֹם / ha'ma'qom) to~WOMAN~him (לְאִשְׁתּוֹ / lê'ish'to) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) SISTER~me (אֲחֹתִי / a'hho'ti) SHE (הִוא / hi) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~FEAR(Verb) (יָרֵא / ya'rey) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) WOMAN~me (אִשְׁתִּי / ish'ti) OTHERWISE (פֶּן / pen) they(m)~will~KILL(Verb)~me (יַהַרְגֻנִי / ya'har'gu'ni) MAN~s (אַנְשֵׁי / an'shey) the~AREA (הַמָּקוֹם / ha'ma'qom) UPON (עַל / al) Rivqah (רִבְקָה / riv'qah) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) FUNCTIONAL (טוֹבַת / to'vat) APPEARANCE (מַרְאֶה / mar'eh) SHE (הִיא / hi)

and the men inquired to his woman and he said, she is my sister, given that he feared saying, my woman, otherwise the men of the place will kill me because of Rivqah given that she is functional in appearance,

8 and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) they~did~PROLONG(Verb) (אָרְכוּ / ar'khu) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) THERE (שָׁם / sham) the~DAY~s (הַיָּמִים / hai'ya'mim) and~he~will~make~LOOK.DOWN(Verb) (וַיַּשְׁקֵף / wai'yash'qeyph) Aviymelekh (אֲבִימֶלֶךְ / a'vi'me'lekh) KING (מֶלֶךְ / me'lekh) Peleshet~s (פְּלִשְׁתִּים / pê'lish'tim) in~UNTIL (בְּעַד / bê'ad) the~WINDOW (הַחַלּוֹן / ha'hha'lon) and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) Yits'hhaq (יִצְחָק / yits'hhaq) much~LAUGH(Verb)~ing(ms) (מְצַחֵק / mê'tsa'hheyq) AT (אֵת / eyt) Rivqah (רִבְקָה / riv'qah) WOMAN~him (אִשְׁתּוֹ / ish'to)

and it came to pass that the days were prolonged to him there and Aviymelekh the king of the ones of Peleshet looked down round about the window and he saw and look, Yits'hhaq was laughing with Rivqah his woman,

9 and~he~will~CALL.OUT(Verb) (וַיִּקְרָא / wai'yiq'ra) Aviymelekh (אֲבִימֶלֶךְ / a'vi'me'lekh) to~Yits'hhaq (לְיִצְחָק / lê'yits'hhaq) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) SURELY (אַךְ / akh) LOOK (הִנֵּה / hin'neyh) WOMAN~you(ms) (אִשְׁתְּךָ / ish'te'kha) SHE (הִוא / hi) and~HOW (וְאֵיךְ / wê'eykh) you(ms)~did~SAY(Verb) (אָמַרְתָּ / a'mar'ta) SISTER~me (אֲחֹתִי / a'hho'ti) SHE (הִוא / hi) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) Yits'hhaq (יִצְחָק / yits'hhaq) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I~did~SAY(Verb) (אָמַרְתִּי / a'mar'ti) OTHERWISE (פֶּן / pen) I~will~DIE(Verb) (אָמוּת / a'mut) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah)

and Aviymelekh called out to Yits'hhaq and he said, surely look, she is your woman and why did you say, she is my sister and Yits'hhaq said to him given that I said otherwise I will die because of her,

10 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Aviymelekh (אֲבִימֶלֶךְ / a'vi'me'lekh) WHAT (מַה / mah) THIS (זֹּאת / zot) you(ms)~did~DO(Verb) (עָשִׂיתָ / a'si'ta) to~~us (לָּנוּ / la'nu) like~SMALL.AMOUNT (כִּמְעַט / kim'at) he~did~LIE.DOWN(Verb) (שָׁכַב / sha'khav) UNIT (אַחַד / a'hhad) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) AT (אֶת / et) WOMAN~you(ms) (אִשְׁתֶּךָ / ish'te'kha) and~you(ms)~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבֵאתָ / wê'hey'vey'ta) UPON~us (עָלֵינוּ / a'ley'nu) GUILT (אָשָׁם / a'sham)

and Aviymelekh said what is this you did to us, one of the people might have laid down with your woman and you will make guilt come upon us,

11 and~he~will~much~DIRECT(Verb) (וַיְצַו / wai'tsaw) Aviymelekh (אֲבִימֶלֶךְ / a'vi'me'lekh) AT (אֶת / et) ALL (כָּל / kol) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) the~TOUCH(Verb)~ing(ms) (הַנֹּגֵעַ / ha'no'gey'a) in~the~MAN (בָּאִישׁ / ba'ish) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh) and~in~WOMAN~him (וּבְאִשְׁתּוֹ / uv'ish'to) >~DIE(Verb) (מוֹת / mot) he~will~be~make~DIE(Verb) (יוּמָת / yu'mat)

and Aviymelekh directed all the people saying, the touching with this man and with his woman, he will surely be put to death,

12 and~he~will~SOW(Verb) (וַיִּזְרַע / wai'yiz'ra) Yits'hhaq (יִצְחָק / yits'hhaq) in~the~LAND (בָּאָרֶץ / ba'a'rets) the~SHE (הַהִוא / ha'hi) and~he~will~FIND(Verb) (וַיִּמְצָא / wai'yim'tsa) in~the~YEAR (בַּשָּׁנָה / ba'sha'nah) the~SHE (הַהִוא / ha'hi) HUNDRED (מֵאָה / mey'ah) SHA'AR~s (שְׁעָרִים / shê'a'rim) and~he~will~much~KNEEL(Verb)~him (וַיְבָרְכֵהוּ / wai'var'khey'hu) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH)

and Yits'hhaq sowed in that land and he found in that year a hundred sha'ars and YHWH exalted him,

13 and~he~will~MAGNIFY(Verb) (וַיִּגְדַּל / wai'yig'dal) the~MAN (הָאִישׁ / ha'ish) and~he~will~WALK(Verb) (וַיֵּלֶךְ / wai'yey'lekh) >~WALK(Verb) (הָלוֹךְ / ha'lokh) and~MAGNIFIED (וְגָדֵל / wê'ga'deyl) UNTIL (עַד / ad) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~MAGNIFY(Verb) (גָדַל / ga'dal) MANY (מְאֹד / mê'od)

and the man magnified and he walked a walking and magnified until he much magnified,

14 and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) LIVESTOCK (מִקְנֵה / miq'neyh) FLOCKS (צֹאן / tson) and~LIVESTOCK (וּמִקְנֵה / u'miq'neyh) CATTLE (בָקָר / va'qar) and~SERVE(Verb)~ed(fs) (וַעֲבֻדָּה / wa'a'vu'dah) ABUNDANT (רַבָּה / ra'bah) and~they(m)~will~much~BE.ZEALOUS(Verb) (וַיְקַנְאוּ / wai'qan'u) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) Peleshet~s (פְּלִשְׁתִּים / pê'lish'tim)

and livestock of flocks and livestock of cattle and an abundant household existed to him and the ones of Peleshet were envious of him,

15 and~ALL (וְכָל / wê'khol) the~WELL~s (הַבְּאֵרֹת / ha'be'ey'rot) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they~did~DIG.OUT(Verb) (חָפְרוּ / hhaph'ru) SERVANT~s (עַבְדֵי / av'dey) FATHER~him (אָבִיו / a'viw) in~DAY~s (בִּימֵי / bi'mey) Avraham (אַבְרָהָם / av'ra'ham) FATHER~him (אָבִיו / a'viw) they~did~much~SHUT.UP(Verb)~them(m) (סִתְּמוּם / sit'mum) Peleshet~s (פְּלִשְׁתִּים / pê'lish'tim) and~they(m)~will~much~FILL(Verb)~them(m) (וַיְמַלְאוּם / wa'ye'mal'um) DIRT (עָפָר / a'phar) [158]

and all the wells which the servants of his father dug out in the days of Avraham his father, the ones of Peleshet shut them up and they filled them with dirt,

16 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Aviymelekh (אֲבִימֶלֶךְ / a'vi'me'lekh) TO (אֶל / el) Yits'hhaq (יִצְחָק / yits'hhaq) !(ms)~WALK(Verb) (לֵךְ / leykh) from~WITH~us (מֵעִמָּנוּ / mey'i'ma'nu) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(ms)~did~BE.ABUNDANT(Verb) (עָצַמְתָּ / a'tsam'ta) FROM~us (מִמֶּנּוּ / mi'me'nu) MANY (מְאֹד / mê'od)

and Aviymelekh said to Yits'hhaq, walk from us given that you are much more abundant from us,

17 and~he~will~WALK(Verb) (וַיֵּלֶךְ / wai'yey'lekh) from~THERE (מִשָּׁם / mi'sham) Yits'hhaq (יִצְחָק / yits'hhaq) and~he~will~CAMP(Verb) (וַיִּחַן / wai'yi'hhan) in~WADI (בְּנַחַל / bê'na'hhal) Gerar (גְּרָר / gê'rar) and~he~will~SETTLE(Verb) (וַיֵּשֶׁב / wai'yey'shev) THERE (שָׁם / sham)

and Yits'hhaq walked from there and he camped in the wadi of Gerar and settled there,

18 and~he~will~TURN.BACK(Verb) (וַיָּשָׁב / wai'ya'shav) Yits'hhaq (יִצְחָק / yits'hhaq) and~he~will~DIG.OUT(Verb) (וַיַּחְפֹּר / wai'yahh'por) AT (אֶת / et) WELL~s (בְּאֵרֹת / bê'ey'rot) the~WATER~s2 (הַמַּיִם / ha'ma'yim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they~did~DIG.OUT(Verb) (חָפְרוּ / hhaph'ru) in~DAY~s (בִּימֵי / bi'mey) Avraham (אַבְרָהָם / av'ra'ham) FATHER~him (אָבִיו / a'viw) and~they~did~much~SHUT.UP(Verb)~them(m) (וַיְסַתְּמוּם / wa'ye'sat'mum) Peleshet~s (פְּלִשְׁתִּים / pê'lish'tim) AFTER (אַחֲרֵי / a'hha'rey) DEATH (מוֹת / mot) Avraham (אַבְרָהָם / av'ra'ham) and~he~will~CALL.OUT(Verb) (וַיִּקְרָא / wai'yiq'ra) to~~them(f) (לָהֶן / la'hen) TITLE~s (שֵׁמוֹת / shey'mot) like~TITLE~s (כַּשֵּׁמֹת / ka'shey'mot) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~CALL.OUT(Verb) (קָרָא / qa'ra) to~~them(f) (לָהֶן / la'hen) FATHER~him (אָבִיו / a'viw)

and Yits'hhaq turned back and dug out the wells of waters which they dug out in the days of Avraham his father and the ones of Peleshet shut them up after the death of Avraham and he called out to them titles like the titles which his father called out to them,

19 and~they(m)~will~DIG.OUT(Verb) (וַיַּחְפְּרוּ / wai'yahh'pe'ru) SERVANT~s (עַבְדֵי / av'dey) Yits'hhaq (יִצְחָק / yits'hhaq) in~the~WADI (בַּנָּחַל / ba'na'hhal) and~they(m)~will~FIND(Verb) (וַיִּמְצְאוּ / wai'yim'tse'u) THERE (שָׁם / sham) WELL (בְּאֵר / bê'eyr) WATER~s2 (מַיִם / ma'yim) LIVING~s (חַיִּים / hhai'yim)

and the servants of Yits'hhaq dug out in the wadi and they found there a well of waters of life,

and the feeders of Gerar disputed with the feeders of Yits'hhaq saying, the waters is to us, and he called out there the well Eseq given that they quarreled with themselves with him,
