Exodus Chapter 3

1 and~Mosheh (וּמֹשֶׁה / u'mo'sheh) he~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיָה / hai'yah) FEED(Verb)~ing(ms) (רֹעֶה / ro'eh) AT (אֶת / et) FLOCKS (צֹאן / tson) Yitro (יִתְרוֹ / yit'ro) BE.AN.IN-LAW(Verb)~ing(ms)~him (חֹתְנוֹ / hhot'no) ADMINISTRATOR (כֹּהֵן / ko'heyn) Mid'yan (מִדְיָן / mid'yan) and~he~will~DRIVE(Verb) (וַיִּנְהַג / wai'yon'hag) AT (אֶת / et) the~FLOCKS (הַצֹּאן / ha'tson) AFTER (אַחַר / a'hhar) the~WILDERNESS (הַמִּדְבָּר / ha'mid'bar) and~he~will~COME(Verb) (וַיָּבֹא / wai'ya'vo) TO (אֶל / el) HILL (הַר / har) the~Elohiym (הָאֱלֹהִים / ha'e'lo'him) Hhorev~unto (חֹרֵבָה / hhor'vah)

and Mosheh had been feeding the flocks of Yitro, his in-law, the administrator of Mid'yan, and he drove the flocks behind the wilderness and he came to the hill of the Elohiym, unto Hhorev,

2 and~he~will~be~SEE(Verb) (וַיֵּרָא / wai'yey'ra) MESSENGER (מַלְאַךְ / mal'akh) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) in~GLIMMERING (בְּלַבַּת / bê'la'bat) FIRE (אֵשׁ / eysh) from~MIDST (מִתּוֹךְ / mi'tokh) the~THORN.BUSH (הַסְּנֶה / has'neh) and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) and~LOOK (וְהִנֵּה / wê'hin'neyh) the~THORN.BUSH (הַסְּנֶה / has'neh) BURN(Verb)~ing(ms) (בֹּעֵר / bo'eyr) in~the~FIRE (בָּאֵשׁ / ba'eysh) and~the~THORN.BUSH (וְהַסְּנֶה / wê'has'neh) WITHOUT~him (אֵינֶנּוּ / ey'ne'nu) EAT(Verb)~ed(ms) (אֻכָּל / u'kal)

and the messenger of YHWH appeared to him in a glimmering of fire from the midst of the thorn bush, and he saw, and look, the thorn bush was burning with the fire and the thorn bush was not eaten,

3 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) I~will~TURN.ASIDE(Verb)~& (אָסֻרָה / a'su'rah) PLEASE (נָּא / na) and~I~will~SEE(Verb) (וְאֶרְאֶה / wê'er'eh) AT (אֶת / et) the~APPEARANCE (הַמַּרְאֶה / ha'mar'eh) the~GREAT (הַגָּדֹל / ha'ga'dol) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh) WHY (מַדּוּעַ / ma'du'a) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~BURN(Verb) (יִבְעַר / yiv'ar) the~THORN.BUSH (הַסְּנֶה / has'neh)

and Mosheh said, please, let me turn aside and I will see this great appearance, why the thorn bush will not burn,

4 and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~TURN.ASIDE(Verb) (סָר / sar) to~>~SEE(Verb) (לִרְאוֹת / lir'ot) and~he~will~CALL.OUT(Verb) (וַיִּקְרָא / wai'yiq'ra) TO~him (אֵלָיו / ey'law) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) from~MIDST (מִתּוֹךְ / mi'tokh) the~THORN.BUSH (הַסְּנֶה / has'neh) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) LOOK~me (הִנֵּנִי / hin'ney'ni)

and YHWH saw that he turned aside to see, and Elohiym called out to him from the midst of the thorn bush, and he said, Mosheh, Mosheh, and he said, here I am,

5 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) DO.NOT (אַל / al) you(ms)~will~COME.NEAR(Verb) (תִּקְרַב / tiq'rav) AT.THIS.POINT (הֲלֹם / ha'lom) !(ms)~CAST.OFF(Verb) (שַׁל / shal) SANDAL~s~you(ms) (נְעָלֶיךָ / nê'a'ley'kha) from~UPON (מֵעַל / mey'al) FOOT~s2~you(ms) (רַגְלֶיךָ / rag'ley'kha) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) the~AREA (הַמָּקוֹם / ha'ma'qom) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) YOU(MS) (אַתָּה / a'tah) STAND(Verb)~ing(ms) (עוֹמֵד / o'meyd) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) GROUND (אַדְמַת / ad'mat) SPECIAL (קֹדֶשׁ / qo'desh) [234] HE (הוּא / hu)

and he said, do not come near to this point, cast off your sandals from upon your feet, given that the area which you are standing upon is the ground of a special place,

6 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) Elohiym (אֱלֹהֵי / e'lo'hey) FATHER~you(ms) (אָבִיךָ / a'vi'kha) Elohiym (אֱלֹהֵי / e'lo'hey) Avraham (אַבְרָהָם / av'ra'ham) Elohiym (אֱלֹהֵי / e'lo'hey) Yits'hhaq (יִצְחָק / yits'hhaq) and~Elohiym (וֵאלֹהֵי / wey'lo'hey) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) and~he~will~make~HIDE(Verb) (וַיַּסְתֵּר / wai'yas'teyr) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) FACE~s~him (פָּנָיו / pa'naw) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) he~did~FEAR(Verb) (יָרֵא / ya'rey) from~>~make~STARE(Verb) (מֵהַבִּיט / mey'ha'bit) TO (אֶל / el) the~Elohiym (הָאֱלֹהִים / ha'e'lo'him)

and he said, I am Elohiym of your father, Elohiym of Avraham, Elohiym of Yits'hhaq and Elohiym of Ya'aqov, and Mosheh hid his face, given that he feared from staring toward the Elohiym,

7 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) >~SEE(Verb) (רָאֹה / ra'oh) I~did~SEE(Verb) (רָאִיתִי / ra'i'ti) AT (אֶת / et) AFFLICTION (עֳנִי / a'ni) PEOPLE~me (עַמִּי / a'mi) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~Mits'rayim (בְּמִצְרָיִם / bê'mits'ra'yim) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) CRY~them(m) (צַעֲקָתָם / tsa'a'qa'tam) [235] I~did~HEAR(Verb) (שָׁמַעְתִּי / sha'ma'ti) from~FACE~s (מִפְּנֵי / mip'ney) PUSH(Verb)~ing(mp)~him (נֹגְשָׂיו / nog'saw) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I~did~KNOW(Verb) (יָדַעְתִּי / ya'da'ti) AT (אֶת / et) MISERY~s~him (מַכְאֹבָיו / makh'o'vaw)

and YHWH said, I surely saw the affliction of my people who are in Mits'rayim, and I heard their cry from the faces of his pushers given that I know his miseries,

8 and~I~will~GO.DOWN(Verb) (וָאֵרֵד / wa'ey'reyd) to~>~make~DELIVER(Verb)~him (לְהַצִּילוֹ / lê'ha'tsi'lo) from~HAND (מִיַּד / mi'yad) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) and~to~>~make~GO.UP(Verb)~him (וּלְהַעֲלֹתוֹ / ul'ha'a'lo'to) FROM (מִן / min) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) the~SHE (הַהִוא / ha'hi) TO (אֶל / el) LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) FUNCTIONAL (טוֹבָה / to'vah) and~WIDE (וּרְחָבָה / ur'hha'vah) TO (אֶל / el) LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) ISSUE(Verb)~ing(fs) (זָבַת / za'vat) FAT (חָלָב / hha'lav) [236] and~HONEY (וּדְבָשׁ / ud'vash) [237] TO (אֶל / el) AREA (מְקוֹם / mê'qom) the~Kena'an~of (הַכְּנַעֲנִי / ha'ke'na'a'ni) and~the~Hhet~of (וְהַחִתִּי / wê'ha'hhi'ti) and~the~Emor~of (וְהָאֱמֹרִי / wê'ha'e'mo'ri) and~the~Perez~of (וְהַפְּרִזִּי / wê'ha'pe'ri'zi) and~the~Hhiw~of (וְהַחִוִּי / wê'ha'hhi'wi) and~the~Yevus~of (וְהַיְבוּסִי / wê'hai'vu'si)

and I will go down to deliver him from the hand of Mits'rayim, and to make him go up from that land to a functional and wide land, to a land issuing fat and honey, to the area of the one of Kena'an and the one of Hhet and the one of Emor and the one of Perez and the one of Hhiw and the one of Yevus,

9 and~NOW (וְעַתָּה / wê'a'tah) LOOK (הִנֵּה / hin'neyh) CRY (צַעֲקַת / tsa'a'qat) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) she~did~COME(Verb) (בָּאָה / ba'ah) TO~me (אֵלָי / ey'lai) and~ALSO (וְגַם / wê'gam) I~did~SEE(Verb) (רָאִיתִי / ra'i'ti) AT (אֶת / et) the~SQUEEZING (הַלַּחַץ / ha'la'hhats) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) Mits'rayim (מִצְרַיִם / mits'ra'yim) SQUEEZE(Verb)~ing(mp) (לֹחֲצִים / lo'hha'tsim) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam)

and now look, the cry of the sons of Yisra'eyl had come to me, and also, I saw the squeezing because Mits'rayim is squeezing them,

10 and~NOW (וְעַתָּה / wê'a'tah) !(ms)~WALK(Verb)~& (לְכָה / lê'khah) and~I~will~SEND(Verb)~you(ms) (וְאֶשְׁלָחֲךָ / wê'esh'la'hha'kha) TO (אֶל / el) Paroh (פַּרְעֹה / par'oh) and~>~make~GO.OUT(Verb) (וְהוֹצֵא / wê'ho'tsey) AT (אֶת / et) PEOPLE~me (עַמִּי / a'mi) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) from~Mits'rayim (מִמִּצְרָיִם / mi'mits'ra'yim)

and now walk, and I will send you to Paroh, and make my people, the sons of Yisra'eyl, go out from Mits'rayim,

11 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) TO (אֶל / el) the~Elohiym (הָאֱלֹהִים / ha'e'lo'him) WHO (מִי / mi) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I~will~WALK(Verb) (אֵלֵךְ / ey'leykh) TO (אֶל / el) Paroh (פַּרְעֹה / par'oh) and~GIVEN.THAT (וְכִי / wê'khi) I~will~make~GO.OUT(Verb) (אוֹצִיא / o'tsi) AT (אֶת / et) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) from~Mits'rayim (מִמִּצְרָיִם / mi'mits'ra'yim)

and Mosheh said to the Elohiym, who am I that I should walk to Paroh and that I will make the sons of Yisra'eyl, go out from Mits'rayim,

12 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I~will~EXIST(Verb) (אֶהְיֶה / eh'yeh) WITH~you(fs) (עִמָּךְ / i'makh) and~THIS (וְזֶה / wê'zeh) to~~you(ms) (לְּךָ / lê'kha) the~SIGN (הָאוֹת / ha'ot) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) I~did~SEND(Verb)~you(ms) (שְׁלַחְתִּיךָ / shê'lahh'ti'kha) in~>~make~GO.OUT(Verb)~you(ms) (בְּהוֹצִיאֲךָ / bê'ho'tsi'a'kha) AT (אֶת / et) the~PEOPLE (הָעָם / ha'am) from~Mits'rayim (מִמִּצְרַיִם / mi'mits'ra'yim) you(mp)~will~SERVE(Verb)~must (תַּעַבְדוּן / ta'av'dun) AT (אֶת / et) the~Elohiym (הָאֱלֹהִים / ha'e'lo'him) UPON (עַל / al) the~HILL (הָהָר / ha'har) the~THIS (הַזֶּה / ha'zeh)

and he said, given that I will exist with you, and this is the sign for you that I had sent you, in your making the people go out from Mits'rayim, you must serve the Elohiym upon this hill,

13 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) TO (אֶל / el) the~Elohiym (הָאֱלֹהִים / ha'e'lo'him) LOOK (הִנֵּה / hin'neyh) I (אָנֹכִי / a'no'khi) he~did~COME(Verb) (בָא / va) TO (אֶל / el) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~I~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמַרְתִּי / wê'a'mar'ti) to~~them(m) (לָהֶם / la'hem) Elohiym (אֱלֹהֵי / e'lo'hey) FATHER~s~you(mp) (אֲבוֹתֵיכֶם / a'vo'tey'khem) he~did~SEND(Verb)~me (שְׁלָחַנִי / shê'la'hha'ni) TO~you(mp) (אֲלֵיכֶם / a'ley'khem) and~they~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמְרוּ / wê'am'ru) to~~me (לִי / li) WHAT (מַה / mah) TITLE~him (שְּׁמוֹ / shê'mo) WHAT (מָה / mah) I~will~SAY(Verb) (אֹמַר / o'mar) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem)

and Mosheh said to the Elohiym, look, I am coming to the sons of Yisra'eyl and I will say to them, Elohiym of your fathers had sent me to you, and they will say to me, what is his title, what will I say to them,

14 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) I~will~EXIST(Verb) (אֶהְיֶה / eh'yeh) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I~will~EXIST(Verb) (אֶהְיֶה / eh'yeh) and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) IN.THIS.WAY (כֹּה / koh) you(ms)~will~SAY(Verb) (תֹאמַר / to'mar) to~SON~s (לִבְנֵי / liv'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) Ehyeh (אֶהְיֶה / eh'yeh) he~did~SEND(Verb)~me (שְׁלָחַנִי / shê'la'hha'ni) TO~you(mp) (אֲלֵיכֶם / a'ley'khem)

and Elohiym said to Mosheh, I will exist which I will exist, and he said, in this way you will say to the sons of Yisra'eyl, Ehyeh had sent me to you,

15 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) YET.AGAIN (עוֹד / od) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) IN.THIS.WAY (כֹּה / koh) you(ms)~will~SAY(Verb) (תֹאמַר / to'mar) TO (אֶל / el) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) Elohiym (אֱלֹהֵי / e'lo'hey) FATHER~s~you(mp) (אֲבֹתֵיכֶם / a'vo'tey'khem) Elohiym (אֱלֹהֵי / e'lo'hey) Avraham (אַבְרָהָם / av'ra'ham) Elohiym (אֱלֹהֵי / e'lo'hey) Yits'hhaq (יִצְחָק / yits'hhaq) and~Elohiym (וֵאלֹהֵי / wey'lo'hey) Ya'aqov (יַעֲקֹב / ya'a'qov) he~did~SEND(Verb)~me (שְׁלָחַנִי / shê'la'hha'ni) TO~you(mp) (אֲלֵיכֶם / a'ley'khem) THIS (זֶה / zeh) TITLE~me (שְּׁמִי / shê'mi) to~DISTANT (לְעֹלָם / lê'o'lam) and~THIS (וְזֶה / wê'zeh) MEMORY~me (זִכְרִי / zikh'ri) to~GENERATION (לְדֹר / lê'dor) GENERATION (דֹּר / dor) [238]

and Elohiym said yet again to Mosheh, in this way you will say to the sons of Yisra'eyl, YHWH the Elohiym of your fathers, Elohiym of Avraham, Elohiym of Yits'hhaq and Elohiym of Ya'aqov had sent me to you, this is my title for a distant time and this is my memorial for a generation and a generation.
