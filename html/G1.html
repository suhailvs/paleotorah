Genesis Chapter 1

1 in~ORIGIN (בְּרֵאשִׁית / bê'rey'shit) he~did~SHAPE(Verb) (בָּרָא / ba'ra) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) AT (אֵת / eyt) the~SKY~s2 (הַשָּׁמַיִם / ha'sha'ma'yim) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets)

In the origin Elohiym shaped the skies and the land,

2 and~the~LAND (וְהָאָרֶץ / wê'ha'a'rets) she~did~EXIST(Verb) (הָיְתָה / hai'tah) CONFUSION (תֹהוּ / to'hu) and~UNFILLED (וָבֹהוּ / wa'vo'hu) and~DARKNESS (וְחֹשֶׁךְ / wê'hho'shekh) UPON (עַל / al) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) DEEP.WATER (תְהוֹם / tê'hom) and~WIND (וְרוּחַ / wê'ru'ahh) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) much~FLUTTER(Verb)~ing(fs) (מְרַחֶפֶת / mê'ra'hhe'phet) UPON (עַל / al) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) the~WATER~s2 (הַמָּיִם / ha'ma'yim)

and the land had existed in confusion and was unfilled, and darkness was upon the face of the deep water and the wind of Elohiym was fluttering upon the face of the waters,

3 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יְהִי / yê'hi) LIGHT (אוֹר / or) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) LIGHT (אוֹר / or)

and Elohiym said, light will exist, and light existed,

4 and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) AT (אֶת / et) the~LIGHT (הָאוֹר / ha'or) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) FUNCTIONAL (טוֹב / tov) and~he~will~make~SEPARATE(Verb) (וַיַּבְדֵּל / wai'yav'deyl) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) BETWEEN (בֵּין / beyn) the~LIGHT (הָאוֹר / ha'or) and~BETWEEN (וּבֵין / u'veyn) the~DARKNESS (הַחֹשֶׁךְ / ha'hho'shekh)

and Elohiym saw the light given that it was functional, and Elohiym made a separation between the light and the darkness,

5 and~he~will~CALL.OUT(Verb) (וַיִּקְרָא / wai'yiq'ra) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) to~LIGHT (לָאוֹר / la'or) DAY (יוֹם / yom) and~to~DARKNESS (וְלַחֹשֶׁךְ / wê'la'hho'shekh) he~did~CALL.OUT(Verb) (קָרָא / qa'ra) NIGHT (לָיְלָה / lai'lah) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) EVENING (עֶרֶב / e'rev) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) MORNING (בֹקֶר / vo'qer) DAY (יוֹם / yom) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad)

and Elohiym called out to the light, day, and to the darkness he called out, night, and evening existed and morning existed, a day unit,

6 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יְהִי / yê'hi) SHEET (רָקִיעַ / ra'qi'a) in~MIDST (בְּתוֹךְ / bê'tokh) the~WATER~s2 (הַמָּיִם / ha'ma'yim) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וִיהִי / wi'hi) make~SEPARATE(Verb)~ing(ms) (מַבְדִּיל / mav'dil) BETWEEN (בֵּין / beyn) WATER~s2 (מַיִם / ma'yim) to~WATER~s2 (לָמָיִם / la'ma'yim)

and Elohiym said, a sheet will exist in the midst of the waters, and he existed, making a separation between waters to waters,

7 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) AT (אֶת / et) the~SHEET (הָרָקִיעַ / ha'ra'qi'a) and~he~will~make~SEPARATE(Verb) (וַיַּבְדֵּל / wai'yav'deyl) BETWEEN (בֵּין / beyn) the~WATER~s2 (הַמַּיִם / ha'ma'yim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) from~UNDER (מִתַּחַת / mi'ta'hhat) to~SHEET (לָרָקִיעַ / la'ra'qi'a) and~BETWEEN (וּבֵין / u'veyn) the~WATER~s2 (הַמַּיִם / ha'ma'yim) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) from~UPON (מֵעַל / mey'al) to~SHEET (לָרָקִיעַ / la'ra'qi'a) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) SO (כֵן / kheyn) [1]

and Elohiym made the sheet, and he made a separation between the waters which are below for a sheet and the waters which are above for a sheet, and he existed so,

8 and~he~will~CALL.OUT(Verb) (וַיִּקְרָא / wai'yiq'ra) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) to~SHEET (לָרָקִיעַ / la'ra'qi'a) SKY~s2 (שָׁמָיִם / sha'ma'yim) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) EVENING (עֶרֶב / e'rev) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) MORNING (בֹקֶר / vo'qer) DAY (יוֹם / yom) SECOND (שֵׁנִי / shey'ni)

and Elohiym called out to the sheet, skies, and evening existed and morning existed, a second day,

9 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) he~will~be~BOUND.UP(Verb) (יִקָּווּ / yi'qaw'u) the~WATER~s2 (הַמַּיִם / ha'ma'yim) from~UNDER (מִתַּחַת / mi'ta'hhat) the~SKY~s2 (הַשָּׁמַיִם / ha'sha'ma'yim) TO (אֶל / el) AREA (מָקוֹם / ma'qom) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) and~she~be~SEE(Verb) (וְתֵרָאֶה / wê'tey'ra'eh) the~DRY.GROUND (הַיַּבָּשָׁה / hai'ya'ba'shah) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) SO (כֵן / kheyn) [2]

and Elohiym said, the waters will be bound up below the skies to one area, and dry ground appeared, and he existed so,

10 and~he~will~CALL.OUT(Verb) (וַיִּקְרָא / wai'yiq'ra) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) to~DRY.GROUND (לַיַּבָּשָׁה / lai'ya'ba'shah) LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) and~to~COLLECTION (וּלְמִקְוֵה / ul'miq'weh) the~WATER~s2 (הַמַּיִם / ha'ma'yim) he~did~CALL.OUT(Verb) (קָרָא / qa'ra) SEA~s (יַמִּים / ya'mim) and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) FUNCTIONAL (טוֹב / tov)

and Elohiym called out to the dry ground, land, and to the collection of the waters he called out, seas, and Elohiym saw that it was functional,

11 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) she~will~make~SPROUT(Verb) (תַּדְשֵׁא / tad'shey) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) GRASS (דֶּשֶׁא / de'she) HERB (עֵשֶׂב / ey'sev) make~SOW(Verb)~ing(ms) (מַזְרִיעַ / maz'ri'a) SEED (זֶרַע / ze'ra) TREE (עֵץ / eyts) PRODUCE (פְּרִי / pê'ri) DO(Verb)~ing(ms) (עֹשֶׂה / o'seh) PRODUCE (פְּרִי / pê'ri) to~KIND~him (לְמִינוֹ / lê'mi'no) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) SEED~him (זַרְעוֹ / zar'o) in~~him (בוֹ / vo) UPON (עַל / al) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) SO (כֵן / kheyn) [3]

and Elohiym said, the land will make grass sprout, herbs producing seeds, trees of produce making produce to his kind which his seed is in him upon the land, and he existed so,

12 and~she~will~make~GO.OUT(Verb) (וַתּוֹצֵא / wa'to'tsey) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) GRASS (דֶּשֶׁא / de'she) HERB (עֵשֶׂב / ey'sev) make~SOW(Verb)~ing(ms) (מַזְרִיעַ / maz'ri'a) SEED (זֶרַע / ze'ra) to~KIND~him (לְמִינֵהוּ / lê'mi'ney'hu) and~TREE (וְעֵץ / wê'eyts) DO(Verb)~ing(ms) (עֹשֶׂה / o'seh) PRODUCE (פְּרִי / pê'ri) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) SEED~him (זַרְעוֹ / zar'o) in~~him (בוֹ / vo) to~KIND~him (לְמִינֵהוּ / lê'mi'ney'hu) and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) FUNCTIONAL (טוֹב / tov)

and the land brought out grass, herbs sowing seeds to his kind, and trees making produce which has his seed in him to his kind, and Elohiym saw that it was functional,

13 and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) EVENING (עֶרֶב / e'rev) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) MORNING (בֹקֶר / vo'qer) DAY (יוֹם / yom) THIRD (שְׁלִישִׁי / shê'li'shi) [4]

and evening existed and morning existed, a third day,

14 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יְהִי / yê'hi) LUMINARY~s (מְאֹרֹת / mê'o'rot) in~SHEET (בִּרְקִיעַ / bir'qi'a) the~SKY~s2 (הַשָּׁמַיִם / ha'sha'ma'yim) to~>~make~SEPARATE(Verb) (לְהַבְדִּיל / lê'hav'dil) BETWEEN (בֵּין / beyn) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) and~BETWEEN (וּבֵין / u'veyn) the~NIGHT (הַלָּיְלָה / ha'lai'lah) and~they~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיוּ / wê'hai'u) to~SIGN~s (לְאֹתֹת / lê'o'tot) and~to~APPOINTED~s (וּלְמוֹעֲדִים / ul'mo'a'dim) and~to~DAY~s (וּלְיָמִים / ul'ya'mim) and~YEAR~s (וְשָׁנִים / wê'sha'nim)

and Elohiym said, the luminaries will exist in the sheet of the skies to make a separation between the day and the night and they exist for signs and for appointed time and for days and years,

15 and~they~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיוּ / wê'hai'u) to~LUMINARY~s (לִמְאוֹרֹת / lim'o'rot) in~SHEET (בִּרְקִיעַ / bir'qi'a) the~SKY~s2 (הַשָּׁמַיִם / ha'sha'ma'yim) to~>~make~LIGHT(Verb) (לְהָאִיר / lê'ha'ir) UPON (עַל / al) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) SO (כֵן / kheyn) [5]

and they exist for luminaries in the sheet of the skies to make light upon the land, and he existed so,

16 and~he~will~DO(Verb) (וַיַּעַשׂ / wai'ya'as) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) AT (אֶת / et) TWO (שְׁנֵי / shê'ney) the~LUMINARY~s (הַמְּאֹרֹת / ham'o'rot) the~GREAT~s (הַגְּדֹלִים / ha'ge'do'lim) AT (אֶת / et) the~LUMINARY (הַמָּאוֹר / ha'ma'or) the~GREAT (הַגָּדֹל / ha'ga'dol) to~REGULATION (לְמֶמְשֶׁלֶת / lê'mem'she'let) the~DAY (הַיּוֹם / hai'yom) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~LUMINARY (הַמָּאוֹר / ha'ma'or) the~SMALL (הַקָּטֹן / ha'qa'ton) to~REGULATION (לְמֶמְשֶׁלֶת / lê'mem'she'let) the~NIGHT (הַלַּיְלָה / ha'lai'lah) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) the~STAR~s (הַכּוֹכָבִים / ha'ko'kha'vim) [6]

and Elohiym made two of the great luminaries, the great luminary for the regulation of the day, and the small luminary for the regulation of the night, and the stars,

17 and~he~will~GIVE(Verb) (וַיִּתֵּן / wai'yi'teyn) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) in~SHEET (בִּרְקִיעַ / bir'qi'a) the~SKY~s2 (הַשָּׁמָיִם / ha'sha'ma'yim) to~>~make~LIGHT(Verb) (לְהָאִיר / lê'ha'ir) UPON (עַל / al) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets)

and Elohiym placed them in the sheet of the skies to make light upon the land,

18 and~to~>~REGULATE(Verb) (וְלִמְשֹׁל / wê'lim'shol) in~the~DAY (בַּיּוֹם / ba'yom) and~in~NIGHT (וּבַלַּיְלָה / u'va'lai'lah) and~to~>~make~SEPARATE(Verb) (וּלְהַבְדִּיל / ul'hav'dil) BETWEEN (בֵּין / beyn) the~LIGHT (הָאוֹר / ha'or) and~BETWEEN (וּבֵין / u'veyn) the~DARKNESS (הַחֹשֶׁךְ / ha'hho'shekh) and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) FUNCTIONAL (טוֹב / tov)

and to regulate in the day and in the night, and to make a separation between the light and the darkness, and Elohiym saw that it was functional,

19 and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) EVENING (עֶרֶב / e'rev) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) MORNING (בֹקֶר / vo'qer) DAY (יוֹם / yom) FOURTH (רְבִיעִי / rê'vi'i)

and evening existed and morning existed, a fourth day,

20 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) they(m)~will~SWARM(Verb) (יִשְׁרְצוּ / yish're'tsu) the~WATER~s2 (הַמַּיִם / ha'ma'yim) SWARMER (שֶׁרֶץ / she'rets) SOUL (נֶפֶשׁ / ne'phesh) LIVING (חַיָּה / hhai'yah) and~FLYER (וְעוֹף / wê'oph) he~will~much~FLY(Verb) (יְעוֹפֵף / yê'o'pheyph) UPON (עַל / al) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) UPON (עַל / al) FACE~s (פְּנֵי / pê'ney) SHEET (רְקִיעַ / rê'qi'a) the~SKY~s2 (הַשָּׁמָיִם / ha'sha'ma'yim)

and Elohiym said, the waters will swarm with swarmers of living souls and flyers will fly upon the land, upon the face of the sheet of the skies,

21 and~he~will~SHAPE(Verb) (וַיִּבְרָא / wai'yiv'ra) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) AT (אֶת / et) the~CROCODILE~s (הַתַּנִּינִם / ha'ta'ni'nim) [7] the~GREAT~s (הַגְּדֹלִים / ha'ge'do'lim) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) SOUL (נֶפֶשׁ / ne'phesh) the~LIVING (הַחַיָּה / ha'hha'yah) the~TREAD(Verb)~ing(fs) (הָרֹמֶשֶׂת / ha'ro'me'set) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) they(m)~did~SWARM(Verb) (שָׁרְצוּ / shar'tsu) the~WATER~s2 (הַמַּיִם / ha'ma'yim) to~KIND~them(m) (לְמִינֵהֶם / lê'mi'ney'hem) and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) ALL (כָּל / kol) FLYER (עוֹף / oph) WING (כָּנָף / ka'naph) to~KIND~him (לְמִינֵהוּ / lê'mi'ney'hu) and~he~will~SEE(Verb) (וַיַּרְא / wai'yar) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) FUNCTIONAL (טוֹב / tov)

and Elohiym shaped the great crocodile and all the living souls, the treaders which swarm the waters to their kind, and all the flyers of the wing to his kind, and Elohiym saw that it was functional,

22 and~he~will~much~KNEEL(Verb) (וַיְבָרֶךְ / wai'va'rekh) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) !(mp)~REPRODUCE(Verb) (פְּרוּ / pê'ru) and~!(mp)~INCREASE(Verb) (וּרְבוּ / ur'vu) and~!(mp)~FILL(Verb) (וּמִלְאוּ / u'mil'u) AT (אֶת / et) the~WATER~s2 (הַמַּיִם / ha'ma'yim) in~the~SEA~s (בַּיַּמִּים / ba'ya'mim) and~the~FLYER (וְהָעוֹף / wê'ha'oph) he~did~INCREASE(Verb) (יִרֶב / yi'rev) in~the~LAND (בָּאָרֶץ / ba'a'rets)

and Elohiym exalted them saying, reproduce and increase and fill the waters in the seas, and the flyers increased in the land,

23 and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) EVENING (עֶרֶב / e'rev) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) MORNING (בֹקֶר / vo'qer) DAY (יוֹם / yom) FIFTH (חֲמִישִׁי / hha'mi'shi)

and evening existed and morning existed, a fifth day,

24 and~he~will~SAY(Verb) (וַיֹּאמֶר / wai'yo'mer) Elohiym (אֱלֹהִים / e'lo'him) she~will~make~GO.OUT(Verb) (תּוֹצֵא / to'tsey) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) SOUL (נֶפֶשׁ / ne'phesh) LIVING (חַיָּה / hhai'yah) to~KIND~her (לְמִינָהּ / lê'mi'nah) BEAST (בְּהֵמָה / bê'hey'mah) and~TREADER (וָרֶמֶשׂ / wa're'mes) and~LIVING~him (וְחַיְתוֹ / wê'hhai'to) [8] LAND (אֶרֶץ / e'rets) to~KIND~her (לְמִינָהּ / lê'mi'nah) and~he~will~EXIST(Verb) (וַיְהִי / wai'hi) SO (כֵן / kheyn) [9]

and Elohiym said, the land will bring out living souls to her kind, beasts and treaders and living ones of the land to her kind, and he existed so,
