Leviticus Chapter 12

1 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh, saying,

2 !(ms)~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דַּבֵּר / da'beyr) TO (אֶל / el) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor) WOMAN (אִשָּׁה / i'shah) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) she~will~make~SOW(Verb) (תַזְרִיעַ / taz'ri'a) and~she~did~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (וְיָלְדָה / wê'yal'dah) MALE (זָכָר / za'khar) and~she~did~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (וְטָמְאָה / wê'tam'ah) SEVEN (שִׁבְעַת / shiv'at) DAY~s (יָמִים / ya'mim) like~DAY~s (כִּימֵי / ki'mey) REMOVAL (נִדַּת / ni'dat) >~ILL(Verb)~her (דְּוֹתָהּ / dê'o'tah) she~will~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (תִּטְמָא / tit'ma)

Speak to the sons of Yisra'eyl, saying, a woman that will produce and bring forth a male, then she will be dirty seven days, like the days of removal of her illness, she will be dirty,

3 and~in~the~DAY (וּבַיּוֹם / u'vai'yom) the~EIGHTH (הַשְּׁמִינִי / hash'mini) he~will~be~SNIP.OFF(Verb) (יִמּוֹל / yi'mol) FLESH (בְּשַׂר / bê'sar) FORESKIN~him (עָרְלָתוֹ / ar'la'to)

and in the eighth, the flesh of his foreskin will be snipped off,

4 and~THREE~s (וּשְׁלֹשִׁים / ush'lo'shim) DAY (יוֹם / yom) and~THREE (וּשְׁלֹשֶׁת / ush'lo'shet) DAY~s (יָמִים / ya'mim) she~will~SETTLE(Verb) (תֵּשֵׁב / tey'sheyv) in~BLOOD~s (בִּדְמֵי / bid'mey) CLEAN (טָהֳרָה / ta'ha'rah) in~ALL (בְּכָל / bê'khol) SPECIAL (קֹדֶשׁ / qo'desh) NOT (לֹא / lo) she~will~TOUCH(Verb) (תִגָּע / ti'ga) and~TO (וְאֶל / wê'el) the~SANCTUARY (הַמִּקְדָּשׁ / ha'miq'dash) NOT (לֹא / lo) she~will~COME(Verb) (תָבֹא / ta'vo) UNTIL (עַד / ad) >~FILL(Verb) (מְלֹאת / mê'lot) DAY~s (יְמֵי / yê'mey) CLEAN~her (טָהֳרָהּ / ta'ha'rah)

and thirty and three days she will settle in the bloodshed of the cleanliness, with all special things she will not touch, and she will not come to the sanctuary until the filling of the days of her cleanliness,

5 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) FEMALE (נְקֵבָה / nê'qey'vah) she~will~BRING.FORTH(Verb) (תֵלֵד / tey'leyd) and~she~did~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (וְטָמְאָה / wê'tam'ah) WEEK~s2 (שְׁבֻעַיִם / shê'vu'a'yim) like~REMOVAL~her (כְּנִדָּתָהּ / kê'ni'da'tah) and~SIX~s (וְשִׁשִּׁים / wê'shi'shim) DAY (יוֹם / yom) and~SIX (וְשֵׁשֶׁת / wê'shey'shet) DAY~s (יָמִים / ya'mim) she~will~SETTLE(Verb) (תֵּשֵׁב / tey'sheyv) UPON (עַל / al) BLOOD~s (דְּמֵי / dê'mey) CLEAN (טָהֳרָה / ta'ha'rah)

and if she will bring forth a female, then she will be dirty two weeks, like her removal, and sixty and six days she will settle upon the bloodshed of cleanliness,

6 and~in~>~FILL(Verb) (וּבִמְלֹאת / u'vim'lot) DAY~s (יְמֵי / yê'mey) CLEAN~her (טָהֳרָהּ / ta'ha'rah) to~SON (לְבֵן / lê'veyn) OR (אוֹ / o) to~DAUGHTER (לְבַת / lê'vat) she~will~make~COME(Verb) (תָּבִיא / ta'vi) SHEEP (כֶּבֶשׂ / ke'ves) SON (בֶּן / ben) YEAR~him (שְׁנָתוֹ / shê'na'to) to~ASCENSION.OFFERING (לְעֹלָה / lê'lah) and~SON (וּבֶן / u'ven) DOVE (יוֹנָה / yo'nah) OR (אוֹ / o) TURTLEDOVE (תֹר / tor) to~FAILURE (לְחַטָּאת / lê'hha'tat) TO (אֶל / el) OPENING (פֶּתַח / pe'tahh) TENT (אֹהֶל / o'hel) APPOINTED (מוֹעֵד / mo'eyd) TO (אֶל / el) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn)

and in the filling of the days of her cleanliness for a son or for a daughter, she will bring a year old sheep for a ascension offering, a son of a dove or a turtledove for the failure, to the opening of the appointed tent, to the administrator,

7 and~he~did~make~COME.NEAR(Verb)~him (וְהִקְרִיבוֹ / wê'hiq'ri'vo) to~FACE~s (לִפְנֵי / liph'ney) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~he~did~much~COVER(Verb) (וְכִפֶּר / wê'khi'per) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah) and~she~did~BE.CLEAN(Verb) (וְטָהֲרָה / wê'ta'ha'rah) from~FOUNTAIN (מִמְּקֹר / mim'qor) BLOOD~s~her (דָּמֶיהָ / da'mey'ah) THIS (זֹאת / zot) TEACHING (תּוֹרַת / to'rat) the~BRING.FORTH(Verb)~ing(fs) (הַיֹּלֶדֶת / hai'yo'le'det) to~the~MALE (לַזָּכָר / la'za'khar) OR (אוֹ / o) to~the~FEMALE (לַנְּקֵבָה / lan'qey'vah)

and he will bring him near to the face of YHWH, and he will make a covering upon her, and she will be clean from the fountain of her bloodshed, this is the teaching of the bringing forth for the male or for the female,

8 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) NOT (לֹא / lo) she~will~FIND(Verb) (תִמְצָא / tim'tsa) HAND~her (יָדָהּ / ya'dah) SUFFICIENT (דֵּי / dey) RAM (שֶׂה / seh) and~she~did~TAKE(Verb) (וְלָקְחָה / wê'laq'hhah) TWO (שְׁתֵּי / shê'tey) TURTLEDOVE~s (תֹרִים / to'rim) OR (אוֹ / o) TWO (שְׁנֵי / shê'ney) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) DOVE (יוֹנָה / yo'nah) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) to~ASCENSION.OFFERING (לְעֹלָה / lê'lah) and~UNIT (וְאֶחָד / wê'e'hhad) to~FAILURE (לְחַטָּאת / lê'hha'tat) and~he~did~much~COVER(Verb) (וְכִפֶּר / wê'khi'per) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~she~did~BE.CLEAN(Verb) (וְטָהֵרָה / wê'ta'hey'rah)

and if she does not find her hand sufficient with a ram, then she will take two turtledoves or two sons of a dove, one for the ascension offering and one for the failure, and the administrator will make a covering upon her and she will be clean,
