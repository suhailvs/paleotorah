Leviticus Chapter 25

1 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) in~HILL (בְּהַר / bê'har) Sinai (סִינַי / si'nai) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh in the hill of Sinai saying,

2 !(ms)~much~SPEAK(Verb) (דַּבֵּר / da'beyr) TO (אֶל / el) SON~s (בְּנֵי / bê'ney) Yisra'eyl (יִשְׂרָאֵל / yis'ra'eyl) and~you(ms)~did~SAY(Verb) (וְאָמַרְתָּ / wê'a'mar'ta) TO~them(m) (אֲלֵהֶם / a'ley'hem) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) you(mp)~will~COME(Verb) (תָבֹאוּ / ta'vo'u) TO (אֶל / el) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) GIVE(Verb)~ing(ms) (נֹתֵן / no'teyn) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) and~she~did~CEASE(Verb) (וְשָׁבְתָה / wê'shav'tah) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) CEASING (שַׁבָּת / sha'bat) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH)

Speak to the sons of Yisra'eyl and you will say to them, given that you will come to the land which I am giving to you, and the land will cease a ceasing for YHWH.

3 SIX (שֵׁשׁ / sheysh) YEAR~s (שָׁנִים / sha'nim) you(ms)~will~SOW(Verb) (תִּזְרַע / tiz'ra) FIELD~you(ms) (שָׂדֶךָ / sa'de'kha) and~SIX (וְשֵׁשׁ / wê'sheysh) YEAR~s (שָׁנִים / sha'nim) you(ms)~will~PLUCK(Verb) (תִּזְמֹר / tiz'mor) VINEYARD~you(ms) (כַּרְמֶךָ / kar'me'kha) and~you(ms)~did~GATHER(Verb) (וְאָסַפְתָּ / wê'a'saph'ta) AT (אֶת / et) PRODUCTION~her (תְּבוּאָתָהּ / tê'vu'a'tah)

Six years you will sow your field, and six years you will pluck your vineyard and you will gather her production,

4 and~in~the~YEAR (וּבַשָּׁנָה / u'va'sha'nah) the~SEVENTH (הַשְּׁבִיעִת / ha'she'vi'it) CEASING (שַׁבַּת / sha'bat) REST.PERIOD (שַׁבָּתוֹן / sha'ba'ton) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) to~the~LAND (לָאָרֶץ / la'a'rets) CEASING (שַׁבָּת / sha'bat) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) FIELD~you(ms) (שָׂדְךָ / sad'kha) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~SOW(Verb) (תִזְרָע / tiz'ra) and~VINEYARD~you(ms) (וְכַרְמְךָ / wê'khar'me'kha) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~PLUCK(Verb) (תִזְמֹר / tiz'mor)

and in the seventh year a ceasing rest period will exist for the land, a ceasing for YHWH, you will not sow your field, and you will not pluck your vineyard.

5 AT (אֵת / eyt) AFTER.GROWTH (סְפִיחַ / sê'phi'ahh) HARVEST~you(ms) (קְצִירְךָ / qê'tsir'kha) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~SEVER(Verb) (תִקְצוֹר / tiq'tsor) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) GRAPE~s (עִנְּבֵי / in'vey) DEDICATED~you(ms) (נְזִירֶךָ / nê'zi're'kha) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(ms)~will~FENCE.IN(Verb) (תִבְצֹר / tiv'tsor) YEAR (שְׁנַת / shê'nat) REST.PERIOD (שַׁבָּתוֹן / sha'ba'ton) he~will~EXIST(Verb) (יִהְיֶה / yih'yeh) to~the~LAND (לָאָרֶץ / la'a'rets)

You will not sever the after growth of your harvest, you will not fence in the grapes of your dedicated place, a year of a rest period will exist for the land,

6 and~she~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיְתָה / wê'hai'tah) CEASING (שַׁבַּת / sha'bat) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) to~FOOD (לְאָכְלָה / lê'akh'lah) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) and~to~SERVANT~you(ms) (וּלְעַבְדְּךָ / ul'av'de'kha) and~to~BONDWOMAN~you(ms) (וְלַאֲמָתֶךָ / wê'la'a'ma'te'kha) and~to~HIRELING~you(ms) (וְלִשְׂכִירְךָ / wê'lis'khir'kha) and~to~SETTLER~you(ms) (וּלְתוֹשָׁבְךָ / ul'to'shav'kha) the~IMMIGRATE(Verb)~ing(mp) (הַגָּרִים / ha'ga'rim) WITH~you(fs) (עִמָּךְ / i'makh)

and a ceasing of the land will exist for you for food, for you and for your servants and for your bondwoman and for your hireling and for your settlers immigrating with you,

7 and~to~BEAST~you(ms) (וְלִבְהֶמְתְּךָ / wê'liv'hem'te'kha) and~to~the~LIVING (וְלַחַיָּה / wê'la'hhai'yah) [755] WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) in~LAND~you(ms) (בְּאַרְצֶךָ / bê'ar'tse'kha) she~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִּהְיֶה / tih'yeh) ALL (כָל / khol) PRODUCTION~her (תְּבוּאָתָהּ / tê'vu'a'tah) to~>~EAT(Verb) (לֶאֱכֹל / le'e'khol)

and for your beast and for the living ones which are in your land, all of her production will exist for eating,

8 and~you(ms)~did~COUNT(Verb) (וְסָפַרְתָּ / wê'saph're'ta) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) SEVEN (שֶׁבַע / she'va) CEASING~s (שַׁבְּתֹת / sha'be'tot) YEAR~s (שָׁנִים / sha'nim) SEVEN (שֶׁבַע / she'va) YEAR~s (שָׁנִים / sha'nim) SEVEN (שֶׁבַע / she'va) FOOTSTEP~s (פְּעָמִים / pê'a'mim) and~they~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיוּ / wê'hai'u) to~~you(ms) (לְךָ / lê'kha) DAY~s (יְמֵי / yê'mey) SEVEN (שֶׁבַע / she'va) CEASING~s (שַׁבְּתֹת / sha'be'tot) the~YEAR~s (הַשָּׁנִים / ha'sha'nim) NINE (תֵּשַׁע / tey'sha) and~FOUR~s (וְאַרְבָּעִים / wê'ar'ba'im) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah)

and you will count for yourself seven ceasings of years, seven years seven times, and days of seven ceasings of years will exist for you, nine and forty years,

9 and~you(ms)~did~make~CROSS.OVER(Verb) (וְהַעֲבַרְתָּ / wê'ha'a'var'ta) RAM.HORN (שׁוֹפַר / sho'phar) SHOUT (תְּרוּעָה / tê'ru'ah) in~the~NEW.MOON (בַּחֹדֶשׁ / ba'hho'desh) the~SEVENTH (הַשְּׁבִעִי / hash'vi'i) in~TENTH.ONE (בֶּעָשׂוֹר / be'a'sor) to~the~NEW.MOON (לַחֹדֶשׁ / la'hho'desh) in~DAY (בְּיוֹם / bê'yom) the~ATONEMENT~s (הַכִּפֻּרִים / ha'ki'pu'rim) you(ms)~will~make~CROSS.OVER(Verb) (תַּעֲבִירוּ / ta'a'vi'ru) RAM.HORN (שׁוֹפָר / sho'phar) in~ALL (בְּכָל / bê'khol) LAND~you(mp) (אַרְצְכֶם / ar'tse'khem)

and you will make the ram horn of shouting to cross over on the tenth one of the seventh new moon, on the day of atonements you will make the ram horn cross over in all your land,

10 and~you(mp)~did~much~SET.APART(Verb) (וְקִדַּשְׁתֶּם / wê'qi'dash'tem) AT (אֵת / eyt) YEAR (שְׁנַת / shê'nat) the~FIVE~s (הַחֲמִשִּׁים / ha'hha'mi'shim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) and~you(mp)~did~CALL.OUT(Verb) (וּקְרָאתֶם / uq'ra'tem) FREE.FLOWING (דְּרוֹר / dê'ror) in~the~LAND (בָּאָרֶץ / ba'a'rets) to~ALL (לְכָל / lê'khol) SETTLE(Verb)~ing(mp)~her (יֹשְׁבֶיהָ / yosh'vey'ah) JUBILEE (יוֹבֵל / yo'veyl) SHE (הִוא / hi) she~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִּהְיֶה / tih'yeh) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) and~you(mp)~did~TURN.BACK(Verb) (וְשַׁבְתֶּם / wê'shav'tem) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) TO (אֶל / el) HOLDINGS~him (אֲחֻזָּתוֹ / a'hhu'za'to) and~MAN (וְאִישׁ / wê'ish) TO (אֶל / el) CLAN~him (מִשְׁפַּחְתּוֹ / mish'pahh'to) you(mp)~will~TURN.BACK(Verb) (תָּשֻׁבוּ / ta'shu'vu)

and you will set apart the year, the fiftieth year, and you will call out a free flowing in the land to all her settlers, she, she will exist for you as a jubilee, and you will turn back a man to his holdings, and a man to his clan you will turn back.

11 JUBILEE (יוֹבֵל / yo'veyl) SHE (הִוא / hi) YEAR (שְׁנַת / shê'nat) the~FIVE~s (הַחֲמִשִּׁים / ha'hha'mi'shim) YEAR (שָׁנָה / sha'nah) she~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִּהְיֶה / tih'yeh) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) NOT (לֹא / lo) you(mp)~will~SOW(Verb) (תִזְרָעוּ / tiz'ra'u) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(mp)~will~SEVER(Verb) (תִקְצְרוּ / tiq'tse'ru) AT (אֶת / et) AFTER.GROWTH~her (סְפִיחֶיהָ / sê'phi'hhey'ah) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(mp)~will~FENCE.IN(Verb) (תִבְצְרוּ / tiv'tse'ru) AT (אֶת / et) DEDICATED~s~her (נְזִרֶיהָ / nê'zi'rey'ah)

She is a jubilee year, the fiftieth year will exist for you, you will not sow, and you will not sever her after growth, and you will not fence in her dedicated places.

12 GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) JUBILEE (יוֹבֵל / yo'veyl) SHE (הִוא / hi) SPECIAL (קֹדֶשׁ / qo'desh) she~will~EXIST(Verb) (תִּהְיֶה / tih'yeh) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) FROM (מִן / min) the~FIELD (הַשָּׂדֶה / ha'sa'deh) you(mp)~will~EAT(Verb) (תֹּאכְלוּ / tokh'lu) AT (אֶת / et) PRODUCTION~her (תְּבוּאָתָהּ / tê'vu'a'tah)

Given that she is a special jubilee, she will exist for you, from the field you will eat her production.

13 in~YEAR (בִּשְׁנַת / bish'nat) the~JUBILEE (הַיּוֹבֵל / hai'yo'veyl) the~THIS (הַזֹּאת / ha'zot) you(mp)~will~TURN.BACK(Verb) (תָּשֻׁבוּ / ta'shu'vu) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) TO (אֶל / el) HOLDINGS~him (אֲחֻזָּתוֹ / a'hhu'za'to)

In the year of this jubilee you will turn a man to his holdings,

14 and~GIVEN.THAT (וְכִי / wê'khi) you(mp)~will~SELL(Verb) (תִמְכְּרוּ / tim'ke'ru) MERCHANDISE (מִמְכָּר / mim'kar) to~the~NEIGHBOR~you(ms) (לַעֲמִיתֶךָ / la'a'mi'te'kha) OR (אוֹ / o) >~PURCHASE(Verb) (קָנֹה / qa'noh) from~HAND (מִיַּד / mi'yad) NEIGHBOR~you(ms) (עֲמִיתֶךָ / a'mi'te'kha) DO.NOT (אַל / al) you(mp)~will~make~SUPPRESS(Verb) (תּוֹנוּ / to'nu) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) AT (אֶת / et) BROTHER~him (אָחִיו / a'hhiw)

and given that you will sell merchandise to your neighbor, or purchase from the hand of your neighbor, you will not make a man suppressed by his brother.

15 in~NUMBER (בְּמִסְפַּר / bê'mis'par) YEAR~s (שָׁנִים / sha'nim) AFTER (אַחַר / a'hhar) the~JUBILEE (הַיּוֹבֵל / hai'yo'veyl) you(ms)~will~PURCHASE(Verb) (תִּקְנֶה / tiq'neh) from~AT (מֵאֵת / mey'eyt) NEIGHBOR~you(ms) (עֲמִיתֶךָ / a'mi'te'kha) in~NUMBER (בְּמִסְפַּר / bê'mis'par) YEAR~s (שְׁנֵי / shê'ney) PRODUCTION~s (תְבוּאֹת / tê'vut) he~will~SELL(Verb) (יִמְכָּר / yim'kar) to~~you(fs) (לָךְ / lakh)

By the number of years after the jubilee you will purchase from your neighbor, by the number of years he will sell productions to you.

16 to~MOUTH (לְפִי / lê'phi) [756] ABUNDANCE (רֹב / rov) the~YEAR~s (הַשָּׁנִים / ha'sha'nim) you(ms)~will~make~INCREASE(Verb) (תַּרְבֶּה / tar'beh) ACQUIRED~him (מִקְנָתוֹ / miq'na'to) and~to~MOUTH (וּלְפִי / ul'phi) [757] >~BE.LESS(Verb) (מְעֹט / mê'ot) the~YEAR~s (הַשָּׁנִים / ha'sha'nim) you(ms)~will~make~BE.LESS(Verb) (תַּמְעִיט / tam'it) ACQUIRED~him (מִקְנָתוֹ / miq'na'to) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) NUMBER (מִסְפַּר / mis'par) PRODUCTION~s (תְּבוּאֹת / tê'vu'ot) HE (הוּא / hu) SELL(Verb)~ing(ms) (מֹכֵר / mo'kheyr) to~~you(fs) (לָךְ / lakh)

By the mouth of an abundance of years you will make his acquirings increase, and by the mouth of the lesser years you will make his acquirings less, given that the number of productions he is selling to you,

17 and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) you(mp)~will~make~SUPPRESS(Verb) (תוֹנוּ / to'nu) MAN (אִישׁ / ish) AT (אֶת / et) NEIGHBOR~him (עֲמִיתוֹ / a'mi'to) and~you(ms)~did~FEAR(Verb) (וְיָרֵאתָ / wê'ya'rey'ta) from~Elohiym~you(ms) (מֵאֱלֹהֶיךָ / mey'e'lo'hey'kha) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) I (אֲנִי / a'ni) YHWH (יְהוֹה / YHWH) Elohiym~you(mp) (אֱלֹהֵיכֶם / e'lo'hey'khem)

and you will not make a man suppressed by his neighbor, and you will fear your Elohiym, given that I am YHWH your Elohiym,

18 and~you(mp)~did~DO(Verb) (וַעֲשִׂיתֶם / wa'a'si'tem) AT (אֶת / et) CUSTOM~s~me (חֻקֹּתַי / hhu'qo'tai) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) DECISION~s~me (מִשְׁפָּטַי / mish'pa'tai) you(mp)~will~SAFEGUARD(Verb) (תִּשְׁמְרוּ / tish'me'ru) and~you(mp)~did~DO(Verb) (וַעֲשִׂיתֶם / wa'a'si'tem) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) and~you(mp)~did~SETTLE(Verb) (וִישַׁבְתֶּם / wi'shav'tem) UPON (עַל / al) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) to~the~SAFELY (לָבֶטַח / la've'tahh)

and you will do my customs, and my decisions you will safeguard, and you will do them, and you will settle upon the land in safety,

19 and~she~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתְנָה / wê'nat'nah) the~LAND (הָאָרֶץ / ha'a'rets) PRODUCE~her (פִּרְיָהּ / pir'yah) and~you(mp)~did~EAT(Verb) (וַאֲכַלְתֶּם / wa'a'khal'tem) to~SATISFACTION (לָשֹׂבַע / la'so'va) and~you(mp)~did~SETTLE(Verb) (וִישַׁבְתֶּם / wi'shav'tem) to~the~SAFELY (לָבֶטַח / la've'tahh) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah)

and the land will give her produce, and you will eat to satisfaction, and you will settle safely upon her.

20 and~GIVEN.THAT (וְכִי / wê'khi) you(mp)~will~SAY(Verb) (תֹאמְרוּ / to'me'ru) WHAT (מַה / mah) we~will~EAT(Verb) (נֹּאכַל / no'khal) in~the~YEAR (בַּשָּׁנָה / ba'sha'nah) the~SEVENTH (הַשְּׁבִיעִת / ha'she'vi'it) THOUGH (הֵן / heyn) NOT (לֹא / lo) we~will~SOW(Verb) (נִזְרָע / niz'ra) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) we~will~GATHER(Verb) (נֶאֱסֹף / ne'e'soph) AT (אֶת / et) PRODUCTION~s~him (תְּבוּאָתֵנוּ / tê'vu'a'tey'nu)

Given that you will say, what will we eat in the seventh year, though we will not sow and we will not gather his productions,

21 and~I~did~much~DIRECT(Verb) (וְצִוִּיתִי / wê'tsi'wi'ti) AT (אֶת / et) PRESENT~me (בִּרְכָתִי / bir'kha'ti) to~~you(mp) (לָכֶם / la'khem) in~the~YEAR (בַּשָּׁנָה / ba'sha'nah) the~SIXTH (הַשִּׁשִּׁית / ha'shi'shit) and~she~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשָׂת / wê'a'sat) AT (אֶת / et) the~PRODUCTION (הַתְּבוּאָה / hat'vu'ah) to~THREE (לִשְׁלֹשׁ / lish'losh) the~YEAR~s (הַשָּׁנִים / ha'sha'nim)

then I will direct my presents to you in the sixth year, and she will do the production for the three years,
