Leviticus Chapter 5

1 and~SOUL (וְנֶפֶשׁ / wê'ne'phesh) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) she~will~FAIL(Verb) (תֶחֱטָא / te'hhe'ta) and~she~did~HEAR(Verb) (וְשָׁמְעָה / wê'sham'ah) VOICE (קוֹל / qol) OATH (אָלָה / a'lah) and~HE (וְהוּא / wê'hu) [664] WITNESS (עֵד / eyd) OR (אוֹ / o) he~did~SEE(Verb) (רָאָה / ra'ah) OR (אוֹ / o) he~did~KNOW(Verb) (יָדָע / ya'da) IF (אִם / im) NOT (לוֹא / lo) he~will~make~BE.FACE.TO.FACE(Verb) (יַגִּיד / ya'gid) and~he~did~LIFT.UP(Verb) (וְנָשָׂא / wê'na'sa) TWISTEDNESS~him (עֲוֹנוֹ / a'o'no)

and a soul that will fail and hears the voice of an oath and he is a witness, whether he saw or knew, if he will not tell, then he will lift up his twistedness.

2 OR (אוֹ / o) SOUL (נֶפֶשׁ / ne'phesh) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) she~will~TOUCH(Verb) (תִּגַּע / ti'ga) in~ALL (בְּכָל / bê'khol) WORD (דָּבָר / da'var) [665] DIRTY (טָמֵא / ta'mey) OR (אוֹ / o) in~CARCASS (בְנִבְלַת / vê'niv'lat) LIVING (חַיָּה / hhai'yah) [666] DIRTY (טְמֵאָה / tê'mey'ah) OR (אוֹ / o) in~CARCASS (בְּנִבְלַת / bê'niv'lat) BEAST (בְּהֵמָה / bê'hey'mah) DIRTY (טְמֵאָה / tê'mey'ah) OR (אוֹ / o) in~CARCASS (בְּנִבְלַת / bê'niv'lat) SWARMER (שֶׁרֶץ / she'rets) DIRTY (טָמֵא / ta'mey) and~he~did~be~BE.OUT.OF.SIGHT(Verb) (וְנֶעְלַם / wê'ne'lam) FROM~him (מִמֶּנּוּ / mi'me'nu) and~HE (וְהוּא / wê'hu) DIRTY (טָמֵא / ta'mey) and~he~did~BE.GUILTY(Verb) (וְאָשֵׁם / wê'a'sheym)

Or a soul which will touch any dirty thing, or a carcass of a dirty living one, or a carcass of a dirty beast, or the carcass of a dirty swarmer, and he was out of sight from him, then he is dirty and he will be guilty.

3 OR (אוֹ / o) GIVEN.THAT (כִי / khi) he~will~TOUCH(Verb) (יִגַּע / yiga) in~DIRTY (בְּטֻמְאַת / bê'tum'at) HUMAN (אָדָם / a'dam) to~ALL (לְכֹל / lê'khol) DIRTY~him (טֻמְאָתוֹ / tu'm'a'to) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~BE.DIRTY(Verb) (יִטְמָא / yit'ma) in~~her (בָּהּ / bah) and~he~did~be~BE.OUT.OF.SIGHT(Verb) (וְנֶעְלַם / wê'ne'lam) FROM~him (מִמֶּנּוּ / mi'me'nu) and~HE (וְהוּא / wê'hu) he~did~KNOW(Verb) (יָדַע / ya'da) and~he~did~BE.GUILTY(Verb) (וְאָשֵׁם / wê'a'sheym)

Or if he will touch a dirty human, for all of his dirtiness, which he is dirty for, and he will be out of sight from him, and he knew, then he will be guilty.

4 OR (אוֹ / o) SOUL (נֶפֶשׁ / ne'phesh) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) she~will~be~SWEAR(Verb) (תִשָּׁבַע / ti'sha'va) to~>~much~UTTER(Verb) (לְבַטֵּא / lê'va'tey) in~LIP~s2 (בִשְׂפָתַיִם / vis'pha'ta'yim) to~>~make~BE.DYSFUNCTIONAL(Verb) (לְהָרַע / lê'ha'ra) OR (אוֹ / o) to~>~make~DO.WELL(Verb) (לְהֵיטִיב / lê'hey'tiv) to~ALL (לְכֹל / lê'khol) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~will~much~UTTER(Verb) (יְבַטֵּא / yê'va'tey) the~HUMAN (הָאָדָם / ha'a'dam) in~SWEARING (בִּשְׁבֻעָה / bish'vu'ah) and~he~did~be~BE.OUT.OF.SIGHT(Verb) (וְנֶעְלַם / wê'ne'lam) FROM~him (מִמֶּנּוּ / mi'me'nu) and~HE (וְהוּא / wê'hu) he~did~KNOW(Verb) (יָדַע / ya'da) and~he~did~BE.GUILTY(Verb) (וְאָשֵׁם / wê'a'sheym) to~UNIT (לְאַחַת / lê'a'hhat) from~THESE (מֵאֵלֶּה / mey'ey'leh)

Or a soul that will swear by uttering with lips to make dysfunctional or make well, for all which the human will utter with a swearing, and he be out of sight from him, and he knew, then he will be guilty to one of these,

5 and~he~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיָה / wê'hai'yah) GIVEN.THAT (כִי / khi) he~will~BE.GUILTY(Verb) (יֶאְשַׁם / ye'e'sham) to~UNIT (לְאַחַת / lê'a'hhat) from~THESE (מֵאֵלֶּה / mey'ey'leh) and~he~did~self~THROW.THE.HAND(Verb) (וְהִתְוַדָּה / wê'hit'wa'dah) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~FAIL(Verb) (חָטָא / hha'ta) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah)

and it will come to pass when he is guilty to one of these, and he will confess what he failed upon her,

6 and~he~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבִיא / wê'hey'vi) AT (אֶת / et) GUILT~him (אֲשָׁמוֹ / a'sha'mo) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) UPON (עַל / al) FAILURE~him (חַטָּאתוֹ / hha'ta'to) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~FAIL(Verb) (חָטָא / hha'ta) FEMALE (נְקֵבָה / nê'qey'vah) FROM (מִן / min) the~FLOCKS (הַצֹּאן / ha'tson) SHEEP (כִּשְׂבָּה / kis'bah) OR (אוֹ / o) HAIRY.GOAT (שְׂעִירַת / sê'i'rat) GOAT~s (עִזִּים / i'zim) to~FAILURE (לְחַטָּאת / lê'hha'tat) and~he~did~much~COVER(Verb) (וְכִפֶּר / wê'khi'per) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) from~FAILURE~him (מֵחַטָּאתוֹ / mey'hha'ta'to)

and he will bring his guilt to YHWH because of his failure, which he failed, a female from the flocks of sheep or a hairy goat of the goats for the failure, and the administrator will make a covering upon him because of his failure,

7 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) NOT (לֹא / lo) she~will~make~TOUCH(Verb) (תַגִּיע / ta'gi) [667] HAND~him (יָדוֹ / ya'do) SUFFICIENT (דֵּי / dey) RAM (שֶׂה / seh) and~he~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבִיא / wê'hey'vi) AT (אֶת / et) GUILT~him (אֲשָׁמוֹ / a'sha'mo) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~FAIL(Verb) (חָטָא / hha'ta) TWO (שְׁתֵּי / shê'tey) TURTLEDOVE~s (תֹרִים / to'rim) OR (אוֹ / o) TWO (שְׁנֵי / shê'ney) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) DOVE (יוֹנָה / yo'nah) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) UNIT (אֶחָד / e'hhad) to~FAILURE (לְחַטָּאת / lê'hha'tat) and~UNIT (וְאֶחָד / wê'e'hhad) to~ASCENSION.OFFERING (לְעֹלָה / lê'lah)

and if his hand cannot sufficiently touch a ram, then he will bring his guilt, which he failed, two turtledoves or two sons of the dove to YHWH, one for a failure and one for a ascension offering,

8 and~he~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבִיא / wê'hey'vi) AT~them(m) (אֹתָם / o'tam) TO (אֶל / el) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~he~did~make~COME.NEAR(Verb) (וְהִקְרִיב / wê'hiq'riv) AT (אֶת / et) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) to~the~FAILURE (לַחַטָּאת / la'hha'tat) FIRST (רִאשׁוֹנָה / ri'sho'nah) and~he~did~SNAP.OFF(Verb) (וּמָלַק / u'ma'laq) AT (אֶת / et) HEAD~him (רֹאשׁוֹ / ro'sho) from~FOREFRONT (מִמּוּל / mi'mul) NECK~him (עָרְפּוֹ / ar'po) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~will~make~SEPARATE(Verb) (יַבְדִּיל / yav'dil)

and he will bring them to the administrator, and he will bring near what is for the failure first, and he will snap off his head from the forefront of his neck and he will not separate it,

9 and~he~did~make~SPATTER(Verb) (וְהִזָּה / wê'hi'zah) from~BLOOD (מִדַּם / mi'dam) the~FAILURE (הַחַטָּאת / ha'hha'tat) UPON (עַל / al) WALL (קִיר / qir) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) and~the~be~REMAIN(Verb)~ing(ms) (וְהַנִּשְׁאָר / wê'ha'nish'ar) in~the~BLOOD (בַּדָּם / ba'dam) he~will~be~DRAIN(Verb) (יִמָּצֵה / yi'ma'tseyh) TO (אֶל / el) BOTTOM.BASE (יְסוֹד / yê'sod) the~ALTAR (הַמִּזְבֵּחַ / ha'miz'bey'ahh) FAILURE (חַטָּאת / hha'tat) HE (הוּא / hu)

and he will spatter from the blood of the failure upon the wall of the altar, and the remaining blood will be drained to the bottom base of the altar, he is the failure,

10 and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) the~SECOND (הַשֵּׁנִי / ha'shey'ni) he~will~DO(Verb) (יַעֲשֶׂה / ya'a'seh) ASCENSION.OFFERING (עֹלָה / o'lah) like~the~DECISION (כַּמִּשְׁפָּט / ka'mish'pat) and~he~did~much~COVER(Verb) (וְכִפֶּר / wê'khi'per) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) from~FAILURE~him (מֵחַטָּאתוֹ / mey'hha'ta'to) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~FAIL(Verb) (חָטָא / hha'ta) and~he~did~be~FORGIVE(Verb) (וְנִסְלַח / wê'nis'lahh) to~~him (לוֹ / lo)

and he will do the second as a ascension offering, according to the decision, and the administrator will make a covering upon him because of his failure, which he failed, and he will be forgiven for him,

11 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) NOT (לֹא / lo) she~will~make~OVERTAKE(Verb) (תַשִּׂיג / ta'sig) [668] HAND~him (יָדוֹ / ya'do) to~TWO (לִשְׁתֵּי / lish'tey) TURTLEDOVE~s (תֹרִים / to'rim) OR (אוֹ / o) to~TWO (לִשְׁנֵי / lish'ney) SON~s (בְנֵי / vê'ney) DOVE (יוֹנָה / yo'nah) and~he~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבִיא / wê'hey'vi) AT (אֶת / et) DONATION~him (קָרְבָּנוֹ / qar'ba'no) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~FAIL(Verb) (חָטָא / hha'ta) TENTH (עֲשִׂירִת / a'si'rit) the~EYPHAH (הָאֵפָה / ha'ey'phah) FLOUR (סֹלֶת / so'let) to~FAILURE (לְחַטָּאת / lê'hha'tat) NOT (לֹא / lo) he~will~PLACE(Verb) (יָשִׂים / ya'sim) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah) OIL (שֶׁמֶן / she'men) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~will~GIVE(Verb) (יִתֵּן / yi'teyn) UPON~her (עָלֶיהָ / a'ley'ah) FRANKINCENSE (לְבֹנָה / lê'vo'nah) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) FAILURE (חַטָּאת / hha'tat) SHE (הִיא / hi)

and if his hand is not able to reach two turtledoves or two sons of the dove, then he will bring his donation, because he failed, a tenth of an eyphah of flour for the failure, he will not place upon her frankincense given that she is the failure,

12 and~he~did~make~COME(Verb)~her (וֶהֱבִיאָהּ / we'he'vi'ah) TO (אֶל / el) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) and~he~did~GRASP(Verb) (וְקָמַץ / wê'qa'mats) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) FROM~her (מִמֶּנָּה / mi'me'nah) FILLING (מְלוֹא / mê'lo) HANDFUL~him (קֻמְצוֹ / qum'tso) AT (אֶת / et) MEMORIAL~her (אַזְכָּרָתָה / az'ka'ra'tah) and~he~did~make~BURN.INCENSE(Verb) (וְהִקְטִיר / wê'hiq'tir) the~ALTAR~unto (הַמִּזְבֵּחָה / ha'miz'bey'hhah) UPON (עַל / al) FIRE.OFFERING~s (אִשֵּׁי / i'shey) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) FAILURE (חַטָּאת / hha'tat) SHE (הִוא / hi)

and he will bring her to the administrator, and the administrator will grasp from her a filling of his handful, it is a memorial, and he will burn it as incense upon the altar upon the fire offerings of YHWH, she is the failure,

13 and~he~did~much~COVER(Verb) (וְכִפֶּר / wê'khi'per) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) the~ADMINISTRATOR (הַכֹּהֵן / ha'ko'heyn) UPON (עַל / al) FAILURE~him (חַטָּאתוֹ / hha'ta'to) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~FAIL(Verb) (חָטָא / hha'ta) from~UNIT (מֵאַחַת / mey'a'hhat) from~THESE (מֵאֵלֶּה / mey'ey'leh) and~he~did~be~FORGIVE(Verb) (וְנִסְלַח / wê'nis'lahh) to~~him (לוֹ / lo) and~she~did~EXIST(Verb) (וְהָיְתָה / wê'hai'tah) to~the~ADMINISTRATOR (לַכֹּהֵן / la'ko'heyn) like~the~DEPOSIT (כַּמִּנְחָה / ka'min'hhah)

and the administrator will make a covering upon him, upon his failure, because he failed, from one of these, and he will be forgiven for him, and she will exist for the administrator as the deposit,

14 and~he~will~much~SPEAK(Verb) (וַיְדַבֵּר / wai'da'beyr) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) TO (אֶל / el) Mosheh (מֹשֶׁה / mo'sheh) to~>~SAY(Verb) (לֵּאמֹר / ley'mor)

and YHWH spoke to Mosheh saying,

15 SOUL (נֶפֶשׁ / ne'phesh) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) she~will~TRANSGRESS(Verb) (תִמְעֹל / tim'ol) TRANSGRESSION (מַעַל / ma'al) and~FAILURE (וְחָטְאָה / wê'hhat'ah) in~ERROR (בִּשְׁגָגָה / bish'ga'gah) from~SPECIAL~s (מִקָּדְשֵׁי / mi'qad'shey) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) and~he~did~make~COME(Verb) (וְהֵבִיא / wê'hey'vi) AT (אֶת / et) GUILT~him (אֲשָׁמוֹ / a'sha'mo) to~YHWH (לַיהוָה / la'YHWH) BUCK (אַיִל / a'yil) WHOLE (תָּמִים / ta'mim) FROM (מִן / min) the~FLOCKS (הַצֹּאן / ha'tson) in~ARRANGEMENT~you(ms) (בְּעֶרְכְּךָ / bê'er'ke'kha) SILVER (כֶּסֶף / ke'seph) SHEQEL~s (שְׁקָלִים / shê'qa'lim) in~SHEQEL (בְּשֶׁקֶל / bê'she'qel) the~SPECIAL (הַקֹּדֶשׁ / ha'qo'desh) to~GUILT (לְאָשָׁם / lê'a'sham)

A soul that will transgress a transgression and is a failure in error from the special ones of YHWH, then he will bring his guilt to YHWH, a whole buck from the flocks, with your arrangement of silver sheqels, with the special sheqel for the guilt,

16 and~AT (וְאֵת / wê'eyt) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) he~did~FAIL(Verb) (חָטָא / hha'ta) FROM (מִן / min) the~SPECIAL (הַקֹּדֶשׁ / ha'qo'desh) he~will~much~MAKE.RESTITUTION(Verb) (יְשַׁלֵּם / yê'sha'leym) and~AT (וְאֶת / wê'et) FIFTH~him (חֲמִישִׁתוֹ / hha'mi'shi'to) he~will~make~ADD(Verb) (יוֹסֵף / yo'seyph) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) and~he~did~GIVE(Verb) (וְנָתַן / wê'na'tan) AT~him (אֹתוֹ / o'to) to~the~ADMINISTRATOR (לַכֹּהֵן / la'ko'heyn) and~the~ADMINISTRATOR (וְהַכֹּהֵן / wê'ha'ko'heyn) he~will~much~COVER(Verb) (יְכַפֵּר / yê'kha'peyr) UPON~him (עָלָיו / a'law) in~BUCK (בְּאֵיל / bê'eyl) the~GUILT (הָאָשָׁם / ha'a'sham) and~he~did~be~FORGIVE(Verb) (וְנִסְלַח / wê'nis'lahh) to~~him (לוֹ / lo)

and he will make restitution for when he failed by the special thing, and he will cause to add his fifth upon him, and he will give him to the administrator, and the administrator will make a covering upon him with the buck of the guilt, and he will be forgiven for him,

17 and~IF (וְאִם / wê'im) SOUL (נֶפֶשׁ / ne'phesh) GIVEN.THAT (כִּי / ki) she~will~FAIL(Verb) (תֶחֱטָא / te'hhe'ta) and~she~did~DO(Verb) (וְעָשְׂתָה / wê'as'tah) UNIT (אַחַת / a'hhat) from~ALL (מִכָּל / mi'kol) DIRECTIVE~s (מִצְוֹת / mits'ot) YHWH (יְהוָה / YHWH) WHICH (אֲשֶׁר / a'sher) NOT (לֹא / lo) they(f)~will~be~DO(Verb) (תֵעָשֶׂינָה / tey'a'sey'nah) and~NOT (וְלֹא / wê'lo) he~did~KNOW(Verb) (יָדַע / ya'da) and~he~did~BE.GUILTY(Verb) (וְאָשֵׁם / wê'a'sheym) and~he~did~LIFT.UP(Verb) (וְנָשָׂא / wê'na'sa) TWISTEDNESS~him (עֲוֹנוֹ / a'o'no)

and if a soul that failed and did one of any of the directives of YHWH, which was not to be done, and he did not know, then he will be guilty and he will lift up his twistedness,
